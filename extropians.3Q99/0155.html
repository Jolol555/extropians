I am pleased to announce the launch of a full service web site for those excited about the ideas of Ayn Rand: The Daily Objectivist at dailyobjectivist.com TDO has news of special interest to Objectivism, including announcements and coverage of meetings, lectures, and conferences. We also have feature works of theoretical and applied Objectivism, with at least one new feature a day. Unlike most Objectivist web sites, TDO is not an hobby of the site owner, full of personal reflections, nor is it just a listing of sites that would be of interest to Objectivists, nor is it a sterile organization site which must play it safe. TDO is here to satisfy the preferences of you!

Some features of particular interest are: poems and short stories, book and movie reviews, hero of day, love advice column, and philosophical consulting column.

TDO's primary goal is to constantly supply the best of Objectivist news and ideas to those committed to reality, reason, individual rights, and capitalism. Please visit and bookmark dailyobjectivist.com very soon. We are working hard to encourage your flourishing.
