Hello everyone,

I have really enjoyed the discussion between Natasha and Muse about the nature of art for itself and as a vocation. With all the engineering talk around here it is nice to hear from people who are interested both in art and science. I realize that person grounded in the "hard sciences" such as engineering could still be very artistic or at least appreciative of such and so do not wish to stereotype. I just found it very refreshing to have the subject of art, self and culture discussed in a prominent way.

My question for everyone is were you to come into a great deal of money and create your own club/restaurant in the present day how would you go about it?? The building architecture? What would the interior decor be like? The dress of employees?

And let's say you were reanimated after a long cryonic sleep in the early 22nd century only to learn the investments you owned while in storage had made you very rich! With the incredible technologies(and changed social mores) available then how would you create your dream club??

I look forward to the replies! Considering the imaginations and intellects here it could be indeed interesting!
