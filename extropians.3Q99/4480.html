I can only assume that your experience with mailing lists is very limited, because the ExI lists are in fact more free from this kind of mail than most. The fact is, the internet is /full/ of crap like this, because the world is full of idiots who believe it. I personally get about 5-10 such emails a day, only rarely from the lists here.

As in most security problems in real life, the draconian measures required to eradicate it completely would also stifle too much useful discussion. So most sensible admins take reasonable measures to cut down on the crap (if they didn't you'd get 10 times as much as you get now), and learn to live with the rest. That's one price of freedom: putting up with some crap you don't like.
