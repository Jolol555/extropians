For myself, as a mainframe programmer I'm always on the CLI. In micros (I was part of the POS project that bought the first PC sold out the IBM product center in my city) I stuck with DOS until Win3.0, at that point I saw functionality that had previously only been available on SPARC stations start to become a reality on the PC and became a
(reluctant) believer.

At this point I really don't care too much, the fact that Windows (tm) is a bloated mess, likely impossible for any single human to understand, _it works_, it gives me what I want at the moment and I'm more than willing to throw hardware at it as long as the hardware is cheap since the alternative is to wait (possibly for a long time) to get the same capabilities in properly optimized code.
