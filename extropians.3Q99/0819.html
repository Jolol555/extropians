"People Who Died" by Jim Carrol Band. That's another candidate for an extropian music compilation. It's a punk rock ditty that lists a bunch of friends of the writer who all died for one stupid reason or another. Irrevereant jab, totally devoid of any hint that there is a higher meaning to death, unless that meaning is "death sucks".
