it is not flawed, but it is merely in a different state. A waltz, as sheet music and recorded dance steps, is, by definition, a waltz, but it is in a potential or static state, while the performed waltz is also a waltz, but in a kinetic state. Saying that one or the other is not a waltz is like saying that a mere seed or sprout is not an oak tree, merely because it is not 20 meters tall.
