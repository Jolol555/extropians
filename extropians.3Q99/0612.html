Intelligences that are not based on anything that we recognize as intelligent would probably be missed by us anyway. We could indeed be living amongst such entities - who is to say that granite is not a life form beyond our ken, whose intelligence is so fundamentally different from ours that we mistake it for inanimate rock?

Probably there is a good pragmatic reason for concentrating on lifeforms like us (if they exist), which is that we have some hope of detection and useful communication with such forms. The Search for Extra Terrestrial Granite is a dead end.
