I'm not impressed that the FBI would doctor the FLIR in such a way that frames themselves for mass murder since expert analysis confirms that they are not sunlight reflections but gun-muzzle flashes. Here's another expert analysis of the flashes: http://www.rolandresearch.com/SRGv1/B9-Report.htm

But even if I was, the bottom line is the facts in the case, since someone could confess to a crime that wasn't committed.
