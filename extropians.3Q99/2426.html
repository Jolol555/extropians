I would like to invite you to my book signing for the 2nd edition of _Create/Recreate: The Third Millennial Culture_ 

                               TECHNO 2000
	NEW PARAMETERS FOR A CONSCIOUS FUTURE

Forum and book signing in Malibu: Saturday, Aug. 21, 3pm Supercare Drugstore, 3898 Cross Creek Road, Malibu 90265, next door to The Wherehouse at Cross Creek Plaza

Moderated by Astrophysicist, Author and Musician Dr. Fiorella Terenzi

We invite you to join us, as authors gather together for an afternoon of theorizing and socializing. Nine transformative writers, in the fields of Space Science, Biology, Alchemy and Communications Technology - plus some Science Fiction and Paintings of Imagination - unite in a forum to bridge technology with spirituality, art with science, and space with music.

The Techno 2000 forum will also be presented live-on-air Friday, August 20, 11pm, as a one-hour special on "Hour 25" hosted by Warren James on KPFK radio, 90.7 FM.

INVITED AUTHORS

  1. Jerry Pournelle, "Starswarm" (TOR Books)
  2. Larry Niven, "Rainbow Mars" (TOR Books)
  3. Carolyn Kleefeld, "The Alchemy of Possibility" (Merril-West)
  4. Dr. Fiorella Terenzi "Heavenly Knowledge" (Avon Books)
  5. Stanislas Klossowski De Rola, "The Golden Game" (Thames and Hudson)
  6. Natasha Vita-More, "Create/Recreate" (More Art)
  7. Francis Jeffrey, "John Lilly so far...." (Tarcher-Penguin Putnam)
  8. Andy Lakey & Keith Richardson ( to be confirmed)

Party Location: 25160 B Malibu Road at 7pm RSVP: via e-mail at Fterenzi@aol.com
