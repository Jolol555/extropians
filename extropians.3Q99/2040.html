Dear fellow transhumanists,

it is a great pleasure for me to announce that De:Trans, the recently founded German Transhumanist Society, has launched its official website: http://www.transhumanismus.de/

It is intended to serve as a German language source of information about transhumanism and as a contact for German speaking people who are interested in joining us.

Content and design related suggestions and rational criticism are highly appreciated, since the site is still under construction.

Thank you,
Frank
