I am sending out this message as a call for papers from fellow professionals in the area of future studies to share their vision and ideas with professionals in the area of management of technology. The International Association of Management of Technology is holding its millennium conference from Feb.22-Feb.25, 2000 at Miami where a spl session has been planned on the theme "Technology Futures and Future of Technology". As coordinator I would like to extend invitation to all fellow futurists to submit papers for this session and share your vision with other professionals. The selected papers will be refreed for publication.The abstracts or the full papers may be sent directly to me via email <akr2650@netscape.net>

To know more about the Association and the conference pl click search IAMOT2000 on any search engine.
I would appreciate if this information is circulated amongst other members interested in the topic.

with best wishes and hope for good response,
