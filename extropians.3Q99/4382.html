Tides on earth are caused by both the sun and moon, though the moon's effect dominates. If the moon were removed, tides would not entirely stop.

> earths orbit would get really funky,

Because of the moon, the path of the earth in orbit is wobbly, like a rosette with 12 petals. If the moon were gone, the earth's orbit would be a much smoother ellipse.
