From: Mises Institute News <news@mises.org> Date: Friday, July 16, 1999 7:29 AM
Subject: Liberalism in Russian
Ludwig von Mises's great 1927 book Liberalism was translated into Russian in 1995, and now the full text is available on web:

http://www.libertarium.ru/libertarium/l_libmises_mises

The Mises Institute is working with several Russian scholars for translations of other works in the Austrian tradition.

The English version of Liberalism is available for $14.95 through the Mises Institute's on-line book store:

http://www.mises.org/product.asp?sku=B108
