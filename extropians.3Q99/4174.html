Somewhere in our extended network must be someone who knows someone who knows Arnold (after all the Web network is bigger than the people network -- I think its 17-19 clicks to any page on the web and only 9 personal relationship hops to any person in the world). If we can identify the shortest path we might be able to float the idea to Arnold.

Lets put our thinking caps on.
