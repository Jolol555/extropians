> Does anybody knows about anti-matter propulsion?
> What exactly anti-matter means/is?

Anti-matter is simply matter with a reverse time dimension. Every particle has an anti-particle that behaves in reverse the same way the particle does forward in time. A proton and anti-proton collide and they eliminate each other, releasing the entire energy of both their masses.
