This is a lot of fun. Someone took one of the psychological measures of gender and wrote it up as a questionnaire that anyone can take to find out where they fall on the spectrum of gender. (Although it is posted in a women's topics section of a website, it really does apply to anyone. The questions don't relate specifically to men or women) You can also access a statistical chart that will tell you how many other participants scored in your section.
