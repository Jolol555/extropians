$1
$10
$1 Hundred
 $1 Thousand
 $1 Million
 $1 Billion
 $1 Trillion
 $1 Quadrillion
 $1 Pentillion
 $1 Hexillion
 $1 Septillion

This sort of game should replace the lottery.
