There is no doubt that John Stossel is about the best journalist out there. He is strong, witty, and informative. He has been around since I was a little girl and as I have grown so has he. He is now the foremost journalist for what I consider "rational ideas." He isn't afraid to go out and stand against the proclaimed U.S. majority on just about every issue. For those of you know of his work, I encourage you to encourage others to get to know him. If you don't know about Stossel, it is time well spent just to learn and do what you can to get your hands on his ABC special
"Junk Science & Are We Scaring Ourselves To Death" They are. excellent.

With all this said, Stossel is now taking his greatness to the class room. He will be providing videos and other class room materials for discussions of his now well known ABCnews specials. If you are interested in supporting Stossel or just getting to know more about his new project you can do so at:

http://www.stossel.org/

If you have specific questions about Stossel or his new ventures feel free to contact me directly.

Challenge me!
