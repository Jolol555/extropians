NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Dec. 15, 1999--

Searching for the secret to happiness? You'll
find it in the USA, Denmark and Australia,
which rank as the world's "happiest"
countries, a new study shows.

Saying they are "very happy" about their
overall quality of life, citizens of the USA
(46%), Australia (47%) and Denmark (49%)
rank at the top of the "global happiness
barometer," a survey of 22,500 adults ages 18+
years in 22 countries. The study was
conducted by Roper Starch Worldwide, a
global marketing research and consulting
firm.
