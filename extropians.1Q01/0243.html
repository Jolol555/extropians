Three quick announcements related to Nathaniel Branden's web site
(http://www.nathanielbranden.com).

(1) An announcement-only e-mailing list has been created to let people know
when new essays and such are added to the site. This is a low-volume list;
you won't receive more than one message per week from the list.

You can either subscribe through the form at the web site, or by sending an
e-mail to majordomo@nathanielbranden.com with the text "subscribe
nb-announce-l" in the body of the message.

(2) A discussion board for the ideas of Nathaniel Branden has just been
added to the web site. You can find it at:

http://www.nathanielbranden.com/dis/index.shtml

(3) Three new essays are available:

http://www.nathanielbranden.com/ess/ess08.html: "Success Secrets of Leaders
Today" explores the psychological attitudes required to be a successful
leader today and gives strategies for developing those attitudes.

http://www.nathanielbranden.com/ess/ess07.html: "Healthy Self-Esteem"
explains the fundamental elements of self-esteem.

http://www.nathanielbranden.com/ess/ess06.html: "All About Responsibility"
discusses the basics of self-responsibility.

diana.
