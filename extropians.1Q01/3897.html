For some odd reason, I was thinking about the Arizona State Fair yesterday.
My family and I really like going to the fair - it's a great place to people
watch and I especially enjoy the myriad booths hawking everything from
Your-Name-On-A-Grain-Of-Rice to Eternal-Salvation(Haleluljah!) to political
booths of every flavor. I began wondering, "Wouldn't it be cool if we had
an Extropy booth at the state fair?" Then I began trying to imagine what
such a thing might would contain... Perhaps it could be a take off on one
of the Haleluljah booths - we could give away little cards with the
Extropian Principles and maybe sell Darwin fishes. I dunno... I kind of
drew a blank... I think it's a good idea, but what *would* an Extropy booth
look like?

Anyone interested in doing such a thing? I'd be willing to volunteer time
if we had a great idea/theme/whatever...
