At 12:03am -0800 2/25/01, Jeff Davis wrote:
>Thanks, Harvey. By the way, I've been trying to keep up with the recount,
>but I've had difficulty finding reports. Is there a clearinghouse, a good
>site for a compendium? If not, how about some individual links?

One site I found which is attempting to cover the issue on an ongoing
basis is http://www.voter.com/home/. Confusingly, http://www.voter.com/
claims that the site is shutting down, but the first address appears to be
still operational.

They have a link on their front page (near the bottom) to ongoing coverage
of the recounts. Unfortunately many of their links from that story are
to the Miami Herald, which recently reorganized its website and broke all
the links to its archives. The Herald is also charging money to read its
archived articles so it is not a very convenient source. However some of
the links are to non-Herald articles and those still work.

Hopefully this site will continue to track and update the links as the
story develops.
