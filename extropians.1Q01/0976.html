Additional thought:

You are right that "who do you trust?" becomes ever more important as
power concentrates. As I never tire of repeating, even something as
rudimentary as Axelrod's _The Evolution of Cooperation_
(http://pscs.physics.lsa.umich.edu/Software/CC/ECHome.html if you're
lazy) points out that rough parity between players is essential for
cooperation to be a successful, evolutionarily stable strategy.
