                           Hooking Up
                            Tom Wolfe
                       ISBN 0-374-10382-8

Okay, I know your wondering what I'm doing recommending this on
this list, but we don't have to be technical all the time. This is
Tom's look at American life as we enter the new millenium, very
interesting. Typical Tom, I find myself reaching for a GOOD
dictionary, or doing Google searches every other page or so. (so I
didn't know what a filofax was...)

I really like this book (so far), Tom Wolfe is kind of like William
F. Buckley to me; I may not agree with them all the time, but I
love the eloquence and style. And it's odd, but the older I get the
smarter they get.....

A darn good read.
