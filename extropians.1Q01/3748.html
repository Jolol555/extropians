Hi all,

I am pleased to announce that from now until March 11th 2001, Extropy
Institute is offering a 25% discount on all Extropy Institute Membership
and Benefactor orders to members of the extropians list. This means that
for a limited time, a regular membership will only cost $41.25, a foreign
(outside USA) membership will cost $30 (USD), and a Benefactor order will
cost $225 in its first year.

To become an Extropy Institute Member, you can either use our web-based
form at http://www.extropy.org/join.htm with the words "List Discount
Regular Membership," "List Discount Foreign Membership," or "List Discount
Benefactor Order" in the comments field; otherwise, you can send an order
with your details to exi-info@extropy.org. In addition to the regular
benefits, an Extropy Institute membership now will get you a substantially
reduced rate on the forthcoming Extro-5 Conference, on which more
information will be provided in the next day or so.

                                                Thanks!

                                                        -E. Shaun Russell
