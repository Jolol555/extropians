I just realized that a logical tree structure or path-name would
useful. This would allow the reader to determine which subtopic is
being discussed, find the relationship between topics, and filter on
whole categories.

Thus, a flame about the recent elections could have the prefix of
"POLITICS/GOVERNMENT/PROCESS/ELECTION/PRESIDENTIAL/2000/STATE/FLORIDA/BALLOT/SUBTYPE/BUTTERFLY/COMMENT/NEGATIVE/REPLY/LONGISH:"

What could be more user-friendly?
