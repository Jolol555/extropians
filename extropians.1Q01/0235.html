A few nights ago I was fascinated to see a brief interview with Bill Joy on CBS evening news. The topic was "robotic artificial intelligence" and a brief overview was given of some advances in this area. Then Bill Joy was shown(I had never seen him before) and he summed up his quick comments with "what humanity can do, it will do, and so humanity will destroy itself with these new technologies!" For such a supposedly intelligent man I was taken aback by his statement. By his own flawed logic we should have already destroyed ourselves with nuclear weapons.
