FOX television network recently aired a program promoting
the conspiracy theory that NASA's Apollo Moon missions were
actually a hoax staged on Earth -- man never really set foot
on the Moon. This theory was largely supported by allegations
of "anomalies" in Apollo photographs indicating fakery. A new
Goddard's Journal report subjects these allegations of photo
anomalies to experimental investigation with a series of
tests, the results of which provide decisive answers
to questions raised by FOX's Moon-Hoax program:
