Horwitz Seminar
Introductory Chapter Available On-Line

Steven Horwitz's seminar on his new book, _Microfoundations and
Macroeconomics: An Austrian Perspective_ begins Monday, Jan. 22. Steve has
made the introductory chapter of his book available in advance on the web in
both HTLM and WORD format:

HTML (without endnotes):

    http://it.stlawu.edu/shor/Book/introduction.htm

WORD (with endnotes):

    http://it.stlawu.edu/shor/Book/introduction.doc

Please save your remarks until _after_ Steve has sent his informal seminar
introduction to the Hayek-L list, which will arrive in your mailbox Monday.

Steve's book is available from Laissez Faire Books at discount on the web
at:

 http://www.laissezfairebooks.com/product.cfm?op=view&pid=AU8241&aid=10097

Steven has also agreed to extend his seminar through the weekend of February
3-4, in order to allow everyone to have look a his book and to participate
in the seminar.

If you have any questions, send a message directly to: gregransom@home.com

Greg Ransom
Hayek-L list host
