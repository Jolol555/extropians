scerir wrote in reply to what ExI could do for us:
>A book. Very generous. About 500 pages.
>Lots of chapters. Lots of memes. A bible.
 
This is a great idea! For the moment I believe Damien Broderick's book "The Spike" could be considered fairly close to this ideal. I have not yet read it, but get the impression based on what others have told me.

Ultimately, I think this is a book to be written by Max More. He has already written so many articles which could be fleshed out to help create a book. Max has the knowledge and background to do this right.

Of course, I could see room for collaboration. Such people as Nick Bostrom, Natasha Vita-More, Anders Sandburg, Eliezer Yudkowsky, etc. could make very valuable contributions.

I look forward to seeing this happen.
