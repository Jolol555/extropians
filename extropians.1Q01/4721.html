EXTRO-5: SHAPING THINGS TO COME

The initial Web pages for the Extro-5 conference are now up. The program
will undergo further refinement, we'll be adding an initial list of
speakers in just a few days, and we will be opening the "Who Will be There"
section so that registered attendees can add some info about themselves.

For now, check out the introduction, which is something of a battle cry
against the growing anti-extropic forces, along with the current version of
the program, and the registration details.

http://www.extropy.org/ex5/extro5.htm

Organization is moving along nicely, and I'm feeling pumped up at what I'm
confident will be our largest, most stimulating, most practical, and most
networking-rich conference ever since we started these back in 1994.

Onward!
