It's been a while since I've picked up a copy of the
paper version of *National Geographic*, but when I saw
the cover of this month's copy (January 2001), I grabbed
one. I'm a sucker for covers with spacesuits.

As is to be expected, it's (partially) online:
"http://www.nationalgeographic.com/ngm/0101/feature1/index.html"
Interestingly, while the online text is a teaser - the
first few paragraphs of a long article - there are a large
number of useful references included which are not mentioned
in the paper version. Well worth looking at.
