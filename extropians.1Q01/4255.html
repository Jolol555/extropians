Hi,

I think my computer became infected with the win32.mtx virus. As soon
as possible I restored a disk image and that seems to have gotten rid of
it. Don't open any attatchments from me (or anyone) that says
avp_updates or any of the alternatives listed at:

http://www.pspl.com/virus_info/win32/mtx.htm

At that site is a link to 30 day evaluation software that will
apparently remove it.

Sorry if you got infected. The person who infected me had no idea
either.
