Juno Announces Virtual Supercomputer Project

Distributed Computing Facility to Harness Unused Resources of Juno
Subscriber Base, With Theoretical Computing Power of World's Fastest
Supercomputer

NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Feb. 1, 2001-- Juno Online Services, Inc.
(Nasdaq: JWEB - news) today announced the establishment of the Juno Virtual
Supercomputer Project, a distributed computing effort of unprecedented
scope that aims to harness unused processing power associated with the free
portion of its subscriber base in order to execute computationally
intensive biomedical and other applications on behalf of commercial clients
and research institutions.
