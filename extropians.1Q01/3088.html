My main concern with the death penalty is that we keep finding
innocent people on death row. Until the system can be fixed, I don't
trust it.

I grew up in Illinois. The governor has put a stop on executions
because for every one person they execute they find two or three that
are innocent and let them go. The governor declared that this is an
unacceptable ratio.
