>Seriously, folks, if you call yourselves Extropian, ya oughta at least

We already commented on this weeks ago. IT is a two-wheeled scooter
with advanced stability controls. This can be determined from the
patent office filing which is public domain. It may have a new power
system. Many people have denounced the hype surrounding IT as
exaggerated claims. The inventor himself says that he doesn't know
why people are hyping this up so much. Even though he agreed not to
release any details, he says the hype is way out of proportion. The
famous people who have seen it and have been quoted on it have said
that their quotes have been taken out of context and deliberately
distributed to generate more baseless hype for this invention.
Whatever it is, IT is supposed to be good, but not a tenth of what
the hype is suggesting.
