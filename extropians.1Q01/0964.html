A number of Bay Area extropians have asked me privately
my recommendations for a "first pistol". On average,
ergonomics balanced with caliber issues, I'd say the
Glock model 23 in .40S&W is a good bet.

While in the Bay area, I recently dropped in to L. C.
Action Police Supply to check prices. The G23 is available
at the lowest price in the Bay area: $485, the same price
they charge their police customers. They're backordered,
but you can pay now and do your paperwork, then pick up
later.

L C Action Police Supply
1088 N 1st St
San Jose, CA 95112
(408) 294-2677

Follow-ups to exi-bay, especially those who'd like to
get together for some introductory training.

Russell
