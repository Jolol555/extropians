>Harvey so clearly points out), we see the "extremist" are trying to take

Wrong. Everybody tries to take efforts in their direction. You
missed my point.

Extremists don't have a goal. They keep pulling in one direction
forever. They don't have any logical criteria for when they have
gone far enough. As long as there is a more extreme position, they
will strive for it.

Non-extremists have a goal. They pull in one direction until the
goal is reached. Then they stop. They have logical criteria and
stop when the objective goals have been achieved.
