A friend I was visiting in Scottsdale last week turned
me on to the Blue Man Group, a musical performance troupe
that plays the Luxor in Las Vegas:

"http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B000038A2S/o/qid=978903686/sr=8-1/ref=aps_sr_pm_1_1/107-0688990-8556537"

It's not a soundtrack. It's a studio recording which extends
some of their onstage work. The liner notes are online; check
out some of the wild instruments they use, mostly based on
PVC pipes:
"http://www.blueman.com/feature/CD_music.shtml"

The music is rich, melodic, and simply gorgeous. There seems
to be some of Brian Eno's influence here. Great driving music.
