>From: Max More <max@maxmore.com>

>Does anyone know what the law says about "deep linking".

As long as you make it clear that you are not the originator of the linked
page, you have nothing to fear from the courts. However, you should be
careful to make sure that the link you provide does not imply that the
linked document is yours. As always, this is general information only and
does not constitute legal advice. You should always consult a legal
professional concerning your unique situation and most importantly: you are
free to use or ignore the foregoing information AT YOUR OWN RISK.
