>Problems can and do happen, but so do chemical spills and

One of the "side jobs" I had before starting here at the data
center was reactor core phone tech for Zion Nuclear power plant.

There are a series of phones in the reactor core area itself so
techs working there can talk outside the core.

If you've never had the opportunity, entering the core area of a
nuclear reactor is cooler than courtside seats at a Bull's playoff
game during Michael's reign.

I was always very impressed with the skill and care that went into
the plants operation.
