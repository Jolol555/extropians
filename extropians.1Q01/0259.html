Warning, nonextropian meme: John, 20 years ago I was in a chorus
where we were singing the aria from Mozart with the passage

"We all like sheep
Have gone astray..." etc.

The adjacent joker fellow baritone instead sang

"We all like sheep
We like them very much..."

I could not stop laughing, I eventually had to just leave the damn
room. I could no longer sing a note. I guess you had to be there,
but it was about two guffaws short of that secret weapon joke that
is so funny you die laughing. spike
