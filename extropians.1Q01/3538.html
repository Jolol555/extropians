http://news.bbc.co.uk/hi/english/sci/tech/newsid_1160000/1160555.stm

In a move it claims is "democratising and revolutionary", the UN
Environment Programme (Unep) has launched a network portal intended to
transform access to information about green issues.
The site uses capabilities Unep says were "previously limited to military
intelligence agencies". It allows users to create their own maps, and to
add facts and figures to amplify the information on display.
And soon it will allow users anywhere in the world to see what is happening
in their own immediate neighbourhood.
