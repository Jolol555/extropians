Barbara Lamar has just placed the complete text of one of my favourite
stories on line, at

http://www.thespike.addr.com/EARTH.htm#`A_Passage_in_Earth'

Enjoy! (Or detest, or yawn - YMMV, as always...)

Why do I mention this here? Well, I think it might have been one of the
earliest post-Spike stories published. I wrote it in 1977, and evaded the
total incomprehensibility shtick by making the posthumans get drawn into
the attractor of cyclical `god-games', rather as Zelazny did in LORD OF LIGHT.

Damien Broderick
