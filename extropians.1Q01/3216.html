stuffSee http://lsb.scu.edu/~dklein/papers/deepself.html

Abstract: Several Neoclassical economists have criticized Israel Kirzner's
theory of entrepreneurship. I attempt to reconcile the Neoclassical and
Austrian views by borrowing ideas about the mind from Marvin Minsky.
Minsky's ideas lead to a mind theory richer than those of the Neoclassicals
and Austrians. The richer theory accommodates valuable features of both the
Neoclassical and Austrian views.

Thought this piece might be of interest, especially since he uses Minsky to
understand entrepreneurship.
