As I have been noticing the controversial "gun" topic inevitably arise
again, I have a quick request to make. For all topics based specifically
on guns, could the post initiators please use the GUNS tag before the
subject line? Especially if a non-gun thread becomes one based on gun
debates and the like. As I recall, this does much to reduce the complaints
and intoleration of gun discussions on this list, and renders the posts
more identifiable for the sake of deletion.

Thank you.
