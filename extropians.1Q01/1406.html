Terry's suggested prefixes:

    IRR NONSEQ NOTGUN PRIMGUN HURL BIGGUN FAKEGUN
    ONTOPIC RAMBLING POWERTOOLS PRIVATE X

> Please use the "|" character to concatenate the various prefixes which will
> describe your post. Ex:
>
> IRR|NOTGUN|FAKEGUN|PRIVATE: The "Flamethrower"

Come on. You expect people posting to mailing lists---where you actually
have to type!---to write subject lines like that? It would actually be
something like:

    IRR|NoTGUn /FAKEeGN|PRIVIT(sp?): The `Flamethroewr" was (was:Re:arrrgh))

So, anyway, if I've understood your above prefix suggestions then I should be
able to post, say,

    LIRR|NOGUNRI|FAKEGUM|HURD|OCTOPIC|BIGGUS|PRIVATE: Urp

which would be about a low-ranking soldier taking the Long Island Railroad
toward the site of a massacre in Korea while working on the GNU (_not_ GUN)
kernel and looking at an eight-photograph layout of a character from _The
Life of Brian_ after finding some plastic for-display-only chewing gum and
eating it.

Thanks, but I'll have a list without so much gun in it.
