I also recommend "Quantum Psychology" by R.A.W, which is
written to be read with a group, and contains group
exercises. As with "Prometheus" (which also contains
exercises, so it is more of a "self help" book than
a series of essays) it explores intelligence increase,
reality construction and other matters which I have
encountered here on the list. Like everything else
R.A.W. has written, it is witty and doesn't take itself
too seriously.
