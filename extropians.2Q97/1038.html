>Erik Moeller wrote:
>Japan is my dream-economy. They have a relatively high state
>intervention

I like Japan too, but it has its problems. Although far less than any
communist country, government does indeed engage in a lot of intervention in
Japan, and the level is far greater than the optimal amount (zero). The same
thing is true of the USA, both would be much better off without any of it.
I can only think of 3 areas where Japan has substantially more regulation
than the USA.

1) Agriculture
2) Domestic Retailing
3) Telecommunications

I do not think it's a coincidence that those are also the 3 areas where
Japan is furthest behind.
