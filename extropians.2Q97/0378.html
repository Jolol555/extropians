Sean Morgan writes:
>One common point to both of these schemes is that they rely on some measure
>of majority opinion of worth. Ideally we would each filter on our own
>measures of worth, as with Sasha's document rating proposal (not
>coincidentally related to his work at Firefly on rating movies, web sites,
>and now books).

Relying on the opinion of others can *be* part of one's measure of
worth. Firefly ratings are *also* measures of the opinions of others;
they measure the opinions of those Firefly crudely predicts will share
your opinions. Defining the group right, they are even measures of
majority opinion within a certain group. Joining a mailing list with
some sort of joint filtering rule is just another way for you to
choose people to rely on.
