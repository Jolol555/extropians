Subject: Wolfram cellular automata papers available online

For those who may not be aware, the work of Stephen Wolfram in the
early 1980's played a central role in launching the new field of
science concerned with the problem of complexity.

It may interest readers of this newsgroup that most of Stephen Wolfram's
papers on cellular automata and complexity are available online at
http://www.wolfram.com/s.wolfram/. In particular, all of the papers
included in 'Cellular Automata and Complexity: Collected Papers' can be
found at http://www.wolfram.com/s.wolfram/books/ca-reprint/.
