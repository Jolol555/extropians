Welcome to the psychoceramics mailing list!


If you ever want to remove yourself from this mailing list,
send the following command in email to
"psychoceramics-request":


    unsubscribe


Or you can send mail to "Majordomo" with the following command
in the body of your email message:


    unsubscribe psychoceramics Eugene.Leitl@uni-muenchen.de


Here's the general information for the list you've
subscribed to, in case you don't already have it:


[Last updated on: Tue Nov  7 15:17:23 1995]


Psychoceramics is the study of crackpots and crackpot phenomena.  This
includes things such as weird science and pseudoscience, bizarre
religion, kooky solutions to the world problem and other things. 


The psychoceramics mailing list is intended for the discussion of such
phenomena, the distribution of interesting examples of crackpot
literature, and the discussion of issues to do with the pastime of
kook-watching.
