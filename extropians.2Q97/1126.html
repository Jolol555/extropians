Here's a note useful only to people within an easy drive of Silicon Valley:

It seems that there are lots of opportunities for group action among
those of us who want to radically extend our lives. Let's get
together for a Sunday brunch to find out what people want to do.

When: 11am, Sunday, May 18
Where: Fresh Choice
2540 W. El Camino Real
Mountain View, CA

This is near the intersection of San Antonio and El Camino.
For detailed directions, point your web browser at
http://www.zip2.com; their door-to-door directions are free and better
than anything I could provide here. Hmm, they are down as I write
this -- I hope it is not permanent.

To get things started, here are some things we could do that arose from discussions at a recent Extropian party:

Get together regularly and socialize.
Take advantage of economies of scale in packaging & mixing vitamins.
Share information about local doctors.
Share information about suppliments and other life extension
technologies.
Help with cryonics paperwork.
Actively recruit more people.

Feel free to propagate this message to interested persons and relevant
newsgroups or mailing lists. I have sent it to:

sci.life-extension
sci.cryonics
crsociety@lists.csn.net
longevity-digest@vm.ege.edu.tr
cryonet@cryonet.org
extropians@extropy.org
