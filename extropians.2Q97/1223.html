At 06:16 PM 5/19/97 -0700, you wrote:
>Hi.
>
>Since people on this list occasionally inquire about various aspects of
>investing on the stock market (esp. tech stocks), I would like know if
>anyone on this list would be interested in starting some type of investment
>club.

A few of us in So Cal have been meeting to discuss investments. I am very
interested in helping the development and communication of investment
wisdom among Extropians. You know the expression: "If you're so smart, how
come you're not rich?" We should all have a compelling answer... "Just take
a look at my portfolio, buddy!"

>If there is enough interest, I would be willing to donate my experience
>with investing as well as my access to investment information resources to
>help out those of you with less experience than I. And if there are other
>experienced investors out there, I would hope that they would be willing to
>share their knowledge as well.

The Investing in the Future panel at EXTRO 3 would be a good way of finding
out who is interested in an investment club. A brief response form could be
distributed at the session and results published in the Exponent newsletter.
