On Sun, 13 Apr 1997, Mike Rose wrote:

> WoW! You guys really know your brain stuff. I seem way out of my depth at
> the moment but I am really keen on learning more. Can any1 suggest any
> preliminary texts on brain function and basic neurology? Where should I
> start? I am currently reading Lilly's 'Centre of the Cyclone' and I guess
> I will read 'Programming and Meta-Programming....' next - any other
> suggestions?

Well, there are plenty of books about the brain and neurology, but it is
hard to tell which would fit you the best. The "bible", at least here at
Sans and the Karolinska Institute, is Kandel, Schwartz et al. _Principles
of Neuroscience_. It contains almost everything (the drawback is of course
that it contains almost everything, and hence weighs around four
kilograms). There are many simpler and less heavy books, of course.
