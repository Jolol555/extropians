"More powerful"? In one sense, yes. The Inktome engine (which is what Hotbot uses) is based on a loosely-coupled maspar array of commodity computers, and its strong suit is frequent scans of the entire Web. However, I continue to use both AltaVista *and* Hotbot. Each has strong and weak points. Hotbot can over-filter at times.

I also bought AltaVista for my PC. It works adequately. I don't think Inktome is offered for individual sale--so in that arena, at least, AltaVista wins. YMMV.
