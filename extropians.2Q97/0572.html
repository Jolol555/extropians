"conversing" with Extropians *as a research project*. It may be that
her course requires her to conduct face-to-face interviews. If that
is the case and I was in her position, I might want to turn the
research into a comparison of the merits of face-to-face interviews
vs. interviews that could be conducted through the forum of an e-mail
list.
