may be interested to learn that Ye Squirrel is moderating the "Cloning and
Eugenics -- Evaluating the Ethics" panel at BayCon (a west-coast regional
science-fiction convention held in San Jose over the Memorial Day long
weekend). The panel is Saturday, 24 May, at 3:00 PM, in the Monterey
Room of the DoubleTree hotel at Gateway and Airport Boulevards.
