> I would not worry about it. Most of the cult activities that people worry
> about are illegal anyway (like psychedelic drug use, hey wait a second...

The extropian interest in smart drugs could be a problem. Especially the
illegal ones.

> Others are quite stupid and rather opposed to Extropian ideas. Of
> course, the word "cult" most likely would inspire some people to suspend
> all rational judgment, so they might not realize that uploading is a
means,

to become a reality. Most people can't grasp them, or simply dismiss them as

these things (which I would like to see us do) then they'll be problems.

> We have a long way to go before we reach the level of cult that the

we

> start losing political pull, anarcho-capatilism forever!).

How about an extropian party!? Maybe then we'll see some change.
