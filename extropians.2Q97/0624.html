Thomas Petzinger Jr's "Front Lines" column in today's Wall Street
Journal reviews a little-known book by Abraham Maslow (of
self-actualization fame) from 1965 called _Eupsychian Management_ that
Peter Drucker calls "by far Maslow's best book".

The entire book is available on the Web at
http://www.scarletfire.com/maslow.

I browsed through some of it and it looks fairly extropian at a
glance, though much of it will probably seem like 'common sense' to
most of the people on this list.
