How can rational people expect to make good investments in a irrational
economy ? Saying investing in nanotechnology is a good idea is like saying
you should invest in computers. But which company ? The company with the
best idea's and the most Extro views will not be the most successful.

If you want to make good investments you have got to get your head 'round all
those crazy ideals of (apparently) normal people! The best technology will
win out in the end, and over long periods of time 'Extro' ideals will spread.
However, this tells you nothing of what company to invest in.

Even if you understand the technology, do you understand the market !?
