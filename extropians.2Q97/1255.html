A couple people asked:

>I would be interested. But how meager is meager ?

It doesn't take very much. I would say that $50/month is a rough minimum.
I started with even less than that many years ago. However, investing this
much money limits your options considerably. Your best option at this
level of investment is mutual funds until you can grow your cash.

More importantly, though, is that you put aside a minimum amount *every
month*. This is the real key to investing.
