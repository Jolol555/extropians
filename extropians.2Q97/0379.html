that I find consistent with Extropian ideals, but you should consider
that enough of us do to make us a bad resource for information on how
to make legislators do anything but go away. If research on life

the people who can do the research and try to get the funding by free
market voluntary means; if you can't raise the money volutarily, then
the project isn't worth funding by force.
