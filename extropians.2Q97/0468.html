Transhumans,

My article/interview on the Singularity has FINALLY been published in latest
issue (#23) of 21C--Scanning the Future, under the title Mind Children,
starting on page 46. If I may say so, it looks terrific. Featured are the
comments of transhumans you all know and love, John K. Clark, Daniel C
Clemmenson, Eric Watt Forste, Eugene Leitl, Sara Kathryn Marr, and Anders
Sandberg, as well as comments by science fiction author Vernor Vinge. In the
same issue, Mark Dery (author of Escape Velocity) does an in depth interview
with Vinge.

Thank you all.
