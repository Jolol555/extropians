>Tonight on television.

In case you missed the earlier episodes (as I did), here are listings of
future showings in the EASTERN and the PACIFIC time zones, for the next two
weeks. Note that these different local times are actually the same
Universal Coordinated Time (~GMT) times. The CENTRAL and MOUNTAIN times
may be interpolated from these two zone schedules.

Such schedules (for the major 4 U.S. time zones and the major cable
services) are available from
http://www.tmstv.com/ or
http://www.tmstv.com/cgi-bin/tvcgi.cnn/, among others. They can be
generated for a week before the present to two weeks in advance.

LEARN or TLC is The Learning Channel, which will be on a specific
numbered channel on your cable system, if you get it. The times of
cablecast will be the same.

Roll those tapes!
