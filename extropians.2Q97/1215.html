Hi.

Since people on this list occasionally inquire about various aspects of
investing on the stock market (esp. tech stocks), I would like know if
anyone on this list would be interested in starting some type of investment
club.

Investment is essential for financial security and is accessible even to
people of meager financial means. Since, many extropians are planning on
living indefinitely, it would be prudent to start planning your very
long-term finances now. Intelligent investments usually result in being
independently wealthy when applied for ~40 years, even if the investments
appear small.

If there is enough interest, I would be willing to donate my experience
with investing as well as my access to investment information resources to
help out those of you with less experience than I. And if there are other
experienced investors out there, I would hope that they would be willing to
share their knowledge as well.

Additionally, I would be willing to operate a website and other Internet
services dedicated to this. I am half-owner of a couple modest servers and
some redundant T-1 connections, so it would cost me nothing other than my
time.

If anyone is interested in becoming involved in something like this, please
let me know.
