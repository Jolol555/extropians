Sean Kenny writes

> I'm struggling through Deutsch's book "The Fabric of Reality" at the moment,
> about half way through after several months (and it's only a short book!),

I first read that, and carefully too, in 1997. Last summer I re-read it,
but with excruciating care. It's about 1000 paragraphs, in very good and
plain English, that deal with extraordinary concepts, and manage to tie
them together with no inconsistencies. Almost all the flaws or questions
that I had in 1997 were resolved upon my further re-reading. I never
studied any other "plain-English" book so hard in my life. It's simply
great.

If you finish the book, find that you've understood most of it, yet still
have some unresolved questions, you may be interested in posting them on
the "Fabric-of-Reality" list http://www.eeng.dcu.ie/~tcs/List/forlist.html
