Ron, the cookie simply sets your username and login in your cookie file
so you don't have to log in when you access the site, it reads your
login from your cookiefile. NOTE: You can delete this cookie at any time
after you sign up for the list. Once you've signed up, the list messages
are just going to your mailbox, which requires no cookie, so you have no
need of the cookie if you are not going to change your account.
