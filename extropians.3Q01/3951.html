about linking subject line changes... Then one could
follow the conversation more easily. There ought
to be a "meta-links" when it finds a substantive
number of identical number of words in the Subject line.

Hint to list members: Think carefully about whether
you do want or don't want the message tree to lose
continuity. If its part of an old conversation,
changing the subject line should be resisted unless
you are really taking a sharp turn in the discussion.
