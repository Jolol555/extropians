"Technotranscendence" <neptune@mars.superlink.net> wrote:

> I really don't see his speeches -- even
> this one -- as being one people who have distance from the issues will go
> back to. Compare him to FDR, JFK, Reagan, and, yes, Clinton. I'm surprised
> he even made it this far, since oratory usually is the hallmark of the
> Presidential politician.

I thought it was a very good speech. I thought it was a little long, and
towards the end I was thinking 'End it now! You've made it thru this far
w/o flubbing, why take the risk?'. So, I was impressed that Bush
actually made it thru a long speech. Perhaps when judging speeches we
have to consider the speaker, and given that the speaker in this case
was Dubya, it was (imho) a fine speech.
