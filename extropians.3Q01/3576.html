I think PoC's only offense was "praising with faint damns".

Lots of people seek "opposing-side" info. Observing, as PoC said.

This is, in my opinion, preferable to having one's mind made
up by, e.g., some magazine reviews.

Take a look, from that vantage point, at what PoC wrote:

> > As a philosopher-psychologist with half of his career spent in
> > clinical work, I had to coin "WWW Syndrome" for what I was
> > observing. "Whining White Wussy" syndrome. Also, WWW fits the never-ending
> > vocalizations of these people...waaaa, waaaa, waaaaaaa! Come on guys, buck
> > up. If you don't like the culture as it is, design your own.

The big problem is, of course, that Führer-wannabes _are_ "designing their own".
But OTOH, if they keep most of the ugly iceberg hidden on their list, "whining"
might be all that was available for PoC to see, from analysis of the posts as

Anyroad, calling Duke et al "Whining White Wussies" doesn't get PoC invited to any
pointy-head nightshirt shenanigans, I'd bet.
