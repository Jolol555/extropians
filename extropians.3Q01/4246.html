   Moon Base Clavius is an organization of amateur and professional
   engineers, scientists, and astronauts devoted to the Apollo program
   and its manned exploration of the moon. Our special mission is to
   debunk the so-called conspiracy theories that state such a landing may
   never have occurred.
   
   This site is named after the Clavius Moon Base in Arthur C. Clarke's
   novel 2001: A Space Odyssey, and visualized by Stanley Kubrick in the
   film of the same name.
