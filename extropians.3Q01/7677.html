Disable the Pilots
Peter Huber, Forbes Magazine, 10.15.01, 12:00 AM ET

The Enemy Beneath
Rich Karlgaard

for the last 44 years. The trend is decentralization.
http://www.forbes.com/technology/forbes/2001/1015/043.html
