The Word Wide Web Consortium[*] is considering allowing standards
restricted by "Reasonable And Non-Discriminatory" licenses. AFAICT
"reasonable" is never defined, and it's a slipperly slope anyway.
All the links and commentary you need:

http://slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=01/09/30/1454216&mode=nested

Voice your displeasure today to: www-patentpolicy-comment@w3.org

* The W3C Tim "inventor of the web" Berners-Lee's organization,
  and is responsible for developing standards for the web's most
  basic infrastructure, most famously HTML, HTTP, and XML.
