Subject: Objectivism and Homosexuality: Interviews Requested

THE FREE RADICAL has just published the first in a series of articles that
I am writing on the subject of "Objectivism and Homosexuality --
Again." Yes, a subject that has long been discussed within the Objectivist
movement... is being discussed yet again!

The article is posted at:
http://www.freeradical.co.nz/solo/sciabarra_homosexuality.html

What interests me at this time is to hear PERSONALLY from people of
different walks of life -- straight and gay -- who might discuss their own
experiences within the Objectivist movement. I am particularly interested
in hearing stories about how those within Objectivism have received your
sexual orientation, or how you have reacted to those of a different sexual
orientation. I have heard real horror stories regarding the exploits of
gay men and women within Objectivism; I've also seen evidence that
attitudes are changing dramatically. I would like to document some of this
history in my series, and look forward to hearing from you.

Please write to me at: chris.sciabarra@nyu.edu

I will treat all correspondence confidentially.
