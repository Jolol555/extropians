>From an extremely cursory examination of what Eric Chen Yixiong
has written, it would appear as though he disapproves of all
"conflict", e.g.,

>> Hence, in a society with even one irrational member, we have
>> to exercise censorship to prevent conflicts from occurring,
>> or we will have to live outside each other's influence.

Sadly, one reading of this is to infer that what is being suggested
is that debate and pan-critical argumentation are non-optimal.
I bet that this is what is being meant. Exactly the opposite is
true.

(Yes, on another reading, "irrationality" is to be taken literally,
and "conflicts" are to be taken as the use of force, but I disagree
with this too, but for other reasons.)

Lee Corbin
