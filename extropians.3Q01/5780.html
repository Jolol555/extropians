> Since this went on for hours. Each of these ants walked

== From the Journal of Spike Jones ==

"Observation of Ant Behavior"

Trial 1: Watched ant. Ant walked down garbage can and back up again.
Trial 2: Watched ant. Ant walked down garbage can and back up again.
...
Trial 944: Watched ant. Ant walked down garbage can and back up again.
...
Trial 8,302: Watched ant. Ant walked down garbage can and back up again.

Conclusion: Ants exhibit highly sphexish behavior.
