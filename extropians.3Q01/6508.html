Friends,

The sadness that permeates the world is once again reflected in yet another onslaught of coercive and unjust killings.

What many considered to be unfathomable has become a reality: The United States as an open field for destructive terrorism.

My deepest and most sincere thoughts are for all races, religions and people through the planet - that we may accept our diversity and differences, values and goals, and learn to live free of the need to criticize or ostracize others whose ideas and ideas are different than our own unless such values and beliefs promote and perpetuate hatred and terror.

Natasha
