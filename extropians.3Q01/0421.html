Mike Linksvayer said

>Those of you interested in taking a page from the anti-globalism/luddite
>playbook may find this interesting:
>
>http://www.walkforcapitalism.org/
>
> Are you ready?
> Then WalkforCapitalism on D2. <etc>

Well, I'm all for intellectual and personal liberty, including consensual
capitalist acts. I'm no friend of S11 or M1 and I think the global
mercantilist system is proving to be a good thing, on the whole...

Yet I'm not very inspired by the prospect of a walk for mercantilism.

Can't we walk for the life of freedom, reason and non-coercion, or
something?
