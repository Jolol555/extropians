>The stem cell debate in the US House is live on CNN over the web right

Interestingly enough, CNN's "QuickVote" question is "If the technology were
available, would you clone yourself?" Not surprisingly, the answer is
predominantly "No," though I do find it encouraging that 12,000 of 70,000
(as of right now) do say "Yes." It may be a minority, but arguably a
substantial one. We have a lot more work to do, I think, but things like
this can be viewed as positive markers, rather than negative ones.
