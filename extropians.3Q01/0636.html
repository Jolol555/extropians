second, phony Church of Extropology, then randomly sign
up half of us in each and see if the reals live longer than the
fakes. Then if so, we ponder why the real CoE members
live longer, then try to figure out the active ingredient in the
real CoE, and try to reduce it to pill form. Then the Singularity...
