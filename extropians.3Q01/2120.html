At 06:28 PM 7/24/01 -0700, Lee Corbin wrote:

>I claim that an *increase* in the *concentration* of top-class
>wealth is a positive sign, because (I also claim) it is so
>historically well-correlated with general advances in society
>and richer economies.

It is also worth considering that the subjective value acquired by the
marginal unit of monetary "wealth" will decrease as wealth increases;
hence, the standard-of-living gap could be closing while the monetary
wealth gap is widening.
