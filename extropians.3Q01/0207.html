> >The problem for transhumanists is how to debunk _all_ religions equally.
>
> Why is this a goal for transhumanists?

Because we won't move into a better future until we defeat religiosity, which
is
the most regressive force now operating in society.

--J. R.

 consciousness, phlogiston, philosophy, vitalism, mind, free will, qualia,
analog computing, cultural relativism
