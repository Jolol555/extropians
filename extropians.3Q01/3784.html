From: "Zero Powers" <zero_powers@hotmail.com>

> Question (answer honestly): You're waiting for an elevator, two elevator
> doors open at the same time, you're an an equal distance away from both
> doors, in one elevator is a white guy, in the other is a black guy. Which
> elevator do you get into?

This is my honest answer: the elevator into which less people are
streaming. Having taken elevators a lot for decades without a single
unpleasant incident of any kind - ever ever ever - it's difficult for me to
relate to such a dilemma. This question reminds me of a ream of questions I
was once asked to fill out during jury selection for a double-murder trial.
I remember one of them: Do you know any black people?
