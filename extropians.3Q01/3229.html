John Grigg wrote:
> I took the quiz to find my "political self" and discovered I am a... Left-Liberal!! Oh, to be branded with that name! Now, no one will like me on this list(except Damien Broderick lol)...
>...
They rated me that way too (40x80), and I picked most items: M, i.e.,
neither yes nor no adequately describes my position. It's quite
interesting. I am a believer in decentralized controls, interface
standards, etc. Rather like the internet without ICANN, and instead a
substituted collection of Nodes none of which have central control, but
rather a round-robin selection protocol. A CSMACD analog wouldn't
work... it allows one loudmouth to sieze control on a temporary basis,
but it would be better than the current "solution". I think that this
most probably makes me some flavor of anarchist, though I'm not really
sure. It doesn't mean that I believe in centralized controls on any issue.
