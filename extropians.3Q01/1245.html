The current issue of _Critical Review_ 14(1) is devoted to the topic of
globalization. In are pieces by Kevin Dowd on the causes of international
financial instability, Garett Jones on the Asian Crisis, Deepak Lal on
globalization in the Third World, and Charles Wolf on measuring
globalization. There's also a review of George Soros new book _The Crisis
of Global Capitalism_ by Barry Eichengreen along with Soros' response.

All of this makes for interesting and timely reading.

_Critical Review_ maintains a web site at http://www.criticalreview.com/
BTW, The issue on display there is not the current issue.
