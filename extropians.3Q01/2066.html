Hi all,

It may interest some of you to know that I am currently working on my
second album, entitled _Reality's Twin_. I have just released the first
track from the album, titled "Reality Is Leaking Again" * on my Mp3.com
page at http://www.mp3.com/eshaunrussell .

The song is my first excursion into the Drum N' Bass genre, so those of you
who have enjoyed the more minimalist and contemporary instrumental
compositions off of my first album, be forewarned!

The album, _Reality's Twin_ should be released in September.

Let me know what you think.

* = "Reality Is Leaking Again" was the name of a short thread on this list
