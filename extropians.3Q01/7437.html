It's extremist. It's a cartoon. And I see no reason not to require everyone to
load frangible or not board. At the moment, LEOs can load whatever they want.
The data is still out on exactly what credentials the bad guys carried.
Credentials can be faked, as we all know.

Seriously, defensive use of any firearm on board an aircraft is a calculated
risk, and doing it right requires a _lot_ of training and practice. Head shots
down the length of a stretch DC-9? Nein danke. Twenty-way crossfire? Absolutely
not!

However, if the current word of FAA's Notice of Proposed Rulemaking _removing_
the existing "loophole" for _flight crews_ to qualify and carry is true, this
sort of propaganda has its place, I think.

I'll take flames and further comments off list.
