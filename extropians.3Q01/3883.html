It appears as if Miriam English <miriam@werple.net.au> wrote:
|
|Of all the stupid, arrogant isms we humans create, racism has to be one of
|the ugliest. I am so glad it seems to be a dying meme. I can hardly wait
|till we get lifespans exceeding hundreds of years so that people can
|finally get a chance to truly grow up. It is an exciting thought that it
|may well be soon.

potential new species to support the species. A species needs to wish to
exist, or it will die out.

Racism and wish for longevity form the sides of he same coin, one of them
collectivistic (racism), the other individualistic (wish for longevity).

I believe one could say the problem lies with collectivism, of which
racism forms just a small part.
