As an addendum: I have now indeed found Ayn Rand published in the
UK -- however, apart from a HarperCollins paperback edition of "The
Fountainhead" her books are only available as imports.

I had to go to amazon.co.uk and do a search -- none of her books made the
top 1500 sellers in the UK according to Amazon. Compare to amazon.com,
where "Atlas Shrugged" is at 630 and "The Fountainhead" is at 835 in
sales ranking; I'd say the figures support my contention that she has
a popularity problem over here.
