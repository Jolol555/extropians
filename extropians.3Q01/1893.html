(Michael H. Hart) in which Chang Po-Yo is listed as the pre-emminent person

surgery (pseudo-immortality). The brain section is copied, sliced,replaced,
until the entire brain is replaced. The book celebrated similar successes
with other researchers, prior to this, involving body organs and tissue
