>We were attacked because [we in the USA] are a symbol of Capitalism

Well, sort of... but more to the point, from their point of view, a symbol
and active source of decadence, sexual depravity, physical and spiritual
oppression. Do you really imagine the Jihad wake up in the morning and tell
each other, `George, by Allah, that beacon of liberty has to *go*. If
there's one thing I can't stomach, it's *liberty*.' Only in the Simpsons

wickedness striding the world. Persuading such people--whose understanding
of reality has been created by sacred fanaticism--that this is a mistaken
view will not be easy, especially if we allow ourselves to become confused
over what they themselves think they're up to.
