Incidentally, it occurs to me that I should clarify the reasoning here:
LB did nothing wrong in particular that would trigger such a message, so
one explanation is that the message was broadcast; not targeted at her in
particular. The message she describes also has a very generic "feel" to
it. So it seems plausible to me that there's someone, most likely a
single individual, trying to knock new posters off the list. "Quite
probably" was the wrong wording above, and on reflection I would rather
have said "quite possibly". But I think it's important that we keep a
lookout to see if this happens again.
