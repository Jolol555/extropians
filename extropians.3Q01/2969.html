Hello all,

This is to announce that now a mailing list exists for Extropians
(and/or people
interested in futurism, science and technology in general) who seek to
date
other people with similar interests: The PosthumanLounge mailing list is
located
on a private server with me as the owner and only moderator.
I will not collect any private information about the subscribers. Also,
subscribers are not required to subscribe and post messages with their
real
names and/or email addresses.

The PosthumanLounge mailing list was created some time ago and, at
first,
announced here on the Extropians mailing list as a list primarily for
discussions. But there are already so many discussion mailing lists out
there,
so I wanted to change the purpose of the PosthumanLounge.

Please go to:
http://www.posthuman.com/mailman/listinfo/posthumanlounge

Thanks for reading.
