Some information on the Flare project:

Project site URL: http://flarelang.sourceforge.net
Project mailing list: flarelang-devel@lists.sourceforge.net
URL to mailing list:
http://lists.sourceforge.net/lists/listinfo/flarelang-devel

To join the list, send request to
flarelang-devel-request@lists.sourceforge.net.

The mailing list is immediately open for discussion. Please direct
flare-related posts to that list (as opposed to SL4). Currently, the project
is in specification-phase. Hopefully by late August, the specifications will
be close to complete and the team can begin writing code.

If you are interested in participating in the Flare open-source effort,
please register on SourceForge and also reply to me at dmyshkin@hotmail.com.
