You make it sound like it's something major. Your ISP reserves several
MBytes space for you already, just upload the file and tell the URL.

machine, with a redirectioni URL to it if you don't have a static IP.

included), after they realized that the bulk of executable attachements
passing through the mailserver was worms and viruses.
