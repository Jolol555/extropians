Speaking of airline safety, I've often thought that one simple and cheap change
that would help a lot is have all the passenger seats face the back of the airplane
not the front. If I'm going to decelerate from 200 mph to zero in one second or so
I'd rather have the force applied over my entire body rather than just around my
waist by a seatbelt.
