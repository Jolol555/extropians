Last night, I enjoyed watching fireworks under a full moon. From Max's
study window, we had a wide-opened view of the Pacific Ocean's sky blazing
in light and color. My favorite part of the 4th was Morgan Freeman's talk
about the Declaration of Independence and, later, The Charlie Rose Show and
guest David McCullough, Author, "John Adams".
