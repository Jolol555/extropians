Well, no organism was grown from it, so it's not theft, per se. As in
intellectual property, it's not posession of an original copy that is a
crime, it is the unauthorized reproduction, especially with the intent
to make a profit.

In the above case, police are non-profit, did not grow a new organism
from the sample. This seems to be a 'fair use' incident.
