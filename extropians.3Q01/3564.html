> The poisoned rhetoric keeps tumbling from journalists when cloning news is

I was delighted at the front and center story in the SF Chron today
on cloning. This newspaper is all tied up in knots over the whole
issue. They staunchly oppose cloning, yet a perfectly legitimate
*religion*, the Raelians, claims that to clone is part of their religious
creed. It remains to be seen what principle is most sacred here: their
revulsion of the "unnatural" practice of cloning, or their dedication to
protecting *any* religious or cultural practice, regardless of how
wacky. Either way, Im cheering for the Raelians. spike
