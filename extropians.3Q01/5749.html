> "Intelligence scores are rising, James R. Flynn discovered--but he remains
> very sure we're not getting any smarter."

planting potatoes and loiter around in homespun all of my life. Yechhh.

experience deprivation, with very measurable results both in IQ test and
"smarts". Nowadays, you have to actively flee the world to live in such
conditions.
