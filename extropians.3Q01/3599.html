Olga Bourlin wrote,
> Wow, last week a post to this list revealed a doo-doo trail
> straight to a white supremacist site, and now Duke's name shows up.
> What's the connection to extropians?

I wish I knew.

> It seems to me there's a thin veneer between many people who call
> themselves
> libertarians, and other not-very-pleasant (my opinion only, of course)
> institutions. I know not all libertarians subscribe to all these
> institutions, but ... the connections that keep coming up are scary.

I consider myself a libertarian. I do not believe there is a true
connection between libertarians and racism, religion, isolationism, and
ultra-conservative issues. This is why I am so concerned about attempts to
link libertarians or extropians to certain unrelated viewpoints. These are
not libertarian or extropian views. There seems to be an attempt to link
these views, but I don't know why or why now.
