Ever observe how, in governmental service provision

the Bureau of Economic Development will defacto be
the Bureau of Economic Destruction

the Bureau of Drug Suppression will defacto be
the Bureau of Drug Running

the Bureau of Anti-terrorism will defacto be
the Bureau of Terrorism

the Bureau of Safe Medicine will defacto be
the Bureau of Unsafe Medicine

the Bureau of Justice will defacto be
the Bureau of Injustice?

Why dose government consistently tend to contradict the
categories of public service it purports to provide ?

The answer is only too simple:

Because a MONOPOLY, for many reasons, does not
provide but destroys service to the general public.

A monopoly, liberated from the need to provide a better
service to attract monetary reward from consumers, works
only to serve itself, only to serve the private interests
of those at the top of its internal command-structure.

Societies which are incapable of understanding this
most simple rule of human nature will be condemned
to a standard of living that is as low as the
monopoly "providing services" is large.
