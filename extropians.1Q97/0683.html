Clearly few Bay Area Extropians are interested in meeting for
lunch every Thursday at 1 at the Fresh Choice in Sunnyvale.
(Pardon to non-locals, but some locals aren't on exi-bay.)

get-togethers you folks _would_ be interested in having. Dinner?
Sunday brunch? Movies? Bowling? Poker? Lunch elsewhere or
elsewhen? Watercourse Way? <your idea here>?

Please (yes, *you*) let me know what you're up for. Send private
email to kwatson@netcom.com or post to exi-bay@extropy.org.
