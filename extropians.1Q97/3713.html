Gazing

Let's gaze upon the future
And see where tomorrow lies,
Draw patterns in the starlight
And find rhythms in the skies;
Let's find ourselves a new day
And escape from the past's hold,
Let's build a new horizon
And remake the Manifold.
We'll see the space surround us,
We'll welcome the neo-night,
We'll create a Paradise
In our interstellar flight;
So let's look to the future
And find a way to fulfill
Our palpable potentials
And kineticize our will.

By: E. Shaun Russell
