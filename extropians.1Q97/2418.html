My mind in rage, I need know more,
In thirst of life, At the cost of war.
A beast once tamed, I chose to hear,
Escaped it's cage. And now I hunt.
Beware all ye who hide your words, A skull is not a shield;
Your wisdom shall be mine. Your thoughts I can divine.
The truth you seek, I shall not yield.
It shall be a lie. My mercy is a front.
No soul shall know thier striff No one shall shed a tear.
While I prowl around inside. The future shall be mine.
