Please check out my new web site for your fun and information at:

http://www.transhuman.com/

For now, the star attraction is a speedy US mirror of Ander's Transhumanist
Resources site. Also, I'm setting up a new archive of the Transhuman
Mailing List (still not quite debugged, as I haven't got the hang of
hypermail yet - more traffic on this list would help in the debugging,
though.)

Soon, I will be adding an online database of transhuman contact info, so
that visitors to the site can add to, display, and search contact info of
transhuman individuals. Hopefully it will help people to find other
like-minded people nearby - or worldwide! Many other features are planned
as well.
