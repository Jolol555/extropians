Balancing Acts

In a day like today
(Or any other day),
We can count on the moon
To drive the sun away,
But what we do not see
(Or what we don't want to see)
Is that a balance is what
Makes 'we' be a we.

When the time is now
(Or any time but now),
We can rely on others
To show us how,
But what we can't seem to do
(Or seem to not want to do)
Is render that knowledge
And let our minds say it's true.

By: E. Shaun Russell
