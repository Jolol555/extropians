Generous friends,

Please help test "Transhuman Contacts", an online database of transhuman
contact info.
"Transhuman Contacts" will be a facility of http://www.transhuman.com.
Visitors to the Transhuman.com site will be able to add to, display, and
search contact info of transhuman individuals. Hopefully it will help
people to find other
like-minded people nearby - or worldwide!

This facility is not yet linked to the home page of Transhuman.com. You can
get to it by
going to:

http://www.transhuman.com/contacts/

Any information you enter need not be accurate at this time. (Until this
part of the site is really ready to use, any information you enter will be
deleted before this part of the site is open to the general public.)

Your particpation, and suggestions for improvement, are appreciated.
