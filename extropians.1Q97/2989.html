On 26/02/97, Max More wrote:

>(I should note that CBC is specifically interested in the extropian context
>and not simply narrow comment on the cloning thing itself. It seems that
>Extropian thinking is becoming recognized and that media people are
>beginning to solicit our views as a recognized group or school of thought.
>This is progress!)

Yes, a few months ago I had an interview with CBC (via phone)
regarding uploading and the extropian view of how future mechanics will help
humanity rather than hinder it. It appears that extropy *is* beginning to
be its own entity rather than being dubbed as "...a spinoff of objectivism"
or "neo-fascist" as has been the case before. It is particularly
interesting that CBC itself has taken an interest, considering that it is
the only national network that is the earpiece to the Canadian government.
I look forward to seeing how your segment is received on "Big Life".
