EXPONENT NEWSLETTER FREE FOR THE ASKING!

Members of Extropy Institute will have received the issue of ExI's
newsletter, Exponent, detailing our refined focus for the future. We will
be developing our network of thinkers, researchers, entrepreneurs, and
communicators. Forming the core of our resharpened mission, networking will
bring together individuals of diverse interests but shared extropic values
to hasten research and development of technologies crucial to life
extension, intelligence augmentation, and our other goals involving
overcoming biological limits.

If you think you might like to be more involved in moving the world in
extropic directions, e-mail me with your physical mailing address, and we
will send you a free copy of the current newsletter. It contains details of
the Institute's goals as well as reports on local events, news, and photos.
Also included is a survey form. We invite non-members to fill this out too.

Onward!
