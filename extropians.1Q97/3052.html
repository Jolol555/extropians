The best book I have ever read that deals with the nature of art is
called "Art Objects." It is a collection of essays by Jeanette Winterson
that deal with the association of art to the artist and the viewer as well
as the latency of every individual's ability to be art and create art.
Definitely an automorphic book. Its ISBN is 0-679-76820-3 and is published
by Vintage international. Highly recommended!
