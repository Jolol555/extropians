>I imagined a ... body suit
>with metallic circuitry embedded in the suit, to help with augmentation

This is similar to what I was visualizing. A problem might be that
augmentations as internal, rather than external. Also, a transhuman might
want to exhibit various augmentations so that they can be seen, as the
devices can be stylish as well as functional. I'm not pleased with many
designs I have seen, although they might be graphically pleasing they are
hyped. Again, here is the balance between reasonable possibilities and
mystique - to put a damper on "poetic license."
