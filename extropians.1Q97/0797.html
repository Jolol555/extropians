At 07:29 PM 1/16/97 -0600, Eliezer Yudkowsky wrote:

>Art in the classical-painting sense,
>though, is simply dead to me.

Its been "dead" for some time. That's why architects build buildings called
museums with which to house these icons of earlier centuries. Like tombs
encompassing memories of the past, these structures avail themselves for
people to visit to take a look at the past, through images and stories
depicted in this dinosaurs.

Now, fast forward towards the late 20th Century and onto the promontory of
the 21st Century and to new artistic endeavors! Just as humans evolve, art
evolves.
