Oh, hogwash. You're not being "censored" in the least--you're just
being evaluated. That's what art is for--to provoke reaction, and it
did precisely that. The fact that one particular reaction was "hey,

all value. No one is suggesting stopping you or anyone from making
whatever art you please. We are every bit as free to judge works by
our freely chosen standards as you are to make them--and to criticize
us for not evaluating art by your standards.
