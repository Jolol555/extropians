I don't believe in exit polls. They claim to pick a 'random' sample of voters to
question, but its impossible for individual humans to pick other people
randomly. They will always bias their selection to those who seem similar to
them. If its liberal soccer moms running the exit polls, more liberal soccer
moms will get questioned, and more people in similar socio-economic strata will
be selected.
