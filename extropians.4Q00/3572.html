On a Discover Channel-style TV program, `Future Tense', last night in Oz, I
heard of a newly identified modulator called MGH (couldn't make out what
the M is, maybe some version of `muscle', while GH is unsurprisingly Growth
Hormone) which is the gadget that specifically activates muscle growth.
Exercise rips cause it to be excreted at site. They showed a poor guy who
mysteriously went hypertrophic for it, a disorder as yet with no name, and
his Arnie-like limb muscles etc need to be surgically reduced in size (urk)
even though he's doing no pumping. Now if I could get summa THAT!
