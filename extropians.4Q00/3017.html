                 The Social Life of Information
                        John Seely Brown
                           Paul Duguid

A fascinating look at social implication of the information
revolution. A must read for budding AI/SI types in particular. The
first three chapters are online at:

www.firstmonday.dk/issues/issue5_4/index.html

Probably the most interesting reading of the type since
Negroponte's "Being Digital".
