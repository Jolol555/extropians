Eugene, describe to me your dream communication medium. No holds
barred, putting practical considerations aside for the moment, just
what people would use for all their communications in a perfect world.

This is a serious question. It's a waste of time to get bogged down
criticizing existing media unless one has a clear picture of what they
should be like. Then, one can backwards-chain from that ideal to
current reality and figure out what needs to be done to approach the
ideal.
