In the vein of Eliezer's masterful story from last week...

The Magician
(C) Darin Sunley, 2000

What is the magician?
Someone who stands just a little bit closer to the mysteries then we do.

Shall we pity the magician?
What seems to us Magick is his humble mundaneity.
Is he to be denied the awe and wonder we see in his works?

Or is his the place of the master on the hill? His view of the hill traded for the distant mountians of the horizon we cannot see.
