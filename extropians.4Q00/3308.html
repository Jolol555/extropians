Hi all.

We are just in the process of revising the Extropy Institute FAQ, and would
like to hear your suggestions on what should be revised, rewritten, and
included. The revision of the FAQ is quite a large job, but I am hopeful
that the bulk of it can be completed by the end of this month. If you have
an interest in directly contributing to the FAQ revision (including writing
drafts for answers), please contact me at e_shaun@extropy.org; otherwise, I
hope to see some ideas for questions and suggestions for changes to the FAQ
passed around on this list.

                                                Thanks!
