"Giovani per sempre" by Lorenzo Soria

I just received a copy of the L'Espresso magazine (similar to Time
magazine) which topics cover economy, politics and culture.

The 7-page feature covers superlongevity's science, aesthetics and
technology. Interviewed are Michael Rose (evolutionary biology), Roy
Walford (_Maximum Life Span_ and Calorie Restriction) and myself
(_Create/Recreate_ ).

My Italian is non-functional - it's been too many years since I lived in
Italy - but if anyone would like to translate it, I'd be more than happy to
send a copy. The illustrations are very nice, and my future body design
"Primo 3M+" is on page 49.
