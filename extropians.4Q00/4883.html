Of course the answer being looked for here is "d", but my fincky little
brain wonders what's -really- in the middle of middle? Isn't there actually
NO letter in the absolute middle of middle? :)

Nothing is in the middle of middle.
What comes at the end of the end? Well, if it's -really- the end, then,
nothing.
