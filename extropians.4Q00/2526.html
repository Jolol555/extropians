> However we are constantly being
> told by the media that mobile phones have not taken off in the US to
> the same extent as in Europe. I'm curious as to American perceptions
> of this phenomena.

Short version: they're right.

Nonmobile phone service was and is better and cheaper in the US than in
Europe, or so I have heard. That being the case, there is less need
for mobile phones, despite the benefits. Which directly leads to there
being less mobile phones per capita.
