                          Living To 100

     Lessons in Living to your Maximum Potential at any age.
                   Thomas T Perls M.D. M.P.H.
                   Margery Hutter Silver Ed.D.
                       ISBN 0-465-04143-4
 
I've only just started this, but I'm very impressed, it's the kind
of book you can just start reading on any page. It's largely about
those who have lived to be 100 and what we can learn. Full of facts
and interesting personnal stories. An interesting section of
Internet resources as well.
