Michael S. Lorrey wrote,
> Frankly I find all this hubbub to be typical Democrat snotty spoil sport
> arrogance (and I'm wondering how many Daley's live in Palm Beach
> county...) Bore lost, Shrub won, give it up, move on...

No offense Mike, but this attitude really rankles us Floridians!

Bush has NOT won Florida because our vote count is not finished. All Bush
has at this point is the news media's *projections*, which have since been
retracted. Because of our mandatory recount law, preliminary results will
not be known until Tuesday, Nov. 14. Because of our overseas absentee
ballots, final results will not be known until Friday, Nov. 17. Any numbers
you hear before then are *projections* by the news media, the supervisor of
elections, or the party lawyers on both sides.

No official State of Florida results have been determined or released.
