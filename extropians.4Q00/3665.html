We're sort of going in circles on this one, so I'd like to make my final,
definitive comment on this topic:

We are -NOT- information. We are a particular physical discrete instance of
a self-aware dynamic who's constinuent parts can be described by
information, but are not, themselves, information. We exist as an instance
of implementation. Failing to make a distinction between ourselves and the
description of ourselves is imprecise and functionally problematic.

"Good night Springton. There will be no encore."
