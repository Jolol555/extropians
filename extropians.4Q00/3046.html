In my quest for the Perfect Place to Live I now find myself enjoying the
clear spring air, graceful architecture, and abundant parklands of
Melbourne, Victoria. Damien has been showing me around the area, has
introduced me to some of the more interesting people here. It's been highly
entertaining to listen to the reactions of the people here to American
politics, especially at this particular moment.

I've maybe been corrupting Damien--we went dumpster-diving yesterday, found
a lovely bicycle which I will be using for future sight seeing expeditions.

Barbara Lamar
