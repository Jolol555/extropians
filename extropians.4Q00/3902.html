This *is not* stalking. It only becomes stalking when you actually pick
up a phone.

> Here, just to make it interesting, I bet you won't find anything
> (interesting) about me that isn't any of the following:

You're right, if you list every possible interesting fact about yourself,
there's not much left for the Game of Knowing. After that, it isn't fun
any more. It's simply a 'Net search contest. If you just say: "When
will I graduate?" and I say "2001", that's not "stalking", as you call it;
more like a quiz.

The whole point of the Game is to achieve surprise, casual reference,
creepiness. You've been an actor, so you should understand the importance
of preserving the image of casualness - just like maintaining a "morning
shadow" style beard and mustache.
