I found a site which was apparently the source of the graph I
saw in the L.A. Times on Friday, showing Buchanan's vote against
the total vote, by Florida county. Look down to the bottom of:

http://madison.hss.cmu.edu/

This site also graphs Buchanan against Bush votes, against Reform party
membership, and against Buchanan votes in the 1996 primary. In each
case the West Palm Beach county is an extreme outlier. The site also
has links to other papers analyzing the Palm Beach vote. It's amazing
how quickly all this data has been made available via the internet,
and the number of reports which are already available.
