Dan Fabulich <daniel.fabulich@yale.edu> writes:

> I'm no fatalist, but there aren't that many reasonable possibilities,
> even from this side of the Singularity.

I think this is just because we do not have enough information and
imagination. A lot of developments are obvious possibilities in
retrospect but look very unlikely or weird before they happen.

Personally I think civilisations do not converge in the long run,
despite living under the same ultimate laws of physics - cultural
differences may make them branch in completely different directions.
