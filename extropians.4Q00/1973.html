Hi, everyone.

We, Metamorphosis Society, has revised our web site.
I think it has now became more understandable.
The following is the gist of our revised web site:

"Metamorphosis Society is a mutual support group for people who want
to do something for the betterment of the future and wish to "go and
see the future" through cryopreservation and reanimation."

"Through active on-line discussion and exchange of views among a
substantial number of members (hopefully at least 100), we may be able
not only to increase the possibility of reanimation for cryopreserved
patients but also to build a better future.
If we keep the minimum number of 100 members for a long time, it will
be a vital foundation to create even higher possibility of reanimation
as well as to materialize an even better future."

You can see more detail information on our site;
