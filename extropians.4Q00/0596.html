The Orgasmic Bureaucrat

In an interview in May in the trade journal of the American Industrial
Hygiene
Association, the director of the Occupational Safety and Health
Administration's safety standards program, Marthe Kent, said she loves her
job: "I absolutely love it. I was born to regulate. I don't know why, but
that's very true. So as long as I'm regulating, I'm happy." Kent, who heads
the agency's controversial ergonomics program (which oversees the effects
of,
for example, furniture design on back stress), said, "If you put out a reg,
it
matters. I think that's really where the thrill comes from. And it is a
thrill; it's a high."
