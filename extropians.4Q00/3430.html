Ed Merta has written a tense, frightening fictional scenario of a
destructive runaway AI singularity. I found it gripping and exciting
although the outcome is pessimistic. It is available from his web site
at http://www.nmia.com/~emerta/fictscen.html or from sci.nanotech at
http://www.deja.com/getdoc.xp?AN=700480065.

Excerpt:

    *******FLASH*********U.S. FORCES STRIKE SOUTH AFRICA

    Praetoria (Internet News Service) - 03:14 EST, November 17, 2039

    U.S. sea and space forces have conducted a major bombardment of
    scientific and industrial forces on the outskirts of Praetoria,
    according to observers on the scene. Explosions and fires reportedly
    stretch from one side of the horizon to the other. Hundreds of
    human and robot workers were believed killed in the attack.
