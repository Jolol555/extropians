Eliezer S. Yudkowsky wrote:

> There's a *little* more to it than just that, you know.

Oh?

I'd love to know a few tricks!

Here, just to make it interesting, I bet you won't find anything
(interesting) about me that isn't any of the following:

1) I go to Yale, where I fight with foam swords, ran a live-action
Vampire game, and performed in a couple of plays, (I've been mentioned
in Yale's paper a couple of times,) but I'm originally from LA.

2) I'm a fan of Richard Garriott's Ultima series; I wrote a
walkthrough for Ultima VIII.

3) I like puzzles, like the kind you'll find on www.thestone.com. I
even won a gift certificate for logging onto Salon at five past
midnight.

4) The Bobs impress me.

5) I'm interested in hypertext, but I don't think it will work until
Chaum-like anonymous ecash is available.

Hint: I suppose I must be some relation to Jack, but I don't know
what it is.
