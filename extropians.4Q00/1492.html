I am pleased to announce that Extropy Institute has given a lifetime
membership to Brian and Sabine Atkins. In addition to an extremely
generous financial contribution to Extropy Institute, Brian and Sabine have
both provided a large intellectual contribution to the development of ExI,
and will certainly do so for many years to come.

So congratulations to you both!
