stress on the "ankh" syllable, like you said, not the
other way, because a lot of the pharaohs intentionally
chose a name with the word "ankh" in it to represent

intentionally choose a name with "ankh" in it and then
pronounce it in a way which doesn't include "ankh" at
all... :-)
