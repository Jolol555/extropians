Having finished Ian Banks' "About Weapons", here are some culture ship
names I've come up with of a decidely dilbertian scope:
Success By Lowering Expectations
Mediocrity Unleashed
The Cure for Hope
So Long As It's Black
Your Best Just Isn't Good Enough
The Downside of Being Better
Not All Pain is Gain
Serving As A Warning To Others
A Dissatisfying Relationship
Compensating For Lack Of Skill
These would be names for some of the less success oriented ship minds...
