Damien Broderick wrote:
>
> Will AIs be prey to
> such lexical pranks in their own code re-writing? Scary if so...

Dream along with me; dream of an AI that makes no unnoticed slips; dream
of an AI with code that goes somewhere beyond crystalline error-checking
and beyond stochastics, to a realm where a single slip of the code or
radiation bitflip has no more effect than a pebble dropped into a
waterfall.

Perfection is often too expensive to be attainable, but the true craftsman
is never comfortable in the presence of a known imperfection.

(Slaps Damien on the back.) "Don't worry! I'll handle it!"
