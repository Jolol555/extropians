Dear Extropes,

Noting some special days:

On this day, December 27, 1571, Kepler discovered that planets travel on
elliptical orbits.

Also on this day, December 27, 1831, Darwin began his voyage on the
HMS Beagle.

On the day December 25, 1642, Isaac Newton was born.

Of course we all know that every day is a special day, but this is the time
of year when most people like to pause and reflect, therefore:

               Happy Holidays, everyone!

Amara
(presently in SoCal)
