when I didn't check my extropians mail, I received 25 messages from Mike
Lorrey alone, 18 from JR; 84 messages total. I don't think anyone here
is smart enough to make a meaningful intellectual contribution at that
posting level. If people would restrict themselves to half a dozen
posts per day, they'd have time to make their messages more thoughtful
and worth the time others take to read them. How about it?
