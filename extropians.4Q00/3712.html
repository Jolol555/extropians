Knowing intellectually that there is no difference between me and my
copy is one thing. Fighting the self-preservation instincts that
want to keep my current physical body intact will be quite another,
I'm sure.

Similarly, I know that tetanus shots are good for me to have every
ten years. Instinctively, I *hate* needles with a passion.

I'm sure that if the opportunity arises for me to be backed up
destructively, I'll have an awful lot of variables to weigh.
