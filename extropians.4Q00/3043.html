> Love is not limited to reproduction.
>
> Love's only purpose is to serve itself.

Love = The desire for higher organization and higher complexity

Love, whether it is directed towards another person, towards an art form,
towards a study, towards one's self, or towards the universe, is what drives
entities to create- in essence, love is a very extropian principle. Even if
marriage is a dying institution, love isn't leaving- love will continue to
drive humanity and the cosmos onward to it's final destination, wherever
that may be.

> Now we're entering the domain of poets.....

And what a sweet domain it is.
