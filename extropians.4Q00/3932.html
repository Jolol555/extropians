Phil Blake wrote:

>Is Moores Law just based on the speed of the clock or does it tie in
>with performance?

Moore's Law is actually fairly simple: that every two years we will be able
to double the quantity of transistors on an integrated circuit. This
pretty much speaks for a computer's speed, though generally performance is
relatively parallel.
