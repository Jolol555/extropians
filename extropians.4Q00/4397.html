KPJ wrote:
>
> What about the last time some states tried to leave the federation?
> South vs North, and all that. They did not succeed, AFAIK.
> Feel free to elaborate on why secessionists would have more luck today.

It would be politically impossible for American troops to fire on American
citizens, even if a state attempted to succeed. It is almost equally
unlikely that the secessionist movement in any state will attain a
plurality, much less the supermajority that would probably be needed.
