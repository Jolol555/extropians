zeb haradon wrote:
>
> I just realized, if it gets to the point where we can vote online, then what
> is to prevent the elimination of congress altogether, and simply have people
> vote directly for each bill (besides the time it would take)?

The time it would take - for citizens to thoroughly read the bills being
voted on, debate every bill that comes up (not just the ones that
attract much press), et cetera and so forth - is enough of a cost.
Legislative processes, like most processes, seem to flow naturally
towards the most efficient if not constrained (say, by a dictator's
wish to retain power).
