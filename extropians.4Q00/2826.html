Apologies if this is redundant non-news to
anybody, but I just ran into it myself:

"One Half of a Manifesto" by Jaron Lanier,
at http://www.edge.org/3rd_culture/lanier/lanier_index.html

The author is a computer scientist who criticizes
the over-enthusiastic embrace of the elements of
Singularitarian thinking (as it is known around here)
by many of his colleagues,

The author calls this familiar complex of ideas
"cybernetic totalism".
