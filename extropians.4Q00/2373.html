I am an Extropian Mystic.
Mysticism is at the root of extropy.
We seek to uncover the mysteries of the universe.
We seek to explore our potential with magick: amazing powers of will.
Any Extropian embraces the supernatural, the superhuman.
To deny the mysticism of Extropy is to deny it's power.
Theoretical science is magick.
We peer into the future and advance technology
beyond anything that has ever been.
How much more mystical can you get?
I know valuable things that no one else knows,
unless they discovered it too.
That alone makes me both extropian and mystical.
They words become one and the same.
If you don't understand something it does not mean it does not work,
it means you have much to learn.
