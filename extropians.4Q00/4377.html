Last June, FeedMag columnist Erik Davis wrote a lengthy article which
touched on the "adolescent" philosophy of Extropianism. Sortly thereafter,
Max More wrote a response justifying the merits and credible need for
Extropianism. Though FeedMag didn't publish the response at the time, it
came available on their website yesterday (though still in association with
the old article).

You can access it at the following (long) URL:

http://www.feedmag.com/loopframe.html?loop=http://www.feedmag.com/cgi-bin/lo
op/loop.cgi?function=underview&document=fr347&stringhead=347.8.3
