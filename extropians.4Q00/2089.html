> > I wonder what a Florida absentee ballot is going for on Ebay?

Another issue that has not hit the national news yet: The Republican Party
somehow got their own version of absentee ballots that are not printed the
same way as the standard ones. They lack the coded numbers on the bottom
that confirm that each absentee voter only got one vote. The Republican
Party says that they can validate their own ballots outside the regular
system, but the Democrats don't want Republican votes being counted outside
the regular election process. Look for more legal challenges on these.

This election is so close with so many irregularities, that neither side is
going to agree that the other side one.
