As some of you know, I am now Chief Content Architect at ManyWorlds, Inc.
ManyWorlds does (among several things) New Economy business model
consulting. Each week ManyWorlds' home page features new items. This week's
features focus on Information Technology Futures, including Ray Kurzweil's
book, a classic MIT paper on 21st Century Organizations, and a white paper
on the future of business-to-business e-marketplaces.

If you select featured topic, "IT Futures", you will get a list of featured
content on this topic. Use the controls on the left to find even more
items. One recent entry in this section is Robin Hanson's paper. You don't
need PowerPoint, since ManyWorlds' Epiture-powered site will display the
slides within your browser. Have fun at:
