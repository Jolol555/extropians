A new concept: The Presidential Agnostic

As a Presidential Agnostic, I do not admit or deny that either candidate has
won the election. I merely believe that it is not possible for the human
mind to detect a national race within 229 votes. There is simply no way for
a mere human to detect or prove the existence of this theoretical margin.
