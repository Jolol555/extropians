137 is the numerical value of
"Kabala" (Quf-Beit-Lamed-Hey)

1/137 means the alpha constant = e^2/hc/2¼

That made a deep impression on W. Pauli
(and many others) who started thinking
about a strange link between magic and physics

Pauli died in Zurich in the room
number 137 of the Red Cross Hospital
