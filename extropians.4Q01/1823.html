This is great news...

Conner JM, Darracq MA, Roberts J, Tuszynski MH.
Nontropic actions of neurotrophins: subcortical nerve growth factor gene
delivery reverses age-related degeneration of primate cortical cholinergic
innervation.
PNAS (USA) 98(4):1941-6 (13 Feb 2001).

http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov:80/entrez/query.fcgi?cmd=Retrieve&db=PubMed&list_uids=11172055&dopt=Abstract

Bottom line: NGF turns back the clock (at least in Rhesus monkeys).
