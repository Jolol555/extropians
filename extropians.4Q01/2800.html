At long last, a Singularitarian FAQ exists.

http://homepage.mac.com/sing_rc/faq.html

The FAQ provides answers to the most common questions concerning the
Singularity and Singularitarians. The recommended reading level is
Transhumanist and up, but there are more details on that in the document

(I don't expect that to be a problem here, but if you post the link
elsewhere you may want to mention this).

If you have any comments to make on the FAQ and you post them to the
list, please CC me since I'm not a member of the Extropians list (I
don't like it when my INBOX explodes ;-)), unless of course you'd prefer

I'd read your message in the archives rather than via e-mail.
Otherwise, just send them to me. I'm interested in feedback people
might have, but thus far the Singularitarian community has received it
well.
