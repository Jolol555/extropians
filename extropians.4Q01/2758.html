...more limericks

Whenever he got in a fury a
Schizophrenic from Upper Manchuria,
Had pseudocyesis,
Didiadochokinesis
And haematoporphyrimuria. 

---

Pulmoray tuberculosis
Is all very well in small doses.
But a gap in the lung
As big as a bung
Means years while you twiddle
 your toeses.

---

We're proud of humanity's powers
But these potions and medicines of ours
Coffee, garlic, and spices,
Evolved as devices
So that insects would stop bugging flowers.

---

There was a young fellow of Burma
Whose bethrothed had a good cause to murmur.
But now that he's married, he's
Been using cantharides
And the root of his love is much firmer.

---

Your turn.
