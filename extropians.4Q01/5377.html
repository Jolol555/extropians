Modafinil isn't extremely effective. I advise my patients to take 400mg/day
and not to expect too much. In general it is still dangerous, if not
foolhardy, to try to modify the sleep cycle, except in certain unusual
circumstances (narcolepsy, idiopathic hypersomnia).
