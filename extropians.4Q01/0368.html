100,000 people die everyday.
5000 people died in new york one day
10,000 people die in the US everyday
Does anybody care.

Many of them are children
They haven't lived very long.
Many of them are old
They haven't lived very long.
Does anybody care.

I want to declare a war on death.
Human death.

-I
Don't care whose on my side.
