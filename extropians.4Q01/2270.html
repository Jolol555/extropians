Pioneering heart disease researcher in the use of genetic therapy, Dr
Jeffrey Isner died suddenly of a heart attack on the morning of October
31, 2001. He was only fifty-three.

http://www.heartcenteronline.com/myheartdr/home/research-detail.cfm?reut
ersid=2046&name=moreover

Reading the article made me think back to an audiotape a friend gave me
about five years ago. It was entitled, "Dead Doctors don't lie." The
author was Dr. Joel Wallach, who is on a crusade to tell people it is a
lack of colloidal minerals which leads to early deaths like this. On
the tape he told story after story in a gregarious yet chilling way, of
cardiac doctors who died of unforeseen heart attacks while only in
middle age.

http://www.deaddoctors.com/html/whathelps.htm
