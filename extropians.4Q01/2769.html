There once was a Extrope, named Egan
who writing science fiction, was seek'in
a means, past an end,
which is better to fend
off, either dy'in or freak'in

There once was a Vernor, named Vinge
who once was considered, the fringe
His book was greated with hilarity
Now he flips his critics, the bird
safely esconced past the Singularity

There once was an inventor named Kurzweil
who quipped, once or twice, with a smile
"no need to be so psychotic, because computers are symbiotic,
they'll help us be better, in a while."
