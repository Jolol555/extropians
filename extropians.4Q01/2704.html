"I tell you, as long as I can conceive something better than myself
I cannot be easy unless I am striving to bring it into existence or
clearing the way for it. This is the law of my life. That is the working
within me of Life's incessant aspiration to higher organization, wider,
deeper, intense self-consciousness and clearer self-understanding."
-- George Bernard Shaw ('Man and Superman')
