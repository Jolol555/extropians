    This is the 50'th update of my fly longevity experiments. All bottles
contain 1/4 tsp citric acid as a standard additive. Recorded average
temperature during the experiment was 25.7 C.
    In the current experiment I test the effect of different doses of a
variety of fibers on fly mortality. No significant benefit on average
longevity was seen. I attribute the poor maximum longevity of the control
bottle, to their being only 10 flies in this bottle. The toxicity of
xanthan gum is here confirmed. Some protein sources are tested in the next
run.
