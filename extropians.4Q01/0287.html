A great online game (requires Flash) to try is Bin Laden's Liquors:

http://www.fieler.com/terror/

This is a first person shooter game where the object is to shoot as many
terrorists and as few hostages as possible, much like "Hogan's Alley".
The setting is Osama bin Laden's Liquor Store, where his al Qaeda
network has its secret headquarters and his holding hostages bound up
and in their undies. Complimentary Slush Puppies are included, reload as
much as you want. Game ends when 10 terrorists escape. Points are
deducted for shot hostages.
