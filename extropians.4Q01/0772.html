Google has cooperated in the past whenever government agencies have removed
a webpage from public view. Google's lawyers correctly assume that anything
that causes problems for the original page owners is likely to cause Google
similar problems later. These actions are usually the result of copyright
lawsuits, but has occurred for other reasons such as national security,
international relations, and national security.
