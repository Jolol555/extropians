Well, people finally seem to be getting serious again about
space development. Strategies for making fuel and solar cells
out of available materials were discussed at a recent conference
at the Colorado School of Mines.

URL:
http://dailynews.yahoo.com/h/space/20011114/sc/deep_space_exploration_-_looking_for_planetary_paydirt_1.html

This is along the lines of my arguments for Space Development Paths:
http://www.aeiveos.com/~bradbury/Papers/SDP.html
