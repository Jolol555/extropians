A nice description of the "is the copy *me*"-debate, Harvey. Thanks!

On Thu, Nov 29, 2001 at 01:58:19PM -0600, Harvey Newstrom wrote:

> I would love to see a discussion about this, but I don't think it belongs in
> this forum. It is a very specific philosophical question about how to
> define the self, others, identity, and other meta-questions about what it
> means to be "me".

Maybe the way forward is a metameta debate? The problem is that we end
up in semantics while really trying to say something relevant about
selfhood. So the goal should maybe to start a discussion about in what
ways we could settle such questions. Is it only an issue of getting
everybody to agree on the same semantics, and then everything will be
clear, or could there be experiments or other procedures to settle
selfhood issues?
