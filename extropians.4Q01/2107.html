Dear Extropes,

If one colors the travelling path of a Jovian dust streams particle
with the electrostatic charge potential that the dust particle is
carrying, then those tiny dust motes generate their own Art Forms.

Spaghetti colorati!

courtesy of the Jovian dust streams particles.
