  The American Association for the Advancement of Science
  and Science magazine are pleased to announce the launch of
  SAGE KE, our new Science of Aging Knowledge
  Environment. http://sageke.sciencemag.org

  Free to all users, SAGE KE brings together the best of the
  myriad scientific disciplines dealing with aging. Anyone
  involved in the science of aging or related fields will find this
  resource invaluable. Features include:

  * GENES/INTERVENTIONS DATABASE: A searchable
  compendium of genes and interventions related to aging. It
  includes references, functional information, homologs, links to
  sequence databases, and much more.

  * KNOWLEDGE MAP: A unique search/browse tool
  exclusive to SAGE KE. It is a taxonomy that organizes aging
  research by model organisms, experimental approaches, levels
  of analysis, biological processes, and diseases.

  * ORIGINAL NEWS, REVIEWS, PERSPECTIVES, AND
  VIEWPOINT ARTICLES

  * VIRTUAL LIBRARY OF CLASSIC PAPERS

  * DISCUSSION FORUMS

  * MUCH MORE

  Log on to http://sageke.sciencemag.org today and check out
  this new online community of aging.
  If you already have a username and password for any type of
  individual access to Science Online, STKE, or
  AAASMember.org, there is no need to register. Your
  username and password will work for SAGE KE.

  See you at SAGE KE!

  Evelyn Strauss and Kelly LaMarco
  SAGE KE Editors

  SAGE KE was funded by a generous grant from the Ellison
  Medical Foundation.
