Art Bell, who does a late-night talk radio show with an emphasis on
mysterious phenomena, will have nanotech expert Ralph Merkle as a guest
Tuesday night / Wednesday morning. The guest usually starts at 11 PM
Pacific Time and the show runs for several hours. The program is widely
syndicated or you can listen to it at www.artbell.com. I notice that the
listing at http://www.artbell.com/schedule.html has links to Foresight,
Zyvex and Alcor, so it will be interesting to see how much cryonics
is mentioned.
