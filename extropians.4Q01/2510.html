Kevin Kelly neatly summarized the importance of networks in his book, _Out of
Control_:

"The only organization capable of unprejudiced growth, or unguided learning,
is a network."

"One can imagine the future shape of companies by stretching them until they
are pure network."

"In network economics, more brings more."
