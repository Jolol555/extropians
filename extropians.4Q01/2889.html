Even here, in cloudy and light-polluted Stockholm the leonids were
visible. I was walking home, thinking about other things and
absent-mindedly watching the sky when suddenly a bright white meteor
flashed to the north. A beautiful clear white line ending in a slight
bulge, making a elegant contrast to the black sky and the orange glow
from the trainyards north of where I live.

I immediately wished for the Omega Point.
