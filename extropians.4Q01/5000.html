>Me too.

I wrote about the "Transhumanist Spirituality" some years ago concerning
the benefits of paying attention to what we say, how we say it, and for
what reason. I asked that we pay more attention to a humane understanding
and instill this sensibility into transhumanness.

Unless and until those who provoke and promote irritability, insensitivity
and depravation of common sense take hold of themselves and instill a more
refined emotional stance, the level of intelligence cannot and will not
move forward.

The credo of transhumanity has been and will continue to be desirous of
electing to bringing a better and longer life to all humanity, not just a
chosen few. Anyone who attempts to dispel this doctrine that has been so
preciously nurtured, find an outlet for their xenophobia elsewhere - where
it can grow, but not here. Here we are propagating a more promising
outlook for all humanity and transhumanity.
