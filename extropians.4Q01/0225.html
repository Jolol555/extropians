The father was reading the school report which had just been handed to him by
his hopeful son, Nasrudin. His brow was wrathful as he read: "English, poor;
French, weak; Mathematics, poor: History, weak," and he gave a glance of
disgust at the quaking lad.
"Well, Dad," said Nasrudin, "It is not as good as it might be, but have you
seen that?" And he pointed to the next line, which read:
"Health, excellent."
