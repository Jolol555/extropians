A couple of weeks ago there was a brief thread on the SF author Ken Mcleod.
Having spent the last fortnight reading his collected works I am very
greatful to Damien Broderick and Charlie Stross for bringing him to my
attention, just fantastic. I came across Greg Egan originally on this list
in 1995, the spinoffs from this list have given me some of my most enjoyable
reading experiences. All I need now is for Anders Sanberg to start writing
novels.
