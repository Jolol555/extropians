Quite awhile ago I saw a videotape preview for a Dimension Films production called "Imposter." I was extremely impressed by the storyline, cast, and FX. On a tv ad I saw yesterday the release date was announced to be Christmas day.

The film is based on a shortstory by Phillip K. Dick. Gary Sinise stars as a respected scientist and family man in the year 2079, who wakes up one day to find the gov't believes he is a imposter. I got the impression the gov't thought a nanotech device was used to restructure him to be a traitor in the midst of a ongoing war.

http://www.dimensionfilms.com/pls/front_con/dim_movie_page.entryPoint?midStr=1368

This could be a film Extropians will really enjoy! I hope so.
