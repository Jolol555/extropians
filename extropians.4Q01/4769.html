> communication with those they admit as superiors (father; boss; priest) or

> return. Because of this, they have a set of practices (innately) that

Speaking as an INTJ, most of the above is nonsense. We don't
have any superiors! :-) And I don't think anyone notices me
not saying directly what is on my mind. If people attempt to
flag me with silly body gestures then I purposefully ignore them
as a matter of policy. It is not meant as an offense but as
self-defense against group coercion.
