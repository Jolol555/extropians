5 October 1956

The Dodgers come back from a 6-0 deficit in Game 2 to win 13-8 behind the
sterling relief pitching of Don Bessent. Yankee catcher Yogi Berra becomes
the only player in World Series history to hit a grand slam in a losing cause
as he sends one over the RF screen off Don Newcombe in the 2nd.
