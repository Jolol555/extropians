Here're two articles from the Premises Of Post-Objectivism web site that are
classified under the above trio, plus the abstracts from them. I thought
some of you might find them interesting.

"The Culture as an Ideal Society" at:
http://folk.uio.no/thomas/po/the-culture.html

"Thomas Gramstad explains why he thinks science fiction writer Iain M.
Banks' utopian future society ought to be recognized as the ultimate
embodiment of Randian (or any liberty-loving) social theory."

and

"Immortality Would be Worth it" at:
http://folk.uio.no/thomas/po/infinity-and-immortality.html

"Svein Olav Nyberg on infinity and immortality in Objectivity, in response
to Stephen Hicks."

Another article there which I've already mentioned on this list is "Can
Cryopreservation Solve "The Abortion Problem"?" at:
http://folk.uio.no/thomas/po/cryobortion.html

"Thomas Gramstad thinks it might."
