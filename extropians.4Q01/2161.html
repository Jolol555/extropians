                  Letters to a Young Contrarian
                      Christopher Hitchens
                       ISBN 0-465-03032-7
                           U.S. $22:00

This was my first introduction to Mr Hitchens, and I've read
several of his pieces online since I started this book.

A popular columnist for "Vanity Fair" and "The Nation". Mr Hitchens
presents a series of arguments in the form of letters on the
purpose and value of disagreement on a wide variety of subjects.

Sacred cows are in danger wherever this man walks.

Delightfull.
