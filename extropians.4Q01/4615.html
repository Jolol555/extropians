'stabilize' in a direction different than that of the target-nation. thus,
the target-nation is in unwanted transition and is destablized for a time
period (dependent upon how flexibility of the target-country [in receiving
these new rules] and on the mass of the external force).

actually behave as preservative stabilization. but why would an external
