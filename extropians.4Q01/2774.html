just on the edge
there was a hedge
and upon a ledge
the total knowledge
as a precious pledge.

Tipler was the gambler
or perhaps the assembler
but rather the stumbler
who made that cobbler.

That mass and proper time
clinch together like a vine
for sure it is not mine:
was said from time to time.

-s.
