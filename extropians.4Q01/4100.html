                          Small Spaces
             Inspiring Ideas and Creative Solutions
                      (Sir) Terence Conran
                       ISBN 0-609-060940-8
                           U.S. $40.00

The latest from Terence Conran, one of the worlds leading
designers, furniture makers, restauranteurs, and retailers.

Not merely the next great coffee table book, but an intriguing look
at good designs for small spaces. Don't just admire the many
beautifull photographs, but read the interesting text.

Anyone with any sense of aesthetics will find this interesting.

Extropians should live in well designed (small?) spaces....
