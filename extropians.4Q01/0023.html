> If we mistakenly assume that these terrorists are cowards, we will again be

measures we take against them in accordance with this belief... and
what we publicly tell them and anybody who listens. Given that they
come from a culture that values bravery and honor, hammering away at
their cowardice in every public forum might in some small way make it
harder for the opponent to indoctrinate future terrorists.
