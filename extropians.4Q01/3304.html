I can only speak for myself in this, but I personally feel a bit
uncomfortable when my name shows up in subject lines. I also wish I
wasn't identified quite so personally with the ideas I advocate, although
I recognize that this is mostly my own fault as the result of not
realizing until much too late that this would become a problem.
