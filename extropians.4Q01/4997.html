Mike Lorrey wrote:
>
>
> While I support free markets, I also support anti-dumping laws. The
> degree to which the Indian government subsidizes its domestic computer
> industry indicates that this is a case of labor dumping. Dumping is NOT
> free trade.

Basically empty accusation I believe. The cost of living is so
much lower in India that without any subsidies whatsoever Indian
programming shops can severely underbid American ones. So, we
who intend to stay here either upgrade skills and our niche to
something not available in such shops, change professions, or
move.
