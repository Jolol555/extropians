> Grok: verb. To fully understand, see, get it, dig, etc.

Serfocracy: a polity of serfs who only think they govern themselves.

Big Swell: different from Hard and Soft Takeoffs, the Big Swell assumes
that every individual follows their own individual path to the
singularity, based on their own abilities, skills, intelligence, and
economic means (as well as the society they live in), such that the
number of individuals transcending at any given time follows roughly a
bell curve from the beginning of the singularity period to the end.
