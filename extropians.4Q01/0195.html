                      Creative Destruction

Why Companies That Are Built to Last Underperform the Market-and
How to Successfully Transform them.

               By Richard Foster and Sarah Kaplan
                       ISBN- 0-385-50133-1
                           U.S. $27.50

A fascinating book by a current partner and former member of
McKinsey & Company. The parts on mental maps alone justifies the
purchase.

Don't confuse this book with the one by the MIT press that has the
same name/subject matter.
