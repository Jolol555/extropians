Anders Sandberg, Sun Nov 11, 2001
>I think we need more homefull people. Not everybody has to be one, but
>just like a small amount of fibers added to a matrix make a composite
>material far stronger a small number of homefull people with a greater
>understanding of other cultures and the complexities of their
>interactions can help make the matrix of their (current) countries
>stronger.

For homefull people, the effect might work both ways. As the matrix of
cultures become stronger with the fibers of homefull people, the
fibers of homefull people become stronger by the matrix of cultures.

At least that's how it works for me. Diversity of cultures is a
necessary nourishment. I can't imagine any more living without it.

Amara
