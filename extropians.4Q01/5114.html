Whether or not you agree with his views on the market economy, you'll have
to admit Damien Broderick is one of the best sf writers alive today.

TRANSCENSION is set in one possible post-Spike world, so Extropians should
find it particularly interesting. Don't want to over-fill everyone's mailbox
by sending a long post to the list, so here's a link to my website where
you'll find the opening chapters and a scan of the cover. The novel will be
released by Tor Books in February 2002, too late for newtonmas alas, but
there's always Charles Darwin Day.

The cover is here: http://www.thespike.addr.com/index.htm

or if you want to go directly to the opening chapters, they're here
http://www.thespike.addr.com/TRANSCENSION.htm
