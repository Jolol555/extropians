Economic Terror

by Congressman Ron Paul, MD

Dealing with the slumping economy will prove every bit as challenging to
Congress as fighting terrorism.

No one challenges the need to protect American citizens from further
terrorist attacks, but there is much debate throughout the country as to
how it should be done and whether personal liberty here at home must be
sacrificed. Many are convinced that our efforts overseas might escalate
the crisis and actually precipitate more violence. A growing number of
Americans are becoming concerned that our efforts to preserve our
freedoms and security will result in the unnecessary sacrifice of that
which we've pledge to protect- our constitutionally protected liberty
