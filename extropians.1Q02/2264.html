I'll gently chide Max by asking him to read this essay by Richard Stallman
discussing how to discourage the use of closed source software:

http://www.newsforge.com/article.pl?sid=02/01/10/1711238&mode=thread

I'm sure Harvey could comment here as well.

I know of no greater risk to humanity than a breakout amoral AI
that piggybacks on closed source software.

Choose open source -- even if it means sacrificing functionality.
Your life may depend on it.
