Over the past couple of months, David McFadzean and I have been trying to
figure out how a certain address was subscribed to this list...one that
hadn't asked to be subscribed in the first place. I have no records of the
subscription of the address, so can only assume that an alternate address
currently subscribed to this list has been routed to:
regiondeutschland@vemission.org...yes, the address of a mission in Germany.

If anyone can shed some light on how this organization came to receive
--unwarranted-- the extropians list, please let me know so I can get these
poor people off the list!
