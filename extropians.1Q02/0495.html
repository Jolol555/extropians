Hi all,

This is just a quick reminder that for those of you interested in joining
or forming a transhumanist local group, please type in your information on
the form at http://www.extropy.org/join.htm. Just your name, email
address, city and country are fine...you don't need to become a member or
Free Electronic Member.

So far we have interested people in such diverse areas as: Singapore,
Switzerland, Australia and Canada, as well as a number from the Continental
USA.
