HEIDELBERG, Germany, Jan 9, 2002 /PRNewswire via COMTEX/ -- Cellzome AG
today announced the publication of a pioneering study in the proteomics
field in the January 10 issue of the journal Nature describing the first
draft of a functional map of the yeast proteome. The map visualizes an
entire network of protein complexes and their interactions in yeast
Saccharomyces cerevisae, forming a basis for the operative organization
underlying a cell's activity under different conditions, thereby making
the map a first of its kind.
