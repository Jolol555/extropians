Damien Broderick wrote:
>>Make up your mind, Are you a `free thinker' or an atheist?<<

I am both an atheist & a freethinker. Though it is rare even believers can
be considered a freethinker depending if they do not follow an authority
figure, book, etc. For example, even though Deists are not atheists many of
them can be considered a freethinker. Christians by definition are not
freethinkers and it is impossible for a Christian to be a free thinker.
