On Wed, 6 Feb 2002, Brian D Williams wrote a story
that I think I'll title "Extrication strategies
when one finds oneself in the jam".

Its one of the best stories I've heard in a long time.
I hereby nominate it for post of the month and
hope that I have the opportunity to hear him
tell it live and in person at a future extro
conference.

In my book the purpose of life is to collect
(and share) good stories. Perhaps it should
be a standard feature of extro conferences to
have a story telling session.
