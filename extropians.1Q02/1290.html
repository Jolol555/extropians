At 12:09 AM 1/18/02, Damien wrote:

>I can only read this as a rebuke from one of the founders of extropianism,
>telling me that a discussion of one way to enhance health broadly in the
>community, and the varieties of ways to fund that, is OFF TOPIC for this list.
>
>It seems to me just barely possible that what got up T0M's nose about my
>posts wasn't the *topic*; it was my horrid opinions.
>
>I need a ruling on this from Max or Greg.

Damien, as far as I am concerned, your topic of discussion is *completely*
relevant. I may not agree with your views on this (though I am very
concerned to figure out effective ways to improve access of poorer people
to life-sustaining and extending medical treatments), but I can't see how
it can possibly be off-topic.

Back when we had stricter and *different* rules, your POV may have been
inappropriate to the List, but those times are long past.
