Thanks to all the replies off-list and on-list.

I may need further help with this CD. To explain, at the request of the
Smithsonian Institute's Cooper-Hewitt National Design Museum, I am writing
a proposal titled "Radical Body Design" which features "Primo". My paper
reflects the foundations of the transhumanist perspective as it relates to
superlongevity and from the design perspective of Artificial Intelligent
Skin (AiS), Smart Skin (sS), nanoskin, and reference to Extropian
philosophy, Robert Freitas's _Nanomedicine_. It also reflects knowledge of
the scientific team which I collaborated with for "Primo's"
conception. The CD is one aspect of my proposal, and which will be a copy
of the net.art piece http://www.natasha.cc/primo.htm.

Now that my paper is in final draft, I am working on the design layout for
the brochure which will also be used for the CD.

Thanks again for all the informative replies I received. You all are really
FANTASTIC!
