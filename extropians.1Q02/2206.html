On Fri, 1 Feb 2002, Eliezer S. Yudkowsky wrote:

> For what it's worth, Wayne, if we somehow wind up in a scenario where both
> Eugene and I are around as uploads, and we both have the unrestricted
> ability to affect Earth, and you're still around on Earth, then I'll
> protect you from Eugene. I can't make any realistic commitment on behalf
> of a transhuman future self because my goals might change, but that's the
> current output of my goal system.

I'll toss my generosity into the ring. I'll promise to keep an uploaded
version of Wayne around (no information loss, very extropic), however I
can't guarantee that I'll give you real-time CPU rights. Being realistic
I think one can assume that the goals of near-gods probably trump the
goals of pre-post-humans.

Wayne, let us know what the worst moment of your life has been. I'm sure
I can setup the VR such that I can arrange to have it endlessly replayed.

Robert
