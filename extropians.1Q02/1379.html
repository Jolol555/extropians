- all you need to protect yourself from the sunlight, mosquitoes, deer ticks
and rednecks.

Isn't naturism more hassle than it's worth? I have no hangups about nudity
and I am not traumatized by seeing ugly old people naked, but there are very
few places in the continental US where lack of clothes enhances the
experience of living.
