Max More wrote:
> [someone] wrote:
> >transhumanism = CMT + humanism
> >extropy = CMT + libertarianism
>
> No no no no no no no NO!!!!

Sorry, Max, but you don't get off this lightly. It doesn't have to come
across as a slogan or an aphorism, but I'd like a counter-proposal or a
rebuttal rather than a denial. I'm pretty sure you can be clearer if you want to.

I'll admit I was surprised to read that extropianism was this simple, but I
don't have a counter proposal or a rebuttal at hand. I hope you do.

When I run through my quick litany (BEST DO IT SO and later enhancements), I
can easily categorize all of it as either CMT, as defined, or libertarianism.
Except Dynamic Optimism, but I don't think that's enough to count as a rebuttal.
