Nah ... it's not THAT kind. Some of you may find this useful for those
emails that have been forwarded a few times and have lots of ">>>>" in them
and the sentences are chopped up with extra carriage returns which breaks up
a sentence into 2 or 3 lines and makes them hard to read. Usually I
manually clean them to retransmit, or, do a cut and paste and search and
replace in Word, both a time consuming process.

 With Email Stripper, the program removes all the >s plus the extraneous
carriage returns and makes the email as it was originally. The program is
free.

You can download it at:
 http://www.papercut.biz/emailStripper.htm

 It is just a couple hundred k in length and downloads quickly.
