> Here's another quote from the same page of your quote :
>
> "When a woman has an interest for science, it is most often the sign
> that her sexuality is troubled." [my translation]

"The world's biggest fool can say the sun is shining, but that doesn't
make it dark out."
        -- Robert M. Pirsig, "Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance"

Corollary: If a philosopher says that it is daytime and that it is
raining, and it is actually nighttime, this does not prove that it is not
raining.

I close with some other quotes from famous individuals, which I have made
up out of thin air especially for this occasion.

other two sides."

"Two plus two equals four."
        -- Adolf Hitler, in kindergarten

"Child abuse is wrong."
