>
> My favorite toast on a birthday..
>
> Guiness in hand
>
> "Ten thousand years,
> ten thousand worlds,
> an eternity at your back,
> still berift of a
> denouement

I've always like the daringness of Montrose's Toast:
"He fears his fate too much, for his desserts are small, who dares not
put it to the touch, to win or lose it all."

An extropic version might go something like this:
"Se grasps sem future too little, for sem's visions finite end, who
dares not put mind to metal, to die or reach transcend."
