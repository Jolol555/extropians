This list is a bit strange, because it is fullfilling
various purposes. For example, Extropians talk with each other AND
with newcomers, so it is both an internal forum and an interface to the
general public and journalists. When it gets tactical (how the ideas
should be conveyed), this is even more tricky, as one would be tempted
to proceed differently when adressing a journalist or someone who
already shares an experience of reflecting about these themes.

But at the same time, it reflects the incredible openness of the
movement and of the people behind it, and it speaks enormously in
favor of them.

Jacques
