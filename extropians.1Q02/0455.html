J. Hughes wrote:

>Given the recent discussions of politics on this list, especially of
>extropianism as libertarian transhumanism, I invite list members to read my
>recent essay on "The Politics of Transhumanism." Thanks to comments from
>Natasha and Max I've cleaned up a couple of factual errors. I would
>appreciate your comments as well as I'm preparing it for submission for
>publication.

I've read this paper through thoroughly, but I am honestly at a loss as to
its purpose. It is written in such a way that any apparent falsities
cannot be proven as being intentional, and any implied meanings can be
argued as being incidental. In other words it is suggesting something on a
certain level without quite saying it. I do hope that anyone who reads
this paper does so objectively, and sees through any possible bias. That
should uncover a lot.
