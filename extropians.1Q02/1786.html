>Chen -- the best discussion of that difficult and ill-formed question is by philosopher Robert Nozick in his book Philosophical Explanations. I recommend it. Nozick doesn't pretend that he has *the* answer. In keeping with the approach of that book, he explores the variety of possible meanings.
