Gus diZerega on his book_Persuasion, Power and Polity: A Theory of
Democratic Self-organization_

Feb. 3 - Feb. 14, 2002

hosted by http://www.hayekcenter.org

Gus diZerega, who teaches political theory at Whitman College in Walla
Walla, Washington, will be hosting a seminar on his recent book
_Persuasion, Power and Polity: A Theory of Democratic Self-organization_
between Sunday, Feb. 3 and Thursday, Feb. 14 on the Hayek-L email list.

An introduction to diZerega's book _Persuasion, Power and Polity: A
Theory of Democratic Self-organization_ is available on the web in HTML
format at:

http://www.dizerega.com/p_f.html

the table of contents for the book is available at:

http://www.dizerega.com/p_f.html

All list members are invited to take part in the conversation as it
develops over the next few days. Please remember to give the subject
header DIZEREGA SEMINAR to your postings. Everyone should feel free to
participate in the discussion.

During the seminar Hayek-L subscribers are asked to restricted all
postings to the diZerega seminar.

REMINDER: Please remember to accompany your posting with your
"signature" -- your full name -- along with affiliation information,
such as your institutional affiliation, its snail mail address, your
email address, web page, etc.
