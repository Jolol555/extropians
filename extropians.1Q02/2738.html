Mike Lorrey wrote (13.2.2002/10:23) :
> Yes, it sounds like a rather sick solitary hobby many lonely individuals
> ingage in, but it is actually a real game many on the internet play
> avidly.
>
> Googlewhacking is the sport of coming up with two words which only
> result in ONE page being found by Google. I'm sure the bright minds here
> should have no trouble with such a challenge...

Now a quasi-algorithmical solution. First type

  "rare words"

in Google, and push "I'm feeling lucky". Click a random letter (Q),
and pick a word on the list ("qualtagh"). Feed it back to Google and
feel lucky again. Now take a quick look around the page and find a
rare and unrelated word. I found "perambulating". Back to Google with

  qualtagh perambulating

Bingo. :-)
