> In response to physicist Stephen Hawking's prediction that the human
> race will not survive the next 1,000 years,

In his new book "The Universe in a Nutshell" Hawking actually expresses
transhumanist core ideas:

co-evolution of human and artificial intelligence, refinement of emotional
and rational intelligence by genetic engineering, artificial wombs to grow
embryos with a larger brain, expansion of mankind into outer space.
