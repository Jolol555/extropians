Simon McClenahan wrote:
>
> Friendliness and Seediness I see as different applications of AI, each with
> its own purpose. These applications are programmed on top of a General AI,
> which is the theoretical base of all AI.

Seed AI = General AI + self-improving AI + Friendly AI. I'd guess that, in
terms of effort, building a seed AI is likely to be 80% building general
intelligence, 10% making it self-improving intelligence, and 10% making it
Friendly.
