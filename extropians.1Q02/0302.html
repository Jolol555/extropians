http://www.space.com/scienceastronomy/solarsystem/mars_flapper_011205-1.html
This beast of burden may carry out flight duties flapping smartly through
the thin, carbon dioxide-laden atmosphere of Mars. Billed as a revolutionary
new class of refuelable robotic flying machine, aerospace engineers envision
a fleet of the patented mechanical insects fluttering over Mars' surface,
each toting scientific sensors from spot to spot.
