Samantha, Amara,

Samantha Atkins wrote:
... I will gladly donate generously to such a program myself once I am no
longer supporting my own 2 dependents.

Amara Graps wrote:
...Great. As someone who put myself through BS/MS/PhD Physics, I would
have been happy to have this kind of support. :-) I would donate to
such a program too. Maybe we can change things for the next
generation.

This is an *excellent* idea. Indeed, over the past four years I have paid
for a young woman's college education, someone not related to me or even
known to me personally (at first). She was recommended by a trusted friend
as having intelligence and willpower, but no money... so I supplied some.
She is now graduated and (hopefully) on her way out of poverty. I encourage
other to do the same: Help people to help themselves.

Talking about and planning the future is very important, but so is helping
people in the *present*.
