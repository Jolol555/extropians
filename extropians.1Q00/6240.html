Eliezer wrote:
>and *I* never got a "threshold of the adventure" scene.

For you the path seemed clear from the time you first read about the
singularity and realized how important it would be to you. But didn't you
hear any exciting music playing in the background at the time? With all
this advanced tech coming we should all get to have our own background
music.

Lee Daniel Crocker wrote:
After deliberation, he decides to seal his fate. He presses the <Enter> key
and watches the moving bar on his display track the progress of the upload.
The adventure begins...
(end)

Eliezer could wind up in a T2 scenario where a hostile AI sends through time
killing machines of various sorts to finish him off before he can accomplish
his work and save our timeline. Be very careful Eliezer! Of course, the
freedom fighters of the future who are known today as the Foresight
Institute, would send forth a sophisticated fighting machine to protect him.
  It would take on a close likeness to Buffy in appearance, so we would
think he just had a really cute girlfriend that always tagged around with
him and have no idea she/it could singlehandedly fight off the 82nd
Airborne.
