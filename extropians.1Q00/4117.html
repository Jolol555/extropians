Communications experts!

This week, I am taping an appearance on the Ricki Lake show. This will be
my first talk show experience.

I am a young person who "gets naked on the Internet," and that is the
topic of the show. I expect to face crusaders for moral puritanism. My
goals on the show are to 1) advance dynamic optimism and boundless
expansion through style and content, 2) promote my site by building
interest in those Ricki viewers with 'net connections.

I hear that daytime talk shows are hostile environments for each guest,
that they're very conflict-oriented. I'm wondering about the best ways to
diffuse that, to maintain rapport with everyone in a large, mostly hostile
(assume the worst!) group situation.

Observations?

Advise me!
