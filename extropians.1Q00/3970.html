of each
of the front lower teeth. The upper teeth got posted and set with gold
body/ceramic faced crowns, and have been fine ever since (18 years). The two
lowers got cement castings bonded to the remaining teeth surfaces. One of these
popped out eleven years ago in an Air Force dentists office, which was replaced
with no problems since (it was the smaller one), while the other one is
original
and has not worn away.
