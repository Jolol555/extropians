Richard Feynman's very funny book "Surely You're Joking Mr. Feynman" is
available on audio tape, so is the biography of him called "Genius" by Gleick.
I think every Extropian should read (or listen to) these two books, they're great!
With books like these I don't even mind when I get into a traffic jam.
