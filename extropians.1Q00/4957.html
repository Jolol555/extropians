Regarding the current gun debate and all previous incarnations, why bother?
Aside from venting -- which, in itself is counterproductive; people who vent
only vent again; they don't move to some new equilibrium -- do any of you
really think you're going to persuade someone on this list? If not, then
parading arguments, statistics, slogans, anecdotes, and the like is just a
waste -- whichever position you take.

stuff on this list is going to my killfile.)
