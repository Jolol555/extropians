Arginine and ornithine are also viral growth stimulants. While
taking ornithine, I got chicken pox for the second time, which is
very unusual.

As to testosterone precursors, steroid metabolism is very complex.
Generally testosterone is thought to harm health. Eunuchs seem
to live about 2 years longer than comparable uncastrated males
(No, this is *not* a recommendation! :-)
