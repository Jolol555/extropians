"Observe constantly that all things take place by change, and
accustom thyself to consider that the nature of the Universe loves
nothing so much as to change the things which are and to make new
things like them. For everything that exists is in a manner the
seed of that which will be. But thou art thinking only of seeds
which are cast into the earth or into a womb: but this is a very
vulgar notion."

book 4 Meditations by Marcus Aurelius
