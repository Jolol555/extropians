Spike Jones wrote:

> Its this, friends: most all of us here
> must have some mental architecture that is wired seriously
> different, yet we have all become quite skillful in compensating,
> in *acting like* normal humans, in order to get along in this
> century on this planet where we are trapped, this world filled
> with huuuumans. spike

You rather make us sound like a bunch of inadequate personalities
suffering from a benign form of autism. I don't think it's quite as
bad as that. Most of us would probably score relatively high in
introverted and intuitive thinking on the Briggs-Meyers. You will
recall from Jung that introverted and extroverted personalities
have a TERRIBLE time understanding each other, not to mention
the communication difficulties of feeling and thinking types.
