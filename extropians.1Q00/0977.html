Hello all,

There will be a meet and greet chat session on the Transhuman Philadelphia
website tonight at 9PM. If I'm a little late showing up, it will be because
I had to go see a patient<g>. It's nothing too fancy, but it will give those
who haven't had a chance to come to one of our monthy meetings some realtime
conversation with other transhumanists.

The website is at
http://region.philly.com/community/ignore?Mlval=cge&GID=0100801060094752042599
0598, also accessible by typing in
http://region.philly.com/community/TranshumanPhiladelphia.
Some people have been reporting having problems getting there directly. If
so, first go to http://www.philly.com, then click on the Community section.
In the Community section check the Site Directory for most recently updated
pages and that should bring up Transhuman Philadelphia. Can also search
alphabetically or in the Channels section under Technology.

    To participate in the Chat session, you will have to go through a free
registration process, but this is really a formality (also once you register
as a participant on the Transhuman Philadelphia site, you can just go to
www.philly.com and log in, then see the site under My Profile.

    Hope some of you can make it. If not, don't worry, we'll be scheduling
more in the future.
