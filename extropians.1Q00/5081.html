Place: Los Angeles Public Library, 630 W. Fifth St., Los Angeles 90071
Hours: Mar 19 Sun 2:00 pm
-- Susan Sontag has more interests than a bank. The women of letters has
examined everything from Ingmar Bergman to Vietnam to pornography. At the
Public Library, she discusses her latest novel, "In America." Michael
Silverblatt, host of KCRW's "Bookworm," moderates. The event is part of the
Library's "Words in the World" literary series. At Dutton's, she reads from
"In America." -- LA Times, March 16, 2000
Natasha Vita-More
