At UAA there are alot of Russian students who are all studying business to
go back home and become hopefully rich and powerful while having developed a
tender spot in their heart for the good ole U.S. of A. while having been
here(I don't know about that). Alot of them come from families that were
part of the old Communist elite power structure and some have very haughty
attitudes to be honest. But others are from very humble backgrounds and got
their based on brains and hard work only(and maybe some connections, lol).
But I have made good friends with several and really enjoyed learning about
their homeland.

Very few Russian and American students become couples which initially
surprised me. The Russians pair off among eachother almost always. In a
way I feel much more in common with visiting Asian students! lol

Sometimes I think Russia must have had a secret genetic engineering project
going to produce the world's most beautiful women! I cannot believe all the
gorgeous six foot tall blondes that 'just happen' to be Russian and
attending school here! Of course, I have some fun mock arguments with
Russian guys over whose country has the prettiest women.
