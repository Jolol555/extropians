I just saw the film "Being John Malkovich" and highly recommend it. We've talked about
qualia, identity, and immortality on this list but I was surprised that a Hollywood movie would
use such subjects as the focus for the entire plot; we may have done into a little more detail
but the movie was much, much, funnier. No fancy special effects, just great writing acting and
direction; I think it may be the best movie of the year, it's certainly the funniest.
