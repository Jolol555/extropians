Sorry, I missed your original post. Is it *pure* energy that you desire to
transform your mind to? I'm no physicist, but it does strike me that it
would be pretty hard to maintain the coherence of your mental patterns in
the form of pure energy. What little I know of quantum mechanics and the
laws of thermodynamics leads me to believe that you'd be in an uphill battle
against the encroachment of entropy.

But if you don't mind a non-obtrusive physical storage medium, I think
you'll be in luck.
