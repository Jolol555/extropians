I just checked out our team stats, at:
http://setiathome.ssl.berkeley.edu/stats/team/team_393.html

and I noticed some things. There seems to be a discontinuity in our
member rankings/units processed, jumps from 69 up to 104 units
processed, a jump from 206 up to 433 units, then another jump from
almost 600 units up to over 1000 units. Are the large producers using
clients installed on many machines using the same account (possibly a
classroom full of PCs?), or do they have any supercomputers running? I
have noticed that since I stopped using the client as a screen saver, my
processing time has dropped from 52 hours down to the 22-28 hour range
(my average time is dropping slowly as I use this mode more).
