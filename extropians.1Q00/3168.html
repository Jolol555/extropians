Much interesting commentary from Bert Ely about monetary policy is
available as free .pdf downloads at
http://www.ely-co.com/monpolcy.htm

A comprehensive list of Federal Reserve links is at
http://www.ely-co.com/links.htm

These sites are mostly concerned with monetary matters, rather than
investing or economics in general.

George Reisman's views, which differ from Ely's, are presented at
http://www.capitalism.net/, where there is a Nov. 1999 article concerning
the high-flying stock market.

The Motley Fool, http://www.fool.com, is a popular and reasonably
unbiased source of commentary on investing.
