Some of my favorite "last words" that might apply in this situation:

"Oh my God, it's full of stars"
-- Dave Bowman in A.C. Clarke's "2001, A Space Odyssey"

"More light"
-- Goethe's last words

"And now for something completely different."
-- John Cleese, Monty Python's Flying Circus

"Apre moi, le deluge"
-- Napoleon Bonaparte

"Hey ya'll watch this!"
-- Billy Bob, right before he tried that trick with his 1977 Trans Am he saw
in "Smoky and the Bandit"
