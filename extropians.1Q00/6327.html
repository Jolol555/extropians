I've been an on and off member of this list for about three years. I
probably spend an hour a day looking at all the posts, and have on rare
occasion posted myself. I've noticed that regular posters will often
memorialize the death of someone who deeply influenced them, and so in the
same spirit I’d like to honour my dad, Anthony Kenny who died today of heart
attack at the age of only 58. My dad introduced me to Science Fiction and
his favourite author was Richard Dawkins and I had been planning to start
talking to him about Transhumanist ideas this Easter when I saw him again,
and I know he would have been receptive. So I’m just posting this so that at
least his name appears in the archives of Transhumanist material, if nothing
else of him is able to live on.
