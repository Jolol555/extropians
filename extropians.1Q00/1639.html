As usual I am thinking in terms of images:
Architect suggests that Nanotech in architecture will have the effect of
making buildings extremely temporary. Buildings will spring up overnight and
disappear just as quickly. Corporate property as we know it will become
obsolete as businesses are not conducted from "brick and mortar" institutions
any more.
Buildings can anything of course.
One line of thinking is they be constructed out of very thin, strong surfaces
and serve a place for projectionary illusion. A mixture of Virtual space and
real.
