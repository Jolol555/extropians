I'd ask him this:
"Suppose several bioethicists made up a petition/list, to the effect that
they declare certain genetic and biological advances immoral, and that they
refuse treatment, and grant all companies and medical institutions
permission to deny them treatment, if the treatment is based on "immoral
technologies" like genetic engineering and cloning. This is expected to
include treatments for parkinson's, alzheimer's, diabetes, general aging,
and any type of organ transplant. Would you be willing to sign such a
petition?"
