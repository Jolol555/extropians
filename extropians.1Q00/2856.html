> Life Extension isn't just taking vitamins, you have to have mental control
> too. If you sit around and take vitamins even though you're depressed,
it's
> not going to help much. You'll live your life to the same potential if
you
> didn't take the vitamins. In the studies the people probably lead a happy
> life. And I forgot what else I was going to say...

Assuming that the studies are done with random enough samples, I would
expect both happy and unhappy people in both groups studied. (In the first
vitamin C study, there was no control, just two groups. One taking 800
mg/day, the other taking 60 mg/day. To have a bona fide control, we'd need
a group not taking any amount of it.) I.e., the happiness factor -- which
should be studied too -- is probably not playing a big role here.

Don't overplay the role of attitude in life. It accounts for something, but
surely not everything.
