At 11:40 AM 1/01/00 -0800, you wrote:

>Whenever
>I can get away with it, I'm going to be calling 2000
>the "Fake Millenium" and 2001 the "Real Millenium".

>Anyway, happy Y2K!

That would be 2048, of course. An even more astounding blunder. People
should have been saying Y2M.

(In our 1999 novel THE BOOK OF REVELATION, Rory Barnes and I spoke
retrospectively of the Millennium Riots of 2000 and the even worse True
Millennium Riots of 2001. Annoyingly, it seems as if this is *already* a
falsified prediction. I'm still waiting to hear what became of the idiots
perched on Meggido hill awaiting the return of the guy in the sky.)
