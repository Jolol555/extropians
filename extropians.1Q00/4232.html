Andrew Breese wrote:
I hear that daytime talk shows are hostile environments for each guest, that
they're very conflict-oriented. I'm wondering about the best ways to diffuse
that, to maintain rapport with everyone in a large, mostly hostile(assume
the worst!) group situation.
(end)

The problem is that the talk show hosts and producers actually want to
create a certain level of conflict!! Guests on these shows are even
"coached" regarding what they should say and how they should behave!

Of course, you can be in control of yourself and choose how you will react
to others. But then you are an anarchist who will be on a show where
sometimes anarchy reigns! lol It should be a challenge for you.
