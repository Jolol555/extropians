Sasha,

I saw "Black Mask" also and though it was extremely corny even by Hong Kong
action film standards I did enjoy the performance by the lead actress who
was quite humorous. I have read she is considered the Lucille Ball of Hong
Kong!

If you want to see an excellent film with normal but very skilled human
beings see "Iron & Silk." It is based on the real-life experiences of Mark
Salzman who journeyed to Asia to master the martial arts and is based on his
book of the same title. I could see Natasha really enjoying this if she has
not already seen it.
