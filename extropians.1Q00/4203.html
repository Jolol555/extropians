Hello all,

There will be a meeting of Transhuman Philadelphia on Sunday March 12th, 1PM
EST at Barnes and Noble in Jenkintown/Abington on Old York Road (Rte 611).
We will be meeting in the cafe in the back of the bookstore. The Barnes and
Noble is one block North of the Noble train station. From 276, get off at
611 and drive South, in about 12 minutes you will pass Abington Memorial
Hospital. A few lights later you will come to the Barnes and Noble on the
left. At the light before the Barnes and Noble, make a left, then you can
easily get into the parking lot. From Philadelphia, simply take 611 North
until you reach Barnes and Noble on your right. More info will follow.
