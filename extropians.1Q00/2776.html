the very gifted Queenemuse wrote:
With their Buckyballs, Nanotech, (transhuman fare),
They bring fear to the Luddites in Ludditeland Lair.
Living forever, they say isn't fair!!
We must leave things alone, now -- and how do we DARE!!
(end)

This is great stuff! This needs to be fleshed out into a full-size book
with Seussian style artwork to go along with it! When you said you were an
artist I thought you meant a painter/sculpter and not also an author.

Don't drop the ball! This incredibly fun book-in-the-making will be a
classic, not just among transhumanists but future oriented people
everywhere. And it could be the first of a series. I would order several
copies now if it were available. I remember you mentioning how you would
like to have more money, now this is the way to do it!!

Please consider what I've said. You have created a seed that should be
allowed to sprout and not die.
