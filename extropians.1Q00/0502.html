"Five and a Half Utopias" by Steven Weinberg (in _The Atlantic
Monthly_).

    http://www.theatlantic.com/issues/2000/01/001weinberg.htm

Weinberg discusses the future and the Free-market Utopia, the
Best-and-Brightest Utopia, the Religious Utopia, the Green Utopia,
and the Technological Utopia.
