Just wanted to say, for the record, that I have always appreciated
the efforts of ExI and extropians to promote and further
transhumanist ideas in America and worldwide.

Over the years, Extropians that have stepped up to the plate
to volunteer and lend their support for various projects
and initiatives, whether 'extropian' or 'transhumanist'.
In the local groups, interactions between extropians and
other transhumanists are lively, friendly, and invigorating.

On a personal level, I have received so much enthusiasm, help
and support from longstanding Extropians, and can count many
of them as friends. You are special people.

As we enter the 'new' millenium, I hope that productive and
friendly relations between all subsets of transhumanism
grows.....

Sincerely,
Kathryn Aegis
