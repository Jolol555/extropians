guns. So, if cops carry most of the guns, who is being defended?

are domestic.

Since the '80s, gun related crime is down, and mind-altering pharmaceutical
prescriptions are up. The drop in gun related and other violent crime has very
little or nothing to do with these prescriptions, which are seen in sporadic cases
of this kind of violence.

Gun related crime is down because of gang peace.

If drugs were not illegal, there would not be any motive for violence related to
drugs, and no non-violent drugs users would be forced into a violent lifestyle,

non-violent drug users in prison should be mainstreamed back into working society
over six months to a year, if necessary as part of WPA-like Rooseveltian
endeavors.
