out of it.

The DMV employees must have seen "Double Jeopardy", otherwise they

>
> Nextly, transparent world fans and info nudists: I can easily imagine,
> in my own natural lifetime, being able to sit down at a computer, type
> in any name and have some future version of the web go out and
> collect every stupid thing that person ever posted, ever, by searching

I don't know. Some of my posts to Usenet longevity & life-extension
from '91-93 are still floating around. Reading them gets very scarey
because I really am a different person now. Though I still stand
behind most of the assertions I made, it seems like I no
longer have the knowledge on which the assertions were based.

I wonder if in ten years I'll consider being an Extropian a similar
phase in my development?
