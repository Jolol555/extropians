HealthyPeople 2010 may be an appropriate _text_ to use as a platform for
this discussion. The goals are:
1. Increase quality and years of life.
2. Eliminate health disparities.

Leading the Focus Areas are:
1. Nutrition and Overweight
2. Physical Activity and Fitness

The Leading Health Indicators are:
1. Physical Activity
2. Overweight and Obesity
3. Tobacco use
etc.

http://www.health.gov/healthypeople/

There are 467 objectives for the year 2010. Collated statistics for the
completed HealthyPeople 2000 project provides further insight.
