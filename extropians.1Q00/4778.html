I love how the Weekly World News seems to have the most fun stories!
But this one was a classic. I will share it with all my friends.

>Disk capacity is an issue, however. Only a PC built after 1985 has >the
>storage capacity to house an evil spirit, the minister explained.
This line killed me! So I will be safe from harm if I use my precambrian
era Atari 800!

I remember Siskel and Ebert reviewing a film that came out in the early
eighties named, 'Evilspeak.' It had this bullied cadet at a boy's military
school who found the 'Devil's chatroom' and signed an e.contract to have his
enemies killed off in return for his immortal soul. This was done by demons
that resembled pigs which walked on their hindlegs! Siskel and Ebert could
not say enough bad things about this film. It must have been a stinker!

I have heard alot of stories of demonic influence and possession but never
anything about computers. Perhaps 'old scratch' has so many human
'employees' pervading bad things on the net that he has no need for the
direct approach!
