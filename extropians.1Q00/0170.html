On the run this morning, so only a quick note. In my perception, all
transhumanists are NOT extropians and, if the core transhumanist agenda is to
succeed, it will have to be made to work in a number of ideological and
philosophical systems. Thus, non-extropian transhumanists who work to
broaden the appeal of our ideas without diluting the basic goals and values
are doing Good Stuff, IMO.
