I'd also like to strongly recommend this movie. Never have I seen such a
transhuman movie with so little technology (a calculator was all I
noticed) and such high accessibility, especially to artsy technophobes --
a difficult bunch when you are trying to convey the
mind-expanding concepts in this movie: biological immortality, identity
markets, uploading(sort of, maybe lateral-loading or sideloading ;-)

All this, plus it's funny, well-acted, and surreal. Go see it, you can
bring just about anyone to this movie (maybe not prepubescents)

Geoff Smith
