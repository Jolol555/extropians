-- early Extropian Max T. O'Connor wrote an article DEEP

which was published in Extropy #5, Winter/1990 -- see
<http://www.buildfreedom.com/tl/tl07d.shtml>.

realize that the so-called "USA political system" is a hoax;
should these people keep quiet about the hoax, or should
they attempt to expose it?

3. If some of these 20 people decide to attempt to expose
the hoax, how should they go about it?
