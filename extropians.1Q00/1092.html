Hi, this is Mizuho.

It's been a while since I posted my message last time, but I hope you
all have been doing well.

Today, I would like to make a very important announcement.

Metamorphosis Society, which has suspended its activities for several
reasons, is going to resume its activities before the turn of the
century.

We would be happy if you can take time to read our entirely revised
web site. URL is http://www.kanon.to/ms.htm

The turn of the century is just around the corner. We would like to
take this rare opportunity to actively attract more members.
To do so, we are going to launch a special millennium project -- a
two-year active advertisement campaign to raise the number of members
to at least 100 in total.

Why consider that at least 100 members are necessary to maintain our
group for a long time toward the future.

Our group aims at "creating and actually going to the future."
If we actively exchange information among 100 members, we will be able
to do various kinds of things for the betterment of our future.

Moreover, 100 members may form a vital foundation for making our group
last forever.
With this foundation, there will be more chances for our group to
survive until we reanimate somewhere in the future.
This will be a big support for us when we reanimate.

Come and see us on the web site, and join us!
