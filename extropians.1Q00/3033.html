But seriously, on this issue, the first issue of _The Journal of Ayn Rand
Studies_ has an article by Larry Sechrest on anarchism. This is not your
typical Objectivist "let's bash anarchism" essay that has surface time and
again in that movement. In fact, it would seem Sechrest is a closet
anarchist of the Randian sort. (See "Rand, Anarchy, and Taxes" in ibid.
1(1). The URL for that journal is at http://www.aynrandstudies.com/ )
