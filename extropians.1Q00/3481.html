> << Consciousness is not some magic thing

> operation. >>

> That's like saying the meal is a result of the stove heating up the pot!
>
> You're forgetting a whole series of chemical reactions - sometimes called
> "the soup'.

That's the material substrate, not the dynamically complex and
interrelated recursive patterning which emerges from it. "Minding"
is what the brain DOES. This position is known as emergent
materialism - consciousness emerges from the brain (upon which it
is dependent for its manifestation) as a synergistic product of its
actions and relations, rather than being identical with it (see Daniel
Dennett).
