Based on the fact my weight loss stalled
after 30 lbs, I tried the "fat fast" recommended
by Dr. Atkins in such cases. The fat fast, far
from abstaining from fat, consists of eating only
fat, 900 calories per day, for 5 days in a row.

Within 2-3 days, my ketones went from "trace"
to large. Over, the 5 day period, I dropped 8 lbs.
Over the next 5 days, I stabilized at a 5 lb loss,
going back to the standard Atkins low carb diet.

Maybe I will try this again in a few weeks.

                James

list on the results of my

program, but with fewer reps. Lifting
max weight and reps improving
only a little since I reported in late
November.

    According to most charts, I
should lose another 20-30 pounds.
I have stalled in weight loss for
about 30 days staying pretty
strictly to the Atkins program.
