Over the last couple of weeks I've had a discussion with a friend off-list about smart drugs and other nutrient supplements and had other occasions to wish for guidance on the best available information on anti-aging substances. Perhaps because the supplement business IS a big and growing business, there is an overwhelming amount of material available on the web, much of it in the nature of advertising and promotion.

Can list member suggest resources that add value to the great mass of information that is available? In particular, I and I think a lot of people are looking for two things: Order and Quality. Are there any sites out there that do a decent job of organizing up-to-date information about supplements and offer quality information about them?
