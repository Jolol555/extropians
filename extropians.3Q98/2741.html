At 08:50 PM 9/28/98 -0700, Spike wrote:

>recently i wondered about setting up a website where people could
>post an answer to the question: "if one book, which, and why?"

There is no one book, there is no one discipline of study or interest centralizing "the" way.

This was the point of my post.

Sets of doers, thinkers crossing the boundaries of disciplines and closed-door specializations, have tremendous influence on our lives and our future.

We might be asking ourselves what makes our extropian transhumanity work. Are we actively watching it unfold? Hypothesizing from a distance or emerging into the flow? Looking for just the right book to read, just the right film to see, just the right shirt to wear, or synthesizing knowledge, picking and choosing carefully, the best information across the information lines.
