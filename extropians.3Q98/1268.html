FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE: Pre-Opening on Friday, July 31st in Los Angeles Anders Sandberg *rocked the house* with his entertaining presentation of Ray Tracing Images described as "The Transhuman Condition." In a personal and humorous flare, Sandberg elaborated with descriptive storytelling about the relationship the images to his view of transhumanism.
