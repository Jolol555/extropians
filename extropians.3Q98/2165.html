David Papineau, of the Philosophy of Science Department, Kings College, London, has reviewed two recently published books on cloning for the New York Times.

Nussbaum, Martha C., ed. _Clones and Clones: Facts and Fantasies About Human Cloning_ W.W. Norton & Co., NY

Wilson, James Q. and Kass, Leon R. _The Ethics of Human Cloning_ The AEI Press.

The New York Times maintains an excellent archive of book reviews on their web site and sometimes they provide the first chapter of a book as well. (http://www.nytimes.com)
