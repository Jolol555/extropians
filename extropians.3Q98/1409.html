Yep. In fact, there is a wild man in the Midwest experimenting with a radically simple Roton-inspired design that (at least at first) is just intended to throw hulls into orbit for use as raw material (and to get him cred).

Small ones, something like a hundred kilograms at most.

And he *is* getting the hang of working with LOX (preparatory to building a LOX/kerosene hi-rev Roton. Kind of looks like an open-combustor jet turbine, with O2 assist for when the air gets thin.

He's also willing to accept a high catastrophic failure rate, at least at first. No payload, no pilot...
