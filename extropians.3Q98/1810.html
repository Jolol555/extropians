Michelle Jones writes:
>how can we filter out the fact that poor health can make one poor? spike

Studies of humans have paid close attention to this possibility, and don't seem to find it to be a big effect. With animal studies, they have directly manipulated the status of primates, and found that lowering status lowers health.
