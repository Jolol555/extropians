I think the actual issue is whether extreme longevity will change the situation. consider te following two scenarios:

  1. each woman has four children at age twenty (average) and dies as age forty, men die at forty.
  2. each woman has a child every twenty years. Men and women are immortal.

I think the population curves are identical.

conclusion: we have an overpopulation problem to solve in either case.
