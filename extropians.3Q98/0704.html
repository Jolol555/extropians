  1. We're not archae, and we understand our hardware more or less thoroughly (or at least are just a few years off that stage). We don't have to retraverse all these GYrs.

To sum it up, if we do it right, I think we have excellent chances to leap beyond our current status quo in a few bounds. Let's.
