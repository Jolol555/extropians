I have revised The Transhumanist Art Statement 2.0 from earlier versions first written in 1981-82.

The fine-tuned version is available at both:

www.transhuman.org
www.extropic-art.com/transart.htm

Transhumanist Art is an arts period of many modes of the arts and creativity. It first came into being in the early 1980's and has stretched and yawned, mutated and spread across the universe ("We are transhumans ..." on its way across the Solar System.) To date, hundreds of creative transhumans have communicated about their art.
