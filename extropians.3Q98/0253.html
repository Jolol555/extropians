Greg Burch GBurch1@aol.com proposed some questions for a FAQ

>1. Isn't transhumanism tampering with nature?
Yes.

>2. Won't transhuman technologies make us inhuman?

Possibly inhuman certainly unhuman.

>3. Isn't death part of the natural order of things?

Yes and nature sucks.

>4. Won't extended lifespans cause overpopulation problems?

Probably not.

>5. Will only the rich and powerful have access to augmentation?

Nobody knows.

I think these answers are honest and I'm comfortable with them but except for #4 I see no way to make them palatable to Mr. Joe Sixpack, I wish I did.
