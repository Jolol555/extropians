>I think it's likely that concealed carry laws will result in higher
>gun crimes for the period it takes to purge out those who pass the

>From the figures I've seen the "unsuited" amount to less than 0.1% of
those issued with carry permits, and most are "unsuited" only because of minor technicalities (e.g. taking a gun into a bar, which is normal behaviour in Israel). People who go through all the hassle of getting legal carry permits are the kind of people who don't break the laws in other ways; and since very few violent crimes are committed by people who don't have a prior record, they wouldn't get permits anyway. Of course the criminals don't care and just carry illegally.
