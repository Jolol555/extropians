It seems to me that brusquely-stated opinions as to the quality of the maintenance of this list, and comments as to the personal characteristics, attitudes, and motivations of originators and maintainers of this list could be expressed a bit more constructively. That may not seem like a big deal to those who live in California where extros/cryosare plentiful, but considering that many of us are cryonicists and may need each other to make it to the future, such contacts could be crucial.
