Martin Jarvis of the British Antarctic Survey has authored a report published in the September issue of the Journal of Geophysics--Atmosphere regarding his team's findings based on tracking the fluctuations of the ionosphere. They think that the outer boundary of the thermosphere, which rises and falls daily, has gone into a long-term retreat of five miles closer to the earth.
