The higher level code spoken of here are called the "homeotic genes" that do code for the body plan of - as far as is known - every organism in the animal kingdom. These genes are, of course, regulatory genes and are still DNA but they do code at a "higher level". I think after comparative genomics are done on other animals this IS what will allow humans to genetically program themselves to transcend their current biological limitations as Transhumanism (and I) desire.
