I have recently revised the Transhumanist Art Statement. Version 2.0 is now available at:

www.transhuman.org
www.extropic-art.com/transart.htm

Transhumanist Art is an arts period including many modes and genre's of creativity and the arts. The statement was written in 1981-82, and updated in 1990. The latest version is more cogent.

Over the years Transhumanist Art has stretched and yawned, mutated and spread across our solar system ("We are transhumans ..." in space). To date, I have communicated with hundreds of transhumans who are expressing their creativity and art.
