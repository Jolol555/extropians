Here's a scenario:

The "Ben Franklins" he invested at 5% yearly interest grew faster than inflation and left him with a good nest egg...  

That paled in comparison to the Bill Gates size fortune that had accrued from a small amount of risk capital he left in wireless communication, biotech, and Hans Moravecs robotic companies.(brokers will fulfill the investment wishes of the cryonicized......will their portfolios be known as frozen assets?)

[$10,000 invested in Qualcomm in Jan-99 was worth $148,000 last week! (for example)]

Of course, you are correct that adjusting would not be easy, but its likely an optimistic wealthy resurrectee would soon inhabit a life that Donald Trump can only dream about.  

And even if he is a pauper, forced to reinvent a new life, that is much preferable to the alternative of not existing at all.  (If nothing else, he could get a book deal)..."Refelections From the Dewar" or "An Old Codger Speaks" or "A Funny Thing Happened on The Way to The Thawing".
