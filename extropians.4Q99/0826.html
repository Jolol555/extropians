It's kind of like saying "We don't have to worry about running out of natural resources, because Jesus will be returning in our lifetimes," or the Golden Bullet of nanotech which will magically make all our technological, social, and economic problems disappear.

Raising the average human IQ through a program of Conscious Evolution is a worthwhile goal, and one that shouldn't be quashed merely because one model predicts doom for humanity's future...
