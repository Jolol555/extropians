The American Association for the Advancement of Science will hold its Annual Meeting and Science Innovation Exposition on February 17 through 22, 1999, in Washington, DC, in the Washington Marriott Wardman Park and the Omni Shoreham.

The theme: 'Science in an Uncertain Millenium'.

The Program Tracks (subject to change)

--Changing Landscapes
--Communicating Science
--Education and Public Understanding of Science
--Environment, Food and Natural Resources
--Global Change/Earth Systems Science
--Human Futures
--Industry and Engineering
--Interconnectivity and Life in Cyberspace
--Life Science and the Science of Life
--Looking Back to Forecast the Future
--Looking Beyond Earth
--Mathematics and Physical Science
--Natural History in the New Millenium
--Public Health and Medicine
--Science and Society
--Science, Engineering and Public Policy
--Science Innovation

More information at http://www.aaas.org/meetings/2000/index.htm

Pre-registration has passed, so regular rates apply. Will post info on exposition hall rates if they provide it.
