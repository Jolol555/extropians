Hello,

I was not sure what to make of David Bowman's websites. I think he is serious but sometimes thought it was a joke of some sort. I liked some of the ideas about academic feudal systems and how scientific establishments don't always like new trains of thought. I found his views on life after death interesting. But when he went on about intelligent reptiles among us using mind control tech to pass for human in the British royal family he lost me. And what is the deal with the Luciferian Deception, the black sun and the year 2012? It looks like a trend may be developing to create odd websites about transhumanism. What do the rest of you think?
