It is reasonable to expect that since standards of beauty change from place to place, and from era to era (except for genetically programmed preferences, like facial symmetry), tastes in pornography would also differ between cultures.

But I was intrigued to read that (some? many? all?) Native American men are disinterested in nude photographs, and don't understand the interest other men have in them. They describe looking at pictures of women as being as pointless as looking at pictures of food when you are hungry.
