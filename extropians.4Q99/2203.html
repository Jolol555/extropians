At explorezone.com, we're running a special 5-day series (starting today) titled

"Space Visions: Exploration in the new millennium."

http://explorezone.com/special/space_visions/space_visions_overview.htm

Voice featured include author Robert Zubrin of the Mars Society, Louis D. Friedman of the Planetary Society, and others who know about the final frontier.

About the series

On the eve of a new era in space, as humans ponder colonizing other worlds, when new planets are being discovered, and while hope is high that life exists elsewhere, we asked five people who know about the final frontier to share their visions for the future of space exploration and discovery.
