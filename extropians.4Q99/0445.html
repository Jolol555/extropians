You smiley, but I beg to differ. Koko and Michael, the mountain gorillas cared for, studied, and educated by Dr. Francine Patterson are avid painters. They can paint pure emotion. They can describe what they have painted. Some of their work clearly resembles what they have described.

Check out http://www.koko.org/koko/gorilla_art/index.html and decide for yourself. Many of their pieces are there.
