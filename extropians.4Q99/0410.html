The notion that the male sex drive is any way different or stronger than the female sex drive has been disproved by not-so-recent psychological and medical testing. Both are treated the same, medically, if there are problems. Viagra is now in final testing for use in women who want to increase their sex drive, although many physicians are already prescribing it to them off-label.

Therefore, the title of this thread should be: 'controlling the human sex drive'.
