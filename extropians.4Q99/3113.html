Stevie Wonder has met with medical staff at Johns Hopkins University, for the purpose of consulting with them about undergoing an experimental procedure to give him sight. The procedure would have involved the implanting of a computer chip into his retina.

Unfortunately, it was determined that the damage to his eyes was too great for the procedure to have benefitted him.
