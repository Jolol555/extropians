Actually, I just realized that we may be talking about two different things-- a press kit and a media reference binder. Didn't cover press kits in the media paper, and maybe you could write some sort of guidance on compiling press kits on various topics. I support the development of many versions of this sort of thing, because different people and groups will have different goals for media coverage.

With regard to a media reference binder, which I did mention in the paper, that is a binder of materials to keep handy for quick reference. Mine, which is in pieces-parts right now, contains various FAQs, sets of statistics that I might need, notes from media trainings, contact info on people I can make references for, a section for five-pointers, listings of past/future publications and activities, listings of past media appearances.
