>From the AMATEUR SCIENTIST column of the latest issue
of "Scientific American" -- "Falling into Chaos".

Some of you might like to actually observe the transition of a system from a predictable (ordered) to a chaotic state. This simple apparatus ingeniously retards the state-transi- tion so it is readily observable. The experiment is so easy to perform that your children (with your supervision) can learn some avant-garde physics, and the colored water droplets descending through mineral oil provide a lovely slow-motion study of the phenomenon.

You can buy the November 1999 SCIENTIFIC AMERICAN at your newsstand (which would be nice), or avoid the cost by following this URL:
