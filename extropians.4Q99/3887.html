Over the next two months, as December closes out and throughout January, many local newspapers will be doing specialinterest stories about the future. Probably, these stories will be found in the 'human interest' or 'style features' sections of the papers.

This would be a wonderful opportunity for transhumanists to get their thoughts on the future of humanity published. Please consider making a phone call to your local style reporter, or sending an email, to find out if they are looking for interesting statements or editorials on the future.

And, have a wonderful and restful holiday season.
