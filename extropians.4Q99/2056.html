I'm pretty sure this page is a joke. Check out this brilliant economic observation from the "Socioeconomic Impact of Transhumanism":

"Some might say that as these technologies are explored more fully, the cost would decrease. While economics typically supports this, the fact that transhumanism relies on theoretical technologies and their continuing refinement makes this untrue. Continual refinement costs a great deal of money, and the fact that the technology does not yet exist doesn't lower the cost."

Wow, that's a doozy.
