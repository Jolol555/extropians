STEVIE WONDER WANTS TO SEE!: Superstar musician Stevie Wonder has announced that he will undergo an operation that could restore his sight. The BBC reports that Wonder told fellow worshippers in a Detroit church that the operation "helps the blind to become
sighted with the help of some sort of chip." Wonder, 49, lost his sight within hours of birth, and hopes to have the operation at the Wilmer Eye Institute at Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore.
