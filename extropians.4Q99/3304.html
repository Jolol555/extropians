I am delighted to see all the recent messages concerning this weekend's Mars Polar Lander mission.

I spent all afternoon/night Friday manning a table at Chicago's Adler Planetarium for the Chicago chapter of the Mars Society. see: www.astrodigital.org/mss/adlermpl.html

The results are about as disappointing as a party can be when the guest of honor fails to show....

My comment to one of the interviewing news crews "I guess we need to send a crew up to check on it" brought knowing smiles.

If your in town, check out the Adler, and don't miss "Journey to the Red Planet."
