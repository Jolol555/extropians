One thing I appreciate about the Mormon view on the fall of Adam is that the fall was a good thing that was planned to be to make Adam and Eve mortal and able to reproduce. Mainstream Christianity states that the fall was a tragic mistake and without it Adam and Eve would have brought forth a perfected race of humanity without sin.

I appreciate belonging to a church that teaches about the premortal existance of humanity, an apostasy and restoration and the doctrine of eternal marriage and family life.
