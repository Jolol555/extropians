Stumper of the Day:
If any finite lifetime isn't enough to fulfill your meaning of life, how are you going to do it in infinite time? At any point in time, you'll only have lived a finite amount of time.

Answer:
If you have a goal whose fulfillment is a scalar quantity rather than a
qualitative yes-or-no, then infinite time is infinitely desirable, but
not necessary to the desirability of the finite case.
