The movie itself I believe is very optimistic and uplifting in the sense
and puts the message across that "WE WILL GO OUT THERE!". Hey Mike, Keep
working on that Lorrey Drive! After seeing Geordi's new eyes on "Star Trek:
First Contact" I am beginning to wonder how long it will be before we can
design and create mechanical eyes to allow the blind to see or for our own
augmentation? Also has anyone ever thought of putting together an
organization that would show up at trekkie conventions and create a fund to
pump into R&D for space travel? I myself am not a dedicated trekkie but I
do watch it from time to time.
