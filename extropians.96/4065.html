I think D.Musick's comments are not without merit, we are supposedly
going to be living in a nanotech future where I am assuming synthesised
animal products will be the norm, therefore for those extropians whom
have grasped the questions of life, I do sometimes wonder why such a
simple concept of not having to kill other species for food has not been
rationalised. To draw the parrallel that eating humans is an extension of
carnivorous behaviour while is a little extreme does not fail to get
omnivores attention.

I continually question societies enviroment.
