Many people in the past have tried to tame the wildly spammed newsgroup
alt.extropians in the past. Now that we realize that the audience is out
there, IT IS TIME TO ACT! *MANY* people are interested in these principles
and we *NEED* some type of public debating forum where we can bring the
extropian meme to the public rather than sit in isolation hiding in our
mailinglist. I will begin to post messages to alt.extropians and I *STRONGLY
URGE* you to do the same. EXTROPIANISM CAN DEBUNK ANY OTHER MEME ON THE
PLANET AND I WISH TO PROVE THAT FACT.

Let the Ribbon Cutting Ceremony to the opening of the *NEW* alt.extropians
begin!
