Eliezer Yudkowsky writes:
>The goal: Make citizen privacy complete and government privacy zero
>except for secret weapons and such.

There's a lot of work to do here to delimit all the "and such".
Governments currently enter into a wide range of international
negotiations, on trade, pollution, foreign policy, etc. They would be
at a disadvantage if everyone knew their negotiating position.
This may be a price worth paying, however.
