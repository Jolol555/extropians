can write to Mr. Knight and give one's reasons why breeding isn't such a bad
thing. So, with all this brouhaha over spreading memes, i took it upon myself
to write to him and give him a brief intro to nano (and the address of Ralph
Merkle's site at Xerox). He wrote back. Here is a excerpt from our exchange
that suggests that there may be some hope for the man:

>
> >More fundamentally, we will be the first species to not depend on the
> >biosphere. Presently we require it to take care of our molecular
> >manufacturing needs (e.g., food). That will soon change, and all of our
> >old ways of thinking will have to change too.<<
