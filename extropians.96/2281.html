My first experience with lucid dreaming came after someone gave me Lucid
Dreaming tape to listen to while falling asleep.

Long after I returned the tape I came across another method that worked
very well. Jumping.

Periodically, when it occurs to you, jump up. Hell, try to fly. At some
point you really will fly and then you'll realize that you are
dreaming. Now, you have an opportunity for lucidity
