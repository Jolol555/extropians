The source of Cyc's power is shallow reasoning on a huge database. The
engine is not especially interesting, the database is. Many man-decades of
top-notch knowledge engineers were necessary to encode daily trivialities.
This is unlikely to be reverse engineered soon, imo. Afaik, a crippled PD
Cyc release is intended. As an intelligent filter, Cyc is priceless.
Hardware demand is relatively low, purportedly it runs on a 16 MByte Mac
laptop.
