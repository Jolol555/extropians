We cannot stand together to face the future if we cannot tolerate our own
petty differences. We make cracks at all sorts of serious subjects, but
isn't that what humor is all about? We mean absolutely no offense and you
shouldn't be offended. One thing we learned when we came to the 'new world'
was tolerance for other people's opinions even if we disagreed with them and
this train of thought is spreading across the globe. If we don't agree with
someone else's opinion, you can make a logical response to why that
individual is incorrect, but do not become emotional and fire back a flame!
This only stirs up an argument. I mean, how could you use the internet if
you were offended by every piece of material deemed wrong by your values?
You couldn't. You can't block it all with SurfWatch-ish programs. You must
learn TOLERANCE.
