My company, Firefly Network Inc., formerly known as Agents, Inc.,

is working in partnership with Yahoo on employing Collaborative

Filtering technology for making personalized recommendations of

Websites. (You can find some additional information on the

partnership at http://www.firefly.net/yahoo_pr.htm).

I will let you know when we launch public betas of our service

and final products, as well as our other partnerships when this

information becomes available.

This will give you an excellent opportunity to improve your

navigation on the Web, and help influence the experiences of

other people.

Meanwhile, I would recommend that you submit your favorite Web

pages to Yahoo, and make sure that URLs for your sites in Yahoo

database are correct. The update process can take you and Yahoo

personnel several weeks or more; if you take action in the near

future, the world will have better chances to access the best

Websites it has!
