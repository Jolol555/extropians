Apart from the suggested items to bring listed in the invitation, Jerome
reminded me to ask party-goers to bring sleeping bags in case you want to
stay overnight. Last time (three years ago) it looked liked Jonestown,
except that everyone got up again in the morning.

Jerome expects the party to go from Noon Saturday to Noon Sunday, and
welcomes you to stay overnight, but you will need to bring something to
sleep in/on (or hanging from, whatever works for you...).

Also, bring CDs of music you think partiers might like. There's the
possibility of a custom-CD collection being made after the party, so if you
have a favorite track that you think should be on the limited-edited
Freeland '96 CD, bring it along.

A reminder to bring food and drink. It's definitely a pot luck.

See you there.
