*** Acceleration utility boosts hard drive performance

A new acceleration utility, introduced by Acceleration Software
International Corp., can boost the hard disk performance by up to 496
percent and reduce application launch times by up to 75 percent.
SuperFassst! is a software-only upgrade to the hard drive. The utility
is comprised of three parts--a hard disk accelerator, an application
accelerator and a multitasking accelerator. The hard disk accelerator
boosts I/O rates up to five times without compression or caching. A
free demonstration copy of SuperFassst! that accelerates the Netscape
web browser can be downloaded from www.accelerationsw.com. For the
full text story, see
