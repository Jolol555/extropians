There seems to be a section of people on the transhuman and
extropian mailing lists who enjoy transhuman and extropian art. In light of
this enjoyment, I wouldn't mind finding out if there would be an interest in
a >H \ Extropian Art mailing list. The list could include written art forms
(such as poetry :), plays, short-stories, etc.) as well as information
regarding pertinent web-pages; in fact, the ML could have *anything* related
to Extro \ >H
Art. I personally do not know exactly how to set up an ML, and therefore, I
would muchly appreciate any suggestions, tips and comments on how I can go
about doing this. Please send messages either on or off list as to whether
there is enough interest for this to become manifest.

Thanks!!

P.S. Help from anyone 'knowledged' in the field of MLs (is there such a
field? :)) would also be muchly appreciated!
