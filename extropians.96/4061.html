helped immensely. You may have been hit by some unusual and/or
unusually long-lasting side effects.

There are newer versions that have fewer side effects. If you
still have trouble with depression or whatever caused you to try
Prozac, you may want to go to an up-to-date doctor and ask him
what he or she would recommend. Two doctors that I've asked look
favorably on Serzone, but once again, your mileage may vary.
