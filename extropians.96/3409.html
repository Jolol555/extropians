I'm not sure, but I think if you ever want to remove yourself from this
mailing list,
you must send mail to "extropians-request@extropy.org" with the following
command in the body of your email message:

unsubscribe extropians-digest [your email ID]

I don't think you can be removed from the list by asking us, the recipients
of the Extropian mailing list, to remove you, i.e. don't try to unsubscribe
by sending mail to the list at:

extropians@extropy.org

However . . .

1) Formal complaints and administrative requests should be sent to:

postmaster@extropy.org
