>Dear reader!
>
>The first issue of Homo Excelsior: The Transhumanist's Magazine will be
>released on January 1, 1997.
>
>Transhumanists want to improve the human body and mind and to solve existing
>problems like environmental damages, world hunger, poverty and new diseases.
>
>Our non-commercial magazine covers ideas of interest to Transhumanists:
>Nanotechnology, Life Extension, Artificial Intelligence, Cryonics, Genetic
>Engineering, Bio- & Neurosciences, and many many more. All these technologies
>promise humankind to approach higher levels, to exist in new ways and to
>boldly go where no one has gone before..
>
>On the other hand, each of these has dangers connected with it, and we will
>try to show these dangers.
>
>But we will also examine the social and psychological implications of
>technology, new and old political concepts, power structures and belief
>systems. Homo Excelsior does not employ any ideology but tries to view both
>sides of the story instead.
>
>By the way, any help for making our magazine better is greatly appreciated.
>See our FAQ to find out how you can support Homo Excelsior by contributing
>with mind, money, mouth or more...
