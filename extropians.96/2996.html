For those who haven't read it yet: I highly recommend The Truth Machine by
James Halperin. He's an extropian and has written a fascinating book about
the development and effects of a machine that can tell with certainty
whether someone is telling the truth.

You can order it via the web from amazon.com

He has another, highly extropian novel in the works, which I won't say
anything about, but you should look out for it. It may become a classic in
extropian circle...
