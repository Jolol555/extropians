Are people biologically addicted to entropy? People seem to enjoy entropy in
the form of entertainment whether it be a party where you're required to
clean up afterwards, or media stories and movies about disaster. The Laws of
Thermodynamics require entropy physically but we can slow the entire process
down by uploading and thus creating less physical entropy. Think of it, is
evolution merely structured entropy?
