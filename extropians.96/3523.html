sentience@pobox.com (Eliezer Yudkowsky) writes:
>---Quick Intro To The Singularity---
>
>1) Computers double in power every eighteen months.
>2) Computers double in power every eighteen *subjective* months.
>3) Three years after computers match human researchers, computing power
>goes to infinity.

Infinity is a really big number. Claiming without evidence that
a physical process will reach that before Tipler's Omega point is
a sure way to convince smart people that you are sloppy or a crackpot.
