This discussion brings up some family ghosts, and I find it hard to
ignore, despite my desire not to sidetrack the list, so one quick note on
evidence and I promise to drop the subject:

Film evidence does exist of the use of the gas chambers and other
Nazi experiments--Hitler demanded visual and written record that his
orders were being carried out. These films are, of course, too
explicit to have been released to the public, but they are used in
military classes on atrocities and by researchers.
