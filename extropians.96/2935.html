Following up on Kennita's tips for a fun sunrise, allow me to suggest this
exercise: Tilt your head toward the North (rotational) pole and try to
picture the Earth's rotation about the Sun. Can you see the moon? Throw it
in, too.

If that hasn't got your head spinning, work on solar system's trip through
the Milky Way (best done at night, natch).
