On 18/12/96, Nadia wrote:

>I then sort of "bliss out" on the task at hand, and it becomes not only
>effortless, but a euphoric kind of high. Also turning on rythmic music,
>burning sage, bodywork, exercise and dance often "loosen up" my ego enough
>that I can "get out of the way" of my own creativity.

That is a very good way of putting it. It is such an amazing
feeling when one attains that 'euphoric high'. Indeed, for me, last night
was one of my most artistic nights in a long time. I believe I wrote one of
my best pieces of poetry; my mood for the rest of the night was joyous (to
say the least!)

Ingredi Externus!
