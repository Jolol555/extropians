It's possible that some Extropian ideas may appear in a Time magazine story
on religions and the Internet in a couple of weeks. I spoke to a reporter on
the subject on Monday. He started (to my relief!) by saying that he
understood that Extropianism is not a religion, but is a philosophical
movement. He's interested in our take on the effects of the Internet on
religious and philosophical belief.

If we're lucky, not only we will make it into the story, but perhaps the Web
site URL will appear.
