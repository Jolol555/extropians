Reilly Jones, please! Sarah never said that two *contradictory* events ever
happen at the same time, in the same place. All she said is that many things
happen at the same time. For example, I am living here and going about my
daily activities AT THE SAME TIME as billions of other people on earth are
living in their various places and going about their various daily activities.
All the cells in my body are all active and doing various things AT THE SAME
TIME, not one after the other. I don't see what's so damn hard about
understanding this. And I don't see how anyone could misinterpret what Sarah
said, unless they did it intentionally, or were in such a hurry to disagree
that they didn't read what she said carefully.
