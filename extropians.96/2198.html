I doubt if many here would dispute that Capitalism is the closest we
get at present to a politico-economic system that supports individual
freedom. It's unfortunate that its name contains the word "Capital"
though, as that is going to make it appear very dated quite soon if
things go as predicted. We need a word that expresses the ideas of
free trade and individual responsibility without tying it to what will
become merely a funny historical concept before long.
