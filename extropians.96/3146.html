Don't miss this week's issue of Time magazine. While Newsweek's cover
feature tells us of the proper way to die, Time's cover feature is on life
extension. A pretty good review of the state of research written in a fairly
optimistic and favorable way. They even have a sidebar on possibilities like
nanotechnology, telomere research, and cryonics (with an Alcor picture of
Hugh Hixon guiding a pod into a dewar).

Well worth buying.
