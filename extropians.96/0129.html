I was sitting in traffic the other day, my mind wandering because my body
couldn't, when an image popped into my head which I thought I'd share:

Picture a t-shirt. On the front is a picture of Max More (which was
tough to imagine, since I have never met him and have only seen the
picture on his Web site...). Beneath the picture are the words "I have a
meme..." On the back of the shirt is the Extropian symbol with 8 arrows
and the acronym BEST DO IT SO.

I suppose that the picture need not be of Max, and that the symbol need
not be an Extropian one, but I think that we could get some mileage and
inspire some questions from curious bystanders with the slogan.

Enjoy.
