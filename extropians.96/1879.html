>Only about 6 or 7 percent of the people on this planet have what you or I
>would consider an acceptable standard of living, at most one in a thousand

this planet have an acceptable standard of living. About one in a hundred of
them have a lifestyle we would envy. Most people do live in poverty, but less

power of the human mind to develop new technologies which do more with less.
