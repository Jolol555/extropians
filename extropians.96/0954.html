Extropy Institute (in conjunction with the Institute for Optimal Living) are
holding two seminars during September:

Saturday, September 21st, 10am-1pm

"How Ethics can Improve Your Life" by Peter Voss

This is an introduction to a radical new approach to optimizing your life:
Personal Rational Ethics. Subjects covered include: What is ethics and how
can we know
right from wrong? Why bother with ethics? How living by principles can help
us both define and achieve desirable goals. Rational virtues and how to
re-program yourself.
WARNING: Some common 'moral' beliefs that may be hazardous to your health.
Practical examples and Q&A.

Venue: The New Venice Library, 501 S.Venice Blvd, Venice
For further details call Peter Voss at (310) 822-4533 or email
p.voss@ix.netcom.com

Saturday, September 28th, 10am-6pm

"Dynamic Optimism" by Max More, Ph.D.

* Learn how to transform self-defeating thinking into unstoppable action
* Discover principles that promote high-octane thinking, confidence and
motivation
* How to recognize and apply the 12 characteristics of dynamic optimists
* How to empower your thinking with 15 thinking styles and patterns
This is an interactive workshop and includes practical exercises.

For details call Max More at (310) 398-0375 or email maxmore@primenet.com

Hope to see you there!
