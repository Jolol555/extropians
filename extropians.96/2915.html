The trick is to know what's going on and be able to take on multiple points
of view. Sometimes when I watch a sunrise, I switch back and forth. I see
the sun rising, then I see the earth turning towards the sun and the horizon
falling away beneath it. It's an interesting exercise; like looking at the
boxes illusion and seeing the boxes going into the page, then coming out of
the page. How many times you can switch from one to the other in a minute
may be a measure of some cognitive capacity worth measuring, but I don't
know.
