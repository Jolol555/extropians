I have visited a ton of Transhuman/Extropian websites by now. I think that
there's one thing they all have in common. Great content. Poor
presentation. This hurt my aestehic sense, and probably make for
inefficient comunication.

Is anybody interrested in me doing a design guide for Transhuman/extropian
web pages? I don't want to tell anybody what to put on their pages, nor do
i wish to tell anybody how their pages must have to look to be correctly
presented the "extropian way".

I would offer it for anyone who haven't got idea 1 about how to do a
layout. Some simple rules of thumb. It might help to make the message
stronger if most of the sites have a similar look and feel.

Of course it will cost nothing. :)
