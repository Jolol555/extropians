annoyed at finding themselves back on the list:

We are extropians, so you must do it yourself. Here's how:

If you ever want to remove yourself from this mailing list,
you can send mail to "extropians-request@extropy.org" with the
following command in the body of your email message:

unsubscribe extropians yourname@gaper.net
