On 4\11\96, Suresh Naidu wrote:

>Businesses can thrive quite well even if they don't serve the consumers.
>they can subsist solely on the desires of the wealthy.

Who's to say the wealth aren't consumers? There is so much
discrimination towards the rich and I have never quite understood why.
Generally, the rich *earn* the money that they have. The 'Ten Pennies In A
Paradigm' thing. Generally, the poor *don't* earn their money. Generally,
they rely on either governmental subsidiaries or quarters flicked by the
occasional passerby. This is a simple concept that many people aren't able
to willingly accept.

P.S. I *do* mean 'generally'
