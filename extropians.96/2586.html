Very good. This is close to "We are all self employed". The only difference
is that being self employed means being responsible to yourself AND to your
"customer".

I think WaaSE is *better* because IARFM-Own-L could mean a win-lose "kill the
other guy" situation. WaaSE means only Win-Win. Win-lose is undesirable. I
still like WaaSE: responsibility for BOTH buyer and seller e.g. even trade.

Giving a gift to someone implies an even trade in exchange, not altruism and
not theft. Even giving a gift of a kidney the "seller" (giver) gets a
feeling of worth (saving a person's life) as "payment" from the "buyer". I
think this is the PROFIT in helping people. Not true altruism.
