P R E S S R E L E A S E

"THE AUTOMORPHER"
by
Natasha Vita More

A one-hour multi-media essay performance to be held at Electronic Cafe
International interactive performace space.

DATE: October 5, 1996

LOCATION: 1649 18th Street, Santa Monica California.

TIME: 7:30 p.m.

Linkups: http://flexeon@primenet.com/~flexeon
http://www.ecafe.com
http://infozone.telluride.co.us/InfoZone.html

There will be a live performance at http://www.ecafe.com and an interactive
question and answer at http://flexeon@primenet.com/~flexeon. A cuSeeMe will
be provided by The InfoZone in Telluride, Colorado.

Guest appearances Leigh Christian, Max More, Pam Lifton-Zoline, John
Lifton-Zoline. Collaborators include Ebon Fisher, Sarah Marr, Steve Arbuss,
Nadia and other transhumans on the Extropian list and the Transhuman list.
