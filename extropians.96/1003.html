On 17\09\96, Chris Hind forwarded a document:

>[inflated document containing the timetable for an open-source military
>forum. Many topics touched upon the abolishment of telecommunications, i.e.:
>internet.]

Seeing as how the whole thing is totally open-source and uncensored
from the public, I wouldn't worry about the abolishment of
telecommunications coming to pass anytime soon. Also, I think that the
American (indeed, the world) public has grown to depend upon
telecommunications. I have no doubt in my mind that this could not feasably
happen. All this lengthy article has to say is that a bunch of people got
up and griped about how technology is truly plaguing our society. Chances
are, they went on to state how man is just one of thousands of species in
the world and that we have no 'right' to be greater. In effect, I wouldn't
worry. (Was there really any need for this response? :-))
