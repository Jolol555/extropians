> Could somebody explain the title to me?

Looks to me like a piece of creative imagery. If I had to give the
flavor, it would be: "There is a great Power born from the depths of
time, A Fire Upon The Deep, arising from a hidden and unknown realm in
power and majesty and rebirth."

(A belatedly added note: I do not necessarily approve of this
sentiment, but it does happen to be the emotion I think Vinge was trying
to convey.)
