Last night I went to a multi-media, live performance exhibition at EZTV in
Hollywood. It was a costume party featuring Burning Man [Wired magazine
features article by Bruce Sterling] photographs and video clips, live
musicians, dancers, and video artists including Fiorella Terenzi and myself.

Burning Man photos were stunning. The sculpture itself looked dazzling and
radiant. Just the enormity of the sculpture is intense. It was difficult to
see the video clips clearly due to the live band in front of the screen. I
don't know if anyone on this list went to the event (ritual, rave ?), but if
you did, I'd like to hear about it.
