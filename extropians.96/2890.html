In the tradition of Future Shock and Silent Spring -
Beyond Humanity: CyberEvolution and Future Minds
Sample chapters: http://www.charlesriver.com
ROCKLAND, Mass., Nov. 12 /PRNewswire/ -- Two best selling authors
from
very different backgrounds, Gregory Paul and Earl Cox, team up to present the
scientific, sociological, and theological issues that we will face when
brains
are down loaded into receptacles and machines become better than humans. The
resulting book, Beyond Humanity: CyberEvolution and Future Minds, is
published
by Charles River Media, Inc. (sample chapters: http://www.charlesriver.com,
price: $20.95):
* Explains how the greatest revolution in Earth's history -- the
replacement of human intelligence by cyber-intelligence -- will probably
occur
within the lifespan of many who are alive today;
* Combines the knowledge of an evolutionary biologist and computer
scientist to project the evolution of robotics and its effect on society;
* Describes the ultimate Internet, the direct co-mingling of human
consciousness in electronic computer systems.
* Presents a realistic, often irreverent, look at the future of
religion
in the 21st century.
"This book argues that the next century will prove to be nothing
like this
one, nor any forecast so far. Computing power, neuroscience, and
nanotechnologies are advancing so rapidly that they will combine to produce
the most significant evolutionary developments since the origin of life
itself.
"We maintain that the human mind and conscious thought are
exclusively
natural and physical in origin and nature. Ultimately, their natures and
fundamental processes are knowable and can be replicated for the purposes of
personal immortality." -- from Chapter 1: Down the Yellow Brick Road
The Authors
Gregory Paul, an evolutionary biologist, is both an artist of prehistoric
life whose work was used in the movie Jurassic Park as well as an
accomplished
writer for such magazines as Scientific American, Science News, Nature and
The
Smithsonian. His previous book, Predatory Dinosaurs of the World, is a best-
seller and Book-of-the-Month Club selection.
Earl Cox is an international authority on the applications of
artificial
intelligence. He is founder and president of The Metus Systems Group and
regularly contributes to many AI periodicals and consults for many Fortune
500
companies including AmEx, Motorola and the FAA.
Pre-Publication Comments

have captured the very essence of now-based potentials... I shall refer to
it
many times." Lee Kent Hempfling, Chairman/CEO Neutronics Technologies
Corporation.
