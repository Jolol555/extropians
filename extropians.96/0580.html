Here's a few options I've thought of (in my order of usability):

PIF: Progressive Individual Freedom -denotes one major facet of
transhumanism and extropianism...ordained progress.

OIF: Objective Individual Freedom -gives a semblence of direction
as opposed to a mere statement.

UIF: Ungoverned Individual Freedom -modifies the term 'freedom'
though it may be slightly redundant.

TIF: Transcendant Individual Freedom -similar to PIF, but more
indulgent, public wouldn't like it.

Just a few thoughts on this issue.
