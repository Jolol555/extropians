On Thu, 15 Aug 1996 Chris Hind wrote:

> Could rational logical thought be considered a Memetic Immune System for
> destructive memes such as the great religions of the world. If so then I
> would have a STRONG meme immune system being forced as a kid to go to church
> against my will, having a mother as a church secretary who also ran a bible
> study as well as a highly christian extended family and being able to debunk

True. But the analogy of an immune system is misleading, it implies
that logical thought is somehow above other memes and acts as a
controlling system. Logic itself is a meme, and it opperates on the
same level as religious memes. Different maps for different chaps,
different semantic funnels for different reality tunnels.
