An isolation tank is a large self-contained box about 8 x 4 ft. Inside is about 10-12 inches of water kept at a steady 93.5 degrees F. and about 800 lbs. of Epsom salt - so you float weightless. One you are inside the tank, you are completely isolated from all environmental light and sound. Imagine feeling totally weightless and relaxed beyond anything you've preciously experienced along with incredible peace of mind because there is nothing to distract you. I highly recommend you try it! :-)

See http://www.samadhitank.com/
