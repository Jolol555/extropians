None of the people who post purportedly 'kooky' posts consider their own posts kooky, only those of others. The same goes for the authors of purportedly 'rational and reasonable' posts. Since Jahweh is not accesible on an ISDN line to render celestial judgments, these things must be decided for him/herself by the post-receiving individuals, in each individual case. To do otherwise is to deny them the right to make their own individual judgments on these issues, and to usurp their intellectual freedoms and arrogate them to others who may be less qualified to render veridical verdicts on these multifarious matters.
