noisesense: Words which come to mean so many things they no longer have sufficient precision to facilitate communication. ie (cleave : 1) to cut apart, separate, put asunder 2) To join, to profoundly connect, to glue.

prostruction: method for initiating increasing complexity in systems thru at least three components 1)Instructions 2) Minimal bootstrap mechanism 3) Bootstrap energy 4) location facilitating initiation of instructions (ie: a germinating seed)

provolution: teleologically engineered interventions using prostructive principles (ie genetic code insertions to allow underwater breathing)

Historical biology: The study of the history of biological systems
