ANNOUNCING A NEW BOOK SERIES
Central Problems of Philosophy
Series Editor: Dr John Shand

To be published by ACUMEN
Dear Philosophy Colleagues,

I am looking to commission authors for a new major series of books CENTRAL PROBLEMS OF PHILOSOPHY, of which I am the Series Editor, to be published by the new London based press ACUMEN PUBLISHING. I am an Associate Lecturer in Philosophy at the Open University in Manchester, England. This is the third set of books of which I am Series Editor. The series is aimed primarily at upper-level undergraduate/postgraduate students and, therefore, expects a reasonable degree of familiarity with philosophical argument from its readership. The books will be around 160pp or 55,000 words, and will aim to convey to the student a clear and rigorous understanding of persistent philosophical problems. Although introductions, the books are opportunities for authors to make a striking and fresh contribution to the subject, in the way it is tackled and the conclusions reached.
I am therefore making an open request to professional philosophers who think they may be able and willing to make a contribution to the CENTRAL PROBLEMS OF PHILOSOPHY series to contact me. Please send this email on to any other appropriate philosophy lists. We are looking to commission authors for the following titles (those marked * have already been filled)
