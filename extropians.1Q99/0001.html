Well, the poor get richer too.

Example:

Joe Sixpack has $1000. A year later he has $1100.

Scrooge McDuck has $1000000. A year later he has $1100000.

Joe is richer by $100, Scrooge by $100000.

Of course, basic expenses are proportionally higher for Joe, he may be only $50 richer.
