This body, Primo 3M+, is a digital ad for life extension and uses an art/science mix of nanotechnology and artificial intelligence in the designing of an ageless body. It also reflects my continuous drive to bring art and science together to challenge human limits.

This ageless body is durable, flexible and heat resistant with a life span far exceeding our 122 maximum years. It is available in several models (xx, xy, xyy, for example) and equipped with add-ons. It is affordable, upgradable and comes with a safety neuro non-erase encryption and neuro cryonics warranty.
