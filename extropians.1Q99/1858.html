Well, I'm back from Capricon XIX, and it's now definite: Sleep deprivation produces transhumanist music. This is the second time in a row I've written a song during a con.

This is to the tune of "Santa's Gonna Kick Your Ass" from "Christmas Turkey" by "The Arrogant Worms". (Part of it, anyway - in mine, each chorus sounds like the first one.) It is no coincidence that this song is rattled off in one of the fastest voices I've ever heard. If you don't know the tune, you're missing half the fun. Sadly, I haven't found any MIDI or MP3 of it... not that I'm suggesting anyone should violate copyrights.

The original lyrics may be found at:
http://www.fortunecity.com/tinpan/harrison/375/xtlyrics.html
