All this reinforces my earlier notion that there are *plenty* of mechanisms to explain the rise of god memes. I will toss in yet another: The god meme supplies a nifty shortcut for instilling the moral values in its members that any stable society needs. With kids, it is much more difficult to explain why they need to wash before meals than to just issue the command, then shriek "Because IM the MOM, THATS why now DO IT!"

To explain a rather complex prisoner's dilemma situation where the societal members must all cooperate, it provides a shortcut to just say "god said so, now shut up and DO IT!" spike
