The best news source, in my opinion, is DJInteractive http://www/djinteractive.com/ You can set up agents to sort through thousands of newsbits, wires, articles and such daily for specific terms and subjects. Additionally, the service has a searchable archive of over 6,000 journals, magazines, newspapers, newswires, and other publications. The cost might prove prohibitive to many but I have found it invaluable as a research tool.
