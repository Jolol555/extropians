My only real sticking point as I keep repeating is I think it would be nice to somehow make the list more "appealing" to newbies. Of course the people who've been around a while can setup their software to filter out messages/people if they want, but newbies should not have to be subjected to a "raw feed". You can argue that we could put the info regarding how to setup your email client to filter onto the subscribe info page at extropy.org, but a lot of people hear about the list second-hand and wouldn't know that coming in.
