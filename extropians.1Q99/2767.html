Thought it might be helpful in our discussion of the Alternatives to imprisonment if I listed some of the goals that I could think of as having been advanced as important in penalties for crime.

Goals of Punishment
- Justice/Fairness

  • Reparation
  • Revenge
  • Rehabilitation/Redemption
  • Inducing Regret
  • Preventing Future crime by Offender
  • Deterrent to Others

If anyone can think of others, please let me know.
