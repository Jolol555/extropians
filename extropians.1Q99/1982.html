They are actually suggesting incarcerating people indefinitely, without trial, for having a personality disorder which may, or may not, cause the person to be a danger to society.

Two questions:
1. Define 'personality disorder'
2. Define 'danger to society'

This is a gross breach of human rights, and seems another step on the road to turning Britain into a police state. The recent Criminal Justice Act gave the police the right to arrest people for just about anything (Illegal Assembly: an unauthorised gathering of persons numbering more than 1)
