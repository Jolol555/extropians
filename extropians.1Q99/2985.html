Hugo Gernsback's
Forecast Science Fiction E-zine</A>

http://www.twd.net/ird/forecast/index.html

This is a collection of some of Hugo Gernsback's annual forecasting efforts from the early 1950s to the mid-1960s. His essays make fascinating reading, both for what he got right (nearly everything about video and media) to what he got wrong (nuclear power, for instance).
