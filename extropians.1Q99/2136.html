> This faction is composed at least of Billy Brown in addition to myself.
> I'm not sure, but I think this might also be Hans Moravec's position.

Actually, in his latest book "Robot", Moravec explicitly proposes that laws require that robots be built "securely nice in the first place". "Every neuance of their motivation is a design choice. They can be constructed to enjoy the role of servant to humankind." And it is a "matter of life and death to humans" that they "do not have a right to vote on the laws that govern and tax them." (pp139-40). In the long run, Moravec looks forward to advanced robots superseding humans and taking over the universe, but we both can and should ensure a comfortable retirement for humanity by programming in suitable "internal laws" in our mind children.
