Samael makes a puzzling comment:

>I just believe there are greater goods than property protection
>(life protection, for instance).

Are you saying that your life is not your property?

You can't divorce your life from the resources that make your life possible. It's nonsense to write as if property weren't part and parcel of your existence.

Since property includes life itself, there can be no higher good than protection of property.
