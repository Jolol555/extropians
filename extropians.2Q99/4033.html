Contrary to my usual practice, I am dashing off unedited posts because of the volume of traffic developing on the "gun moratorium". Here's some not particularly well-organized thoughts on the matter:

  • My announcement of a TEMPORARY MORATORIUM on the subject of guns was NOT intended as an act of censorship, but rather as a very temporary means to address the many complaints about list quality that had been building as a result of the volume of posts on the subject and the incivility of some of those posts.
  • I was frankly amazed to see any new "gun posts" -- It seemed to me that any person who had seen the activity on the list over the last few weeks would simply take the opportunity to let the subject die down for a while.
  • I did not anticipate that I would be too busy during this week to address issues that arose as a result of this TEMPORARY MORATORIUM.
  • I TRIED to send an offlist note to everyone who posted on a gun thread. I've appaently missed at least one person who should have gotten a note.
  • I received many off list replies that posters had either missed the announcement or interpreted it in good faith differently than I had intended it. This has made it difficult for me to immediately and automatically enforce the previously announced sanction without devoting some thought to the posters' situations on a case-by-case basis
