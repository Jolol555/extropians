I was issued that particular test along with the all-too-obligatory MMPT while I was in therapy. Or maybe they got those particular results from the MMPT and I'm remembering wrong -- it was several years ago, and I took a lot of tests. I have no idea if you can get a copy of those tests as an individual; they're supposed to be given and interpreted by trained psychologists and the like. I know that my therapist wasn't the one that interpreted the results; she sent to somebody else. Do an internet search for the Minnesota Multiphasic Personality Test and see what you come up with -- that's my best advice.
