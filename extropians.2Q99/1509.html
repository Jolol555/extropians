"Civilization is protest against nature; 

                  progress requires us to take control of evolution."
                                      -- Thomas Huxley

Civilization is a natural, biological phenomenon. There is nothing in civilization that has not been a well established aspect of life for billions of years.
To think of civilization as somehow "unnatural" shows a profound lack of understanding of life and nature as well as a profound lack of understanding of civilization.
