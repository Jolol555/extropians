I remember this happening. The guy was dressed outlandishly because it was Halloween and he was on his way to a Halloween costume party when his car broke down. It also was during a pretty bad storm, and it was not clear if the student couldn't hear the warnings or couldn't understand the English. He did not try to get in the back door, as I recall. He merely didn't go away, but continued to knock on the door after the homeowner told him to go away, speaking through the door without answering. There was no threatening behavior on the part of the student, but the court ruled that the homeowner perceived a threat and shot him without malice.
