I fail to see how your opinion makes things suddenly objectively true. I fail to see how a democratic vote would make anyone's opinion suddenly and objectively true. And I don't believe in god(s).

Life is individual. Death is individual. Noone has as much to lose from dying as the person who would be dead. Every person must be free to defend their life. If your neighbors possession of gun(s) bothers you, then perhaps you ought to *get over it*. I'm not willing to let myself be killed, stolen from, wounded, etc, because you feel unsafe due to my gun(s).

However, once your neighbor starts shooting at you (or punching you, or something along those lines) then there is reason to discuss disarmament. But a peaceful person needs to be free to work to support his life, and he needs to be free to fight to defend it.
