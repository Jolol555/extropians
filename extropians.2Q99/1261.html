It has been asked,

"what is the ultimate goal of the post human form?"

The ultimate goal is to be able to do anything that is possible for as long as possible. Since we do not fully know what is possible, it is difficult to predict what we will become. The best we can do now is to keep learning what is possible and to increase our capacity for action.
