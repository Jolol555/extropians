Be aware that transdermal patches still deliver nicotine and can affect the heart. If someone is supposed to quit smoking due to heart problems or high blood pressure, they must not use nicotine in any form. Any form of nicotine will constrict the blood vessels, raise the blood pressure, and increase the chances for stroke or heart attack.
