For the benefit of new participants it should perhaps be pointed out that there is a dedicated channel for discussion of transhumanism-relevant technologies. The transhumantech list is hosted at excelsior.org (planetx.com) and is archived at http://mail.planetx.com/transhumantech/

The volume is low to medium (<20 posts/day). So far, we don't need moderation. Please consult the archive for the material covered. Your contribution in trawling the Net for relevant material is solicited.

To subscribe, send "subscribe transhumantech" (sans quotes) in message body to majordomo@excelsior.org.
