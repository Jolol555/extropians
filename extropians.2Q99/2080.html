Scott Badger writes:

> What's the trick to join? Required fields are:
>
> Email address of your SETI@home account:

After you download your client, during configuration you use an email address as identifier. You can use the same identifier if running several instances of a client (notice that the UNIX version needs private directory for each instance if run on the same machine).

> Your SETI@home password:

You enter the above address into the field, and press the submission button. Your password will be mailed to you in a few minutes. Use this password to join a team.
