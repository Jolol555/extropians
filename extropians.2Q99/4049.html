ProSpace in cooperation with the Space Frontier Foundation invites you to attend the 8th Space Frontier Conference

September 23-26, 1999
8th Space Frontier Conference
Los Angeles, CA

Join leaders of private space enterprise, government, finance, and the media at the 8th annual Space Frontier Conference, September 23-26, at the Sheraton Gateway near LAX. SFC is the leading annual commercial space conference. Topics range from commercial space marketplaces of today, such as Reusable Launch Vehicles and the International Space Station, to potential markets of tomorrow, such as space-based solar power, the Moon, Mars, and asteroid mining.

For more information or to register on-line by credit card, please see:

http://www.space-frontier.org/events/sfc8/

Call for Presentations:

The Space Frontier Conference seeks innovative, professional presentations in commercial space subjects such as the above. Abstracts of less than 200 words should be emailed to Colette Christiansen at colettec@cwo.com.

Exhibit Opportunities:

SFC provides an outstanding opportunity to showcase commercial space endeavors. For information on corporate exhibits and sponsorship, please contact Kerinia Cusick at kcusick@rain.org.
