Registrations for EXTRO 3 are coming in at an accelerating pace. 63 people
have already registered. This is far more than at the same stage for the
last conference, so this looks set to be the largest extropian gathering
yet. It looks like we'll have 200 or so attendees from all over the world
(besides the US, extropians are coming from Canada, Venezuela, England,
Germany, and Sweden.

Registration rates go up after July 10, so now is the time to register. We
are extending the deadline for lowest-rate registration until July 12. The
quickest way to register is via the web site:
http://www.extropy.org/registra.htm All registrations received by midnight
on the 12th qualify. After that the rates go up by $5 to $25 depending on
your status (member/non-member, student, etc.)

Onward!
