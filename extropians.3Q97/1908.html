A currently annoying but otherwise harmless medical condition of mine
(vestibular neuronitis, if anyone's interested) has taken me off
caffeine because the vaso-constriction properties of it aggravate the
condition. Having been a java junkie all my life, this leaves me
significantly under-stimulated. I'd like to find something easier to
get than dexedrine, safer than ephedrine, and without that specific
side-effect (other side effects might be OK). I've tried that new
Guarana drink, but that doesn't do it. Any ideas?
