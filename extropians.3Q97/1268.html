YakWax asks what changes we'd make to our body.

I'd keep my skin (I enjoy it) and skull (for continuity),
and otherwise replace as much as possible with Foglets;
distribute my mind throughout my new body, replacing
hard neural connections with network protocol.

The Foglets should be of at least three different designs,
so that they don't all fail at once the first time I find
myself in (e.g.) a strong magnetic field.
