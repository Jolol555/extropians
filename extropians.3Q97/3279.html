Subject: Thought you'd want to see this: Anti-Cassini Protest Schedule

I gleaned this info off of the "Stop Cassini Newsletter" from Russell
D.
Hoffman.

Thought you might want to have it in order to remain aware of the
activities
of anti-Cassini forces in your neighborhood.

According to their recent newsletter, ". . .Congressional support for
hearings grows and grows. Our understanding is that now five
Congresspeople
-- Lynn Woosley, are calling for hearings and others are listening. .
."

There's even been talk of getting boats located outside the 3-mile
"International water" zone -- but in the path of the Titan IV's launch
trajectory (based on a Shrimp boat incident that delayed a Delta launch

at
the Cape a week ago) as a means of delaying the launch.

The latest FCPJ news about Cassini

September 7
Dr. Michio Kaku speaks about Cassini at the Cathedral of the
Incarnation

(University Parkway & North Charles) in Baltimore at 7:00 pm.
Call (410) 323-7200 for details.

September 8
Press Conference at the National Press Club in Washington DC at
noon.
Will feature Dr. Michio Kaku, Karl Grossman, Bruce Gagnon and many
others.
(Will be on the 13th floor in the Lisagor Room). At 1:00 pm we
will
hold a vigil at
the White House and attempt to deliver our Cancel Cassini
petitions
to
President
Clinton

September 10 (Wednesday)
11:00 am to 1:00 pm
Debate with NASA/USAF at Daytona Beach Community College (DBCC),
Student Gov't Assn. (SGA)
Participants: Tony Ehrlich, David Hartgrove, Alan Kohn, Shannon
Larsen,
and Mary Beth Sullivan. There will also be at least 2
representatives
from
NASA/USAF.
Format: each side will have 20 minutes to expound their case.
After
that, there
will be 40 minutes of questions from the audience.

September 13
Bruce Gagnon speaks at a cultural event to Cancel Cassini in
Gainesville
sponsored by the S.E. Friends of Reclaiming. It will be held at
the
School of
Massage on south Hwy 441 from 6-9 pm. For more information,
call (352) 377-0753

will be
held at the Melbourne, Florida theatre called Metro Cinema (3020
New
Haven
Ave). Show starts at 7:00 pm followed by discussion led by Mary
Beth
Sullivan.
Call (407) 727-7819 for more information.

Bruce Gagnon will debate representatives of the Air Force and NASA

in a
"Community Conversation" being hosted by the "Florida Today"
newspaper
at their building on U.S. 1 just south of Rockledge and north of
Melbourne.
The public is encouraged to participate in this event. Begins at
7:00
pm.

September 20
Bruce Gagnon will be the luncheon speaker at the United Nations
Association
monthly meeting at the Langford Hotel in Winter Park. Begins at
noon.

September 20
Rally at the United Nations in New York calling for the
cancellation of
Cassini. From noon to 8:00 pm.

Sept 21 - Oct 3
Round-the-clock vigil at Cape Canaveral main gate will be

pm.
