Reminiscing on some of William Burroughs ideas:

"After one look at this planet any visitor from outer space would say 'I
WANT TO SEE THE MANAGER.'"

"Kerouac opened a million coffee bars and sold a million pairs of Levis to
both sexes. Woodstock rises from his pages."

"A partner evoked by sophisticated electric brain stimulation could be as
real and much more satisfying than the boy or girl next door. . . . All the
stars in Hollywood living or dead are there for your pleasure. Sated with
superstars, you can lay Cleopatra, Helen of Troy, Isis, Madame Pompadour, or
Aphrodite. You can get fucked by Pan, Jesus Christ, Apollo or the Devil
himself."

William Burroughs
