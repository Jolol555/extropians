Thin Film Microelectrode Bibliography Update 5.

Danny Banks <D.Banks@surrey.ac.uk>
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
This file lists the documents that I have added to the thin film
microelectrode bibliographies that can be found on the web at:

http://www.ee.surrey.ac.uk/Personal/D.Banks/bibs.html

I am very grateful to the IEEE for permission to reproduce abstracts from
the 1996 IEEE/EMBS conference in Amsterdam. Also to Thomas Laurell and
Thomas Stieglitz and their colleagues for permission to reproduce copies of
the posters they presented at the conference.

Many thanks to P Heiduschka (Tuebingen) for corrections and references,
Thomas Laurell (Lund) for reprints, Steve Potter (CalTech) for references,
Wim Rutten (Twente) for web page references, and Jos Smit (Twente) for a
copy of his thesis. Steve Potter has kindly published his list of references
on the web (see the links page mentioned below).

Since the last update, I have also added quite a number of links to web
pages of people involved in thin-film microelectrode work. These can be
found on my links page:

http://www.ee.surrey.ac.uk/Personal/D.Banks/links.html#tflinks

As usual: the bibliography only lists publications that I have, and is far
from complete. Citations / reprints gratefully received. The date of the
update file is the date I started to compile it, in case anyone is
wondering.

Danny Banks.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
