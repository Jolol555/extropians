Hagbard Celine:
>What changes in your personal lifestyle have you taken to increase the
>amount of extropy therein (changes = mental, physical, social, etc.)?

Thank you for your question, it makes a nice thing to reflect upon as
I pack my bags for Extro. How do I pack and type at the same time,
you ask? Well, there's that extra arm I keep for busy days.....

WHAT I DO NOW

--pursue dual careers to meet both my financial and personal goals
--vitamin/herb supplement program
--push my doctor for medical protocols that focus on prevention and
holistic health
--life experimentation with breaking the barriers that form the nexus
of gender and personal identity
--reading up on developments in science, sociology, psychology, etc.
--pursue open-ended philosophies and thinking, to the exclusion of
dogma and ideology

WHAT I PLAN TO DO

--develop a more stable set of polyamorous relationships
--develop a more flexible career path
--learn the technical end of web publishing
--make new transhuman friends!
--clean out my refrigerator (ok, so it's not an Extropian goal, but
it needs doing)

Sin,

Kathryn Aegis
