Well, I am not, not even in a little way, a racist...
This way I would like to expose a possible use of the genetics:

Considering:

1. It is observed that the mind of a person answers to physical
effects in different ways (very obvius...). And the light is a
physical effect. So it's natural that the minds (be of "whites"
(light roses) or "blacks" (browns)) react differently when seeing a
white or black person... It is not racism... Its just a
"photo-neuronal" effect... Another example is the colour of the hair
or eyes of a person...The effect is similar and I should add a
psychological attraction too, possibly based *also* on light
attraction...
2. Recently we have heard about the discovery of the gen responsible
for the whitening of the hair. This way, in some more years, men
and women, as they wish, will keep their hair on the original
colour, when get older...

So, extending:

. In the future, it will be possible to change the colour of the
hair, eyes or even skin... Obviously, depending on the wish of each
individual...
This is a very interesting possibility, also because of racism.
Indeed, if a white person would feel better in the mirror seeing
itself black ( or vice-versa, white and blond..., or even other
colour - maybe even phosphorescent...) he/she will have the option of
choice... It will be good for us, whites, and for the blacks as
well... It will decrease any psychological difference, where possibly
still exists (in both minds...).

Just scientific talks...

Gomes.
