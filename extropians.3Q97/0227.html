I can't help being amused by all of This UFO bussines. I mean, I don't
doubt that there is, or has been, life elsewhere in the universe. If you
are an atheist like me it would seem strange, that in this great
universe, that the basis for life hasn't happened in thousand of places.

But I would think that any civilization that could traverse the universe
in something as small as one of the proposed UFO's they would probably
be in such a state of development that they would have reached some kind
of singularity, or at least have developed nanotechnology.

Civilizations like that could just send a seed the size of a grain of
sand to any planets close by to check it out. Much cheaper to send out.
Not something the size of a UFO. Hmm?

Just a thought.
