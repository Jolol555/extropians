I previously posted the URL for Dymocks, an Oz bookstore that e-sells my
new book about the Singularity. Another place to try is

http://db.gleebooks.com.au/gleebooks/default.asp

where you enter the title simple as SPIKE and author simply as BRODERICK.

US readers should be encouraged by the news that $US 1.00 currently buys
around $AUST 1.36, so the trade paperback price is cheaper than it appears.
On the other hand, I imagine there's a charge for airmail delivery.
