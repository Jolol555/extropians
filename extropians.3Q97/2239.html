Women's rights, gay rights - the idea of "rights" is wrong. It's a way for
the 'white heterosexual males' to *allow* you to be yourself without having
to admit their wrongs. This 'allowance' is worse than outright
discrimination. It's the weapon of hypocrites, those who say one thing yet
do another, those who can be your friend yet remain your enemy, and those who
accept yet hate. It's time we stopped granting rights and started taking
them away. Such as the right to discriminate, something society and
government passed out many years ago.

I refuse to accept anyone, because there's nothing to accept. I refuse to
let minorities be my friends, because they're already welcome. And I refuse
to give others rights, because they were never wrong.

--Wax
