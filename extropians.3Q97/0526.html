Sarah Marr writes:
>It seems to me that you're talking about the interplay between two
>_functions_ of belief (and clothes). I'd term one the reflexive
>function ('How does this belief affect me alone, in isolation?') and
>the social function ('How does this belief affect my interactions
>with, and opinions of others, and their interactions with, and
>opinions of me?').

I know "reflexive" is a popular term in certain academic circles, but
its connotation is pretty vague for most people. How about "personal"
vs. "social" roles of clothes and belief? Or "individual" vs.
"social" roles?
