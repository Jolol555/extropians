At 12:10 PM 7/27/97 -0400, Yakwax2@aol.com wrote:

>Since we seem to be rapidly approaching a time when we will be able to alter
>our own bodies via genetics, nanotechnology, etc. I have a simple question:

>What will you change?

Everything! Even the aspects of myself that have formed who I am now. Take
these memories and reference points and make them clearer and crisper in my
mind. Even my finest qualities. Make them even kinder and more sincere.
Even my passions and wonder. Make them more exciting. And the areas that
are problematic that have been catalytic in my growth, make them remain ever
so memorable that I can recall them in my private moments as I reflect over
my long, long life.

(Please refer to my essay "Ageless Thinking" at http://www.extropic-art.com")
