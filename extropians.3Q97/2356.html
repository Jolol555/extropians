Eliezer writes
: Episodic memory might also be divided into two parts: Relational
: episodic memory and narrative episodic memory. Just a guess.

Or an episode might be associated with an item of semantic memory.
I remember (episodic) sniffing a morning-glory on the wall of an
apartment building; I remember (semantic) that the only time I
lived in a building with morning-glory vines was in Chicago before
my fifth year.
