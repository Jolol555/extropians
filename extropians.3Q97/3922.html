Dear fellow Extropians:

In order to obtain the current usable definitions of the words "Election"
and "Fraud"
the most appropriate place to look (but only if you wish to be realistic)
is a law dictionary.

than a few practicing attorneys who regularly darken the justice system by
their very presence and otherwise obscure the facts and evidence in any
given case with their rhetoric.

Addendum:
The number of attorneys who seem to think that the best jury is one that
is incapable of making an independent value judgement would astound many
of you.
