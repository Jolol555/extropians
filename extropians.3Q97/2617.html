Check out the latest issue of Forbes magazine. The cover story is about the
"cyber-libertarians", profiles Sameer Parekh, quotes David Friedman and does
a fair job of describing private digital currency. The basic ideas of the
cypherpunks are given a fairly positive treatment. More "mainstreaming" of
some of our core ideas. Here's the "snag line" from the article:

They're wirehead heirs to Thomas
Jefferson: libertarian computer geeks
plotting a cryptographic revolution.

You can read the article at http://www.forbes/com
