On Mon, 28 Jul 1997, Rick Knight wrote:

> I'm assimilating a glossary of terms relative to the concept of
> singularity. It is some research for a story. Also, pointers to
> previous editions of Extropian digest that might have pertinent info
> would be beneficial.

I have gathered some neologism and terms on my web at
http://www.aleph.se/Trans/Words, you might want to take a look.

Some terms I think are related are: AI, autoevolution, autopotent,
biological fundamentalism, bionics, cybergnosticism, distributed
intelligence, dividuals, enhanced reality, exoselves, fork, hive
computing, infomorph, internalnet, memetics, nanotechnology,
nootropics, now shock, posthumans, technosphere, transcend,
uploading.

> disbelief merely to enjoy the 1.5 to 2 hours.
