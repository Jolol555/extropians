Hi, just a quick comment:

Having a hysterectomy does not mean that a person is not interested in,
perform, dream about or enjoy sex or eroticism. It means surgical removal
of part or all of the uterus. It does not mean that the brain or vagina (the
two sexual organs) no longer work.
