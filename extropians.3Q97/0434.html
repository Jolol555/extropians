Natasha wrote:

>The scientific books already mentioned are indeed favorite readings. To
>broaden horizons and introduce other important modes of thinking, you might
>consider reading the following to fully exploit your brain power!
>
>_Creativity_, Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi
>_Toward A Psychology Of Being_, Abraham H. Maslow
>_The Farther Reaches of Human Nature_, Abraham Maslow
>Anything by De Bono
>
>These books explore developed intelligence and well-being by engineering the
>ability to think creatively.

Or, to go even further out on a transhuman\automorphic limb, the
book _Art Objects_ by Jeanette Winterson is a tremendously good collection
of essays dealing with the immortal necessity of creativity in the present
and the future. Also, check out _Brave New World Revisited_ by Aldous
Huxley. Although it is an older book (1957), it contains many plausible
extropic solutions to overpopulation and other such social maladies.
