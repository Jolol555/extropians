I know that a number of people are planning on coming to EXTRO 3 but have
not yet registered. Especially for local extropians, it may seem to be easy
to register at the door. One reason to register before Wednesday: We have
to give the hotel the final banquet numbers by then. We will add some extra
places, but will have to be conservative in our estimates since the banquet
is quite expensive. If you don't register until the day, you *may* not be
able to get a seat at the banquet.

This does *not* mean missing Drexler's talk (ironically titled "How to be
Cautious and Conservative") since we will let in everyone after the banquet
and before he begins to speak. But you'll have a less favorable position
and miss out on the critical mass socializing!

See you in a week.
