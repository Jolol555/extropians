No. The banquet is included in the registration.

My point was that we have to tell the hotel on Wednesday how many banquet
places we want. If we underestimate, some folks will miss out on the meal.
Everyone who registers before Wednesday is guaranteed a banquet place. We
will also add on an estimated number so those showing up to register soon
after 8am on Saturday will also get in. But if there's a big surge of
attendees wanting to register on Saturday, it's possible that we will run
out of banquet places. (If you arrive at the event closer to the starting
time of 9am rather than the beginning of registration at 8am, your chances
of not getting a banquet place obviously will be higher.) As I said, we can
still let those folks in to hear Drexler.
