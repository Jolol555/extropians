The Aug issue of Scientific American has a profile of Jeremy Rifkin,
biological fundamentalist number one. Here's an eloquent description of
how many of us here view the world by Mr Rifkin from that article.

"We're creating multiple personas. We're creating a thespian
sense of personality where we see ouselves as works of art, and we see
everything in our environment as a prop, as a set, as a stage, as a
backdrop for filling ourselves in. We don't see ourselves as ever
completed. We are in-formation."

You know you're diametricly oposed to someones point of view when their
description of the horrific is to you one of beauty.

Maybe Max More would like to use this quote to greet visitors to the EXI
web site! ;>
