    Harvey Newstrom wrote:
    > Is anyone else seeing repeated postings after a few days, or is it just me
    > and my wonderful ISP? This started less than a week ago, and doesn't occur
    > with all postings. But when it does occur, I get a burst of a dozen old
    > postings from this list that I had already seen 2-3 days before. I have not
    > noticed this behavior on any of my other lists (of which there are about
    > 50). The mail headers look like they are being freshly resent directly from
    > tick.javien.com (javien2-3.spots.ab.ca [209.115.169.3]) to my ISP for my
    > address.

    This happened on SL4.org, which also runs off the same mailserver. I made
    a change to majordomo that I think fixed the problem on SL4 (it caught a
    repeating message and bounced it).

    On SL4, I added the taboo header:

    taboo_headers << END
    /majordom set sender to owner-sl4/i
    END
