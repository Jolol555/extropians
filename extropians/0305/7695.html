    Mike Lorrey wrote:

    > Perhaps if you want a truly 'natural' hunting experience, you should go
    > out in the woods in a loincloth and make your own bow with ash and gut,
    > chipping your arrowheads from flint...

    Why not take this all the way? One could argue that ash
    bows and flint arrows are modern technology (even if not
    a particularly advanced example) since they were cutting
    edge to some human groups only a couple hundred years ago.

    I would suppose that the first use of weapons in humans
    was stones, hurled like a pitched baseball. Perhaps this
    even predated chasing carnivores from their kill using
    a burning branch.

    Now that would be an interesting contest: see who can bag
    a rabbit or a squirrel using only hurled stones. spike
