    > 'Ad hominem' is the term given to one of two kinds of fallacious
    > *objections* to an argument. X is false because Y, where Y is one
    > or more facts about the person asserting X. Or, X is false because
    > Y, where Y is one more facts about the circumstances of the person
    > asserting X. There is no such thing as a positive ad hominem
    > assertion except as it is an objection to some other assertion.
