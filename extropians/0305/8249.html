    Emlyn wrote:
    > On the subject of the probe getting lost, I'd fix that by sending a
    > really large amount of probes(like 1 million?). There's heaps of iron
    > to bob around in and a really large space.

    ### Actually, this would also solve the problem of sending the signal up -
    the thousands of probes being lost on the way would establish a relay,
    connected by relatively low volume seismic transmissions. Probably you would
    need to develop some really high-temp electronics, maybe with doped diamond
    as the main material.

    You could safely explode a 20 megaton nuke under some desert, deep enough so
    as not to breach the surface. Carting all this iron together would be pretty
    expensive, though.
