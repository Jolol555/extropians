    > So, is there an attack here I don't see? If so, can you plug the
    > hole, or come up with a system with the same safeguards?

    Your system makes it straightforward for people to buy and sell votes.
        ("After the election, bring me your ballot, and if it includes a
    vote for X, I'll pay you $Y.") This is normally considered a weakness
    in a voting system. Unfortunately, any system that tries to enforce
    accountability in the voting system would seem to have this problem.
    It would be a great contribution to show that they are compatible.
