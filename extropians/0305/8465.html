    June 21-29
    For those interested in attending the Escape to New Hampshire
    conference, hosted by the Libertarian Party of New Hampshire and the
    Free State Project, I will be in attendance and will be setting up a
    Wi-Fi (802.11b) wireless network service free to attendees covering the
    entire Campground area as well as surrounding wilderness to a range of
    1350 yards, more or less, from the base station. The base station will
    be located at tent campsite #28. Those interested in developing web
    content for the base station server should contact me ahead of time for
    testing. Hard line access to the greater internet from the base station
    server may or may not be available. Contributions of equipment, time,
    and content to enhance this basic service are all appreciated. At this
    time, the reserved campsite has electricity, I have several servers,
    and one Wi-Fi base station.

    The conference will be at Rogers Campground in Lancaster, NH on Route
    2.

    Further information on Escape to NH and reservations information:
    http://www.lpnh.org/escape.htm
