    The next ExtroBritannia lunch/get-together is scheduled for
    Saturday the 24th of May at 12 noon in central London.

    The theme of the meeting is: “Life-extension diets”

    Caloric Restriction, Low Carbs, Paleo, Atkins, whatever your experience or
    interest come along to ask questions, gather tips and share experiences in an
    informal chat with other life-extensionists.

    WHERE & WHEN******************************************

    If you want to attend, you have three choices:

    1) you can show up at the basement café of Europe's largest
    bookshop (Waterstone's in Piccadilly, London) at 12,00

    2) you can go directly to the restaurant for lunch ("Chowki", 1,00
    o'clock). The table will be booked under my name (Fabio).

    3) you can join us back at Waterstone's for coffee/drinks and
    further discussion at about 2,30/3,00 pm (either in the basement
    cafè or restaurant).

    If it's your first time at an ExtroBritannia meeting I'll be the guy
    clearly displaying a copy of Kurzweil's "The Age of Spiritual
    Machines" on the table we'll be sitting at.

    DETAILS/MAPS/ETC****************************************

    WATERSTONE'S

    From Piccadilly Circus, Waterstone's is about 50 yards down
    Piccadilly (on the left handside). Once inside take the stairs on
    the right, down to the lower floor cafe'/restaurant: the cafe will
    be right in front of you.
