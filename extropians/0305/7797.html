    Hungary:

    A becoming mother is litigating because the
    Scientfic Wealth Administration (SWA) has not
    allowed to take and the store stem cells
    of her child for possible future medical use.

    This operation (taking blood from the
    umbilical cord) has been performed routinely
    in the past. Now the SWA announced that
    this is a research area and the hospitals do
    not have the right to do it. They are obliged
    to ask for permission of the SWA which makes
    the decision in each individual case.
