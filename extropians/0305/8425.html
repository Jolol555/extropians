    Anyone who is really into electronics experimentation
    in the OC area Monday might want to check this out.
    This place used to be the main West Coast center for
    service of a lot of older electronics display
    technology. The owner died and they are dumping
    everything for a song. Monday may be the last day.
    There are all kinds of test equipment, projectors,
    etc. that originally filled several large industrial
    units. I got a big opaque projector for $2, a box of
    video tape for free, and an 800 Watt industrial use
    halogen light with integrated cooling fan in new
    condition for $2. There is literally tons of stuff
    which is destined for the trash if not sold.

    They are at 2413 S. Broadway, as best I recall, in
    Santa Ana. 714-979-0400.
