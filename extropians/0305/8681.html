    Harvey Newstrom wrote:

    > I still dispute this spurious claim that humans are less
    > healthier than in former times.

    Excuse me but I am not making that claim. Diamond is not making that claim.
    No one is making that claim. No one is supposing we should dispense with
    medical science. Strawman.

    >> agricultural products rank very
    >> poorly on a nutrients/calorie scale.

    > Just because you keep repeating this over and over does not
    > make it so.

    So then refute the claim, Harvey!

    If we compare the nutrients per calorie in a slice of whole wheat bread, as
    a fine example of a common product of agriculture, to just about any wild
    game meat or vegetable, we see that calorie for calorie, it rates very low
    for nutritional content. Do you really disagree? If you do then show me why.

    Dairy products also rate poorly. Cheese for example is typically about 70%
    of calories from saturated fat.

    -gts
