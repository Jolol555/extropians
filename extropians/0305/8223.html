    on neutrino beam weapons that made no sense, someone
    brings order to the universe:

    Neutrino beam could neutralize nuclear bomb
    14 May 2003, New Scientist.com
    http://www.newscientist.com/news/news.jsp?id=ns99993734

    It would be nice to get an actual citation to the
    hard physical arguments involved so the Extropian
    list could one again regain its title as the list
    where one has to "be there" or "be square".
