    > From: "Harvey Newstrom"
    > > > check your ID to vote? You mean at the voting booth?
    > >
    > > Yes. In Florida, they won't let you into the voting place
    > without showing
    > > TWO forms of ID first.
    > >
    >
    > Do you know how long they have been doing that?

    It seemed to start when Jeb Bush became governor. We always had to show at
    least one form of ID. But the multiple forms of ID plus voter registration
    card plus being on the voter rolls plus being currently live online in the
    computers in Tallahassee was new. A lot of people couldn't vote because the
    computers in Tallahassee said no, even though the local voter rolls and
    everybody thought they looked find to vote. It wasn't until after the
    election that some people got the problem corrected and were told that it
    was a mistake and they should have been allowed to vote.
