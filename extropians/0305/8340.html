    spoiler space

    Two other things that must be considered:

    1. Couple of days ago they announced an online game for next year that
    is said to take place after the events of the movies, yet still within
    the Matrix. So what this says is it isn't getting destroyed, and quite
    likely the resolution of the problem does not entail everyone in there
    "waking up".

    2. Going with that then, what makes most sense is that somehow the
    humans and machines are going to develop a truce, or perhaps the humans
    will gain complete control over the Matrix and decide to continue living
    in it for the most part. This is already hinted at in at least two
    different dialogs in Reloaded. The title "Revolutions" may refer to both
    a revolution within the Matrix, and also a revolution amongst the
    machines as they are forced to face the fact that they cannot maintain
    control and must cooperate.
