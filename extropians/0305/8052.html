    --- Party of Citizens <citizens@vcn.bc.ca> wrote:
    > We are told so often about how strong ants are. Here is a problem for
    > nanotechnologists:
    >
    > How many ants, harnessed by carbon nanofibres would it take to make
    > the equivalent of one horse power?

    I can see it now, little ant sized treadmills in huge farm towers, each
    ant in its own life sustaining pod, completely unaware of the real
    world around it, fed a virtual reality by nanonetware and nutritious
    slurry to keep it walking, generating energy for us, its gods, to use
    for our every whim. What is this hellish distopia? The Antrix.

    Oh, there will be dissidents, escapees, living in the bowels of the
    earth in a low tech colony called Anton. We will have agents among
    them, seeking out insurgent ants... they will seek our destruction,
    burrowing into the structure of our homes, destroying from within...

    "Mister Anterson, we have had our eyes on you," said Agent Smith.
    "My Name.... is Neo...."
