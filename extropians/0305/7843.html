    A curious and delicious look into 3 physicists analysis of what the future might hold for getting the nuclear genie back in the bottle. I viewed it in Adobe's PDF format, but its located on Thurs May 8th HEP-Phenomenology section, in other viewing formats.

    -Mitch

    http://xxx.lanl.gov/PS_cache/hep-h/pdf/0305/0305062.pdf
    Destruction of Nuclear Bombs Using Ultra-High Energy Neutrino Beam
    Authors: Hirotaka Sugawara (Univ. of Hawaii), Hiroyuki Hagura (KEK), Toshiya Sanami (KEK)
    Comments: 19 pages, 9 figures, LaTeX2e

    We discuss the possibility of utilizing the ultra-high energy neutrino beam (about 1000 TeV) to detect and destroy the nuclear bombs wherever they are and whoever possess them.
