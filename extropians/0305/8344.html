    I'm afraid Amnesty has said it has no connection to this petition. Amina Lawal's case is still working its way through the judicial system. Her case is due to be heard by a (sharia) appeal court in June (this hearing was postponed to avoid causing a rumpus during the recent election) after which there is further redress via the federal courts. Don't know who is behind this, but it seems best to wait until the next hearing has been held at least.
