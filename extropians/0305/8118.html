    "The same Micahel Fumento who touted the "myth of heterosexual AIDS"? This

    I don't think a comment such as this one adds to the ongoing discourse.
    Especially given John and Matus's subsequent responses to you, I will be
    interested to see exactly how you consider Mr. Fumento "personally culpable
    for millions of needless deaths." I find the fact that you would make a
    comment such as that one particularly ironic considering you yourself have

    Africa, even though to my knowledge he was not spreading the disease sexually

    the Communist victories in 1975 since you questioned the "myth of the
    Communist bloodbath"?
