    Considering the numbers of deaths due to flu each
    year, and knowing the main source of the problem -
    huge numbers of pigs raised together with ducks in
    mainland China, the amazing thing is that nothing has
    been done about it to date.

    If any country sent in assassins to kill 36,000
    Americans in a year (10X the 9/11 deaths), we would be
    at war tomorrow. Yet somehow the millions who die
    worldwide due to Chinese irresponsibility are ignored.
     Forget flu shots (or don't, really; I get mine every
    year and almost died (or felt like it) one year that I
    missed). Go to the source. China has had the
    attitude culturally and governmentally for a few
    thousand years that they can get away with murder,
    literally, and they are usually proven correct.

    For dozens of centuries, China has used its enormous
    military might to extort protection money from all its
    neighbors, by whom it is universally hated. These
    days, according to many reports, its agents go to
    countries like Indonesia (or the U.S. - a la Gore,
    Lorel, etc, etc, etc.), offering sweetheart trade or
    military supply deals to the political and business
    leadership - offers that they effectively "can't
    refuse." Chinese companies undercut U.S. firms by
    stealing all the intellectual property for the
    machinery used to produce a product and then
    scrupulously abiding by the law for the final sale to
    the dealer or distributor, thus destroying much of the
    incentive to innovate.

    They are past masters at the game of corrupting and
    then looting. When the piper has to be paid, when the
    man on the street discovers that the reason he is out
    of work and starving is that his country has been
    looted, it is the small Chinese shopkeeper whose
    business is burned, wife and daughter gang-raped,
    etc., while the crooks from the highest circles of
    power leave to spend their ill-gotten gains.

    Such a culture/nation cannot be expected to act
    responsibly unless it is forced to. The only real
    solution to SARS and the flu is to impose a quarentine
    on China, period. Nothing goes in or out until the
    Chinese can verify to U.N. inspectors that they are no
    longer raising ducks and pigs together.
