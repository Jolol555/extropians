    At 10:40 PM 5/17/03 -0500, Greg wrote:

    >Recently (in connection with my report on weight loss and fitness)<

    Greg looked very healthy and svelte. What an amazing host - he created a
    whirlwind of a trip from take off out of Houston to our landing on Cozumel,
    into the town and to the resort. We had our own boat and dive master and
    went on approximately 2 dives a day from the boat, and as many as we wanted
    from the shore. My favorite was going through the cave-lets and the night
    dive where we descended about 50 feet and turned our flashlights off and
    watched the luminous lights of life in the dark.

    The 100+ foot dive was also great. It's amazing that the water is still
    clear at 100 feet. We saw octopus, barracudas and all sorts of quixotic coral.
