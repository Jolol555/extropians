    > At 08:04 AM 5/7/03 -0400, gts wrote:
    > >If
    > >I spit on the sidewalk and some passerby sees me and calls me a bum
    > >then he is making an implied ad hominem argument

    'Ad hominem' is the term given to one of two kinds of fallacious
    *objections* to an argument. X is false because Y, where Y is one or
    more facts about the person asserting X. Or, X is false because Y, where
    Y is one more facts about the circumstances of the person asserting X.
    There is no such thing as a positive ad hominem assertion except as it
    is an objection to some other assertion.

    Keith
