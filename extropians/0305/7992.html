    Is anyone else seeing repeated postings after a few days, or is it just me
    and my wonderful ISP? This started less than a week ago, and doesn't occur

    postings from this list that I had already seen 2-3 days before. I have not
    noticed this behavior on any of my other lists (of which there are about
    50). The mail headers look like they are being freshly resent directly from
