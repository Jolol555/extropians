    What's the problem? Haven't you people ever looked these terms up in the
    dictionary before? Sheesh!

    Seriously, everyone should look these words up in the dictionary. Don't
    trust just one dictionary, look in as many as you can find. Don't look for
    just one meaning you like, read every single possible meaning. Don't just
    look at modern dictionaries, find older ones and look in the etymology of
    the word to verify historical accuracy.

    And when you've finally convinced yourself that you really understand what
    these English words mean, decide if you are at heart a "conservative" or a
    "liberal".
