    >
    > Harvey Newstrom wrote:
    >
    > > Mike, you may have missed the way that Brian and gts

    > I believe Mike was referencing other studies mentioned in addition to the

    Maybe so. But you seemed to imply that these studies supported the Paleo
    Diet, and then he quotes your thread when he said he was "glad to see some
    studies...." I was just making sure that he was not being mislead by your
    statements in the exact way I thought they seemed misleading.

    > In any case please read our words again, Harvey. Brian was pointing out
    that
    > the authors gave no reason to believe fiber from grains is superior to any
    > other kind of plant fiber. I was merely agreeing with Brian. Neither of us
    > were reinterpreting the study to "say the opposite of what the original
    > authors said."

    There is a big difference between "gave no reason to believe..." and saying
    the study supports your position. The Paleo Diet that you are pushing
    forbids eating grains. This study was about eating a diet including grains.
    To say that it supports the Paleo Diet seems misleading when the diet in
    supports directly violates the anti-grain provision of the Paleo Diet. You
    are interpreting that the same high-fiber effects of that diet can be
    achieved with the Paleo Diet, and would hopefully give similar results, but
    this is your belief (even if right) and does not derive from this study in
    any way.
