    spike66 wrote (14.5.2003/22:10) :
    > Does *anyone* who hangs out on extropians or SL4
    > ever read actual *fiction*, without the prefix
    > science? Sears and Roebuck catalogs?

    I would distinguish between literature and fiction. I don't believe
    that fiction is an essential part of literature. So I do read some
    literature, yes (in an extremely selective way, though), and it
    occasionally include some fictional elements.

    To me, your list of unrelated questions is a modest attempt at
    literature, for example. Humour in general is a particular case of
    literature.

    In fact, most people restrict their attempts at literature to humour.
    (In some special cases, I have had the feeling that people were
    practicing such restriction in a "nervous" way: actively denigrating
    the very idea of literature, while trying hard to overcome their own
    literary inhibition.)
