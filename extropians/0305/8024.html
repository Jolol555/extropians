    Just when you got over my chicken jokes of the last month, I have
    noticed a true story on the FOX News channels show "War Stories with
    Ollie North" that the US Marines Expeditionary Forces in Iraq have a
    rather sophisticated NBC detection and countermeasures system. The
    heart of this is a sealed NBC detection vehicle that costs $2 million
    apiece. It contains sophisticated NBC weapons detection devices, from
    the most advanced digital devices to a heretofore unannounced and
    classified weapons system known affectionately by the Marines as
    "Geraldine".
    Geraldine is obviously the product of a highly advanced defense weapons
    system R&D project. She probably soaked up millions of dollars of
    taxpayer money developing, testing, and proving her effecacy in her
    mission.
    Geraldine is a chicken. The only surviving chicken, it seems, of a
    flock shipped over to Iraq from highly secretive chemical weapons labs
    in the US. Most couldn't take the harrowing journey by cargo ship.
    Geraldine, however, is cut from a different cloth. She is a Marine, by
    god, and dammit, she doesnt' take any crap. She is on the front line of
    detection of WMD, willing to do her part for God, Country, and Colonel
    Sanders. Geraldine rides along with the boys on convoy, nonplussed by
    the dust, the dirt, the noise and stench of battle. The boys rely on
    her, and she knows it. She will cross any road, bridge any stream,
    highway, trail or track in search of WMD, and won't stop until she
    finds it. She is The Chicken of Mass Destruction.....
