    Mike Lorrey wrote,
    > June 21-29
    > For those interested in attending the Escape to New Hampshire
    > conference, hosted by the Libertarian Party of New Hampshire and the
    > Free State Project, I will be in attendance and will be setting up a
    > Wi-Fi (802.11b) wireless network service free to attendees covering the
    > entire Campground area as well as surrounding wilderness to a range of
    > 1350 yards, more or less, from the base station.

    Excellent work, Mike! Certainly we should use this opportunity to promote
    <Extropy.org> and other transhumanist websites. You certainly would want to
    set up a list of "interesting" links if you get Internet Access.

    Would you be interested in some on-line stores selling books, magazines,
    videos, DVDs, music, toys, games, electronics, software, hardware, and
    wireless phone accessories via Amazon.com? I have such as store set up at
    <http://Newstaff.biz> that sells security-related items. Other example
    sites would look like these:

            <http://Newstaff.biz?Libertarian> "The Libertarian Store"
            <http://Newstaff.biz?Conservative> "The Conservative Store"
            <http://Newstaff.biz?Liberal> "The Liberal Store"
            <http://Newstaff.biz?Republican> "The Republican Store"
            <http://Newstaff.biz?Democrat> "The Democrat Store"

            <http://Newstaff.biz?Political> "The Political Store"
            <http://Newstaff.biz?Liberty> "The Liberty Store"
            <http://Newstaff.biz?Freedom> "The Freedom Store"

            <http://Newstaff.biz?Computer> "The Computer Store"

    You could point to these, or set up a similar store on your own. (My site
    gets a referral fee from Amazon for each item sold. You could set up your
    own affiliate account, or use ExI's or support some other worthy cause.)

    If you don't have Internet Access, you might be able to grab interesting
    content from interesting web-sites and ask them if they mind if you make
    this available on your local server for this event. You might even be able
    to set up a cache engine to get your server to duplicate some popular
    transhumanist websites for local access for this event. With nothing else
    to browse (if you don't have Internet access), these sites could get a lot
    of browsing....
