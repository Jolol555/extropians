    On Wednesday 14 May 2003 11:56 am, Anders Sandberg wrote:

    > It is about underlying ideology, something which quite a few
    > politicians and citizens forget these days but which actually has
    > rather profound controlling effects on ideas. Conservatism is in
    > the abstract about conserving existing institutions against change,
    > liberalism (American sense, social democracy in Europe) is based on
    > the idea of using the government to create equality, and so on.

    Beware the excluded middle! Old-style liberalism is about individual
    freedom and rights with a minimal government strictly limited in the
    extent of its intereference with individuals and their organizations.
    Extropian old-style liberals (libertarians and so on) do not believe
    it desirable to create equality generally much less by government
    power and are at all interested in conserviing existing institutions.

    -s
