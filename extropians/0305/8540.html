    Pretty much my entire social scene (goths and hangers on) is on Friendster.
    The real use for the system is not in meeting new people blind, but in
    keeping track and in touch: enhancing and managing networking within your
    own social group. It makes it very easy to identify social nodes and take
    advantage of their existence to meet useful new people.
