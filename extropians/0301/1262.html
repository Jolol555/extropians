EvMick:
>Recently I've been reading about Plasma....the "fourth state of matter".

For those not aware, at least 99% of the Universe is in a plasma state.
Those such as us, on top of rocks such as this, live in special conditions.

>It seems that Hans Alfven (an early researcher in the field) proposed
>some pretty off the wall things....but not SO off the wall that he
>still didn't receive a Nobel for his work.

>Currently there are multiple websites on the subjects (HoloScience,
>The Electric Universe, The Plasma Cosmos) to mention just three.

this is kooky science, fringe stuff

>Has anyone any comments?

Alfven is very respected (and revered) in the plasma and astrophysics
(magnetohydrodynamics) fields. For example, the concept of
frozen-in-flux and flux tubes, are his ideas. You'll find Alfven waves
in every standard text book on plasma physics, and anyone studying
plasma physics today stands on his giant shoulders.
