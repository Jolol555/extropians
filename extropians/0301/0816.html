Not that this will help anyone to obtain a rare hardcover edition of
_The Road to Serfdom_, but anyone interested in Hayek would do well to
browse the Friedrich Hayek Scholars' Page at:

http://www.hayekcenter.org/friedrichhayek/hayek.html

The page contains many interesting links, including one to Hayek-L, a
moderated list dedicated to discussion of Hayek's thought and influence.
