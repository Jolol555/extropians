Ron asks

> ...a more important work of Hayek is "The Road to Serfdom",
> in which he does endorse concepts of natural rights.
>
> Mr. Lorrey,
> Do you know where I can get a hard back copy of Hayek's Road to
> Serfdom? My PB copy tore up before I could finish it so obviously I don't
> want another copy of the PB. But when I go looking for an HB copy I get
> nowhere.

Have you tried abebooks.com? That's for American Book Exchange.
You can get a used one at a fair price, I think. Besides, with
certain people acting like the Ministry of Truth going around
changing what's in books, it's getting to be safer to get an
old copy anyway.

Lee

P.S. At the very same time that Hayek was writing
"The Road to Serfdom" in 1938, a certain Joseph
Stalin was writing "The Road to Power". I've always
believed that there is a great lesson in that.
