Lee Corbin wrote:
 
>> It is patently false that humans are born free or with a
>> right to his or her own person.

You were born free with a right to your own person. Your parents did not
own you as an infant. You were not their personal property. They were
merely your legal guardians.

As an infant you were a free person with the natural right to do with
your body everything you were capable of doing. You were free to eat,
drink, sleep, cry, giggle, pick your nose, and crap in your diapers. Had
your parents violated your rights then they would have been guilty of a
crime and possibly sent to prison.
