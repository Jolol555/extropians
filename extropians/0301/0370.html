>I was rethinking and a society would only be dominant if it had a meme to

This may be a tad oversimplistic and deterministic. To supplement this
reductionist approach, I would recommend that you consider memeplexes
instead of just 'memes' (i.e. systems like capitalism, democracy, judicial
systems, etc.), and analyze the emergent effects of these dynamic and
normative systems on society as a whole, and how they interact with each
other over time.
