    Huh! After seeing another road accident last night on my
    bike ride home (this one was between a motorcycle and a car),
    and having a couple near misses of my own, I started thinking that
    it might be good to look into blood banks in Italy, if such
    a thing exists for a private person to store their blood.
    (I have a blood type that is present in only 2% of the
    population.)

    However I found something odd or maybe amusing - can one
    actually 'eat for one's blood type' ? According to this
    web page ...

    (blood type AB)
    http://www.ez-weightloss.com/ez-weightloss/articletypeabbloodtype.html

    Is this for real? According to this page I should be a vegetarian!
    [I am, almost (eating meat only a few times a month).]
