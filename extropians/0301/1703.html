This whole concept is idiotic. "World Peace"? When did we have that to begin
with?

If you want to ask, "Which country represents the biggest threat to
totalitarian thugs named Saddam Hussein" then the answer is the United
States. If you want to know "which country most threatens the domestic
tranquility of South Korea and is ruled by everyone's favorite Communist
child molester" then the answer is North Korea. If you want to know "Which
country is led by the totalitarian thug that the western press and
intelligentsia will most likely apologize for" then the answer is Iraq. I
would also more importantly ask, "Which country poses the greatest threat to
reason, objectivity, freedom, and progress?" That in my estimation is far
more important than non-sensical fantasy land beauty pageant world peace
queries.

Public opinion polls are no way to determine foreign policy. If they were,
then we'd be dealing with Kim Jong Il's quest for WMD in a unified communist
Korea.
