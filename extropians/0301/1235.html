scerir wrote:
> > Why does the USA still have troops in rich Europe?

Every nation should have bases in every other
nation, with a crew as large or as small as
that country chose. I give you a square km
in my country for your base in exchange for
a square km of land in your country for my base.
Any attack on any base is equivalent to an attack
on all foreign bases. This would encourage
cultural exchange to the benefit of (almost) all.

Then if some group decided that a certain piece
of ground is sacred and must be cleansed of all
infidels, they would be taking on every nation
on earth.
