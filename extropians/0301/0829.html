Lee Corbin wrote:
> State the logical conclusion:
>
> (1) No ducks waltz
> (2) No officers ever decline to waltz
> (3) All my poultry are ducks

I respectfully point out that, as it is extremely unlikely that anyone has
ever *asked* your poultry to waltz, they may well be officers.
