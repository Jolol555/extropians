If you're a Transhumanist, or just interested in futures issues and
technologies, and you're in the Toronto area, please come join us at the
Toronto Transhumanist Association.

We get together reguarly and host casual social events and focused
discussion groups. This year we're looking to host some very exciting
events, including a possible evening with cyborgicist Steven Mann, various
local cryonicists, life-extentionists, and much more.

We attend conferences, seminars, and other related events. We also strive to
influence public opinion in the form of strategically timed press releases
and other means. So, if you're interested in exerting some sociopolitical
influence, the TTA is also for you.

If you're interested in joining us, or if you simply want information,
please contact me at george@betterhumans.com.
