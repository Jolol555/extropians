> Eliezer S. Yudkowsky wrote:
>
> >My advice is to ignore nontechnical articles, as of the popular news
> >media, as they are rich on accusations (on both sides) but short on facts.
>
> It is really amazing to follow this story. And not just a little sad.
> The critics have worked themself up into a frenzy, and it is impossible
> to have a rational debate. And this is from so called scientists.
>
> Especially here in Denmark the debate is very heated, with populist
> politicians on both sides jumping into the fray.

Have you read the report? The denouncements of the report in Reason, The
Economist and TCS have been strong, but I wonder if they have read it. I
have this awful feeling that a lot of the international sources are just
repeating from each other (on both sides), especially since
Danish-reading people are somewhat rare. It might be nice to check what
the report actually says, and if it is as bad as it seems (like using
*Scientific American* as an authoritative scientific source - it is
after all a popular science journal, if a good one).
