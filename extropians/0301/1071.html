I noticed in myself an amount of piont scoring. However most people on this
list such as myself will admit thier wrong. The only problem is when someone
won't admit that. Like holding one to a few "pionts" that evolution doesn't
exsist when someone else has over a thousands "pionts" that it does. This
does bring up another argument. What is sufficient evidence? Some type of
mathematical formula that would take into account human error and the chance
of finding new evidence in opposition. Once a database of all current
scientific data is set up the statistics of this should be quite simple.

The dynamics of back and forth replies is kind of annoying.

I would favor using an instant messager between two people's full
conversation. Then that conversation can be posted as one entry.

Doesn't more opposing viewpionts just speed up the process?
