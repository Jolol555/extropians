The Toronto Transhumanist Association will be conducting its January
administration meeting on Wednesday January 8, 2003 at 6:30 PM. It will be
held in Toronto at 187 King St. E. Suite 200. Members and non-members are
welcome.

AGENDA:
1) Review 2002
2) Set goals for 2003, including event and membership goals
3) Determine the most important positions we need to fill
4) Begin the creation of a calendar for 2003 and assign calendar
maintenance to a volunteer
5) Determine events for January 2003
6) Discuss status of wta-chapterofficers list and next steps
