I'd strongly urge people, especially those in Europe, to
read this NY Times editorial:

"Bad Herr Dye"
William Safire
http://www.nytimes.com/2003/01/23/opinion/23SAFI.html?pagewanted=print

It raised some points for me (as an American) that I was rather
clueless about and also points out some rather interesting
political cracks in a number of dikes.
