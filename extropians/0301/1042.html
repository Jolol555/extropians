Rush Limbaugh relates that the London Telegraph over the
weekend had a story that nuclear development documents
have been found at physicist's home in Iraq. A separate
British newspaper allegedly also has written up the story.

So far as I know, while there was admittedly a tiny
mention in some American newspaper of "documents"
found at the home of an Iraqi physicist, nothing on
this side of the Atlantic in print makes any mention
of "nuclear documents". Is the print media in America
deliberately making little of this story?
