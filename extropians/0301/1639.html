to wear reading glasses low on one's nose (people who
wear reading glasses must read a lot, right?) and then to
peer at people over one's reading glasses as one speaks.

eyebrow slightly raised.

Depending on how well you pull it off, people will think you
are smart, or an ass, or possibly both. :-)
