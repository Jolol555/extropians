Dear Extropes,

My Wavelet Resource pages have been updated. These are a set of
pages (currently four for the main pages, and ten for the tutorial)
which I wrote in 1994 to help teach myself about wavelets.

Curious? Wavelets have probably been used in some aspect
of your present technical world. Take a peek.

Last Date Modified: March 2000.
