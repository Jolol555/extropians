#Extropy Chat Reminder - Jan 25, 2002

On Sat 25 Jan 2002, at 2:00 PM EDT (GMT-5; see the official US clock
at http://www.time.gov/timezone.cgi?Eastern/d/-5/java for the current
EDT time), all interested entities (extropians and other
transhumanists, cryonicists, immortalists, etc.) are invited to
participate in the Extropy Institute chat. (You do not have to be an
ExI member to attend.) Logs of previous chats are available at
http://www.extropy.org/forums/

Extropians chat takes place on ExI's own IRC server, at
irc.extropy.org:6667, channel #extropy . If you don't already have
an IRC client program, you can either download one from
http://www.irchelp.org/ (which also has good introductions for new
users), or use a Java chat applet at http://www.extropy.org/bbs/

Schedule FYI: Chats are held at 2:00 PM EDT on the last Saturday of
each month.

This means next month's will be on February 22.
