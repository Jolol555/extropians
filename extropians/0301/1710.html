Europeans rally behind Bush
January 31 2003
By Peter Fray
London, Karen DeYoung
Washington

A group of eight key European countries yesterday called for a united front
against Saddam Hussein, boosting American diplomatic efforts to secure UN
backing for war and further isolating France and Germany.

The move came as the Bush administration accelerated its diplomatic efforts
to persuade allies to adopt a unified position on Iraq, while emphasising
that the President's decision on whether to launch an attack is only weeks
