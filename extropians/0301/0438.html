Dan writes:
> What do you some of you think of the family in the context of
> transhumanism and Extropianism?
>

I think family is a potent meme. And with scientific validity we
can adopt it, extend it, and validly use it, as part of a pleasant
theme of inclusivity. Especially now that it looks like homo sapiens
arose in one location in Africa rather than in a number of places
separately. Could work very well against the darker memes that
pop up or are invoked from time to time.
