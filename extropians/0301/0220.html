I have made two postings today in which I happened to begin a line with
the word "From". In each case, the version of my message in the ExI BBS
at http://www.extropy.org/bbs/index.php?board=67 is truncated just before
that line. It is perhaps a mercy to readers since both of my messages
were rather long-winded and the shortened versions could be considered
an improvement! But in general the lost portions could be significant and
so I hope that the BBS maintainers will be able to look into this problem.

Hal
