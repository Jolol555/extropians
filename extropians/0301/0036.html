It appears the term has expanded over the years to include
hydrocarbons, methane, and whatever else is seeping from the seafloor
that is not hot.

and countless other places around the world -- you may find globs of
crude oil rolling in the surf. This usually is derived from nearby,
offshore seeps of oil. A "natural" oil spill of sorts.
