Folks, please don't vote in each others voting posts unless you are
addressing an amendment the other person has proposed. I've tried
to set up a system to get opinions on the particular amendments.
Refraining from voting within each others posts will facilitate counting
and clarity help keep the focus on the specific amendments to the
ExI principles proposed in the original thread.

To vote please just strip the subject line of any recent post down to
the orginal thread header, delete all the contents and cast your vote
for or against or make any proposed amendments. If you make
an amendment perhaps add a tag amendment 1 etc in the header.

Thanks,
