I let my ExI membership lapse several months ago due to perceived lack of
direction and results within the organization. The recent restructuring
announcement, while lacking substance, gives me renewed hope that efforts
may improve to realize the potential of such an organization so I've decided
to give it another chance and renew. US$55 per year is not much -- if in
fact it does help advance extropian thinking and *action*.

Unfortunately, the link to the Online Order form at
http://www.extropy.org/membership/index.html seems to be invalid. Not an
encouraging sign.
