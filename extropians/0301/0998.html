> I'm no hawk, but I'm also not a pacifist, and I can't for a minute

Absolutely correct! I think some posters who are now saying they know
exactly what the proper policy toward Iraq should be could learn a little
humility by rereading some of their old posts about Afghanistan written to
this list just one year ago. If I thought the war with Iraq would go half as
well I'd be a super hawk, but will it? I don't know.

politically correct to say so and I'm not saying by itself it justifies

Iraq has oil and Afghanistan does not.
