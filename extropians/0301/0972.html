years ago by Thomas L. Friedman in his book about The Olive Tree and the
Lexus. Modernity is placing a tremendous strain on the Middle Eastern
culture. What seems to be happening is that a culture war is occurring.
They see themselves as a great people and modernity is destroying their
world. A controlling percentage of them are lashing out violently to destroy
the culture that is destroying theirs.
