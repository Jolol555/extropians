    Subject: Re: religion: dalai lama

    I believe the proper address is "Your Holiness". I went to hear him
    speak in 1996 in Bloomington, IN. It was great to see him in person, but
    I didn't get to speak to him personally. krs

    I was in the general area where they were having a
    major hootenanny for him, perhaps it should be called a
    lamananny. We were at North Beach, the City Lights
    bookstore, looking for a definition of the term
    "hipster daddio" as used by Kerouac, et.al. I did
    not find an actual definition, but I did see what I
    believe were several excellent hip examples.

    I don't believe we saw the dalai lama himself however.
    He would be hard to miss, for I believe a lama is
    about halfway between a goat and a camel, or perhaps
    halfway between a giraffe and a yak. We saw nothing
    that looked even vaguely like that, even tho we stood
    about gawking like a couple of prairy chickens, or
    like Ma and Pa Walton taking a vacation in the big city.

    The bookstore was a kick. I feel way hip now, man.

    spike
