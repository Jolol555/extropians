    At 10:31 AM 9/10/03 -0700, Robbie wrote:

    >Can you site examples for us when the wealthy have decided voluntarily to
    >let the lower-and-middle-classes share in their power?

    What do you mean by "power"? Knowledge, Influence, jobs, benefits,
    advantages?

    In sharing position, benefits, and advantage, 2 people come to mind
    immediately:

    Dr. Richard Clark: plastic surgeon: Has voluntarily donated time and
    money to help people with physical complications regain their limbs, face,
    or other body parts.

    Pamela Lifton-Zoline: writer/artist: Has voluntarily donated money to
    help children of low income families get an education through a school she
    established. (Just one of her many contributions to lower income families.)

    Natasha
