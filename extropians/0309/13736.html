    I figure that for most of us, there really
    isn't much practical difference between martial
    law and democracy. In democracy, a criminal
    gets a long trial, then either goes free or goes
    to prison, depending on how much money she has.
    Under martial law, a criminal gets a short trial
    then either goes free or gets shot, depending on
    how much money she has.

    Other than that, the two systems are pretty
    similar. From a business perspective, they
    would be indistinguishable.
