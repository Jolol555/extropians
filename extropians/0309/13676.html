    I know little about law, but my limited understanding
    is that law must be self-consistent, even if wrong.
    My libertarian side says we shouldn't even have borders,
    so there are no illegal aliens. But as it is, giving
    drivers licenses to illegals leads to the following
    oddities: An illegal immigrant gets a license under the
    new law. I want to have a spare in case I get stopped,
    so I too go to the local bureau and produce the exact
    same documentation of identity that my compatriot
    produces (none). Our Detroits accidentally smite
    each other, the local constable appears, we produce
    the licenses. The constable recognizes me as a
    legal US citizen, therefor my alien license is
    illegal, so I go to jail. But the other guy is
    a *real* illegal alien, so his license is legitimate
    and he goes happily along his way. {8-[ spike
