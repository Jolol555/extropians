    > I think there are better ways of teaching scepticism
    > and critical thinking to children than adding noise/spam
    > to message on the internet.

    No way. I taught my son to count like this:

    Daddy, may I have some candy?

    Sure, son, how much would you like?

    Five.

    (I give him three)

    Thanks, dad!

    How many did I give you son?

    Hmmmmmm. Three, hey! Give me more.

    How many more, son?

    etc.

    My son can count/ad/subtract and he's 3 1/2!

    Skepticism is a very very good teacher.

    "What does not kill us makes us stronger."

    Now I just have to teach him that he can trust me SOMETIMES.

    Anyone think I'm a monster?
