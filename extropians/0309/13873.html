    At 12:43 AM 9/11/03 -0700, Robbie wrote:

    >Number 2 killer - Cancer. Number 1 cause of cancer - unknown
    >environmental factors. I personally attribute them to the pollutants
    >required by modern cities to keep the engines greased.

    Robert Weinberg, a notable cancer expert, sez not. (Except in especially
    nasty hot-spots.) The rise is Western cancer incidence is in step with the
    increase in longevity; the longer you live, the more onco prequel events
    occur stochastically. Some of those are associated with modifiable diet
    choices, of course. Meanwhile, we gain improved methods of fixing cancers
    once they show up, and those methods are attributable to modern cities and
    their greasy engines.

    Damien Broderick
