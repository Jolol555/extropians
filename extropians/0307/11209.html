    Inspired by this recent cryptome article about confidential RFID
    technical details being available simply by visiting
    www.autoidcenter.com and searching for 'confidential', ( see
    http://cryptome.org/rfid-docs.htm ), I googled

    'not for public release' 1,370 hits.
    'internal use only' 136,000 hits!!!

    Who knows what fun stuff can be found by searching specific sites with
    phrases such as these?
