    Natasha, what ever you are contemplating, I hope that
    it is nothing excessive, and that you don't allow Harvey
    to egg you on into doing something extreme.

    I don't care what other organizations or activities you
    want to sponsor or engage in, but I deeply care about
    any radical changes to the nature of the Extropians forum,
    and that would be a real shame.

    I would urge others to speak up and their views be known,
    before it is too late---or else risk that what we take for
    granted may be no more.
