    The next ExtroBritannia get-together is scheduled for
    Saturday the 26th of June at 2pm, in London.

    The theme of the meeting is: “Cryonics: meet Alcor UK”. Do you have
    any questions regarding the practice or theory of cryonics? Are
    you thinking about signing up with a cryonics organisation? Or
    are you just curious about it? Come along and ask your questions
    to the experts or just have a chat with other cryonics-oriented people.

    The meeting will take the form of small discussion groups made up of
    4-5 people each so to allow easy flow of information and an informal
    atmospere. Each group will include an Alcor member.

    WHERE & WHEN******************************************

    This time we are trying a new venue for our get-together: the "Founder's Arms"
    on the Bankside (next to the Tate Modern), right on the river.

    See URL for map (NUMBER 3).
    http://www.alpc.co.uk/pcrawl8.htm

    It might be worth pointing out that, unlike previous ExtroBritannia meetings,
    we will not meet at 12 noon for lunch. We will instead meet at 2pm and those
    that want to stay for dinner will be ordering food at the Founder’s Arms at 5
    or 6 pm.

    If it's your first time at an ExtroBritannia meeting look for me
    clearly displaying a copy of Kurzweil's "The Age of Spiritual
    Machines" on the table we'll be sitting at.

    ExtroBritannia homepage:
    http://groups.yahoo.com/group/extrobritannia

    Cheers,
    Fabio
