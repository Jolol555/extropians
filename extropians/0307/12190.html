    fnerd is short for FutureNerd, but also a pun on fnord,
    that word they train you not to see even though it's
    peppered through all the text you read.

    i got back on the list to respond to some questions from
    Robin Hanson, & i think i gave the best answers i have
    for the time being. they're not great; all answers to
    the Fermi paradox are stretches as far as i can see.

    meanwhile it's too easy to get sucked in. and then
    PAMgate happened. holy moley. i just spent a day
    emailing people like a distraught lunatic. ... okay,
    so maybe i am a distr, never mind, the point is i
    need to do other things.

    it's been inspiring and puzzling.

    ya lousy bums,
      -fnerd
