    >
    > On Wed, 16 Jul 2003, Spike wrote:
    >
    > > Cryonics has had a few setbacks, and it is
    > > becoming clearer all the time that many of our
    > > biotech dreams are going to be fouled up by
    > > several parties, the Greens, the anti-globalists,

    >
    > Spike, don't count biotech down and out yet

    Actually, I don't see anything that Spike wrote as counting biotech down
    and out. I think he made a realistic assessment of the current
    situation. Pointing out the reality of current and future obstacles is
    a future-enhancing tactic that will help us achieve our goals. I
    consider Spike's and similar comments as working toward the future, not
    against it. The first step to solving a problem is admitting there is a
    problem. Those who won't admit there is a problem and only focus on the
    positive will delay the future more than those who keep harping on the
    negatives.
