    One of the things I like about life is that it always brings
    up strange things of interest.

    Today we have "Death by Chocolate" (and the means for achieving
    such).

    I would note that the product to result in the death of rodents
    using "essence of chocolate" are on sale now (at least in the UK).

    So I have to revise my previous insensitive proposal with
    respect to segments of humanity that one might consider
    eliminating to facilitate its survival -- obviously the
    only humane way of dealing with this problem is to
    bury them (figuratively) in chocolate. The problem
    takes care of itself and does so with a great deal
    of enjoyment on the part of the individuals involved.

    R.
