    generation want to revive a whole bunch of old people from the past?

    unwanted 350,000 frozen embryos - most will be destroyed. I think unless
    someone is either *very* rich or very lucky most frozen people will find
    themselves in the same position as frozen embryos - abandoned, unwanted
    and eventually destroyed.
