    don't need to be perfect, it's Star Wars defense that needs to be perfect or
    it's not worth building. And it's obvious that if the Laser could cause a

    of just a degree or two it could destroy it, but a similar increase in the
    temperature of the solid fuel in a ICBM would have no effect.
