    This list is an open forum. What people state on this list is personal
    opinion unless he or she states that it is representative of his or her
    business, organization, club or other affiliates.

    My personal views, which I post from time to time, are my views unless and
    until I state that they are reflective of Extropy Institute. In such
    cases, I try to make this very clear.

    Please do not misconstrue a personal post of any member of ExI, or Board
    member, Advisory Council, Executive Advisory Team, and officers to be other
    than their personal voice unless they state that they are speaking on
    behalf of ExI.

    Thank you,

    Natasha
