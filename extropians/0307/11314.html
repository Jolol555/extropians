    This is an important topic for extropians. When and
    how do downloads, copies of downloads or other
    entities with real, partial, or potential sentience
    acquire rights? What rights do their creators have re
    them?

    The best model in real life that I've seen is that of
    the ancient cultures in which the family introduced a
    young adult to the bigger society and stood to make
    good any debts or damages that resulted. A modern
    version of this based on a personal trust and a
    general social contract (yes, I'm still on that kick)
    can be easilly envisioned.
