    It has been suggested that I make some things clear.

    I wish to state very clearly and in plain language that any
    thoughts I may have posted recently on the Extropians email list
    (since July 15, 2003) are NOT related to any conversations or
    discussions of the Extropy Institute Board of Directors or the
    officers of the Extropy Institute. They *are* not the opinions,
    in so far as I am aware, of any other members of the board or
    officers of the Extropy Institute.

    The opinions I have expressed are my own and motivated by my
    own desire to determine the paths by which the survival
    of humanity might be promoted.
