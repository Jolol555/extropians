    On Monday 14 July 2003 22:38, Steve Davies wrote:

    > Am I just being simple but isn't a simple solution to charge for
    > sending emails? Quite a modest charge would make spam unprofitable

    Who would collect the money? Where would it go?
    How would you collect if someone hijacked your
    email address and sent out a billion spams? How
    would we collect from those Nigerian bankers, etc.

    Actually I see spam as causing the rise of a
    major driver toward the singularity: it creates
    the need for ever more sophisticated software
    to filter the stuff. Recall that mosquito control

    environmental-modifcation technology. Spam is
    the mosquito of the e-world.
