    I am reading Transcension, and it is GOOD!
    In summertime I go down to the pool with a bag full of books. Damien's book
    is better than all books currently in my bag, too bad I have it in e-book
    version: I have to read it on the PC, and despite my enthousiasm for e-books
    I must acknowledge that reading a paper book is much, much more confortable.
    I am now reading Transcension because the weather is no good.
    I have the same book in Mobipocket format on my Nokia 7650 phone, but the
    screen is really too small for prolonged reading. I do not currently own a
    Tungsten or a Ipaq, but even if I had one I believe I would not take it down
    to the pool.
    I look forward to some really comfortable and convenient way of reading
    e-books. I hope these new flexible paper-like displays come to the shelves
    soon.
