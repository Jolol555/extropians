    Dear Natasha,

    I am so sorry to hear of your fight with cancer. I have seen other people
    battle with it also so I have some idea, as much as is possible without going
    through it myself, of how hard and scary that is. Dealing with cancer truly
    is "major".

    But the fact that cancer and other vagaries of our current limits can strike
    any of us at any time is a very very strong reason that no part of our
    efforts should be so dependent on one person where at all possible.

    I don't feel too good about you stating that you are not going to state your
    plans at this time. If more of us are not in the loop then how can we
    contribute? This seems to be not taking advantage of potential resources and
    possibly holding on much too tightly and asking people to remain in the dark
    with just the assurance that things are being done. That doesn't feel very
    reasonable.

    It is quite understandable if a lot else is on your mind and heart heavily at
    this time. But please delegate and share where you can.

    - samantha
