    Science Daily is reporting:

    "Revolutionary Tungsten Photonic Crystal Could provide More Power
    for Electronic Devices"
    http://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2003/07/030709064316.htm

    Looks like one can use photonic crystals to channel heat energy
    into specific photon emission energies in such a way that one
    gets heat-to-photon conversion efficiencies significantly better
    than allowed by Planck's Law of Blackbody Cavity Radiation.

    Interesting applications for various energy conversion
    strategies are discussed.
