    Another book to glance at:
    Natural-Born Cyborgs
    Minds, Technologies and the Future of Human Intelligence
    by Andy Clark
    0-19-514866-5 ISBN
    2003

    cognitive hybridization

    to better appreciate what we already are: creatures whose minds are special
    precisely because they are tailor-made for multiple mergers and coalitions

    what the human brain is best at is learning to be a team player in a
    problem-solving field populated by an incredible variety of nonbiological
    props, scaffoldings, instruments and resources

    the process of deliberately building better worlds to think in

    motor-loop dependent adaptations

    Ramachandran's principle:
    mechanisms of perception are mainly involved in extracting statistical
    correlations from the world to create a model that is temporarily useful

    etc.
