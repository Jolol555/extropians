    Sure but the action is on the part of each one
    of us, not anything on the part of ExI or a
    list moderator. I am not proposing we moderate
    the list at all, but rather suggesting that when
    flamed, take the high road, don't flame back,
    let it slide like water off a duck's back.

    > What exact changes do you propose to make that will cut down on
    > rancor? If no changes are made, none will occur. Harvey Newstrom...

    The change is a voluntary non-reply policy to those posts
    that make one want to flame. That was my interpretation
    of that thing I heard about where a moderator asked
    some folks not to reply to this or that. I didn't
    think of it as any kind of censorship, but rather
    like a friendly bartender asking a couple of
    quarrelling drunks to cool it down a bit. It
    really won't hurt us to just ignore those posts
    that are mean-spirited or inflammatory.

    under). Instead of flame wars we can settle it
