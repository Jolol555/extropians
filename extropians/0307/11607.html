    > Robin Hanson wrote:
    >
    >
    >
    >> I just came across this thought-provoking future speculation by
    >> Geoffrey Miller, author of "The Mating Mind":
    >> http://psych.unm.edu/faculty/moral_vision.htm
    >> There's something broken about this idea, but its hard to say
    >> exactly what it is.
    >>
    ### To put it briefly - Miller believes that successful companies exist
    because investors invest in them. A more correct vision, IMO, is that
    companies exist primarily because customers demand their services and are
    willing to pay. Investors come later.

    You can't really change this system by fiddling with investment alone.
