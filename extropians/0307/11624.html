    Kevin Freels wrote,
    > One of the risks of putting an idea into the open is that you
    > are subjecting your opinion to ridicule. If I put an idea out
    > there and someone shoots it down or makes me look like a
    > fool, I have the option to ignore them just as much as they
    > have the option to ignore my statements. Someone has to take
    > the higher ground.

    This is not what we are talking about. We are talking about personal
    attacks, not criticism of ideas. Criticism of ideas is not only inevitable
    on this list, but it is encouraged!

    > So can we PLEASE put this "flame wars" topic behind us? I
    > know I am fairly new to this board, but it just seems to be a
    > huge waste of time!

    Yes, we most certainly can put these flame war behind us. We have put
    flame-wars behind us every few months for the last ten years. Eventually,
    many of the old-timers get tired of putting flame-wars behind us.
    Eventually, we start wondering why the same problems keep occurring over and
    over. Instead of ignoring problems, eventually we might actually try to fix
    some of them.
