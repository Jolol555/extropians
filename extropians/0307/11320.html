    I read something today on the site of the Cryonics Institute that makes a lot of sense: if you are successfully revived after cryonics suspension, this means that some technologies (nanotech, ...) have really taken off in the meantime (otherwise you cannot be revived).
    So the smartest thing for cryonicists is to invest in these technologies, of course not on single company stocks (you never know) but on a managed industry-specific fund.
    Does anyone know of such funds?
