    The Internet never forgets! I wrote this spoof of Star Trek: The Next
    Generation back in 1989! Boy was I surprised to run across it on the Web!
    Someone must have grabbed it from the Star Trek discussion group and saved
    it all these years! Who knows what other embarrassing stuff is floating out
    in the ether that I have forgotten about?

    This is a ST:TNG spoof using the idea from "The Five Doctors" episode of Dr.
    Who. That episode had five of the first Doctors get together in a single
    episode. In this spoof, I brought back seven of the Enterprise doctors
    together for a single episode. This spoof was written while Dr. Pulaski was
    on the Enterprise and before we knew that Dr. Crusher was coming back.
    (Although I was able to guess that this would occur because Diana Muldaur,
    the actor playing Dr. Pulaski, continued to be credited as a "guest star".)
