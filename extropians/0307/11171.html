    Designing extropian foods is fun job.

    The grunt work to sell it is the less desirable work.
    Along the way I have captured a list of visitors to my site that
    includes
    the IP address and hostname.
    Can these be translated into a link to email the original sender?

    ex. a friend of mine has a real name of Durand Fritz and I know his
    email address
    to be durand.fritz@sasktel.net
    From my capture program he shows up as: IP address-
    142.165.98.117
                                   hostname-
    wybnsk03d010101117.sk.sympatico.ca

    This should be an easy question to at least one of you,

    Morris Johnson
    http://www.angelfire.com/on4/extropian-lifespan/

    PS- I use a little freebie visitor watcher called statcounter
    What I would ideally like is to be able to create a list of all my
    visitors email addresses and then be able to link it to the info I get
    now which tells me for example that they used specific search words at
    google to find me. So far I don't have an unmangeable rate of
    visitations so a simple little program will do.
