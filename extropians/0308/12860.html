    At 09:02 AM 8/11/03 +0200, Amara wrote:

    >The CGD index is a crude first stab at measuring the rich world's
    >help for the poor and the authors themselves admit that it has many
    >flaws, but it is a good first step that emphasizes that aid alone is
    >a misleading guide; trade and peace matter at least as much.

    Thanks Amara.

    Thinking from countries to organizations, I've been wondering the past
    months how ExI can produce philanthropic aid to underdeveloped
    regions. While we are not rich by monetary measures, we are rich in ideas
    about the future and do have first hand experience in dealing with the
    challenges of change.

    One way that I think we can do this is by helping the learning centers and
    schools in poor areas. Once our critical thinking online course is
    developed and other reading sources, we could donate thousands of easy to
    print books to the schools.
