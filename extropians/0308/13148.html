    Inspired by the above notice board at an Indian restaurant, I feel that
    a similar notice board at the entrance to Extropians could be designed.

    A database of prohibitions could be constructed, with the facility for
    members to vote on their favorite prohibitions.

    Votes for no prohibitions at all would be ignored, of course.

    Footnotes could be added to explain the more technical prohibitions, as
    'ad hominem' still seems to cause some comprehension problems.

    As problems arise, we can add more prohibitions to the list.
    An unlimited future of prohibitions opens before us! ;)
