    At 12:18 AM 8/4/03 +0200, you wrote:
    >Yes, there are plenty of antecedents to transhumanism. It would be a
    >mistake to assume that it all started with Max More or FM 2020. The FAQ
    >contains a partial list of forerunners
    >(http://www.transhumanism.org/resources/faq.html#View). It mentions
    >Condorcet but not Fourier.

    Precisely. Each philosophy or cultural movement has antecedents. (By the
    way, it's 2030 :-).) Ideas change according to the times and we rework
    already established ideas. We take ideas and add to them and subtract from
    them to fit the specific needs of the time in which we live.

    Natasha
