    A very well-done site in Denmark about using "soft kill option" methods
    to create optimal mechanical structures:

    http://www.topopt.dtu.dk/

    They apply it to MEMS, satelites, phonon bandgap materials, materials with
    negative Poisson coefficient, bridges and everything else - and have an
    interactive applet where you can try it out for yourself!

    The basic idea is to take a domain subjected to forces, calculate where it
    needs reinforcing and where it is lightly loaded, and redistribute
    material to where it is needed. The results often have a nice organic
    style - I like the branching pylons that often result.
