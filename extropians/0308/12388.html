    UK's main news and sports network, "BBC Radio 5Live" just completed a
    snapshot interview with Nick Bostrom and myself for a news program on the
    future human.

    Interview focus was on man becoming 1/2 cyborg, with a reference to
    "Terminator 3" movie, and comparing it to "Primo" transhumanist design concept.

    I'll write about it and other design issues for the upcoming ExI Exponent
    Newsletter.
