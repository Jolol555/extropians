    The word is out that long time poster Mike Lorrey has
    run afoul the list authorities for some unspecified
    reason. This is pretty hard to believe. I don't know
    exactly what list rules he is alleged to have broken,
    but in my opinion this really bodes ill for the future.

    Natasha writes

    > Extropes -
    >
    > We are in the process of changing our "extropian" list. I'll be making an
    > announcement about the new lists very soon. Up until our new lists are
    > generated, please help to make this "extropians" list a smart and pleasant
    > place to discuss, debate and challege ideas.
    >
    > Regarding the "extropians" list, we have now tightened our belt and are
    > taking subscribers off the list who have abused list rules in the past and
    > those who continue to disrespect the ideas and values of ExI, "extropians"
    > and list members.

    So who else has been chopped? I really found Mike Lorrey's
    posts to be intelligent and extremely informative. He will
    be sorely missed.

    > We have asked over and over again to please post with a
    > sense of respect to others and we have always welcomed
    > criticism, when done productively. Not all criticism is
    > productive. Some criticism is harmful to our community.

    The list rules used to say "engage in personal attacks"
    but are now to be "harmful to our community"? For one,
    I don't like the sound of that. (I hope that this remark
    itself by me is not taken as "harmful to the community".)

    > I have received so many complaints from list members
    > about out of hand postings that I have had default to
    > either asking people to take a break from the list or
    > remove them from the list.

    It's remarkable that there haven't been any on-line
    complaints that I've been aware of. I wonder how
    recent are these complaints that you refer to. I for
    one have not found the list to be problematical.
    There exists courtesy here absent from most such
    forums.

    Lee
