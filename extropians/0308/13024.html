    SF author Engdahl doesn't think much of living forever, but her logic is DOA. Read her essay and reply in discussion forum at

    She may never qualify as an immortalist but this is a person who is intelligent, strongly supports technological progress in its myriad forms, and can obviously write at a level most of us only dream of. I am grateful for the link provided because I got the chance to become aware of her work.
