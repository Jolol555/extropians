    At 10:34 PM 8/5/03 +0200, Anders wrote:

    >I actually have them in my current sf setting. "Nanoists" are people
    >with nanoenhancements (ranging from a bit of respirocytes or an onboard
    >wearable to Freitas' artificial circulatory system and laminated diamond
    >skeletons). Some of them (who have the right product activation keys ;-)
    >can transfer a "seed" to normal people, giving them parts of their own
    >powers (usually a complete package). This way you get clades of
    >nanoists.

    A version is in Ken MacLeod's richly detailed third volume of his Engines
    of Light trilogy, ENGINE CITY. The alien Multipliers are sort of fractal
    Moravecian Moties whose `children', all the way down, can infest your
    bloodstream...
