    At 12:15 AM 8/6/03 -0400, Eliezer wrote:

    >>>>(Event) The Accelerating Change Conference

    >Er, why is the term "Singularitarian" being used in connection with this
    >conference on the supposed deterministic acceleration of technological
    >change, a claim which is neither made by, nor relevant to, Singularitarians?

    That's a question to be taken up with John Smart, I should imagine, but I
    don't think he's on this list any longer. Then again, maybe he doesn't use
    the term `Singularitarian' anyway, although last time I talked to him the
    Singularity prospect was his major concern.

    The far more interesting question, I'd have thought, is why a
    crypto-creationist like Dembski wishes to associate himself with a
    conference dealing with the Singularity, and why the organizers would
    welcome him.

    Damien Broderick
