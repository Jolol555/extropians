    Barbara,
           You might look at Agri-Forestry and Sustainable Farming. Some
    scientists make much of agriculture having progressed by moving from one level of
    expertise to another. One example is how my Indian ancestors had both the wild
    apple and the wild grape available for domestication but never did anything
    with either. The agricultural scientists say the American Indians had not
    achieved the level of expertise required to handle moving up to the level that
    growing apples and grapes requires.
           Today it appears that Agri-Forestry and Sustainable Farming are the
    next level to be achieved.
