    The next ExtroBritannia meeting is scheduled for Saturday the 6th of September
    2003, in Cambridge, UK.

    The theme of the get-together is "The engineering approach to
    life-extension". Life-extension researcher and promoter Aubrey de Grey will
    give a presentation on the subject, followed by an informal Q&A/discussion
    session.

    THE PLAN

    We will meet at 12,00 noon at the Salisbury Arms, 76 Tenison Road, Cambridge
    CB4 1PA, not far from the train station, and will order lunch at about
    12,30. If this is your first time at an ExtroBritannia meeting, look out for a
    copy of Ray Kurzweil's "The Age of Spiritual Machines" being displayed at the
    table we'll be sitting at.

    If you cannot make it for lunch, you can still reach us at the Salisbury
    Arms until about 2pm, when we'll decamp for the university’s department of
    genetics, for the presentation.
