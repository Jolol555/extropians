    On Wed, 27 Aug 2003, Eliezer S. Yudkowsky (commenting on Robbie's
    comments) wrote:

    > So just for the record, you typed in:
    >
    > ( planck's constant * c / pi ^ 2 * G ) + 2 cups of sugar
    >
    > WHAT ARE YOU TRYING TO DO, YOU LUNATIC? BREAK GOOGLE?

    It is *so* interesting to see what Eliezer gets upset about...
    (As normally he is rather calm and composed...)

    Of course one of the points of the new calculator feature
    is to see what "breaks" it. How else are the programmers
    who developed it going to improve it?

    I've tried to get in touch with the manager of the project
    so I could give them some high praise, but google appears
    to have their phone "firewalls" set too high. I've done a
    couple of *really* cool programming tasks in my life -- it would
    be nice to let the people responsible for the calculator
    know that what they have done is *really* cool.

    Hmmm... Perhaps I could take out a google ad to compliment
    the google programmers. Something to think about.

    R.
