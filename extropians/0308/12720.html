    <<### This is the old communist attitude - envious hatred for businessmen,
    usually coupled to the populist dream - high wages, little work, low prices
    and well-stocked stores. But, the world is a bit more complex than images of
    evil bosses scheming to take the worker's pay would suggest.>>

    Actually, this is the new hatred of a pampered class-driven bunch of people
    who, appear to have NO entrpenuerial accumen. They achieved their places in
    corporations, by knowing the right people. Its not as if businessmen and women
    who have started suuccessful companies, themselves have achieved their place on
    the boards of directors, and are now guiding companies. Rather, they are
    suck-ups, who, with no sense of responsibility to their stockholders, let alone,
    society at large, are guiding their corporations, right onto the iceberg.

    <<The protectionist demands cheap imported goods, the right to export abroad,
    initiation of force to suppress competition, and total indifference to the
    suffering of others. All moral and intellectual failings are attributed to
    the "others" - the colored foreigners, and the rich CEO>>

    I do not want cheap imported goods, I do seek the right to export abroad, I
    wish to increase competition, but not at the expense of the entire republic,
    and I do object to the suffering of American workers at the hands of fortune 500
    punks who would export all jobs, to line their own personal pockets. I
    personally like many of the H-1B's and L2's that I work with, and do not blame them
    personally for scumbag fortune 500 pricks, who would line their own pockets,
    no matter what it does to the country.
