    Mitch,
           You gave us a link to IMB's last three Annual Statements. Isn't that
    Income Statement revealing?
           The big decline in Operating Profits seems to be due to the President
    and the Sales/Marketing departments not delivering the sales that are their
    responsibility to deliver. I don't see any numbers to indicate to me that IMB
    ran into problems because EDP was deficient.
           However there is one thing we have to remember as operating people.
    Most times top management is sales oriented and relatively ignorant of
    operations in all its aspects. It is an to the best of my knowledge a competition
    between those of us in operations and our competitors in other companies. We
    have to constantly be concerned with delivering better and cheaper work to our
    employers.
           I am not saying this to point a finger at anyone. Instead I am only
    reminding. Hopefully as GT win the boardroom wars we will get managers that
    understand the need to communicate realistically to the operating departments.
    This communication is needed so the operators can make realistic evaluations
    of the job in front of them.
    Ron h.
