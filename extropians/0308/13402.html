    This note is about life from another 'world', still on Earth.

    http://www.educeth.ch/stromboli/perm/lengai/index-en.html

    "Introduction

    Ol Doinyo Lengai is an extraordinary stratovolcano in northern
    Tanzania (2.751°S, 35.902°E, 2890m, map) producing
    natro-carbonatitic lava, very rich in sodium and carbonates. It is
    highly fluid and emerges at 500 to 590°C only. It glows dull red in
    the dark and is the «coolest» lava of any known volcano. Major ash
    eruptions were observed in 1917, 1926, 1940 and 1966-67 (image).
    Since April 1983 lava has been extruded and gradually has been
    filling up the summit crater[...]"
