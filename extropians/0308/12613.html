    Taking a look at a couple of Max's short pieces, I
    note his mention of the Gramein Bank - the micro-loans
    program - as an example of how to be socially
    conscious and profitable, or something like that - in
    his BLOG on "Why Marx Was Even More Wrong Than You
    Thought."

    I'm surprised on that note that Mondragon was not
    mentioned. The Mondragon Cooperative is a
    world-class, outstanding business which breaks all the
    prevalent business models.

    http://www.mondragon.mcc.es/

    Of course, there are reasons:

    The "Progressive (in a pig's eye! as progressive as
    "Pro-Life" is...) Left" won't talk about Mondragon
    because they aren't a socialist model at all. In fact
    they came out of the Basque Anarcho-Syndicalist school
    if anything, and they have never taken a penny of
    state money or assitance that I know of and have never
    hocked their future to any state bank, either. So
    there, stupid socialists! Look at all the utter
    failures of socialism. Try to find a success, just
    one, anywhere???

    But Mondragon bootstrapped itself into being an
    $8billion business, with its OWN bank, its OWN schools
    and university, its OWN social security and health
    coverage, ALL paid for voluntarily by its
    worker/owners. Ie., Mondragon is actually DOING all
    the great social things that the socialists have been
    holding up as an ideal. Workers runing their own
    businesses, themselves - Mondragon (NOT red China, or
    Cuba, or the former Evil Empire). What guns and
    terror couldn't accomplish, hard-headed practical
    business sense did with flourish and taste.

    And the Right (what, another total misnomer??? When
    were they ever right about anything?) hates Mondragon
    because Mondragon has succeeded spectacularly as a
    profit-making business WITHOUT the top-down leadership
    of megabuck-salaried brave captains of industry
    leading the stupid workers. NO. The WORKERS RUN
    MONDRAGON! And they have ALL the benefits that the
    socialist left wants, plus job security, plus high
    wages, and they made a $250Million net profit last
    year during a world-wide economic downturn, while
    doing major worldwide community outreach, such as with
    the Doctors Without Borders.

    so. there.

    Check it out. ;)
