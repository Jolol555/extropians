    Lee,
           I am not going to address any specific issue you raised. I don't have
    all the answers and I am just as mystified as most on this thread.
           I believe my discussion of this thread began with the two predictions
    of first the Singularity and second a new Renaissance.
           In particular both of those seem to be powered by the mobilization of
    under utilized GT (top 5% of the Bell Curve for Intelligence) I gave some of
    my sources for believing that.
           I see the general discussion we have had as beneficial for this
    reason. There is a great deal of evidence that the GT are increasingly being
    recruited to operate and manage the laboratories, workshops and boardrooms of our
    high tech economy. Put in simple terms those GT are us.
           Further, there is some observations that the GT are having an
    increasing number of boardroom wars as they wrest control of corporations from the old
    money. I can't document where I read that.
           But increasingly it is the class of people you see around you at this
    site that are making the leadership decisions in our society. Look at Bill
    Clinton (include the group of associates he has) and tell me what he is but an
    example of a GT that found a way to accumulate power. I am not taking sides on
    his philosophy just pointing out that he is a highly intelligent man that
    gained power because others needed someone like him -- so he took over. Arnold
    S. seems to be another GT gaining power and wealth. Some will think he is a
    simple muscle head but I say reserve your opinion until you dig out his academic
    record. On a lesser plane the same is happening all around us. As far back
    as the 60s I was working for inherited money that was trying to operate a
    small manufacturing business without the intelligence to do so. Eventually they
    were forced out of the business world.
           Returning to the world economy my guess is that we are going into at
    least a Renaissance and yes there will be turmoil. Jobs and markets will be
    unsettled for a long time to come. But it looks like the GT are going to
    replace a lot of the old crowd just as the merchants and scholars replaced the
    nobility in the last Renaissance.
           Then there is the question of the Singularity and the effect of AI --
    but my head hurts thinking about it.
    Ron h.
