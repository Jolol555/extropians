    Friends, we gather here today to mourn the e-passing of one Mike
    Lorrey, or as he was known by his neighbors, "Gun-Nut Mike."

    Mike was a person who was able to blend the seemingly disparate worlds
    of the gun-toting New England hillbilly mechanic with the rarefied
    hi-tech ouerve of the computer programmer, and the protectionist blue-
    collar, kitchen-table economist with the Extropian globalist-
    freemarketeerist.

    Although we here will no longer be able to grin at his fiery email
    antics here on this list, we know that somewhere out in "meatspace"
    Gun-Nut Mike is flipping off a government vehicle on the backroads of
    New Hampshire ("damn revenooers!"), or dashing off a vitriolic
    editorial letter to the local Thrifty Nickel newspaper.

    May his electrons rest in peace.....

    -------------
    -Randy
