    Brendan Coffey wrote:
    > I'm not typically one for breathless technofetishism, but blessed be
    > the killfile. It's a monstrously powerful and wonderful tool.

    I'd like to point out an alternate strategy that has worked well for me:
    I filter the list, but rather than "kill" posts from characteristically low
    quality posters, I "promote" posts from characteristically high quality
    posters to an email folder separate from the one that collects all the
    Extropy, SL4, WTA, Fabric of Reality, etc. messages that I group together.
    That way I see many of the most interesting posts in my priority reading,
    and if I have the time or interest I browse through the rest. You never
    know when a person you would normally ignore is gonna go and say something
    interesting.
    - Jef
