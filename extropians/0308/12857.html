    Ladies & Gentlemen,
           It is with a lot of regret that I resign from this list. I did not at
    first understand that Mike Lorry had been expelled -- I first misunderstood
    and thought the discussion was over a proposed expulsion. I had faith that
    calmer heads would see the folly of expelling Mike. Apparently my faith was
    misplaced. In any case I do not wish to belong to a list that considers Mike
    unacceptable.
           For the rest of you. Sometimes our relationships were a little
    tumultuous but they were never dull. I learned a lot from you and will always have
    a warm place in my heart for you. You struck me as very intelligent, learned
    and above all humane to one another.
    Ron Harrison
