    Various life extension lists (and people here) are coining some nice
    neologisms associated with bioethics, Leon Kass and Francis Fukuyama. Since
    I'm tired and in a slightly playful mood, I thought I'd share a couple of my
    favorites and see what the wordsmiths here can come up the further amuse us
    all:

    1) The "FukuKass" mindset (nice pun on the way we feel about Kass :)

    2) "the Leon Kass 'biodeathics' commission" <--- I wish I'd coined this one

    3) "Bush's Bio-Nonethics Council"

    4) "Take your tax cut and die" <--- a grand summary of current Republican
    policy

    Words are our weapons...
