    It is a statistical fact that a medically immortal human being has an
    average life expectancy of a little over 4000 years due to accidental
    death involving massive brain damage. This is assuming such an
    individual would not engage in increasing amounts and varieties of high
    risk activities.

    If you never are able to upload or otherwise store the contents of your
    mind in a more durable substrate, then even an immortal has to die of
    something. While I don't think it likely that you could go 4kyears
    without having uploading technology (unless you get marooned on a
    planet without any technological civilization), this is a real possibility.
