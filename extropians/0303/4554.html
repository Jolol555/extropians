    > (Artillo5@cs.com <Artillo5@cs.com>):
    >
    > My question for my fellow Extropians is this:
    > Is war for any reason something an Extropian should be espousing????

    What a silly question: of course we should. No rational person
    can deny that violence is sometimes necessary, especially in defense
    of life and liberty. And no Extropian would ever foreclose the
    technological options open for its use based on some emotional
    discomfort or dogmatic belief that war is always wrong.

    Of course, that still doesn't tell us much about whether to
    advocate it in /this/ situation, where any claims of "defense"
    are at best a stretch. But the idea that one should simply
    never go to war is not one worth taking seriously.
