    OK I looked over the new archive tool, which is
    about the coolest thing Ive seen in months.
    Evidently the karma rating is tied to the number
    of times people go into the archives and read
    one's past posts, and how many replies that post
    gets. If one often posts on arcane topics, even
    if highly extropic, one's karma drops.

    A good coder jockey could easily write a script
    which would wander thru the archives looking
    for one's own posts, and simply open them.
    Such a karma-producing gawkbot would drag one
    out of the red, should one find oneself deep
    in a whuffie hole.

    And of course Spike and the Gawkbots would make
    a great name for a rock band, as would Whuffie
    Hole Surfers.
