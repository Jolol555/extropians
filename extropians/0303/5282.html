    Lee,

    the arguments between us fail at the point where you seem to have a hard
    time to imagine the implications of a new situation (for example the
    possible impacts of Patriot Act 2). Your apparent lack of imagination seems
    to be the cause that you do not feel any emotional reaction when watching
    the war pictures or hearing the news of casualties and war crimes on
    *both* sides.

    If you cannot understand that I am against the US/UK troops *and* against
    Saddam Hussein at the same time you will probably have a hard time to
    understand my emotional rage when I see this poor figure who happens to be
    your President, this false smile and his phony religious thunder he uses to
    recruit new cannon fodder - last night --- afternoon, US time - with the
    vets on the stage. That made me wanna puke.

    As I have already explained before: the low taste here on the list is
    produced by those who cold-bloodedly accept the cruelties any war brings
    along and who calculate on the deaths of the soldiers of their own party and
    don't even feel sorry for that. THIS is truly low taste and really
    disgusting, not my absolutely harmless joke on lubricating the US war
    machine with the puke of war protesters. You may find my innocent 4letter
    word tasteless, that's fine with me, but what can be more tasteless than
    this current war on Iraq?

    "Puke puke puke puke - dirty dirty dirty dirty" *jumps like a Monty Python
    actor to show his despair and powerlessness*

    For future debates: I am just a worthless jester who plays the abstract and
    the visceral card with the same intensity. And I am so glad to be able to be
    a little unstable from time to time instead of being such a tough guy you
    appear to be. The other day you labeled those who are against the war as
    *Traitors* now that the war had begun. So please count me in as one. I'd
    love to be a traitor if you define it this way.

    humania
