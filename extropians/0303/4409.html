        This is the 83'rd update of my fly longevity experiments. Average
    temperature was 20.5 C during this run. Here I continue testing beverages
    from the local Chinatown.
        The maximum longevity of the control bottles was very good this time.
    At 88 days, this is only a little short of equaling the record 93 days for
    control maximum longevity, which was obtained in run #39. The low
    temperature for this run, plus the use of a relatively new breeding bottle
    may account the present good results.
        At 106 days, the maximum longevity of flies fed full strength white
    gourd juice was also only a little short of the previous record of 118
    days held by freeze concentrated Elderberry Nectar in run #36. This also
    compared well with other juices in the present run, with only starfruit
    juice showing nearly comparible longevity.
