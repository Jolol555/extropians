    For the benefit of those list members who, whatever their opinions on the
    subject, prefer to keep up with breaking news on Iraq through a medium
    *other than* the Extropians list, i.e. Google News, please be careful to
    use the IRAQ keyword in your subject lines! Voluntary compliance with
    this measure will prevent open conflict from breaking out on the
    Extropians list. Remember that no matter how many posts are labeled
    correctly, we see only the posts that slip through. So please label ALL
    repeat ALL posts on Iraq with the appropriate subject line.
