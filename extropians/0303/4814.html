    John Spencer, one of our members of ExI's Executive Advisory Team will be
    hosting a Space Tourism Society meeting:

    Space Tourism Society - Annual Meeting - April 26, 2003

    The Space Tourism Society (STS) will have its sixth annual meeting on April
    26, 2003 at Santa Monica College. 1900 W. Pico Blvd. Santa Monica, CA 90405
    Tele: 310-434-4000. www.smc.edu.

    9:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. in room 140, Life & Physical Science Bldg., the same
    room as last years annual meeting. Table top exhibits in the lobby.

    Note: there will be no STS award show this year. The next one is in 2005.

    This meeting is free and open to all STS members, associates, the public, and
    media.

    We will celebrate the second anniversary of Dennis Tito's and first
    anniversary of Mark Shuttleworth's pioneering private citizen space flights.

    Our program will also feature:

    Who will be flying next?
    Updates from space and future groups
    Update on the Columbia investigation
    STS Special Programs updates
    STS Chapter Japan introduction/plans
    Presentations of Special Awards
    Society business and plans for 2003 and 2004
    2004 SpaceCruise to Alaska plans
    Special announcement Space Place and Space Campat the Queen Mary
    Open discussion
    The Space Experience by those who have gone

    Afternoon brainstorming and planning session for April 2004 International
    Design, Financing, and Marketing Symposium dedicated towards building the
    Space Tourism movement and industry.

    There will be a no-host dinner Friday night and Saturday night at a
    restaurant near the College.

    Please e-mail an RSVP and you will receive a detailed agenda and meeting
    package. Please call/e-mail with ideas and suggestions for the meeting and
    society.
