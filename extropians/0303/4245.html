    In a message dated 3/12/2003 4:55:21 PM Central Standard Time,
    neptune@mars.superlink.net writes: Is patriotism an Extropic value? Related
    to this is nationalism an Extropic value?

    Dan,
           The American Founding Fathers spoke often of Enlightened Self
    Interest. I think an enlightened patriotism or nationalism is a positive
    value. It is not the only value. It is not the supreme value IMHO.
           In the comment that we should love our neighbor as ourselves it is
    implied that we should love ourselves. Similarly, if I understand the Sufi
    the first thing we have to do is to understand ourselves. If we do that, and
    only then, we can understand others. That in turn leads to our understanding
    the world. I think the same logic applies to love.
           In the system I have just set up loving our country is just one of the
    steps going from a proper love of self to a love of all mankind and then to
    all life.
    Ron h.
