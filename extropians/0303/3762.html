    I think that Video is a much better medium than Writing. It will have huge
    impacts on the general public when anyone can make and distribute them. If a
    book could be completely changed, without leaving anything out, into video
    it would be a much better source. Our minds will change and adventually
    learn to accept information in video form as well as we do in writin form.

    The alphabet is Primitive. Only 26 letters for english. It's time to move
    on.

    What is a faster way is there than video besides direct download? Lets be
    the most transhuman and use video when it becomes available.
    I encourage all authors to make dvd's equivalents to thier books when it
    becomes affordable. I hope that email lists and it's participants upgrade
    for video compatibilities.

    Why are we spending so much time predicting the far future. This is the time
    in which progress will be made. I think the time for transhuman ideas to
    gain ground is now. We should sway opinion before technology clashes with
    ideology. I believe the next step is making videos that ordinary people will
    watch and learn. Especially if we can provide them at the minimum costs or
    even free. Maybe even get goverment grants.

    How much money will it take to put a dvd in every adults hands. How much
    money for just the scientific community. It's almost time for any individual
    to be able to get into the propaganda game.
