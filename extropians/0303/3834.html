    Someone asked me yesterday, (I was at a party) 'What is an extropian's
    idea of hell; as in the worst possible case scenario at singularity?'
    There followed several suggestions, but the cruncher for me was 'Waking
    up and thinking you've uploaded, finding yourself in a big white space
    full of clouds, and really smarmy-looking Jehova's Witnesses.'
    Aaaaaaaaaaaaggggghhhhh!!!!!!!!

    Anyone beat that?
    AR
