    Mike this notion of prehistoric women picking berries
    while the men were out hunting is a myth. Prehistoric
    humans lived much as we do, except that *everything* was
    made of stone, their cars had no engines so they had
    to make them go by pushing them with their feet, and
    everyone's name had the word "stone" or "rock" in it
    somewhere. Other than that, same as now. spike
