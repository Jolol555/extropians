    Russell has come up with quite the idea: divorce!

    > All Canadians who support the US position on Iraq are invited to migrate
    > westward to the beautiful new State of British Columbia. Canadians presently
    > living in the State of British Columbia will be asked to migrate eastward to
    > Canada at their earliest convenience. The State of British Columbia is
    > currently a conceptual e-state for the purpose of facilitating future >Human
    > land swap migrations.

    Sounds like a great idea for the U.S. too! All our conservatives
    can move to the Rocky Mountains and points west, and all the
    liberals east of the Mississippi. (We can retain a column of
    border states for the wishy-washy.)

    I'm not really sure that the liberals would be able to make a
    go of it on their own, however. It seems to me that all those
    on welfare (whatever their politics) would make tracks east at
    the first hint of such a divorce. But I think that I personally
    would pay for the cattle cars to move east the downtown San
    Francisco protestors who practically shut the city down today.
