    There is a very interesting series of Russian reports on the current
    situation (as of writing) in Iraq. I used this starting point:
    http://www.aeronautics.ru/news/news002/news070.htm

    | The IRAQWAR.RU analytical center was created recently by a group of
    | journalists and military experts from Russia to provide accurate and
    | up-to-date news and analysis of the war against Iraq. The following is
    | the English translation of the IRAQWAR.RU report based on the Russian
    | military intelligence reports.

    It is most remarkable if the Russians are really publishing the
    results of their satellite and signals intelligence. Of course,
    for all I know this may be completely fabricated out of thin air,
    but I intend to follow these reports and give them some provisional
    credence purely from the fact that just about no information on the
    situation in much of Iraq is forthcoming from the Coalition command
    and the mainstream media.

    People who read Russian better than I do may want to check out
    www.iraqwar.ru directly.
