    The story about the alleged Coalition bombing of a marketplace in Baghdad
    mentions this happened in a Shiite neighborhood. Does anyone here know how
    many Shiites live in Baghdad? They are definitely a minority in the
    predominantly Sunni city. The appearance of the explosion site does not show
    a deep crater, as one might expect from a penetrating bomb, but rather
    resembles the traces of a large mortar round.

    While US involvement is not impossible, the two facts mentioned above, and
    the absence of any military installations in the vicinity, make me think
    that the Sunni Baathists lobbed a mortar round on the marketplace at the
    time when it was full, after waiting for a plane to pass within sufficiently
    small distance to be seen.

    Rafal
