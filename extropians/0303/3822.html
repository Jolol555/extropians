    Mike Lorrey writes

    > That nearly half the US population pays no taxes at all, yet receives
    > thousands or even tens of thousands of dollars annually in benefits,
    > while the top 10% pay more than 50% of the taxes while receiving
    > nothing in return is indicative of the drift from a free market to an
    > odd sort of upside down patron/client socialist/mercantilist economy,
    > where the productive class is enslaved by the political elites to
    > subsidize the payoffs to the unproductive class that extorts the
    > payoffs under theoretical threat of electoral revolt...

    The miracle is that a Huey Long or other pure mobocrat-democrat
    hasn't been elected to the executive with a similarly composed
    Congress. More than half the people now have absolutely no
    incentive whatsoever to have lower taxes. On the contrary, the
    higher the taxes, the better for them.

    I do not know to what to attribute this miracle. But I do
    know that it won't last forever.

    Lee
