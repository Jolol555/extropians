    The Klez worm (not virus) searches your Microsoft Address Book and incoming
    mailbox for e-mail addresses and then fakes an e-mail that appears to be
    from someone you know. The person in the "From" line never actually sent
    this e-mail. This is known programmed behavior for Klez. Google on "Klez"
    for a description of this worm.
