    This evening the British Government voted to support Tony Blair in his move
    towards military action.

    If it was never a deciding issue in the move to strike (and it probably
    wasn't), then either way the path is now clear for war in Iraq.

    This goes out to everyone here who has someone serving in the coalition
    forces, is near a danger zone or is under high alert of terrorist reaction.

    Here's hoping for a quick and relatively peaceful (in as much as war can
    be) conclusion to this conflict, with the swift and safe return of the
    servicemen and women of all coalition nations. Here's hoping to the end of
    this regime, and a much improved future for the Iraqi people. And here's
    hoping that the small chance of terrorist response on people living in a
    participating nation remains just that; a small, unfulfilled chance.

    And here's to the troops and their families, who must know by now that war
    is, in all likelihood, not much more than a day away.

    Stay safe out there.
