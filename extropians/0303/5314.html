    These have been posted before I think, but the two primary sources I
    have used for information on the SARS disease are:

    http://www.cdc.gov/ncidod/sars/
    http://www.who.int/csr/sarsarchive/en/

    The WHO page has a link to a press conference held on Thursday which
    had a lot of good, practical information about how contagious SARS is
    and some other issues:

    http://www.who.int/csr/sars/2003_03_27/en/

    Is there a link to a Canadian equivalent of the CDC? That might be useful
    since Canada seems to have more active cases than the U.S. at this point.
