    FYI

    On Feb 23, 2002 I created a subfolder on my Outlook bar titled: Extropian.
    Since that time, I suppose that I've read or scanned a relatively high
    percentage of the list traffic (I'm guessing 65 - 75%) that has passed
    through my inbox.

    Whenever I happened to notice something I thought was especially
    interesting, informative, witty, or otherwise save-worthy (I'm a bit of a
    pack rat you know) I clicked, dragged, and then plopped the message into my
    Extropian folder for future reference. Today I was glancing through those
    entries and it occurred to me that it might be interesting to tabulate them
    according to author then forward the data back to the list.

    Then, just to make it more fun, I decided to award the authors (1)Ideobuck
    for each post that made into my folder. Ideobucks can be redeemed for actual
    hair services at Extropica Salon on Vancouver Island, where great haircuts
    can be had for $30 CD or $20 IDB. You may spend your Ideobucks whenever you
    are happen to be in this neck of the woods, or save them until shortly after
    the singularity when they will probably be worth their weight in heritage
    bits.
