    Damien Sullivan <phoenix@ugcs.caltech.edu> wrote:

    > http://www.smh.com.au/articles/2003/03/30/1048962645191.html
    > suggests the published "coalition of the willing" is misleading.

    I've been wondering lately whether Germany isn't an anonymous member
    of the willing. While the German government has rejected participation
    in the war, it does appear quite supportive of it, allowing the US
    to make full use of the important Ramstein airbase and even detailing
    German soldiers for guard duty there and at other US installations
    in the country. And there remains the question of what those AWACS
    crews in Turkey are really doing.

    Sure looks like a supposed opponent of the war is contributing more
    in the way of support than many of the supposed supporters (are
    able to). Realpolitik, I guess.
