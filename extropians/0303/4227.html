    > I agree with many of your points - but there is certainly no standard
    > libertarian stance on the Iraq war...

    While there are certainly libertarians on both sides of the issue,
    there is a "standard" libertarian stance: non-intervention. The
    platform and the party are pretty clear in opposing any military
    action that is not a direct response to a direct attack or immenent
    threat on our soil. The party was generally silent about Afghanistan,
    but they've come out clearly against going to war in Iraq in all their
    recent press releases.
