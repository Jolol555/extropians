    When Steve Bridge, a former Alcor president, came to the Creekside Preserve, one of the things he brought up on his cryonet post about the experience what that to this great astonishment I was not an extremely loud and outgoing guy!! LOL For some reason my penchant for exclamation marks and "lol's" had given him that impression. He said about me "I also enjoyed meeting John Grigg for the first time. He is a lot calmer in person than in front of a keyboard! (I am using John's trademark exclamation point here in friendly jest. He really is a nice fellow.)"

    I was touched by the compliment. But as my employer says, "being nice is not always such a great thing, instead be known as fair and someone not to mess with." My manager 101 training continues.
