    > "Hubert Mania" <humania@t-online.de>
    >
    > >The USA will be acting against international law and YOU KNOW IT.
    >
    > But that's exactly the problem, many of us don't know it. You claim to

    > that knowledge so tell us, exactly what "international law" did the USA
    > violate, what are the penalties for transgressors, how are they enforced,
    > where are they written down, where can I see the signature of the USA
    > agreeing to these regulations,

    The international law (German: Voelkerrecht = people's law) Bush is now
    acting against is written down in the charta of the United Nations. And I do
    remember that the USA signed at least *this* treaty. And therein it is
    stated that a preemptive war is against international law if it is not
    approved by the security council of the UN. The conditions for the council
    to approve of a preemptive war are very restricted, and so far no sane envoy
    is willing to see such conditions to agree with the US viewpoint that they
    are so deadly threatened by Saddam that they would have to stop him before
    he pushes the button.

    Now, the UN has not approved of a preemptive war, so Bush is acting against
    international law. He doesn`t give a shit about the rest of the world. Maybe
    you should really start turning to European news sources from now on. At
    least here in Germany we can read the statements of all dominant governments
    in the world who condemn Bushs announcement of his solo effort last night
    and his violation of international law. Even the German Christian
    conservative party, for almost 60 years now on excellent terms with every
    US administration, is somehow shocked about the way Bush disregards the
    UN security council.

    Nobody will officially punish the US for this transgressing. So you don't
    need to worry :-) but your country has lost friends by the dozens.

    > and most important of all is there any reason
    > US citizens should bet their lives that this mysterious fuzzy thing called
    > "international law" will protect them from another 911 type attack?
    Sorry
    > for all the questions, I've asked them before but nobody had an answer. Do
    > you?
    >
    > John K Clark jonkc@att.net

    John, there IS no protection. You must protect yourself. You have to choose
    between a deadly cold "security" in a police state where everybody spies on
    everybody else or you accept that you can die any moment. How in the world
    can anybody ever prevent a team of determined mindfuckers to blow another
    skyscraper in Washington, Paris or Berlin? And if I happen to be in this
    Berlin building at the wrong time, I am fucking unlucky. That's all.

    You have to live with the rest risk. You have to loose your fear and your
    desire for revenge somehow. Nobody can protect you against terrorism.One
    preemptive war after the other will suck your economy dry and the further
    downfall of the USA is ensured.
