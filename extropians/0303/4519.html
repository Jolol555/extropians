    How about this-- everyone looks out for their own individual best
    interest. Anybody who claims to be looking out for others' best interest is
    treated with the suspicion they deserve.

    If you live under a tyrant, it's your right, privilege, and duty to depose the
    tyrant.

    If you live free, it's your right, privilege, and duty to make sure to resist
    the slightest hint of tyrrany.

    If you construe any of this as a reason to blindly and uncritically obey your
    leaders, then the joke's on you.
