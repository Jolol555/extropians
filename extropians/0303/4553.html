    Artillo said:

    ====================================================
    My question for my fellow Extropians is this:

    Is war for any reason something an Extropian should be espousing????
    ====================================================

    No, war for *particular* reasons, however, is something extropian can
    support. For example, going to war with a murderous expansionist
    totalatarian regime spreading murder and tyranny country by country where
    all attempts at diplomatic and peacefull cesastions of its expanionism and
    murder have failed can be consider a just cause.

    I would retort a question to you, is the use of force *always* incorrect
    from an extropian standpoint? And if so, *prove* it.
