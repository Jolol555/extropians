    Elizabeth Smart, the teenage girl who was kidnapped
    from her home at knifepoint and was recently found
    alive and well nine months later, has captured a
    nation's full and undivided. There are many
    questions that spring to mind, none of which has
    even a reasonable suggestion of explanation, such
    as why couldn't she escape in all that time, and
    why she denied her identity when found by police.

    Either way, this kid has multi-jillion dollar
    book and movie potential, just from the story
    of her nine months in apparent captivity(?).
    Not a bad payday for nine months of one's life.
    Why the heck else would she stay with the
    captors?
