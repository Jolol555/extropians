    Another problem with tags: there is often a gray area. For example I started
    a thread about FOX's news coverage of the war, which someone has now labeled
    [IRAQ], but in greater terms the thread is really about bias in the media.
    It might just as well be labeled [MEDIA]. Readers interested in news media
    but not interested in the specifics of the war would miss the messages if
    they filtered on "[IRAQ]".
