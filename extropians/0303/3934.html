    > What happened to the extropy servers and domain yesterday? It's like
    > the domain stopped existing to a large chunk of the web for most of the
    > day. Did someone forget to renew the domain name?

    The domain didn't go anywhere. The server didn't go anywhere. However,
    a router immediately 'in front of' the server lost connectivity, as R.
    said. It wasn't just extropy.org, either-- lucifer.com, and all other
    sites on that server (not to mention any other servers in the same
    position as ours) were also inaccessible. And, unless I'm mistaken,
    they were inaccessible to everyone, not just a large chunk-- I tested
    >from systems in California, Texas, Virgina, Australia, and England,
    and each had 100% packet loss at the same place.
