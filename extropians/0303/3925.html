    Max,

    Rafal S. and I had a long discussion here recently about
    selegiline/deprenyl. The thread was called, I think, "performance
    enhancement with selegiline." You might find it interesting. Selegiline
    is drug used in the treatment of neurodegenerative diseases. It seems to
    offer a lot of possible benefits to healthy people, including cognitive
    enhancement.

    Also see the Cognitive Enhancement Research Institute at www.ceri.com.
    Ward Dean, MD, is a personal friend of mine. His and Steven Fowke's
    articles on that site are worth a read.

    The "Erowid vaults" site that you mentioned has some interesting
    information, but it's basically just a hang-out for recreational drug
    users. :)
