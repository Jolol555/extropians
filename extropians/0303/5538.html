    I'm finding it extremely frustrating that my occasional posts to this list
    take an hour or more to show up back here at my computer. I know several
    other list members have been experiencing comparable delays for months, and
    attributed it to their oversubscribed service providers. A year or so back,
    my unimelb service flung email back and forth around the globe at what
    seemed literally the speed of light. Now it's taking the scenic route. What
    gives? I do note that many other posts responding to any thread appear
    almost immediately, so not *everyone*'s sharing this lag.
