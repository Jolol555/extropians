    Concerning the draft of Patriot Act Two. Kee said amongst other delivately
    elaborated comments:

    > So I delved further into the document where each of these is
    > described in more detail. I found nothing alarming after all.
    > Okay, now I've done my part. You tell me just *where* there
    > is something here that we should discuss!

    I have come to know you as a sincere person, Lee, and I am interested in
    further discussion, but if your alarm clocks are NOT ringing when reading
    the draft of Patriot Act II, this probably has to do with the shape of
    your reality tunnel, while my tunnel of reality has been shaped into being
    more suspicious of Government plans to deprive their citzens of their
    freedom
    in exchange for a security they cannot provide at all. At the first signs of
    such plans I am up the barricades while you probably are still thinking you
    are on the save side because you are such a good citizen. Just imagine a
    revengeful neighbor denounces you. Once you show up in the investigation
    machinery, you are lost under Patriot Act II, especially in times of
    war.This
    law introduces the scissors in your brain, it promotes the fear and all the
    obsequious behaviour, a freedom loving country like your's should actually
    hate. If you do not think this is evil, I can't do nothing to convince you.
