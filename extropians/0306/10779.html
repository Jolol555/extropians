    Harvey Newstrom wrote:

    > Why not just label foods so customers can make their own choices of what
    > they want to buy?

    Oh, I'm all for it. I dimly remember in SR Delaney's book _Triton_ (now
    renamed) that when the protagonist visited an Earth restaurant the meal
    included complete info on exactly what he was munching, and further,
    exactly how much of the bill was distributed to the people involved (the
    owners, the chef, waiters, etc).

    I'd like to see comprehensive labelling (or at least a bar code leading
    to web info) on everything, not just food.
