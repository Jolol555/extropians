    5) China's Communist Party to "deepen reforms"
        MSNBC

    "China's Communist Party pledged on Monday, the eve of the
    anniversary of its foundation, to deepen cultural reforms to free
    authors, artists and actors from dogmatic Marxist thinking. Li
    Changchun, a member of the all powerful, nine-man Politburo Standing
    Committee, urged that those in the cultural realm be 'liberated from
    the mistaken and dogmatic understanding of Marxism,' said the
    People's Daily, the party's mouthpiece." (06/30/03)
