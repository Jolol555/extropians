    we reach certain transhuman technologies and how they're distributed.
    Do you want to be able to benefit from a transhuman procedure in your
    home country? Or do you want to have to travel to a clinic in China?

    The "resistance is futile" attitude just frightens and motivates the
    critics of transhuman technology even more.
