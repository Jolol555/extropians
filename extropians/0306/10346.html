    Natasha Vita-More wrote,
    > One suggestion was to start a new list for political postings. Let me
    > know if you want this list, and/or if you think a new political list would
    > help decelerate the traffic that is bottlenecking our conversation.

    I would vote YES for a political list. I believe that virtually all our
    flame-wars and personal attacks on the list have been on political topics.
    They constitute a high volume of traffic and are usually separated from
    other Extropian topics with very little overlap or crosstalk. This would
    seem to be the most obvious way to divide the list if any dividing is to
    occur at all. We could even have different moderators or different
    standards of debate for political discussions and the main extropian
    discussions if we wanted.
