    > mail@HarveyNewstrom.com writes:
    > > Nice jab. But can you buy that the above description is a
    > > far cry from being a communist, a terrorist, a Saddam supporter,

    Hmm. I thought it was a good question (not that I want
    an answer, just that it conveyed fairly what you probably

    > The question is how much do you hate and how much harm

    that you are saying that they *do* hate, and that
    they do a lot of harm. Since it's not been
    established (to my knowledge) that Harvey hates
    anyone, this is incendiary! Isn't there a milder
    way to ask that?
