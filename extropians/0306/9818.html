    On Mon, 9 Jun 2003, Amara Graps wrote:

    > Some implications of this messiness is that:
    [snip]
    > 4) Dust varies from place to place

    This is a critical point that has not been recognized by the
    SETI community and by those that debate the Fermi Question.
    (When Fermi came up with the question he probably had little
    awareness of the distribution of dust in various places in
    space.)

    I believe the ideas of Newman and Sagan (1981, 1983, 1985)
    need to be significantly revised in light of this fact.
