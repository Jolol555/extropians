    In general this patent got me thinking about other improvements.
    After all, there are many other aspects of the body that could be
    made to taste and smell better. We already spend a lot of money on
    this, so why not get to the root of the problem? One approach is to
    use modified microflora, another to add chemicals that make body
    fluids or tissue more aromatic and a third of course to use gene
    therapy to make cells produce new and interesting flavor compounds.
    Pheromones are so old-fashioned!
