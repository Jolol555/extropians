    Friends,

    Please send any complaints to me. E.Shaun is functioning in another
    capacity with ExI. Currently, we do not have a list moderator, as I
    assumed we were adult enough not to need one.

    However, I seem to be proven wrong. I am open to suggestions on how to
    prevent the type of hedging and positioning we have witnessed and which is
    lacking in grace, intelligence and fair-play.

    We have suggested time and time again that if someone wants to start a list
    on politics, please do so. Since it is not the directive of ExI (and the
    only politics I personally find intriguing are one that have yet to be
    determined), why are you all so persistent on expressing your angst and
    disdain for what you don't like about any one political stance on this list!!

    There are so many topics that are worthy of discussion and need to be
    discussed in order for us to aptly deal with the future and how we are
    getting there. Why waste so much of the bandwidth of this list on topics
    that cause problems? Each person should think for him or herself about
    whether or not you actually working toward a transhumanist future or just
    angry at life today.

    In order to move forward, if you have any suggestions or comments about the
    list, I am all ears.
    Please email me privately.

    My best to you all.

    Natasha
