    Dossy wrote:

    > So, how do you profit in the market?
    > Try to guess where the demand is going to be.

    Problem is that stock prices are themselves the best predictors of demand if
    you are referring to the demand for goods and services.

    It is, for example, quite absurd to look at the economy in an attempt to
    predict the stockmarket. Yet hopeful people do it all the time. One can
    hardly watch the business news without seeing some talking head make
    predictions about the market based on the economy.

    But economic data doesn't predict stockmarket data. Stockmarket data
    predicts economic data! It is for this reason that the stockmarket is
    included in the index of leading economic indicators.

    The same principle applies to the shares of individual companies. If you
    want to make a best guess about the business performance of Acme Widgets in
    the future then take a look at Acme Widget's stock performance in the
    present. Your guess might be wrong but there is no better way to make the
    guess.
