    The trick you are using is called "Affirmation of the consequent."

    It goes like this:

            The "Hate America First" crowd criticizes George Bush but doesn't criticize
    other tyrants.
            Person "X" has criticized George Bush without criticizing other tyrants.
            Therefore person "X" must hate America!

    It has recently been made popular by our President's "logic" that "if you
    aren't with us, you're against us." It implies that any criticism of the
    President equals support for terrorism. Sadly this trick will work too, the
    first time around.
