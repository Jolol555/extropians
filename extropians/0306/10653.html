    Ron, I have no idea what you're talking about in terms of a "clique",
    but let me make the status of the list clear. This list belongs to
    Extropy Institute, which is ultimately governed by its board of
    directors and which is run on a a day-to-day basis by its executive,
    which is its president, Natasha Vita-More, assisted where possible by me
    as ExI's vice-president. Most of the old-timers on this list know us
    personally to one degree or another, and I think we have the support of
    those who have been part of this community for a long time.
     
    Natasha has acted to appoint Hal Finney as interim list moderator, a
    decision I support 100% -- Hal's been around as a part of this community
    for a long, long time and is respected as a reasonable, fair and
    intelligent person. I know I've enjoyed corresponding with him through
    this medium for going on a decade, and have learned a thing or two from
    him!
     
    Let's give Hal our support, renew our commitment to reasonable and
    polite dialogue and focus on the values we share: progress and
    improvement in the human condition.
