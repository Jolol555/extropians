    At 06:43 PM 6/19/03 -0700, you wrote:
    >It's certain that some people recently felt as though they
    >had been instructed *not* to reply to certain posts or
    >posters. But the list rules are clear:
    >
    > If someone attacks your character, insults you,
    > or otherwise violates the above rule, do NOT
    > retaliate in like manner. Instead, send us a
    > complaint (to info@extropy.org) and take the
    > matter off-list.

    The few people who were asked not to reply to certain posts or posters were
    giving a warning, as per the list rules. This was issued because there
    were more than 2 complaints about their language and their posts. "...do
    not retaliate in like manner." means do not send a "post" in like manner.

    Free speech is a given on the list, however list rules explicitly state
    that ad hominen attacks are not allowed. Also stated is "This is a
    private forum. We reserve the right to unsubscribe anyone at any time.
    " It seems far better to be asked to please refrain from continued posting
    to a person or post that has caused a red flag than to be removed.

    Lastly, the rules state: "Thank you for your cooperation in making the
    Extropians List a stimulating and enjoyable forum. "

    If anyone suspects that there is a effort to drive these few people off the
    list, let them follow the rules and send their comments to info@extropy.org
    rather than gossiping behind the scenes and poisoning the well. I am
    fully aware that a few people are making public accusations and this is a
    bit annoying.
