    This was possible in the days of AUTODIN (Automatic Digital
    Network) store and forward messaging. SSO (Special Security Office) PANAMA sent
    an operational Immediate (ZZH) to COS
    (Chief of Station) Guatemala, releasable to the GOG (Government
    of Guatemala). The originator a few minutes later said the message
    contents were not releasable to GOG. I sent a Flash (ZZ) service message to COS
    Guatemala which arrived before the original
    message. Can't do this very well in packet switching.
