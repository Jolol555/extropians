    From: Robin Hanson [mailto:rhanson@gmu.edu]
    > I expect the
    > advantages from sharing mental modules and upgrades to be so
    > strong that mental architectures are widely shared. And I
    > expect minds to eventually be more designed than evolved,
    > more like cars today than cats.

    I think in the long term you're correct. However my personal
    expectation is that we'll achieve both uploading and evolved software
    intelligences before we see a top-down designed general intelligence.
    If so, we'll go through a period where transparency (or even mutual
    comprehension) between types of entities will be low.
