    Was feeling kind of pissy today, so I says to myself: I wonder what the
    biofundies of this world are chattering about online these days. I imagined
    myself dropping some thought provoking message onto one of their bulletin
    boards - you know, just to pick at a scab or two covering over some serious
    ideological boo boos. And that's when a delightfully unexpected thing
    happened!

    Not having the foggiest where to look, I googled "gm food bbs", and lo and
    behold, smack dab at the top of the page, pops up a nice little "sponsored
    link" advertisement for betterhumans! The *ONLY* sponsored link no less! Put
    a smile right there on my face it did. Way to go guys!

    Resistance truly is futile.
