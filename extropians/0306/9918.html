    Perhaps you too are getting messages like the one below,
    three or four times a day, from some business manager
    in Africa who discovered a ton of money and wants to
    give some of it to you, since he has it on good
    authority that you are an honest and upright sort.

    How would I write a suckerbot script that would be
    a simulation of a person who has fallen for this
    bonehead scam? I would want to send the message
    to a special folder, then have the mail account
    automatically dispose of any incoming email from
    that source forever. Then I want to create an
    eliza-like script that would encourage the
    reprehensible spammer as long as possible by
    sending replies that appear to have been generated
    by an actual human.

    Any ideas?

    spike
