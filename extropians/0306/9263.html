    Greg Burch wrote,
    > Because of my long experience in various aspects of the maritime
    > industry, it seems to be my fate to rain on this parade here from time
    > to time.

    Thanks, Greg. We need a little rain on unrealistic fantasies. I just
    turned 40 last month (oh, that explains it!) and have a great fear that we
    are wasting our time on trivial matters, bad science, and unrealistic
    schemes instead of doing anything important.

    However, if people would like to start brainstorming on a more realistic
    extropian project costing a few million dollars less.... But first, let's
    set some goals instead of jumping on a boat. What was the boat supposed to
    accomplish? Fun? A vacation spot? A tax haven? An independent country
    with our own freedoms? A haven for scientific research? A money-making
    venture? A chance to get us closer together for interaction? Surely there
    are cheaper and more realistic ways to do any of these things.
