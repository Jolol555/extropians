    Thanks g, this confirms my own intuitions
    about stock investing. I am only smart
    enough to know how dumb I am about this
    topic. During the big runup of 96 thru 2k,
    I felt like such a sucker staying out of
    the market. I watched the net worth of
    my friend's 6 yr old daughter exceed my
    own, as she bought the high fliers and I
    sat out the boom.

    Her net worth is now about average for a
    10 yr old child.

    All the hype of those heady times violated
    my intuitions about how the world should
    work. Those intuitions were correct in
    the long run.
