    Redasoft releases Visual Cloning 3 for life science research

    Redasoft has announced the worldwide availability of Redasoft Visual
    Cloning 3. 'Redasoft Visual Cloning 3 greatly improves life science
    researchers' productivity and enables them to do extraordinary
    research,' said Danny Reda, CEO of Redasoft. 'It has been developed
    in response to feedback from our more than 260 customers who have
    helped guide our efforts to be results-driven and customer-focused.
    We're very excited about Visual Cloning 3, which we believe will
    change the way researchers work and usher in a new era of life
    science research.'

    The software allows access to analytical tools needed to choose
    strategies and better understand sequences by simulating cloning,
    restriction digest, PCR and hybridisation experiments on screen
    before performing them.
    http://www.redasoft.com/
