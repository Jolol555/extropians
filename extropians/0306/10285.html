    Michael Dickey writes

    > I would normally be the first to agree [that] it would be extremely
    > odd and unlikely to suspect someone on the extropian list would support a
    > regime like that of Hussein's. However, I should note in this thread,
    >
    http://www.extropy.org/bbs/index.php?board=67;action=display;threadid=56165;start=15
    >
    > (etc.)

    Well, I do wish that Paul Grant was here to defend himself,
    but (in another sense) it perhaps doesn't matter: how can
    anyone read that post and believe that he is not supporting
    Saddam Hussein? Paul certainly is defending him again and
    again!

    So, sadly, the assertion by many that there is no one on
    the Extropy forum who is a supporter of Saddam Hussein must
    fall. I retract my own statement to that effect, too, of
    course. Thanks for pointing this out again, Michael.
