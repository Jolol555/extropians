    I have been meaning for years to put up a web site
    or something. It's finally gotten to near the top of
    my to do list. I'll share my thoughts and maybe you
    folks can give me a few pointers.

    I have a web hosting space and can get some popular
    web tool. I want a web site to post essays, links, etc.
    I am thinking of using Radio Userland to create a blog
    for my works in progress and misc. LE thoughts.
    Beyond that I think my best bet is to involve myself in
    existing forums (like this one).

    I want to try to be more collaborative and I do not like
    to see work wastefully duplicated. I like the idea of
    keeping most of my thinking online but I don't want to
    spam people with it. I'm going to surf around and see
    what I can find but if anyone has suggestions to speed
    the process up I would appreciate them. I suppose
    surfing and reading a lot more is a good first step.

    -Will
