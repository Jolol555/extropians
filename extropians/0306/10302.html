    Park on June 18 reminder
    Sender: owner-extropians@extropy.org
    Precedence: bulk
    Reply-To: extropians@extropy.org

    Dear Colleague:

    I would like to inform you of an event that you may find of great value. On
    behalf of NCnano, The Northern California Nanotechnology Initiative, you
    are invited to attend the first in a series of Nanotechnology Leadership
    Conferences focusing on commercializing emerging technologies intellectual
    property. The subject of this conference is: "Transforming Bay Area
    nanotechnology, infrastructure and entrepreneurism into companies, jobs and
    regional economic growth."

    The event will be held at SRI International Auditorium in Menlo Park on
    June 18 from 7:00-9:30 PM. We are expecting more than 300 attendees and
    space will be limited so please register to receive a complimentary pass at
    www.ncnano.org. The evening will start with a networking reception.
    Following this, the heads of the licensing groups from the major Bay Area
    laboratories and Universities such as: Berkeley, UC Davis, Stanford, LLNL,
    LBNL, SRI, and NASA Ames will highlight research, facilities and technology
    licensing opportunities at their respective organizations. Corporate
    speakers from NEC, Intel, Agilent, NASA, Philips and Nanosys will then
    speak about nanotechnology intellectual property and funding opportunities
    and commercialization success.

    The event is being co-sponsored by a number of relevant organizations and
    will be widely covered in the press. Registration will only be open for a
    day or two more, so I would recommend registering at your earliest
    convenience. For more info see http://www.ncnano.org
