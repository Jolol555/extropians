    http://www.heise.de/tp/deutsch/special/med/14991/1.html

    E-zine "telepolis" is probably the most important geek magazine of German
    languange. Hard to describe the political tendency to American readers. I
    guess most extropians would think it had a leftist-anarchist character.

    The papers of Nick Bostrom and Robin Hanson are discussed in a pretty
    intelligent discourse. So far approximately 300 readers have made a comment.
