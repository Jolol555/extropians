    Does anyone here remember the 60s? I do a little, and I
    recall that Christianity was big in the hippie movement.
    They called them Jesus freaks. Buddhism was big too
    (ex. Kerouac's Dharma Bums, etc.)

    I heard a reference to a show that I really used to
    like as a child, called Kung Fu. It seemed so much
    deeper and smarter than the two other stations we used
    to get (which were carrying HeeHaw Honeys and The Dukes
    of Hazard.)

    Question please, those who were there: was Kung Fu
    a really good show, or did it just seem that way
    at the time, to a 10 yr old? Has anyone seen that
    show recently? Has it aged well?
