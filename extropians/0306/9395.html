    I saw _The War in Iraq: A Photo History_ at Borders today. It's all
    photos, no text. It's big, ~350 pages. I flipped thru it randomly, and
    quickly got the impression there was something missing. So I again
    flipped thru, much more slowly, trying to scan every photo. Unless I
    missed something, this is the first war photo book I've ever seen which
    doesn't have photos of something one might expect in a war. Can anybody
    guess what it is????
