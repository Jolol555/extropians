    Robert wrote

    > [mailto:owner-extropians@extropy.org]On Behalf Of Robert J. Bradbury
    > Sent: Thursday, February 20, 2003 12:14 PM

    > It seems (to me) that the U.S. bought off a resolution to the 1967
    > and/or Yom Kippur wars and has been paying ever since.

    Yes. I can just hear Jimmy Carter sitting down with Sadat
    and Begin and saying, "Listen heah, yall. There's no need
    here for fightin'. There is something much, much more
    PROFITABLE for all three of us'uns. You each get four
    billion dollars or so a year, and I get to go down in
    history as the great peace-maker, why, I might even
    get a Nobel peace prize or two!

    "This is a sure-fire thing. The three of us come out smelling
    like roses, and the only loser is the American tax-payer, who
    is a stupid chump anyway, and who deserves what he gets,"
    hearty laughter all around.

    Isn't that about the size of it? Ol' Jimmy Carter just got
    us to *pay* them not to fight each other. Great for him,
    and great for them. Stupid for us.

    But then, like he says, we're chumps anyway and so deserve it.

    Lee
