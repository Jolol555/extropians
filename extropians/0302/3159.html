    Guess1a) This might never occur in the face of a national insurrection

    Guess2: There would be a world-wide economic depression, as a result of the
    slowness of any kind of temporary or permanent government to take command.

    a) cessation of trade with China, Japan, South Korea.
    b) These states would likely then cease purchase from Europe.

    Guess4) China and Russia might negotiate spheres of influence, and perhaps
    seeing when the aquistion of American territory might be venturable.

    Guess4a) Australia and New Zealand capitulating, as well as Japan, South
    Korea, and South East Asian under The New Chinese Hegemony

    The Former Soviet Union now establishing itself as nominal leader of the EU,
    or rather military leader. Read Russian hegemony. A new Russian Empire

    5)Allah has judged the Crusader! Now to liberate Al Quds from the Jews.
    Resulting in Israel using nuclear weapons against the newly energized
    Arab-Islamic movement. What would result is not clear. Beyond many dead,
    that is.

    6) Pakistan and India might stay out of this global mess, perhaps until the
    dust clears. Maybe they would get dragged in?

    There are other scenarios and and other games to play with such scenarios.
    The lesson to be learned may not be clear except that you may hate the USA,
    but it may not be until your great grandchildrens' adulthood, before most
    nations will experience the liberty we enjoy now.
