    I think I could live with all messages discussing Iraq containing IRAQ in
    the subject line... but please *do* be careful to label *all* such
    messages. And try to keep the massive vitriol now being generated on this
    list from becoming personal animosity that will spill over onto other,
    non-Iraq threads.

    I'd prefer an outright ban, actually - I don't think that freedom of
    speech in particular fora of the Net is a good idea; people should always
    be free to create new fora, but within any given forum, I think it's
    important to prevent poisoning of the well. I fear that even if all Iraq
    discussion is clearly labeled and cleanly killfiled, it will still detract
    from the tone and the civility of the forum - I can't killfile all the
    people who read the Iraq threads, and I can't undo the virtual killfiling
    of the people who unsubscribe in disgust or just never subscribe to begin
    with. But I can live with a subject line keyword.
