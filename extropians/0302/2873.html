    War may spur bin Laden: ASIO chief
    February 18 2003
    By Mark Forbes
    Foreign Affairs Correspondent

    Osama bin Laden will use a war against Iraq to expand his terror network and
    launch further terrorist attacks, according to the chief of Australia's spy
    agency.

    Australian Security Intelligence Organisation director-general Dennis
    Richardson also said yesterday that a solution to the Iraq crisis would be
    irrelevant to bin Laden and attacks would continue. [etc]
