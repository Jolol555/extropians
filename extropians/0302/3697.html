    I see, but demonstrating that subways are the Luddite
    and terrorist low hanging fruit is not necessarily
    related to either 9-11 or Iraq. The unabomber and
    the KC bomber both predated 9-11-01 and the current
    trouble with Iraq. So I respectfully remove Iraq
    from the subject line.

    I expect lone wolf home grown terrorists will be the
    ones attacking the subways. When I say home-grown,
    I am not necessarily talking about Americans either
    (altho both my examples were USian) since Europe has
    more subways than the U.S. and Europe is more
    dependent on them.

    Regarding Luddite attacks in the U.S. I could imagine
    where two yahoos could arrange over the internet to
    meet at a certain time going on the freeway in the
    same direction using synchronized watches, drive parallel
    for some distance, then in the middle of a tunnel of
    some major artery, they would "accidentally" collide.
    Most likely there would be no injuries, but the
    disruption could be enormous, running into perhaps
    millions of dollars with just an hour backup on a
    major freeway. Furthermore, the perps could get
    away with it, for it would be impossible to prove
    they had arranged the accident. Insurance would
    even cover the cost of it all.

    We have built a system that is so vulnerable it
    almost invites obstructionists to destroy. Humanity
    needs to spread out. spike
