    So finally I received a copy of Futurequest from my best friend Dave Forrest
    for my birthday (he had a dusty copy all along the culprit!). Yes I cheated
    and opened it a day early (shopping day begins officially tomorrow). I was
    so excited that I watched it twice. This was in fact the movie that planted
    the nanotech seed in my mind, which later grew into the full blown case of
    nanofever that you all know! So, it was sentimental to see it again now,
    I've been looking forever for copies. I know I have asked on the list before
    (a few years ago) if anyone has the complete 13 part series, but now that I
    have a copy of the nanotech section I thought I would ask again, just in
    case. Thanx!
