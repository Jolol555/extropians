    Let me not defend the undefensible, but these were the days when Mother
    Russia's Empire was riding high, and it was determined to enforce its empire
    everywhere in the world. Support for brutal dictatorships did not happen in a
    vaccum, but were against the zero-sum game played by the ruling communist
    party, in the Soviet Union. They supported vile, evil, mass murderers, also;
    and were never criticized by the Left at all.

    Holding the US to a "higher standard" because "we expect more of you" is
    merely a head game and did not change the brutal facts on the ground, one
    iota. So we either let the Soviets put in their goons and we lose, or we put
    in our goons and we win a little bit. Of course our claim to a high moral
    standard suffers, but then in a world were that phrase sugar-coats true
    behavior, the dogs bark but caravan moves on.
