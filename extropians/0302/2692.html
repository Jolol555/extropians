    John K Clark wrote:

    > "gts" <gts_2000@yahoo.com> Wrote:
    >
    > >other *similar* universes aren't really about
    > >*your life* in the first place. They're only
    > >about the lives of people who are to various degrees
    > >*similar to you*.
    >
    > If many worlds is true then in the time it takes you say the word "ME"
    > between the "M" and the "E" both you and the entire universe will have
    split
    > trillions of times making the concept of a one true original "you" utterly
    > meaningless.

    My point is that those other trillions of Johns are not the John who is
    reading this message, as evidenced by the fact that any one of them could
    cease to exist at this moment while you would continue to exist as though
    nothing had happened. Indeed many of those other Johns are dying at this
    moment from accidents or illness and such. Their demise does not imply your
    demise, so certainly they should not be considered *you*.
