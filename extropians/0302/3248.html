    On the contrary, they are the leaders and extremist proponents that
    drive the whole movement to further offense. For example, where would
    the luddite movement be today if the members of the Merry Pranksters
    and other similar groups had put their asses where their mouths were by
    doing something equally risky back during 'Nam? It would not exist as a
    movement.

    On the contrary, Saddam has sent assasination teams into the US to kill
    former President Bush, and Iraq remains in violation of the original
    cease fire agreement. Absolutely NOTHING we plan to do in Iraq is in
    violation of international law. We have both the original UN mandates
    of the first Gulf War as well as our national rights of self defense
    under the UN Charter to justify our actions.

    And, no, the US will not be an agressor no matter how terrible Saddam
    is. What we are is out of patience for people like him and bin Laden,
    and once we've wiped the world clean of their type, we will quite
    happily retire to our farms much like Cincinnatus.

    THose of you who fail to see the justice of our cause are quite plainly insane.
