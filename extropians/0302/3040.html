    I'm happy (for myself) to not talk any more on Iraq or preface any email
    with IRAQ, the later seems more sensible to me.

    There are going to be bigger differences amongst transhumanists. You can see
    this already. Those who are enthusiastic for complete computronium uploading
    scenarios seem to assume that these will apply to the entire solar system.
    Libertarians seem to think their principles will apply to all. Capitalists
    seem to think their principles will apply to all. Uploaders often fail to
    consider augmentationists. The extropian/wta community will split just as
    Christianity did. The profound differences on notions go on and on: corporal
    and capital punishment, protection, right to life, stage of sentiency, right
    to vote, brakes on population growth. Currently the forums are great for
    debate and technical information from experts, which is super, and help keep
    us focussed in a world full of mortal orientation, but at some stage in the
    future I'll be able to join the "upwinger, pro-choice, pro-protection,
    pro-growth" mob as well.
