    Due to a number of factors (slow economy etc) I've had a lot of "time on my
    hands" lately and have even gone so far ( ewwwwww) as to actually watch some
    TV.

    Not the "shows" minds you....I'm not that far gone yet...rather watch garbage
    rot....but the so so called "news" programs. A bit.....

    One of my long standing "theories" has been confirmed.....again.

    That is......the "news" media care nothing about informing the
    public.....their goal is "re-forming" the public....their apparent purpose
    is to mold public opinion.

    I acquired this notion decades ago during the Viet Nam thing when what I was
    seeing first hand in South East Asia didn't match what I was seeing second
    hand on the tube......

    The latest bit of data that bolsters my theory ..........

    Former President Clinton gave a speech yesterday at the Erwin Center here in
    Austin Texas. There was quiet a respectable number of protesters. It seems
    that a few folks blame Clinton for the current mess....

    This was give VERY LITTLE coverage on the "media"....in fact I was made aware
    of it mostly via word of mouth and local radio.

    HOWEVER.........war protestors? I hear lots and lots of war
    protestors.....a whole hour was devoted to an "interview" for example....
