    De-obliquifying for anyone who needs it: The expression in question is often
    quoted as part of a famous Neville-Chamberlain-smiling-paper-waving quote,
    shortly before Hitler hit Poland like a ton of bricks.

    Some sources quote him saying "for" rather than "in"....

    "My good friends, for the second time in our history, a British Prime Minister
    has returned from Germany bringing peace with honour. I believe it is peace for
    our time...
    Go home and get a nice quiet sleep."

    O tempora...
