    My cousin, DJ Dak, (David Karla) did a gig yesterday at Melbourne's Federation Square

    in the afternoon, trance-electronic-jazz inspired music, with his line-up
    "Nanotopia", featuring sound-excerpts from the BBC video "Nanotopia"
    from 1995. Melbourne's Federation Square is the newly completed
    city heartland which last week saw 200,000 anti-war demonstrators.
    The performance was part of a sustainable living festival, so it was
    curious to hear the words "nanotechnology" reverberating in the background
    while eco-hippies spoke against GE foods in the stalls and tents. There were
    several hundred people milling around at any one time.

    Avatar Polymorph
