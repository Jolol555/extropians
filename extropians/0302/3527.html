    ### So cool - all the numbers z that are compatible with the formula
    ([xy]-(x+y)=z, such as 9, 72, etc.) are marked by the same symbol on the
    chart, and this symbol shows up on the ball as well, during each try. The
    symbol then changes on subsequent tries. Very neat, because initially you do
    not pay attention to all the other numbers (such as 2 or 73) which do not
    fit the formula, and need to cheat to see the trick.
