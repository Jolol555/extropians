    A question revisited

    Where is a list of every current option for self- enhancement, as in:

    Transhuman / Transhumanist

    Someone actively preparing for becoming posthuman. Someone who is informed
    enough to see radical future possibilities and plans ahead for them, and who
    takes every current option for self- enhancement. [Term: FM-2030, Are You A
    Transhuman?. Definition by Max More] (The word was used earlier by Damien
    Broderick in The Judas Mandala from 1982 (of which an excerpt called
    "Growing Up" was published in Galileo in 1976), and in "Transhuman 2000" by
    FM-2030 in Woman in the Year 2000 (Ed. Maggie Tripp) in the 60s or early
    70s)
