    =========================================================================

    http://www.free-market.net/

    Dear Free-Marketeers,

    We have great news for you! The International Society for Individual
    Liberty has purchased Free-Market.Net. We have long admired ISIL
    as an effective, principled libertarian organization that has built a
    network of activists in nearly 90 countries. FMN now has a new lease
    on life with the backing of a solid libertarian group that has been
    active for 30 years.

    We believe this change of ownership will actually prove a boost to
    both ISIL and FMN, a synergy that will empower both to promote
    more freedom throughout the world than ever before. Did you know
    that ISIL helped counsel the libertarians in Costa Rica who've made
    such dramatic progress in that country? That kind of impact,
    combined with the super-cost-effectiveness of FMN's internet
    operations, will lead to many good things for people who care about
    freedom.

    And that's important, because freedom seems to be in greater
    danger than it has been for many years. Fortunately, in the midst of
    crisis, there are also great opportunities emerging to advance liberty
    in America and around the world.

    So please forgive our dust while we rebuild here at Free-Market.Net,
    and get things back on track with the new FMN/ISIL team. The result
    will be a leaner, more effective freedom network, which we hope you
    will use, enjoy and support.

    If you would like to help jump-start FMN's rebirth -- and help ensure
    the continuation of the best possible services and resources for
    libertarians -- please consider making a contribution to ISIL today.
    There's no such thing as a free lunch!

    They'll put it to work immediately in keeping FMN online and putting it
    back in motion. You can make a tax-deductible contribution to ISIL
    easily, online.

    Thank you for your support -- we look forward to the greatest
    progress yet in realizing our mutually cherished dream of Freedom In
    Our Time.

    In Liberty,

    Louis James
    CEO, Free-Market.Net

    Vince Miller
    President, ISIL
