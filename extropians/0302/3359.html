    Hubert writes

    > I wondered:
    >
    > > If I should survive the global clean sweeping he
    > > has in mind,

    Now look, you are reading a huge amount into one word
    "insane" that Mike Lorrey happened to drop at the end
    of one sentence in a very short, and rather emotional,
    post.

    Even the title of this thread, which still includes
    the questionable phrase "Tim May calls for nuking of
    D.C." which I leave here, reluctantly, only to make
    my point, is highly questionable. That too was *one*
    obviously emotional phrase Tim May used at the end of
    a post. Well, please acknowledge: we all get worked
    up from time to time, and we probably should not.

    No one here has called for all pacifists to be locked
    up, and you know it. So why the tremendous exaggeration?

    > any pacifist from this list: please visit me in my
    > CLIP - CLinic (for) Insane Pacifists -

    Talk about over-reacting!

    You or Samantha also wrote

    > > > and report me if America really has
    > > > already returned to Cincinnatus
    > > > farming or if they are still busy
    > > > building new CLIPS.

    further fantasizing about CLIP, totally a
    product of your and her imagination.

    > And Samantha invited me to her cuckoo-nest cell:
    >
    > > If the world survives you can come visit me in the next room any
    > > time you like. We can compare delusions of hope, peace and
    > > global prosperity and well-being. We can get really
    > > out-of-our-minds crazy and talk about a glorious future where
    > > humans transcended their slightly evolved monkey state and
    > > prospered beyond any decent person's dreams.

    Everyone one on the *Extropians* list, for God's sake,
    agrees with these eventual goals. No one is questioning
    that. They simply contend that it won't be today or
    even next week. At best, this won't happen for decades,
    and again, you know this.

    > We also might muse about some new uploading techniques
    > that are not available yet.

    See, you think that the rest of us have just stopped
    thinking. Few of us accuse *you* of that. Give us
    a break for a while---you are letting this Iraq thing
    obsess you. (Jesus Christ. Now *I'm* starting to
    almost say you're nuts! I'm not so saying, because,
    ordinary, fully sane people do simply get carried
    away from time to time, especially when frustrated.)

    > I would really like to expand the body-mind-continua
    > of some of our war supporters into the bodies of ALL
    > wounded but survived Iraqi civilians after Desert
    > Storm II with no more arms and legs and their guts
    > being fallen out so our hawks might experience their
    > pain in a 10 second wave of encompassing sympathy.

    Gee, thanks for limiting it to ten seconds! That's
    really quite kind of you. Appreciating the extent
    to which some people's feelings have gotten clear
    out of control, that's a nice concession.

    You have to know, when you see yourself start writing
    like this, knowingly advocating greater pain and
    suffering, that something is out of kilter. Yes,
    I do appreciate that from your point of view, there
    are *ABSOLUTELY* no reasons whatsoever to invade Iraq,
    and so from your point of view we are all raving mad.
    (Is this true, or not? Or am I reading too much into
    what *you* are saying, now?) But that's what it sounds
    like.

    How do you explain to yourself wishing totally
    gratuitous suffering on others publicly?

    (I admit that fleeting thoughts of revenge animate
    the thoughts of most of us from time to time, but
    most of us stay in better control!)

    > Power to the insane!

    Try to stop yourself before you write things like
    this. I mean, for all I know, some of those memes
    could really get back to you.

    > Fight the assholicity of the Iraq war plans!

    Okay, but remain calm and try to stay relaxed and
    rational. (And the same for your opposite numbers
    on the other side of the argument---like, hopefully,
    me.)

    Lee
