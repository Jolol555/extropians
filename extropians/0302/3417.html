    >Do you expect Saddam to ship it [WMD] by boat

    Federal Express.

    >I don't think that Iraq is telling the tuth, but

    >free political activities.

    Force? How?

    > In other words: Do you have any proof for your claim?

    I don't need any proof for my "claim" because in my last post I only wrote
    about weapons that Iraq has admitted having or things the UN inspectors
