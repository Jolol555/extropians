    > Robert Sheckley, Bob Shaw, John Sladek, Arthur Schopenhauer and Douglas

    The sci-fi/comedy genre is one which I expect will be
    an upcoming favorite, as we have seen a resurgence of
    comedy/drama in the past several years. A sci-fi
    comedy which I saw on a plane recently surprised me
    that I had not seen it discussed on extropians: s1mOne.

    http://www.s1m0ne.com/

    I howled at this film, funniest I have seen in years,
    my favorite since As Good As It Gets. Extra treats
    in this: extropian themes (well, sorta), Winona Ryder
    playing herself, complete with sly references to her
    legal troubles, Al Pacino playing a good guy, kind of
    a wimpy good guy at that. I thought I would wet my
    diapers laughing. spike
