    "Hubert Mania" <humania@t-online.de>

    >The USA are NOT attacked by Saddam, so they have no bloody right
    >to invade his country and

    Saddam has a right to remain dictator of Iraq? I have a modest proposal,
    rather than ban discussion of Iraq let's just ban the word "right" except
    for the meaning of "correct" or the opposite of left. If you want to
    convince me that USA policy is incorrect then show me why the war will go
    badly, or show me that the occupation will be difficult, or in general just
    show me that the world will be a worse place if the war happens. All this
    talk about the rights of dictators is utter bilge. And so is listing the
    many foreign policy mistakes the USA has undeniably made in the past because
    there is only one question that is relevant, is the coming war another one?
