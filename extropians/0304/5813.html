    Actually, I didn't mean "new" in that nobody had thought of it before. I
    meant it was new to the destructive copy debate. Previous debates had ended
    up with two minds where one was destroyed. This is objectionable to some
    people. Adding this new twist to the argument, that there is only one mind
    that moves between two bodies, resolves that objection.

    Now all we have to do is work on the details.... :-)
