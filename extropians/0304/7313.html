    Cumulus Conference 2003 - Estonia Art Academy

    Friends,

    I will be in Tallinn, Estonia from May 7 through May 11th at the Cumulus
    Conference at the Estonia Art Academy.

    "CUMULUS is an European association of art, design, and media universities
    <http://tmo.uiah.fi/cumulus/>http://tmo.uiah.fi/cumulus/, and the CUMULUS
    conference audience includes educators and researchers in art, design and
    media from over 40 institutions across Europe as well as from China, Japan
    and Korea.

    "As the CUMULUS conference is titled VALID (value in design) conveys
    valuable and unique contribution Artists and Designers make in challenging
    conventional practices and assumptions by proposing new, radical and
    provocative possibilities."

    If anyone is in close vicinity to Estonia or has any information or
    suggestions for me while visiting this part of the world, please let me
    know! I've never been to Russia or Lativa -- so I'm excited to learn about
    this culture.

    Here is the URL to the conference program http://cumulus.artun.ee/program.html

    I'm sure I could learn a lot from Amara Graps and Karlis Ullis about this
    part of the world!

    Best,
    Natasha
