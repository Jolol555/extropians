    It may take me forever, but I eventually get to things that I should do. The
    various webmasters of transhumanist and transhumanist-interest websites
    don't talk to each other enough or in a common forum. Promoting more
    cross-talk and more collaboration to extend, enhance and improve our
    community is a goal for the coming year. Eventually, this will lead into my
    plans for a Grand Distributed Transhumanist Online Information Replicator of
    some sort. For now, we just need to better tie the threads that hold our
    online community together -- because it makes expansion easier.

    Anyway, since my work and the Longevity Meme have been keeping me busier
    than I'd like (and since people have been prodding me about declarations of
    intent made earlier in the year), I have taken the intermediate first step
    of creating an access-by-approval yahoo group for transhumanist webmasters.
    We should be chatting more. Things will come out of a forum that are of
    mutual benefit to sites at all points on the power-law distribution of
    traffic, one would hope.

    http://groups.yahoo.com/group/transhumanist-webmasters/

    So if you fall into the category of "person who runs or helps to run a
    transhumanist website," join up.

    Pass this missive on as you see fit. I'm sure I haven't bcc'd everyone I
    should.
