    That's a great soundtrack. Blues and Bluegrass are not my favorite genres,
    (I'm a classic rock fan, originally from the San Francisco Bay Area), but I
    couldn't resist buying "Oh Brother" for my music library. It's the only
    movie soundtrack I've ever purchased.

    In the first week of this month I was in Cancun on vacation, where I
    performed on karaoke night at one of the resort cocktail lounges (after a
    couple of cold cervezas). I sang "Sympathy for the Devil" by the Rolling
    Stones. The balding 50-ish guy in charge of the karaoke machine thought my
    Mick Jagger impression was great but I don't think it struck a chord with
    the mostly 20-something crowd there on spring break. It was fun, though.
    That's my kind of music and to hell with what those kids thought. :)
