    When one goofs at the keyboard it is called a
    typo, so if one goofs the spoken word, I suppose
    it is called a talko. There was a hilarious
    talko on the radio news today. The newscaster
    was commenting on a flight that was quarantined
    on the runway in San Jose Tuesday for fear of SARS.

    Background: PC-speak has mostly removed the term
    "American" from the language, since it refers
    to two continents and over a dozen nations. "U.S."
    has replaced "American". At the same time the
    term "black" when referrning to humans has been
    universally replaced with "African-American" to
    such an extent that whenever one sees the term
    American, it is almost always preceded by African-.
    Now we have become accustomed to it.

    The radio newsman said "Regarding the African-American
    Air... ahhh, rather, the American Airlines flight
    quarantined in San Jose..."

    I thought I would wet my diapers.

    I would not be surprised if American Airlines
    changes their name soon.

    spike
