    My two were homeschooled throughout. (They're now 15 and 18). As for
    topics, it's easy. Whatever they're interested in, whenever they're
    interested in it. You'll find it's first physical, touchy-feely things.
    Then they'll go mad for stories, fantasy, music, art etc, then they'll
    start getting into facts and figures. Then they start driving you mad
    wanting a lot more input than you can possibly provide, but it gets to
    be a lot of fun at the same time.
    There are some marvellous organisations that can help you; one of the
    best is Education Otherwise, I thing their website is
     www.educationotherwise.edu but I am not sure; I'm sure it's on google.
    Good luck.
