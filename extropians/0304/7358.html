    > >>http://www.second-opinions.co.uk/athletic_diet.html

    Holy Ambiguous Antithesis, Batman! Yes, I did definitely DID misread
    the page.

    It described "The Wrong Approach" at the top. This "wrong" approach
    continues with sub headers and different paragraphs scrolling down
    through multiple screens. It was not clear to me that subsequent
    headings continued the wrong approach. I thought the wrong approach
    had ended and that each new heading started a new topic. I did not
    realize that this was an entire series of bad examples. It sounded
    so reasonable and sincere, and matched a lot of common nutritional
    fallacies out there.

    Luckily, I only slightly embarrassed myself by accurately blasting
    the page. It would have been worse had I agreed with this page and
    then found out it was an example of "the wrong approach."
