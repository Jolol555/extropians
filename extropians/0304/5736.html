    > What questions would people here pose to a deity claimant (or surrogate)?

    1) Why have you always existed rather than always not existed?
    2) Can you make a rock so heavy you can't lift it?
    3) Why is believing in something for no good reason (faith) a virtue?
    4) The ultimate answer is 42 but what is the question?
    5) You already know my next question, what is the answer?
