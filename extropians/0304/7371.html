    Are you ready for the next revolution in evolution?

    Join us in celebrating the evolution of Betterhumans and the relaunch of
    Betterhumans.com.

    We're holding a relaunch party on May 15, 2003, 8:00 PM to 3:00 AM at
    Element Bar (http://www.elementbar.com), 553 Queen Street West, Toronto.

    Two floors, lush slipstream design, and the latest in digital sound
    provide the ultimate sensorial experience.

    Downstairs, Dylan t., Eon Rae and Squid will be spinning house, techno and
    electro. Upstairs, Ruby Jay will be taking care of the funk. $5 cover.

    So just what is Betterhumans?

    Betterhumans explores and advocates the use of science and technology for
    advancing humanity and continuing human progress. We help people
    understand, anticipate and direct change, empowering people to Create the
    Future (TM).

    Dedicated to having the best information, analysis and opinion on the
    future of humankind, Betterhumans has built a leading online portal for
    forward-thinking people at Betterhumans.com. We're now taking the next
    step in our development, launching an improved Website and new services.

    For more information, please contact us
    (http://www.betterhumans.com/Contact_Us/). If you haven't already,
    subscribe to our newsletter (http://www.betterhumans.com/Newsletter/) to
    get the latest Betterhumans updates.

    The time to create the future is now. But we can only show you the door.
    You're the one who has to walk through.

    Where you go from here is a choice we leave to you.
