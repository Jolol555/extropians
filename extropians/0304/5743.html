    Ok, well now we are getting somewhere -- it looks like the virologists
    are concluding what we have is a new type of coronavirus (so the
    paramyxovirus -- related to measles/mumps may be out of the picture).

    Identity of killer pneumonia bug confirmed
    http://www.newscientist.com/news/print.jsp?id=ns99993582

    So we have a "data-point" -- perhaps 6-months from a novel viral
    outbreak to nailing it down. Not a good number -- though it was
    probably doubled due to China's information suppression policies.

    (For comparison -- we have the ability to totally sequence a novel
    virus in less than a day at several major sequencing centers).

    So to respond effectively to possible novel "green goo" situations
    or bioterrorist activities we need to reduce 6-months to 1-day.

    On a negative note, according to the news this morning - the U.S.
    is apparently offering free flights out of China for all family
    members of state department officials stationed in China.
