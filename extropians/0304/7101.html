    Science Daily is reporting that they have found that the PPARd receptor
    plays a key role on fat storage & burning. If this fact can be turned
    into a drug it will likely have a significant impact on weight which
    in turn will help allow people to reduce high blood pressure and
    presumably deaths from one type of stroke known as a "cerebral embolism".
    Cerebral embolisms are about 20% of all strokes. In combination with
    Alteon's-711 drug which increases the flexibility of the arteries
    we might be starting to make a dent in this disease.
