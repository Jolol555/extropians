    Emlyn O'regan wrote:

    [gts wrote:]
    >> Concerning the meats, this gets to a central issue regarding
    >> the Atkins diet:... If an Atkins dieter decides he absolutely cannot
    >> live without his fatty butter and cheeses then he might still improve
    >> on the diet by eating only lean cuts of meat, fish and poultry.

    > That's not correct, actually. The Atkins diet requires...

    I was not stating my interpretation of Dr. Atkins' views. I was stating my
    own views. I disagree with Atkins, and on this subject I am in good company
    with his adversaries like Drs. Ornish and Pritiken.

    Imagine the Atkins diet without fatty cheese and butter and without fatty
    farm-fed meats like sausage and bacon. Still relatively low-carb, but with
    less saturated fat and more nutritious fruits and vegetables.

    That is my diet perspective. It is also the perspective of homo sapiens over
    millions of years of evolution.
