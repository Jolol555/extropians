    When "google answers" was first in beta, I decided to offer $5 to any of
    their supposed experts who could tell me what is wrong with the Doomsday
    Argument. To make it more interesting (I wanted my $5 worth) I didn't tell
    them the official name or origin of the argument. I did not want to steer
    them to any web resources. Instead I just offered the argument in my own
    words, and asked if anyone there could tell me what is wrong with the it.

    I deflected their condescending remarks and wrong rebuttals for a few days,
    until someone told my "advisor" about Nick Bostrom's webpages. I then
    received a humble official answer along the lines of "Gee, I really dunno!
    Very interesting!"

    I paid the guy his $5 anyway, just for being a good sport about it. Such
    fun. :)
