    "We have no guided chickens! A chicken did not cross the road into

    the hands of our Fedeyeen Saddam Chickens, which are trained to cross
    the road to blow themselves up on American military check points. No,
    those are not Syrian Chickens, though many good muslim chickens from

    "We have no Chickens of Mass Destruction, I know nothing about such

    Hollywood special effects stunt fabricated by the evil Bush regime.
    Such chicken stunts are typical of Americans, and beneath the
    capabilities of a superpower. I spit on the Bush regime and throw my
    shoes at Mr. Bush."

    "There are people who think the chicken crossed the road, there are
