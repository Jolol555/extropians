    Most translations have young woman in the cited verse,
    Isaiah 7:14 King James, "...behold a virgin shall
    conceive, and bear a son, and shall call his name
    Immanuel."

    Other translations say "young woman" with the son
    conceived presumably in the customary fashion, but
    without going into the gory details. Altho the
    passage is interpreted as a messianic prophecy, a
    casual reading reveals nothing to suggest that
    such a notion is appropriate.

    Im not actually suggesting that the Virgin Mary
    humped Joseph Christ's brains out on their first
    date or anything, I'm just saying.
