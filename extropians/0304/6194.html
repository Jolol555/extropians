    > Based on Schwarzenegger's recent meeting with Karl Rove we can deduce
    > several things: Arnie will be CA governor in 2-3 years. His bid for
    > President will be successful in 2008.

    Scharzenegger can't be elected President of the United States. Article II
    Section 1 of the U.S. Constitution states,

       No person except a natural born citizen, or a citizen of the United
       States, at the time of the adoption of this Constitution, shall be
       eligible to the office of President...

    http://www.law.cornell.edu/constitution/constitution.articleii.html

    Amending the Constitution requires a strong national consensus, and
    it seems unlikely to be achieved given the inevitable perception that
    the change was being made at this time in order to aid one particular
    presidential candidate. All the rival camps would oppose making the
    change, making consensus virtually impossible to reach.

    Hal
