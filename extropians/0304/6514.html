    Reason suggested http://www.unicornjelly.com/uni001.html and I
    did take a look. Oops, I was stuck in front of my browser all day
    reading - look out! :-) The worldbuilding is fun, a really good
    job of taking a weird physics and running with it to its logical
    conclusion. It is an excellent story with some interesting
    twists, and does include a kind of "fantasy transhumanism"
    (especially in the second half) that feels refreshing - if there
    are magical beasts and powers around (for whatever definition of
    magical), why not use them in enhancing ways? One can base a
    singularity on more than nanotechnology.
