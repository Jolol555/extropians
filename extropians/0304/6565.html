    Spike wrote in a different thread related to diet:

    > My notion is that in general, the evolutionary
    > mechanism of mate selection leads to a number of
    > positive feedback mechanisms, whereas survival
    > selection more often leads to negative feedback
    > mechanisms.

    I'm not sure I can agree with this, spike. Judging by the fame and fortune
    of women like Marilyn Monroe, and by her marriage to the late baseball star
    Joe DiMaggio, I think it's safe to say that many modern men are inclined to
    mate with women like her. Is this going to lead to positive feedback from
    modern mate selection? I dunno. I wince when I think of what it might mean
    for the future of the human species.
