    > " [...] the people can

    > have to do is tell them they are being attacked, and denounce the

    > danger. It works the same way in any country."

    Dirty old man, today he could easily take over the position of the resigned
    Richard Perle. May his bones by now have rotten and completely melted
    into the ground whereever they lie. I do not even want to know where.

    of them even thinking of America as being female.

    This is the true embarrassing and disgusting climax of brainwashing. Hermann
    Georing would have been proud of achieving this peak of abusing his folks
    himself.
