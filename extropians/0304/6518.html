    Lee comments, in relevant part:

    >I'm curious about the attraction of Scuba.

    Although I don't dive, I spend several hours a week (at least on the good
    weeks!) surfing. That experience suggests to me that divers might, like
    surfers, find great appeal simply in spending time in the ocean. It offers
    great beauty; I enjoy gazing at the waves and sky while I'm waiting for
    waves. It also offers thrills; only a short distance from homes and roads, I
    find myself in a wild place. Last time out, for instance, a dolphin surfaced
    near my board.

    If you like getting up close and personal with the untamed world, the ocean
    rocks.
