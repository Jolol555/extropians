    --- gts <gts_2000@yahoo.com> wrote:
    > However I think his weight loss was not a result of eating deli
    > sandwiches, per se. It was rather a result of reducing
    > his caloric intake and forcing himself to walk to the nearest Subway
    > whenever he was hungry.

    Jared ate two six inch veggie subs every day plus a normal meal, plus
    walking to and from the Subway. Don't know how big a walk that was.
    Dieticians have said that his diet was not terribly smart, but I guess
    being young and with a lot of fat to spend, he was able to swing it. It
    is possible to lose weight in all sorts of unsmart ways, ask any
    concentration camp survivor. I've lost weight eating nothing but rice,
    and I've also lost weight eating nothing but tuna, celery, and peanut
    butter. I doubt that either is very well balanced nutritionally. They
    certainly were not very exciting from the perspective of palatability,
    and they certainly are not anything within the realm of learning to eat
    smart on a sustained basis.
