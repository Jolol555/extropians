    Greg Burch wrote:

    > The important thing about Atkins is that it is an at least
    > relatively healthy way to lose weight fast enough for
    > fast-gratification Americans.

    That's true, though the first 10-15 lbs of weight-loss is mostly water, as
    ketosis kicks in. The diuretic effect of ketosis is one reason the Atkins
    diet is so popular for quick (apparent) weight loss.

    > Doing a lot of international
    > travel, I never cease to marvel at how FAT Americans are. Atkins may
    > be the answer.

    My own view is that Atkins works for weight loss because it is a reasonable
    facsimile of the paleo diet. It differs from paleo mainly in the inclusion
    of dairy products like cheeses. But who needs cheese and other dairy
    products? Cheese is essentially pure fat, and milk is little more than fat
    and sugar mixed in water. Furthermore dairy fat is mostly unhealthy
    saturated fat.

    You might be surprised at how much weight you can lose by simply eliminating
    agricultural and dairy products, without regard for anything else. The
    former is basically empty calories and the latter is, as above, mostly
    saturated fat.
