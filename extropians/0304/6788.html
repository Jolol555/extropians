    Samantha Atkins wrote:

    > Is this a claim that our paleo ancestors did not understand,
    > were not around and did not use grains and legumes that grow
    > wild?

    Widespread cultivation of grains did not occur until about 10-12 thousand
    years ago. Likewise with animal husbandry and the production dairy products.
    Most mature, unprocessed legumes are toxic or indigestible and could not
    have been a significant part of the diet of paleos.
