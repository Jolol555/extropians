    Well, I finally got a look at the May issue of GQ which has the article on
    cryonics (Cryo) & life extension (LE).

    It is written from a rather skeptical and judgmental perspective. In
    conclusion, the writer does not like Cryo & LE and is glad he will not be
    around to see the distant future.

    The writer first attends a conference on anti-aging (I think). Lemler of
    Alcor and other people speak at the conference. The writer makes some hay
    makeing fun of the speakers and their weird beliefs.

    Then he goes to Alcor. Makes fun of people there, too.

    However, the sidebars of the artiles which are very prominent, succinct and
    attractive, are very positive. They really are a plus for cryonics and LE.
