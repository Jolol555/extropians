    Robert Zubrin:

    The time has come for chickens to set a bold new goal in
    space. There can be no progress without a goal. The
    Chicken space program, begun so brilliantly with Apollo
    and its associated programs, has spent most of the
    subsequent twenty years floundering without direction. We
    need a central overriding purpose to drive our space
    program forward. At this point in history, that focus can
    only be the chicken exploration and settlement of Mars.
