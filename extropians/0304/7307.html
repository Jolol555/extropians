    > countries. I found that there were major differences in basic numbers. For

    The best place to start is with a standard reference. I usually use
    The World Factbook from the CIA:
       http://www.cia.gov/cia/publications/factbook/index.html
    Unfortunately they require Javascript to be enabled but at least
    they have all of the countries and do provide regular updates.
