    Eliezer S. Yudkowsky wrote:

    > "Adapted" is a term of art referring to the actual history of
    > an organism. The optimally healthy diet may not be the diet to
    > which we are, historically, adapted.

    Apparently you missed my qualifiers.

    In terms of natural foods and the natural ratios between those foods, (i.e.,
    excluding non-paleo diet patterns, including use of supplements and
    medicines), the natural paleodiet *is* the optimally healthy diet. As I've
    stated numerous times, Eliezer, this is NOT to say we should not deviate it
    when we have clear evidence that doing so is beneficial.
