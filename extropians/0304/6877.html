    Samantha Atkins wrote,
    > gts wrote:
    > > I'm not aware of any "greens or beans" that contain more protein
    > > than, say, a slice of turkey breast, which contains 94% protein.
    >
    > Since you obviously have no idea what you are talking about I
    > will discontinue responding to you on this subject.

    You are both over-estimating your positions. The truth is somewhere
    in-between.

    Turkey is not 94% protein. It is 66.3% water, 29.2% protein, and 3.2% fat.

    However, "greens or beans" do not contain more protein than turkey, unless
    you count seaweed and soybeans. Seaweed, tofu, wheat gluten, soybeans,
    nuts, and seeds. These easily beat most animal sources for protein.
