    I don't scuba dive or surf, but I do go snorkelling in the kelp beds just
    off the beach during the summer. It's kind of poor-man's scuba diving.
    You don't go very deep or stay down too long, but it's amazing how
    beautiful it is to be in an utterly strange world just a short distance
    away. And the few times I've had a chance to swim in tropical oceans
    were some of the best experiences of my life.

    Hal
