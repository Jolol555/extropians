    Lengthening our memory is a process some have called
    "time binding". It can also avoid needless duplication
    of effort. I know of two ways to refer to previous posts.

    One is to use the extropian archives. Go to

    http://www.extropy.org/exi-lists/

    and select the view by author or subject (I'm not sure
    that "date" and "thread" work). Unfortunately, posts
    older than a couple of months, and younger than a year
    may not be accessible for some reason.

    Robert Bradbury tipped me off to the Javien archive,
    which can be found at

    http://forum.javien.com/messages.php

    and

    http://forum.javien.com/index.php
