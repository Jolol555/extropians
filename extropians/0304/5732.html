    On Thu, 2003-04-03 at 18:03, Robert J. Bradbury wrote:
    >
    > Ok, I'm going to bring up a topic and may get thrown
    > back into the dust for doing so but here goes.
    >
    > Can anyone currently subscribed to the ExI list cite
    > an actual example where due to the list discussion they
    > "changed" their mind?

    ### Yes, I changed my mind regarding the possibility of
    anarchocapitalist society - I was reasonably convinced it couldn't work,
    now I no longer reject it, although I still have a lot of reservations.

    I also no longer think that prevention of monopolies must necessarily be
    achieved by a state intervention, at least not in certain circumstances.

    I no longer believe in the legitimacy of elective majoritarian
    democracy.

    These are changes only in the last seven or eight years, partially
    brought about by discussions on this list.

    Yes, even old folks like me can change.

    Rafal
