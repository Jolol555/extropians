    Erik Sayle wrote:

    > My own belief is that deli sandwiches are near the perfect
    > diet.

    Well, yes there is that formerly obese guy on TV who lost something like 100
    lbs of weight by eating only deli-style Subway sandwiches. He has become the
    new Subway spokesperson par excellence. His career in Subway commercials
    took a major leap forward recently when he showed himself on a TV commercial
    to be a nice guy with a nice-looking new wife and a big new house. His life
    has changed dramatically for the better now that he's back to a more normal
    weight (and now that he is earning big bucks selling deli-style sandwiches
    for Subway). I wish him well. However I think his weight loss was not a
    result of eating deli sandwiches, per se. It was rather a result of reducing
    his caloric intake and forcing himself to walk to the nearest Subway
    whenever he was hungry.

    -gts
