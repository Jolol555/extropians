    Will Wiser wrote:

    >I am currently studying nutrition.<

    I am currently writing a small book with a friend on my own fitness
    routine. When I obtained a fitness certification, nutrition was essential
    to the curriculum. My suggestion is to take the ACSM (American College of
    Sports Medicine), where I studied, courses on nutrition. It claims to be
    the gold standard.

    Otherwise, take any online course on nutrition. Today, I prefer online or
    distance learning to attending classes. You can get the basics on
    nutrition and get a certification that does not require being an "expert."
    If you don't want to be a nutritionist, just do the online certification.
