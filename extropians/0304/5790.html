    Hal Finney wrote,
    > So we have two scenarios:
    >
    > 1A: Do a duplication and leave the original intact
    > 1B: Do a duplication and destroy the original
    >
    > and you express a strong preference for 1A over 1B.

    Yes. Or more specifically, I, the original copy expresses a strong
    preference for 1A over 1B. The duplicate copy has no strong preference
    either way.

    > What about these two scenarios:
    >
    > 2A: Do a duplication and leave the original intact
    > 2B: Don't do a duplication, just leave the original alone

    I don't care either way. I see no effect on the original in either case. I
    see no basis to prefer one over the other. It would be cool and interesting
    to have another twin, but it makes little difference to me.
