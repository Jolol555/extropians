    April 14, 2003
    Lab Decodes Genes of Virus Tied to SARS
    By DONALD G. McNEIL Jr.
    http://www.nytimes.com/2003/04/14/science/sciencespecial/14INFE.html?pagewanted=print

    Looks like it is an ~30,000 base genome largely unrelated to
    known coronaviruses.

    Unfortunately, just when it looked like this was mostly contained...

    April 14, 2003
    Reports of Virus Jump Sharply in China
    Erik Eckholm,
    http://www.nytimes.com/2003/04/14/international/asia/14CND-SARS.html

    It looks like it has spread to more provinces outside of Guangdong
    meaning that containment may be very difficult.
