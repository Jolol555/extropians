    Lee Corbin wrote:

    > ...How long would it take God, playing fairly, to
    > actually persuade you that He exists, and that all the
    > traditional stories in the Bible are true...?
    >
    > Me, about fifteen minutes. Lee

    For me, less than that. All I *ever* asked of god
    was one really really convincing halucination, vision,
    or even a good lucid dream, in which he showed up and
    explained a few of the most basic questions. The answer
    to that prayer was always hell no. At one point I even
    asked satan for similar indication of his existence,
    but the results were the same. Eventually the entire
    theory had to go into the bit bucket for lack of
    supporting evidence.

    spike
