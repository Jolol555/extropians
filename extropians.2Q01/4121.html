Hi all,

Yesterday evening it suddenly struck me that it has been a year since Sasha
Chislenko passed away. As a result, I also realized that it had been
nearly a year since I had looked through his homepage. I did so this
morning, and it is a reminder of how much the transhumanist community lost
with his death. I encourage you all --particularly those of you who are
new to transhumanism-- to go to http://www.lucifer.com/~sasha/home.html and
look at the comprehensive content and writings which are enclosed within.

Though I am very much looking to Extro-5 and know that it will likely be
the best one yet, I can't help but think that something will definitely be
missing.
