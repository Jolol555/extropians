Hello,

For all of you who have or are in the process of making plans to attend
Extro-5, I have some important information to pass on.

I checked with the hotel today, and of the 30 room block which we had
reserved, 28 rooms have been taken. I was fortunate enough to increase the
amount of rooms in the block to 40 at the conference rate; however, that
only leaves 12 rooms currently available for the Friday and Saturday
evening of the conference. As a result, to avoid disappointment, I
encourage you to register for the conference and reserve your room as soon
as possible. Room sharing is possible for those of you who wish to cut
costs and/or ensure that you will have a room. If you are interested in
doing this, please send me an e-mail directly, and I will match you up with
another person who also wishes to do so.

For those of you who have already reserved a room at the Hilton and have
not heretofore given me your confirmation number, please do so at your
soonest convenience so that I can determine the number of rooms accounted for.

Additionally, there have already been thirteen reservations made for the
Sunday evening at the Hilton. Currently the Sunday reservations are not at
the conference price, though I am awaiting a response from the events
coordinator at the Hilton as to whether or not the conference price will be
extended to the Sunday.

Once again, I urge those of you who plan on staying at the Hilton for the
duration of the conference to both register for Extro-5 and reserve your
rooms as soon as possible.

If you have any questions, concerns, comments etc. regarding any of the
above, please feel free to contact me directly at e_shaun@extropy.org.

                                                Sincerely,
