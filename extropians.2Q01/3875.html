On Sat, May 05, 2001 at 01:22:50PM -0400, Spudboy100@aol.com wrote:
> I don't see why this is not possible? My only concern would be, is a cosmic
> collapse, in the Charles Meissner sense of it, necessary to recover all the
> photons, that would be used for a phase conservation re-gathering (in a
> reverse determinism fashion) so, as, to re-create all past information?

Well, in an Omega Point you don't need every photon, just enough energy.
Tipler has never claimed the reconstruction of past history requires any
information about it! His solution (and probably the only really
workable one) is to reconstruct all possible pasts, the "real" one is
hidden in the middle of them and you will have restored a huge number of
"what if" worlds.
