Last night, driving home late, I tuned to Art Bell's
syndicated talk show on San Francisco's KSFO (AM 560),
which usually
features marginal or outright kooky themes (ufology,
crank anthropology, remote viewing, etc.), for
entertainment, and was shocked to hear someone
expounding eloquently and knowledgeably on
nanotechnology issues.

Turns out it was Mark Pesce. Below is a message
to those of us who signed onto his personal mailing
list during the show.

His website:
http://www.playfulworld.com/

Below is embedded a link to the 4-hour RealAudio
stream of a recording he made of the show.

Enjoy,
Russell
