"J. R. Molloy" wrote:
>
> People have a tendency to avoid depressed individuals.

This is true to some extent, of course, but my experience
is that the reverse effect predominates. The last thing a
depressed person wants is to be around other people,
particularly if the **source** of the depression is the constant
barrage of minor insults to self esteem that is the inevitable
concomitant of playing the social game.
