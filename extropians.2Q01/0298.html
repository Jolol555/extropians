                        * * * * * * * *
You are invited to a reading of my book _Create/Recreate:The 3rd Millennial
Culture_ this Friday evening, March 9th, at 8:00 PM to 8:30 PM, at Borders
Bookstore in Santa Monica. (Location below). Please come early!

                        * * * * * * * *

I will be focusing on key issues in my four topical essays "Art Science
Blend", "SEX", "Ageless Thinking" and "Sensorial Mix" which have influenced
the evolution of my current 3D animated future body net.art design known as
"Primo 3M+ 2001".

As Marvin Minsky says about this future body design, "Wonderful idea!" and
Vernor Vinge concludes, "One thing I might suggest adding to the list are
backup and hot swap options (I'm always safety conscious :-)".
