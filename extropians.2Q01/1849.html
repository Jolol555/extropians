          Negotiation strategies for reasonable people
                        G. Richard Shell
                       ISBN 0-670-88133-3

This is an excellent book on a subject that could be very important
to us in the future. Mr Shell is the director of the Wharton
Executive Negotiation workshop.

As an additional incentive, I found this in the bargain book
section of my local Barnes and Nobel bookstore, you probably can
too.
