Seattle area transhumanists will meet this Saturday, April 7, at 1:00 PM
in the King County Library in Bellevue, WA. We'll be in Meeting Room 4.
There's a map at http://www.kcls.org/brl/bellgen.html#address. Since it
has been several months since we last convened, there is no agenda for
this meeting and we'll probably revert back to the early stages of
brainstorming a course of action to promote transhumanism locally, as
well as the usual spirited exchange of ideas on various topics.

We also have an e-mail list which can be found at
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/NWExtropians.

I look forward to seeing friends from previous meetings as well as
discovering new ones. If you're anywhere in the general vicinity, please
come on down, we need all the help we can get. With no E. Shaun or Gina
on board this time, it's just not going to be the same.
