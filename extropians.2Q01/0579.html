Robin writes:
> Someone who told
> their "holodeck" to take them back to a party at the millennium might
> end that simulation when the party got boring, regardless of how
> interesting the world as a whole was getting.

This raises the possibility that not only do Gods exist, but that they
walk among us. Perhaps that is another reason to be nice to others;
the fact that you are alive and not a zombie means that you are more
likely to run into God without knowing it.
