Friends,

I plan to host a transhuman event in LA this summer. There are various
reasons for producing the event: Getting the many groups of transhumans
together; (2) discussing the opposition to our views on technologies and the
potential ideological conflicts; (3) honoring FM-2030; (4) discussing new
ways to deal with the impact our culture is having on society; (5)
exchanging creative and inventive ideas about our future.

Please let me hear from you. I'd like to make this event one that those who
want to attend will be able to attend, so send me the best dates for you and
I'll work around your schedules to find the best date possible.
