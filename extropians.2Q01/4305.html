Science-Week (http://scienceweek.com)is a (not terribly expensive) pay-for
weekly e-mail periodical, that I have a subscription to. Recently Science-
week has introduced a program whereby every subscriber can designate a
second person to receive a copy of the e-mail for free, so long as the
primary subscriber continues their subscription. I don't know of anyone
who would benefit from a free subscription, so I'm asking the extropians.
If you are interested in a free subscription to science-week, or know
someone who is, let me know. First come, first served.

PS:> I currently have a 3 year subscription, but I don't guaratee that
I'll renew when that term has expired.
