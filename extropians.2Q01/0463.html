Hi all,

We are pleased to announce that Ronald Bailey, science correspondant at
Reason magazine, and author of several books including _Ecoscam: The False
Prophets of Ecological Apocalypse_ (available from Amazon) will be speaking
at Extro-5 this June. Ronald Bailey recently received acclaim on this list
for his talk at the AAAS conference, as well as his recent Reason article
"What Cancer Epidemic." Though more details will be available on the
conference website in the next few days
(http://www.extropy.org/ex5/extro5.htm), Mr. Bailey will be speaking on the
general topic of biotech policy.
