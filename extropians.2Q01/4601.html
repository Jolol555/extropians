The Singularity Institute has just published a major site upgrade,
including three new introductory articles:

"What is General Intelligence?"
  http://singinst.org/seedAI/general.html
"What is Seed AI?"
  http://singinst.org/seedAI/seedAI.html
"What is Friendly AI?"
  http://singinst.org/friendly/whatis.html

The document "Coding a Transhuman AI", which formerly contained "Friendly
AI" as a subpaper, has been renamed "General Intelligence and Seed AI".
"Friendly AI" has been renamed "Creating Friendly AI" and split off into a
separate paper. Their new locations are:
  http://singinst.org/GISAI/
  http://singinst.org/CFAI/
The old locations, http://singinst.org/CaTAI/* and
http://singinst.org/CaTAI/friendly/*, now redirect to the new locations.
