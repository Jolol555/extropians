> A posthuman or SI that constantly continuously grows would eventually
> know everything in this universe that is possible to know. If it
> managed to transcend space/time it would be effectively omnipresent.
> Having all knowledge, time and materials at hand it would effectively
> be omnipotent. What is missing here?

enough bits at t to be able to accurately represent itself at t.
