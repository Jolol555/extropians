I just saw the April article "In Prima Personna: Voglio Vivere Fino a 120
Anni" (by Gloria Mattioni) in the April issue of Marie Claire Italian
high-fashion magazine.

The article is a 3-page piece with pictures which is basically a profile of
my work and life -- covering my current project "Primo 3M+2001" and
transhumanism.

I think it is excellent coverage of transhumanity and extropian ideas in
such a high-profile magazine. (Also in the issue is a story on Ed Harris
and the making of "Pollock".)
