Personally, I think <MARQUEE> is worse than <BLINK>. Try,

    <MARQUEE>Hello World!</MARQUEE>

However, these have nothing on Internet Explorer's "transition
effects". Try this,

    <meta http-equiv="Page-Exit"
        content="revealTrans(Duration=1.0,Transition=23)">
    <body bgcolor="#ff0000">
    <a href="http://www.extropy.org">CLICK HERE!</a>

The above HTML won't validate but it *will* supply you with
endless hours of fun.
