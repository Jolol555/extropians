Max More wrote,
> If the Solar System Simulator used could be somehow plugged into
> Natasha's page at: <http://www.extropic-art.com/extropic.htm>

Try putting the following html paragraph into Natasha's website:

        <p align="center">
        <a href="http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/cassini/english/where/">
        <img border=0 src="http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/cassini/today/cas.jpg"
alt="Views from Cassini">
        </a>
        </p>

This will insert a single image showing four views from
Cassini<http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/cassini/today/cas.jpg>. When the image is
clicked, it links to the Cassini Present Position site
<http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/cassini/english/where/>.
