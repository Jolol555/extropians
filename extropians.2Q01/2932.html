lördagen den 21 april 2001 20:09 Alex Future Bokov wrote:
> Though in the anthropological sense, politics means the negotiation,
> competition, and intrigue among intelligent agents which looks like
> it's here to say. Of course SI is correct, transhuman sentiences will
> be above that type of politics as well.

Why would they? Even completely rational agents might have different values
or competing goals. Imagine bigendian and littlendian tiplerite SIs
struggling with control over the collapse of a closed universe: it doesn't
matter which direction collapses the fastest, but both got started on
opposing projects simply because they were not in causal contact and now risk
losing a lot if the other collapse scheme is implemented.
