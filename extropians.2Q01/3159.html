EXTRO-5: Shaping Things to Come
San Jose, California, June 15-17, 2001

I am pleased to announce the addition of another illustrious presenter for
the conference: Prof. Hal Varian.

Prof. Varian is Dean of the School of Information Management and Systems at
the University of California at Berkeley. He is also a Professor in the
Haas School of Business, and a Professor in the Department of Economics. He
is known for his deep expertise in information economics.

Prof. Varian will participate in the session "Business Architectures in the
Information Economy" along with Steve Flinn, CEO of ManyWorlds, Inc. -- the
leading information economy strategist.

You can find Hal Varian's writings here:

http://www.sims.berkeley.edu/~hal/
