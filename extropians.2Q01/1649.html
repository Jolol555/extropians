While perusing an art show at my local college I came across a work
which was a humorous spoof of a cryonics technical diagram. It
showed a cutaway of a patient in suspension who was floating not
in the usual liquid nitrogen but coca-cola.... Also, there were
hotdogs floating around inside for good measure. Text accompanied
the illustrations to explain things...

I am going to try to track down the artist to interview them and
also if possible get a picture of the artwork online so you can
all see it. I really wonder where the creator of this masterpiece
got introduced to cryonics and what exactly prompted them to do it.

best wishes,

John

P.S. Mentioning coca-cola and cryonics together gave me an idea...
Now, doesn't a dewar look an awful lot like a soda can? Someone call the marketing department at Pepsi!!
