Dear list members,

this is a reminder for all of you who might consider attending
TransVision 2001 in June, but have not registered yet: if you plan to
stay at the conference hotel (Novotel), your reservation has to be
made before May 31 (through the registration form at
http://www.transhumanismus.de/TV2001/register.html), since we have a
number of rooms on hold until that date. Afterwards, you would have to
make your own arrangements for accomodation. You can, of course, still
register for the conference itself.

Best wishes,

Frank
