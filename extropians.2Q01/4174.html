Brian D Williams wrote:
>
> 3) I use a standard QWERTY keyboard because that's what came with
> the machine, and I don't type fast enough for it to make a
> difference (60 words a minute tops.)

Dvorak doesn't require a special keyboard; you just download a Dvorak
keyboard and drag it into your system folder (MacOS) or go to "Properties"
in the "Language" panel in the "Keyboard" control panel (Win).

Besides which, it's not the typing speed, it's the effort.
