AP Corrects Cell Implant Story
By The Associated Press,
In a story about experimental brain cell implants to treat Parkinson's
disease (news - web sites), The Associated Press reported erroneously on
March 7 that researchers had used fetal stem cells, which are master cells
that can develop into many different kinds of cells. Actually, the
researchers used fully developed fetal brain cells.
