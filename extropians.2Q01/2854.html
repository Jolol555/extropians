> What I am dying to know about: are there any personality
> correlations with political beliefs? If they exist, they
> have to be mild, but I am slowly coming to think that they
> do exist.
>
> Lee Corbin
>
It might be interesting to look at the political beliefs of twins in where
the twins are adopted by different families. I think the general consensus
is that genes are a greater determinant of personality that most
psychologist thought. If the twins have similar political beliefs we might
have an argument for biological predispositions for political beliefs.
