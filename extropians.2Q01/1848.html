* Guide to the Technologies of Concealed Weapon and
Contraband Imaging and Detection

This guide describes the technology and applicability of the
concealed weapon and contraband imaging and detection system
(CWCIDS). The guide will assist the law enforcement and
corrections community and other potential users to better
understand the operation and limitations of CWCIDS
technology. It also includes the type of information
obtained by the detection system, safety and radiation
exposure, and recommendations for performance standards.

"Guide to the Technologies of Concealed Weapon and
Contraband Imaging and Detection" (NCJ 184432) is available
on the NCJRS Web site at:

http://www.ncjrs.org/pdffiles1/nij/184432.pdf.
