Tito: ... NASA (should) allocate one or two seats on the shuttle for
citizen astronauts from all sorts of different fields: writers,
composers, poets, journalists, artists -- people that could
experience space and bring it back to the people on Earth.
CNN: What is your compromise with NASA?
Tito: Well, if I break it (space station), I have to buy it. So, if I
break the whole station I guess I'm going to have to buy the whole
station. And that's perfectly reasonable.

[Easy for him to say.]
