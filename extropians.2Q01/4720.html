>
> Yes, I see your point. Isn't one group enforcing their will onto another group
> what the Democrats and Socialists and Big Government are all about.

One group enforcing their will onto another group is what *all* human
politics is about, except in very rare and very precious cases. It
understates the scope of the problem to focus your dislike on a few
prominent archetypes, and certainly the Republicans are every bit as much
within the circle of human politics as the Democrats.
