One word of explanation, so you will understand if we meet
at Extro5. One of the curious consequences of CR is that
one feels cold all the time, even in a room where others are
comfortable. Perhaps it has to do with the very high surface
area to volume ratio. My solution: wool suits. If I wear one
at the meetings, you will know that I am not trying to make
a fashion statement, merely trying to stay warm.

On the other hand, when sushi is on the menu, all thoughts
of CR go out the window, and a sincere attempt is made
by me to devour every swimming creature ever to evolve.
