As you may have already heard, Pacific Gas and Electric
declared chapter 11 bankruptcy yesterday. Our so-called
governor Gray "Red" Davis declared the move on the part
of PG+E as "selfish" for it "does not have the interest of the
people of this state at heart."

Well, imagine that: a *business* that does not have the interest
of the people (tm) at heart. Why those selfish bankrupt bastards,
one would think all they care about is making money! Ample
justification for governmental seizure of their assets, eh Red?
