Hello,

To help some of you out with total costs of conference attendance, we are
now accepting requests for room sharing. As our conference discounted room
rates are $119 per night for both one and two beds, this will cut rooming
costs by half. If you are interested in doing this, please let me know at
e_shaun@extropy.org. Confidentiality will be maintained if requested. Be
aware that all other attendees will have to book their own rooms as per our
request. Book early to ensure you receive the conference rate. For more
information, you can either check out the conference website at:

http://www.extropy.org/ex5/extro5.htm

or contact me at the above address.

One additional point I should make is that the early-registration discount
will end in three weeks time, on April 1st. To avoid paying the higher
rate, please register before this time.
