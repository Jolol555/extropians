Spike Jones wrote:

> What we need instead ... is a table somewhere in which
> anonymous volunteers could rate individual posts with a
> simple number between 0 and 5 for their extropy value.

What a chore! Where would these angels come from, with
the discernment to make such judgments (but nothing better
to do with all that time), the determination to actually wade
through **all** the posts, and the discipline to read them
carefully enough to give each of them a fair shake
(rather than a quick, impressionistic skim). Surely the
burden would take most of the pleasure out of subscribing
to the Extropians'.

Sounds like a full-time job (for pay) to me, rather
than "volunteer" work! ;->

Jim F.
