Yes, I know about NLP, but I think it is a little expensive to learn.
Reading books may not give you enough information because NLP also involves
body language and your voice too.

If you want to take a seminar, I wouldn't suggest taking it from anybody
else but the creator of this field Richard Bandler, and it costs about $3500
for the first level.

I have read many books about this subject and I think it helped me a lot in
my life and my relationships but I still feel something is missing. As soon
as I get my PhD and make some money, I am planning to attend a seminar.
