What do we mean by "brainpower"? When psychologists give IQ tests they
measure the result/product/solution of mental ability. These products
occur in domains like those of reasoning, memory, spatial-visual and
verbal. We can draw profiles now to compare the best of the mind children
with the best of their creators. At least in chess the mind children
win. How about the other measured products of intelligence?
