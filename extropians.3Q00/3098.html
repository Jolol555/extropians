I forget who posted it, but someone on this list had an excellent suggestion
of genetic engineering to cause sterilization so that the species is
naturally sterile, and then taking a pill which will temporarily induce
fertility when you want to have children.
There is the worry that this just means the people who are too careless (or
something else) to get the procedure are the ones whose genes will be passed
on to future generations. I agree - but who cares? What's important is me
and my control of my own self and destinty, not the future of the species.
