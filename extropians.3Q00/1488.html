HEADLINE: Doctors, Farmers, Environmentalists, Natural Food Store Owners, and
  Chefs to Demonstrate Against Genetically Engineered Foods

BODY:
      News Advisory:
      Note: Revised Advisory with Satellite Feed coordinates and photo
   opportunities.
      Genetically Engineered Food Alert, a coalition of consumer and health
   advocates, will launch its nationwide campaign against genetically
   engineered foods with press events in 22 locations including a Safeway
   grocery in Washington, D.C. Activists will demonstrate outside a local
   Safeway to inform customers about food corporations whose products contain
   genetically engineered (GE) ingredients and urge these companies to remove
   them immediately.
      HIGH PROFILE CORPORATIONS TO BE TARGETED AT THE EVENT
      Where:
      Safeway grocery store, 1855 Wisconsin Ave. NW
      When:
      July 19, 2000, 1 pm
      Who:
      David Wallinga, M.D., Physician and Senior Scientist, Inst. For
   Agriculture and Trade Policy (IATP )
      Larry Bohlen, Director, Health and Environment Programs, Friends of
   the Earth
      John Friedrich, local organic food distributor food security activist
