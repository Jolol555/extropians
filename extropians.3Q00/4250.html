Extropians may wish to scan their local PBS listings for two series
by Professor Robert Connolley of UNC chapel hill titled
"Introduction to Macroeconomics", and it's companion series
"Introduction to Microeconomics."

Sadly these are not available on video tape (or better DVD) nor is
there a book available.

Some of the best on PBS since "The Mechanical Universe."
