Lee Daniel Crocker wrote,
>There are different routes to success, and it should not be
>surprizing that different attempts to measure qualities of
>diverse people will come up with different answers for them.

That being the case, we need not worry that a super-intelligent robot may
threaten civilization.
Forget the Singularity.
If it can't form good support groups, establish trust and relationships, and
rise up the ranks of its profession to be rewarded, then it will just have to
settle for odd jobs and second class status, no matter how smart it is.
