Robert Bradbury wrote:
If the luddites win and we have to launch cryoships to Alpha centauri,
then we might as well take Jupiter & Saturn with us.

All I'm looking for is energy cost -- what would that be if we
wanted to take Jupiter from our Sun to Alpha Centauri (start
& stop costs on both ends), in say 100 or 1000 years?

About the asteroids, I think we *should* be able to claim them but
I'm not sure that is currently allowed under Space Law. Tom McKendree
would know more as I think he has studied this.
(end)

Robert, by the standards of 22nd+ century peoples I may be considered a total luddite for saying this but... LEAVE THE DANG PLANETS WHERE THEY'RE AT!!!

I will still probably be in my basic biological form(with hopefully many upgrades though) at the time you envision and wearing a t-shirt which says "We have to stop somewhere!"

Of course you will all laugh at me for one billionth of a second and than go on with whatever projects uploaded and super-enhanced minds do... like dismantling Jupiter and Saturn!

John ;)
