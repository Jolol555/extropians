"David Lubkin" <lubkin@unreasonable.com>

> One of my side-lines, that I mentioned here a while back, is writing
> books for children, primarily aged 4 to 8, on extropian topics. My
> scientific reviewers love them, but I haven't found a publisher yet.

        Way to go! Good luck with finding a publisher! I'd sure love
to get a copy of anything you might have on those lines. Please keep
me posted about your work and progress!

        If you ask me, this kind of work is the most important work
any of us extropians could do. Our children are depending on us to
save them from this deathist society. How will we feel if we are
resurrected from cryonic preservation, and see how much we failed so
many of our children and how much more we could have done to save so
many more of them via this kind of work for our children?

        We are dependent on them to resurrect us, but many of them are
also depending on us! We are all dependent on each other.

                Brent Allsop
