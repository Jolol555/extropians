Eliezer pointed out:

> http://transhuman.ai has a great picture of Death in a crosshairs, with the
> words "Acting in Self Defense" underneath. I think that'd go great on a
> T-Shirt.

No... it's on the right track, but you don't notice the cross hairs at
first glace... and even if you do notice them, without the glowing
transhuman, it's not hopeful enough. Even if you DO notice the
crosshairs, it gives the impression to people who don't think it's
possible to fight back that the sentiment is "Rage against the dying
of the light!" instead of "We can do it!"
