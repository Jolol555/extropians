Fellow Sushi lovers,

As I understand it they are ravenous and undescriminating in the matter of
foodstuffs, and they have the ability to exit the water for short
excursions over dry land. So, if we can get them to eat zebra mussels,
water hyacinth, and a range of government officials, then slither into the
kitchen, through some breading material, and into the hot fat, why, we'll
all be in very good shape.

The best way to solve a problem is to redine it as an opportunity.

All hail the asian swamp eel!

As I recall, the lowly chicken, now risen to icon of the McNugget Empire,
began its career in southeast asia. How long before we see the
McSwampThing Value Meal? (The McM-eel?)
