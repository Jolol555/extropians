Here is an exhibition that brings to the fore a project that
combines investing strategy and conceptual art. (Actually, with
more emphasis on the latter.)

I like this concept a lot. John Beilenberg invented a fictitious
company (Virtual Telemetrix, Inc. (fictitious ticker VT) which
includes an annual report, etc.

Many businesses are hiring consultants to do scenario planning
to conceptual various strategies. What I like about this visual/vitual
conceptualized art piece is that it's theme is so central to
our investment and dot.com culture.

His credits include receiving over 100 awards from international
and US design organizations and his work is in SFMOMA's permanent
collection.
