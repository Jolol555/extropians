Have you ever wished that you could climb inside the shell of a huge robot and
move around the world picking up cars, setting them on fire, and snapping them
in half in your jaws? Well, you can--if you own Robosaurus.
Robosaurus is a 58,000 pound robot/mechanism that transforms from an easily
towable robot rig to a rolling mechanism of destruction that stands 40 feet (13
meters) high and blows 10' streams of fire out of its "nostrils."
The "World's First Car-nivorous Monster". This fire-breathing creature,
standing twice as tall as a Tyrannosaurus Rex can lift a car higher than a
5-story building. Don't miss seeing this amazing 4-story, car eating monster!
Robosaurus is capable of picking up a full-size car and ripping it in half after
setting it on fire and/or biting into it with its jaws. Check out the picture.
That's NOT a Matchbox car. It's full size.
