Well, we'll get the CD together soon, soon (ah, soon). We did finish it,
then the studio lost our final takes (yay, love that), and since then we've
moved city, had a bouncing baby boy, all kinds of stuff going on.

Anyway, we are currently (really) working on a script for a musical based
partly on that music, very TH, about the leadup to the Singularity (and
culminating in it), lots of cool themes re: science vs nature dualism,
ethics of remaking yourself and the universe, and all kinds of other good
stuff; trying to combat the frankenstein meme set. And, of course, it's a
musical, so it's a love story.

The feedback we had about the music said over and over again "should be a
musical", so we're doing it. Don't hold your breath, it might take a while.
Meanwhile, I really want to do some more music soon (time's pretty limited
just now), so you should see something.
