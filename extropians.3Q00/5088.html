A bit of trivia: Stop lights are now being made slightly off-color. The
red is more orange-red, and the green is more bluish-green. You can really
see this when old lights and new lights are side-by-side. The idea is that
now red-green color blind people can see the different colors. They
perceive orange and blue lights instead of similar shades of gray.
