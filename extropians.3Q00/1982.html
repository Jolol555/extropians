Upon reading back over my posting, I'd like to append a little
clarification:

As a -producer- of content, I am necessarily concerned over the mass appeal
of product insofar as it means it will make more money.

However as a -consumer- of content I really don't care. I am interested in
neither conforming to the tastes of the masses or in encouraging content
creators to do so.
