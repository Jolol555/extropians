When given the opportunity, I mention that I'm a transhumanist/extropian and
of course everyone wants an explanation. Everyone in my household shares my
beliefs. However most people are too negative or too involved in their day
to day existences to care about such non-mainstream beliefs. I don't have
enough contact with people to determine whether or not I've "influenced" any
to a large extent but I think I was able to get a few to at least not give
themselves death sentences. :)

Sherry
