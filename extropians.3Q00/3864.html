Hi Everybody,

For the last five years, I've been tinkering with one of the most powerful
self-metaprogramming tools I've found, known as NLP or Neuro-Linguistic
Programming!

I can't help but think of the ways that a community as creative and
intelligent as Extropians could use NLP in ways to extend life and
transform greater!

The funny think is that once people reach a certain level of skill with
NLP, they almost become Extropianistic, in their behaviors! Many only as a
personal level, but most even at a social and technological level. If
there are individuals on this list who would like to bring their personal
life to the same level of Extropy their mind is at, I welcome you to look
at this interesting technology!

Are there any other NLPers on this list!?!

Please make yourself known, and share your thoughts on
Extropianistic-Lingusitic Programming uses!

in SMI2LE,

- Eric
