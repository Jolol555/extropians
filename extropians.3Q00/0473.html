Gentlemen who wrote these (italian) pages
http://erewhon.ticonuno.it/arch/rivi/artific/tecgno/arcip.htm
http://delos.fantascienza.com/delos46/mitolog.html
are telling me they are changing their mind, about extropism. Now they think that extropians are not a *sect* as Ansa (a sort of italian Reuters) announced many months ago (but I did not read this).Of course: they still do not *embrace the future* (the best known extropic motto, in Italy) and they do not believe in *omega-points* (the second best known extropic thing, in Italy).
