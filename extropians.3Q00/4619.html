I changed my name to S. O. Terick.

What words do you use to describe yourself?
I use about 100 words, mostly from philosophy.
Thus If I had to use 1 word I would likely say Philosopher describes me
well.
I enjoy physics and aesthetics.
...and metaphysics
...and psychology
I'd be a scientist if I was not so closed minded.
I can only be sure about things when I'm omniscient.
Otherwise, there is a chance the one thing I don't know is the thing that
proves everything wrong.

Can someone tell me about the polymath list?

Edible Landscaping initiative = food surplus

Computers using scanning tunneling microscopes to read xenon atoms on zinc
plate - Eugene?

Bionic fat to insulate artificial organs from EM waves - Anders?

Can anybody get me a really interesting job I have to travel for? - Anyone?

Can some help me find food? I'm hungery; I fast too often.
...And I would'nt mind having a home either.
I will not work at Burger King;
I am a nutritionist and am morally and ethically opposed to supporting the
non-health food industry.
I'm a Taoist.
I'm poor.
...and simple
...at least I try to be.
...but becoming extropian is quite complicated.
...at least for me.

If we went from town to town, we could all elect eachother mayors.

The Prince of Orange Park
