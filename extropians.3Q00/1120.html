CURT ADAMS SAID: "...Many, many, people will be profoundly motivated to
believe that thinking machines aren't "really" thinking and that they do not

believe..."

relationships with other mammals, up to, and including, their blood-brother
the Chimpanzee.

There are people who will swear on a stack of holy books that dogs, say,
cannot think, or aren't conscious. Just as I swear on a stack of old
National Enquirers that I'm the only truly conscious entity in the Universe.

The rest of you guys are just faking it.
