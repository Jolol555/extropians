   The most important of the 1993 Waco FLIR flashes
   (flashes seen on infrared video taken over Waco)
   are the few flashes associated with objects that
   might be said to look like gunmen. Those flashes
   at Location 2 are the highlight of the gunshot
   interpretation of the Waco FLIR flashes presented
   on the popular Waco documentaries. The gunshot
   interpretation of those flashes is supported by
   several infrared experts. But are the objects
   said to be gunmen really gunmen and are the
   flashes on those objects really gunshots?

   See the most detailed analysis available
   of the premier flashes on the Waco FLIR:

   http://users.erols.com/igoddard/flirloc2.htm

   Also see The Waco FLIR Flashes homepage, which is
   coming together and includes links to all Waco-
   FLIR-flash analyses available on the web. There's
   also an exhaustive chronological listing of over
   100 media reports covering the Waco FLIR flashes:

   http://users.erols.com/igoddard/wacoflir.htm
