Lunar property right have meaning, only if they are somehow enforcable. We
find a similar situation with claims to Antartica and the Seabeds of the
world. If you have a fierce nough military, or you can squeeze your adversary
in some way, with effective trade sactions, boycotts or money withdrawl from
banks; then you have a counter-punch to interplanetary land grabs. If not,
then its laugh at the complainers because nothing will be done.
