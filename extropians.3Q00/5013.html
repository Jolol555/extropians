On Fri, 15 Sep 2000 06:53:01 +0100 Amara Graps <amara@amara.com> writes:
 
> I like books. I like the feel of paper in my hand, and reading
> from a printed page. I like bringing a volume on buses, trains,
> to cafes. I like sitting on my couch with afghan around me with
> my favorite music playing, a small reading light and maybe some
> candles and a glass of wine.

I like all these things about books, as well as the way they smell. But
they DO take up a huge amount of space; I think my books take up more
space than all my other personal property combined, even though I donate
stacks of them to the local library from time to time. I think I could
be happy with e-books if the quality of the display were a little better
than it is now. One could still enjoy pleasant haptic and olfactory
sensations by having a soft leather case for one's e-notebook.
