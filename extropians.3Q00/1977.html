SECOND ANNOUNCEMENT: 32nd DIVISION FOR PLANETARY SCIENCES MEETING

***Limited travel grants available, application deadline August 1, 2000.***
***Abstract deadline (8 PM Eastern Daylight Time), August 21, 2000.***
***Pre-registration and hotel reservation deadline, September 22, 2000.***

You are cordially invited to attend the 32nd annual meeting of the Division
for Planetary Sciences of the American Astronomical Society, on 23-27
October 2000 in Pasadena, California.

The meeting will be held at the Pasadena Convention Center, 300 E. Green
Street, in the heart of Pasadena.

The meeting is hosted by the Jet Propulsion Laboratory. The Program Chair
for the meeting is Paul Weissman. The Local Organizing Co-Chairs are Bill
Smythe and Rosaly Lopes.

The "second Announcement" Web site is now available and can be accessed
through the DPS site (http://www.aas.org/~dps), or directly at:

http://www.aas.org/dps2000/
