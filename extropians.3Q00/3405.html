> personal suspicion is that its one of our old friends that we had a
> little trouble with some time ago who made other accusations of Max. Its

Lets not jump to conclusions however. Have we any writing samples
from that distasteful affair? Recall that the trouble which refer to
was from (I think?) a posthuman group. It would be tragic indeed if
extropians and posthumans could not work together. Our philosophies
are very similar, and likely approximately equally disturbing to any
number of parties which are opposed to both groups. I suggest we
withhold any conclusions until the evidence is heavy, and recall that
our political opponents would be delighted to see posthumanists,
transhumanists, extropians, mensans, etc, waste our energies
attacking each other. spike
