For what it's worth, I vote for the original version, i.e.
"Singularitarians". It may be somewhat hard to write and pronounce, but it
looks and sounds much better than "Singularians", IMHO. As a sort of
compromise, you could create an "official" Ebonics version (quite hip too)
that can be used instead of the original if you're "in a hurry", or have
genuine trouble spelling or pronouncing "Singularitarians".
