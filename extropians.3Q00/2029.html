I'll be there, with a large group of Seattle transhumanists.

My #1 piece of advice is to read the survival guide at
http://www.burningman.com/travel/survival/

My #2 piece of advice is to bring warm clothes - at night it can be
extremely cold in the desert.

BTW, my camp will be part of the village of eVille. Among our many
projects are large signs we'll have up with detailed information on a
number of transhumanist technologies. Feel free to stop by and check us
out.
