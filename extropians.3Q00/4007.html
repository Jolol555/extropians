>
> SINGULARITARIANS.
> Does anyone else here have as much trouble pronouncing "Singularitarians" as
> I do?
> Hey, how about calling people so devoted to the Singularity by some simpler
> name?
> Singulars?
> Singles?
> Singers?
> S-Men?

This issue has been previously raised on the Singularitarian list, and
recently John Smart has requested a formal vote on the issue of whether to

let you know how it turns out.
