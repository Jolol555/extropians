Prof. R.Y. Chiao already showed that a superluminal *group* velocity is possible (Brillouin describes 5 different kinds of velocities).

http://arxiv.org/abs/quant-ph/9810031
http://arxiv.org/abs/quant-ph/9811019
http://arxiv.org/abs/quant-ph/0004047

So the essential question is not *can light travel faster than light?* but *is superluminal signaling possible?*

scerir

p.s.. *is quantum nonlocality a superluminal carrier* is (also) an interesting question, in my opinion.
