One of the best fuel crops known is hemp (Cannibis sativa & Cannibis indica). The plant is also extremely useful for fiber which is far more durable than cotton and more comfortable to wear than synthetic fibers, and the seeds are highly nutritious. The US laws against growing hemp are some of the most outrageous laws on the books, and everyone who has an interest in technological progress should do what he or she can to eliminate these laws.
