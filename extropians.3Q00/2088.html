I've been plugging this now and again for some time now - yes, it's
superb, and I second the recommendation to anyone. However - please,
please watch the subtitled Japanese version. The dubbed English dialogue
is extremely poor, and IMNSHO completely destroys the nature of the
characters and the atmosphere of the film.
