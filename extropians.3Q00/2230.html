On the italian daily paper *Libero* (something to do with the provider
called libero.it) I'm reading (pag. 17, sunday, 30.7) a half-page review
article about extropians.

The title is *Immortal and Perfect*. The writer (Alberto Mingardi) speaks
about extropians (a *clan* of transhumans) and extropic principles. And
about Max More and the life-extension.

The writer says that he met Robert Bradbury (the extropian). Many quotations
from the conversation he had with Bradbury, about genetic engineering,
ageing, cryonics, vitamine pills, etc.

In summa: the first *good* italian article about extropians.
