There's been hints here and there, and now we're confirming this as
official. The Extropy Institute is launching its own Slash site. This
will be a web-zine and portal that gets updated on a daily (sometimes
hourly) basis with the latest news of interest to Extropians. Everyone
will be able to contribute articles to this site, and comment on the
articles that are posted there. Visit us and register for a free user
account today.

http://www.extrodot.org/users.pl

...read our articles.

http://www.extrodot.org/

...contribute an article.

http://www.extrodot.org/submit.pl

...and if you need help, read our FAQ or contact us at
extrodot-admin@spock.extropy.com

http://www.extrodot.org/faq.shtml
