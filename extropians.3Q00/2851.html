Wonderful! I have found some similar catalogues, such as
http://www.xmission.com/~dnash/astrodir/eos/encpages/contents.html
(sunlike nearby stars, very nice interface and discussion) and
http://www.ari.uni-heidelberg.de/aricns/ (rather bad interface, lots
of good data). I'm as usual involved in a sf world construction
effort, and these have been quite useful.

I also recommend http://www.sky3d.com/ - I have been flying around our
spiral arm, thinking of whether I want to go for the Orion complex,
the Cygnus complex or just head for the Core once I turn into a
nanoswarm :-)
