>H. Keith Henson is mentioned in this post. It might be useful for everyone
>here to note that Mr Henson has been an outspoken critic of Scientology,
>and subject to harassment at their hands.

A cursory Usenet search finds this post on alt.religion.scientology from
barb <bwarr@pacbell.net>:

===================================
Dorothy West wrote:

[...]

> Henson is a character whose motives are rather obscure to any normal
> person. Just look at his trail of weirdness including <allegations snipped>
[...]

If you're new here, you probably wonder what this is all about. There is
a website called www.parishioners.org that treads a very fine line
bordering on libel. Scientology denies any connection with this site,
but curiously the site has responded to certain court orders given to
the cult as a whole by removing pages from the site.

In case you're wondering, Dotty here is a scientologist, and is quoting
the site's libellous accusations against Mr. Henson, a critic of the
cult. This is a feeble attempt to discredit him, and not very effective.
If you check out the site, you'll note there is no hard evidence, just
innuendo.
