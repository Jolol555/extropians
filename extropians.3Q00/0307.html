Here is the URL and full quote of President Clinton's "we all want to live
forever" quote:

http://www.whitehouse.gov/WH/New/html/conference2000-06-28.html

THE WHITE HOUSE
Office of the Press Secretary

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

For Immediate Release June 28, 2000

PRESS CONFERENCE BY THE PRESIDENT

The East Room

1:45 P.M. EDT

THE PRESIDENT: Good afternoon. This has been a good week for the American
people: first, the landmark breakthrough in human genomic research, which
promises to eradicate once incurable diseases and revolutionize health care
for a very long time to come;
...

-- we've treated the human genome like a priority every year because we all
want to live forever. And that's good. I'm not minimizing that, I'm not
being flippant about that -- we do. That's a good thing, not a bad thing.
