Brian Atkins wrote:

> I am playing devil's advocate here... why aren't all
> you non-US folk moving here? Laying aside for the
> moment the INS/immigration issues - if you could move
> here, why not?

I am fine where I am for the moment. Things are pretty good in Europe.
I have children so I have to stay relatively close in order to
maintain regular contact. At present I am more interested in Africa
and India. That may of course change.

> Have you become too dependent on your free medical care
> that you are afraid you wouldn't be able to hack it
> here?

Medical help is not free in Europe. The people pay for it here as
well. Our system for paying is different to America's but we pay.

I haven't ever considered moving to America so it is difficult to say
what fears I may have concerning living there.
