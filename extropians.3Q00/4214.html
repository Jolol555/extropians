Ok, maybe I'm a victim of all the hype or maybe I just have the mentality
of a ten year old but I really don't think the colossal success of the Harry Potter
books is a fluke. They're very well crafted, exciting, and the most enjoyable pure
fantasy books I ever read. The last one might be a bit too violent and scary if your
children are very young however.
