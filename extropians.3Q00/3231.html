Yes, but some of us will happier than others.... For example, let's say
that I'm one of the elite philosopher kings in this BNW (Brave New World),
or just one of the police. Clearly, I should have the ability to take
CONTROL when things aren't going exactly the way I think is best, right?
Like today, as I was lashing stuff on my motorcycle, and I see these two
sweet young things (total strangers) approaching on the sidewalk. So,
despite my best efforts as they appoach - moving stuff out of the way, etc.
to be polite, they get to within fifteen feet and then cross the street,
requiring them to fight thru heavy shrubbery -just to not get any closer to
me. In broad daylight in a very safe area with continuous traffic....
But in our BNW, if I had the proper system access - "legal" or not - then I
could just adjust a few little dollips of their hormones here and there as
they approached, and my evening might be very different indeed.
