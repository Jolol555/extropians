I will simply observe that I made a concrete suggestion
about what *we* could all as individuals do to allow
the recreation of "beings", in the not too distant
future, with reasonable probabilities of looking like former
individuals whose mental and biomaterial had been preserved.

This seems to have gone from A, to B, to Z, where Z
represents the "Wouldn't it be nice if we had some more
magic physics so we can have everything we ever wanted."

It would be nice if people, just once, please, humor me,
would try to take baby steps and see how much of what we
want we can get using our own collective little baby feet.

Robert
