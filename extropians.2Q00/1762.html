I am trying to work on Adaptive Evolution now -
where one general formalism is used fro representing organisms,
classes/recognizers of organisms, and operations on organisms
(as well as classes of operations, operations on recognizers of
classes of operations, etc.).

Then, the process builds heuristic rules for better mutations and
crossovers in various conditions, and rules for forming rules, etc.,
thus learning to build, and learning to learn.
The resulting model is closer to creative intelligent experimentation
than blind evolution, and should build better solutions faster, together
with an integrated model of the domain.
