==

"Moments Before the Singularity"
by Eliezer S. Yudkowsky 

--

"Give me a piece of bread," she declared, "green with sin, red with
blood, tainted by the darkness of nihilism but still fresh and tasty. 
Give me a piece of bread, grey with ambiguity, orange with just a hint
of purple.  Give me a piece of bread and six hundred thousand feet of
tram cable."

"Frankly, I liked you better when you were sane," I said.

Her face contorted with rage.  "Big talk for a toaster oven!" she
screamed, and hit the off switch.
