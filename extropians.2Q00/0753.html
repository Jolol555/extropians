Salon magazine reports that an interesting-sounding techno group has written
a song in response to Bill Joy's Wired article. See
<http://www.salon.com/tech/log/2000/04/12/joy_song/index.html>. The group is
Mobius Dick; the song, "Embrace the Machine. Mobius Dick has also recorded
"Trust Your Leaders," (a critique, in fact, of bogus authority) and
"Indistinguishable from Magic" (which you can no doubt figure out). See
<http://artists.mp3s.com/artists/103/mobius_dick.html>.
