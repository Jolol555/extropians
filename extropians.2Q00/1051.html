I admit I got off the wrong track here, I have heard of the libertarian
people plenty of times, the Democrat and Republican parties have been
drummed into my head since I can remember, and the libertarian ideas have
attracted my interest as the alternative to them. Nevertheless, as some of
you has pointed out, the Libertarian Party really needs to work on its
public image. We barely hear of the party from where I'm sitting. Instead,
democratic and republic ideas scream from every media conceivable, you
cannot pass a single hour without hearing something about them. The
Libertarian Party, on the other hand, it barely ripples the multimedia. I've
been doing research and discovering new things, I'm hardly the seasoned,
hardened people you are, but I'm learning, albeit painfully, but I'm getting
there.
