On Tue, 23 May 2000, Eugene Leitl wrote:
> James, are you aware of a cheap Theremin (kit) with MIDI output?

I don't know of a cheap one. Also, I've never seen a Theremin with a
built-in MIDI output. Usually what you find are Theremin-to-MIDI/CV
converters (which in principle could be used on a lot of other line
sources).

Most of them, such as the products described at
http://www.ccsi.com/~bobs/therwher.html cost at least $400, even in kit
form.
