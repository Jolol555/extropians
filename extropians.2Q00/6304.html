Nice quotes. Here are some more related quotes.

-------------------------

"When anger rises, think of the consequences. "
       --Confucius

"If you wish to drown, do not torture yourself with shallow water."
 --A Bulgarian proverb

"Thou frothy clay-brained jolthead" --Shakespearean Insult

"The heart of a fool is in his mouth, but the mouth of the wise
man is in his heart."
     --Benjamin Franklin

"Never underestimate the power of human stupidity." --Lazarus Long

"Never appeal to a man's 'better nature.' He may not have one.
Invoking his self-interest gives you more leverage."
           --Lazarus Long

"Gentlemen! You can't fight here! This is the War Room!"
         -Dr Strangelove

"Nature abhors a vacuum, and if I can only walk with sufficient
carelessness I am sure to be filled." --Henry David Thoreau

"Two things are infinite: the universe and human stupidity; and I'm
not sure about the the universe. " --Albert Einstein

-------------------------

And one of my alltime favorites (but it has nothing to do with this
topic)

"I see the correlation but just because I work for the federal
government doesn't mean I'm an expert on cockroaches."
  
--Agent Mulder (The X-Files)
