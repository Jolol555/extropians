Ok, I admit it, Luddites have a point, because there is one technology that is
inherently evil, I refer of course to the reply button on E-Mail packages.
This diabolical invention is clearly the work of the Anti-Christ designed to
 clog up our communication and even our thinking process with pointless
redundant information. If someone really thinks the world will be a better place
if they quote 150 lines from a message everybody just read 5 minutes ago
then they should have to type it all in manually. I'll bet it wouldn't take long before
they found innovative ways to cut the preamble short and get to the point.
