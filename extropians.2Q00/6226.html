Idiocy: Possible Cheaper Testings.

- To generalize is to be an idiot. (William Blake)

- In the first place, God made idiots. That was for practice. Then he made
school boards. (Mark Twain)

- Have you ever noticed? Anybody going slower than you is an idiot, and
anyone going faster than you is a maniac. (George Carlen)

- Half the world is composed of idiots, the other half of people clever
enough to take indecent advantage of them. (Walter Kerr)
 
- When in doubt, make a fool of yourself. There is a microscopically thin
line between being brilliantly creative and acting like the most gigantic
idiot on earth. So what the hell, leap. (Cynthia Heimel)
 
- The main difference between men and women is that men are lunatics and
women are idiots. (Rebecca West)

- The 100% American is 99% an idiot. (George Bernard Shaw)

- Told by an idiot, full of sound and fury. (from Macbeth)

- People think it must be fun to be a super genius, but they don't realize
how hard it is to put up with all the idiots in the world. (Calvin)
