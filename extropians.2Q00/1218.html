Chapter 1 of a new book on quantum computers is available online at
http://www.simonsays.com/excerpt.cfm?isbn=0684814811. The book is

   Minds, Machines, And The Multiverse
   The Quest For The Quantum Computer
   By Julian Brown

The online excerpt focuses on David Deutsch's contributions; the rest of
the book seems to be a wide ranging discussion of both the philosphical
implications as well as some of the practical problems and possibilities.
