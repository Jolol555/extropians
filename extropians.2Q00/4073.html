To a live person, one I care about. I can express my anger. This will allow
them to behave differently should they prefer not to upset me. When I tell
my sweetheart "Im angry at you" - he examines his behavior to see if it
should be modified, or if he behaved badly. Likewise if a freind does
something I do not like, I can question them to see what the motive is, and
express my nager openly. This opens up deeper communications and we get along
better afterwards.

A dead one, especially someone I consider silly, like Ayn Rand, cannot
recieve my anger. It would be a wasted effort.
