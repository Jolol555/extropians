Reat at : http://www.laspirale.org

Paris, June 13th 2000 : Super-longevity, post-humanity and artificial
implants... the latest edition of La Spirale considers the future of the
human race with Natasha Vita-More, extropian artist and technophile, and
Kevin Warwick, research scientist at the University of Reading and
prospective cyborg.

Interview : NATASHA VITA-MORE

Super longevity, post-humanity and the advent of a post-human
existence... Extropian artist, technophilic boldybuilder and convinced
cryonic, Natasha Vita More shares her optimistic vision of the future of
the human race with La Spirale.

Interview : KEVIN WARWICK

Researcher and Professor of Cybernetics at the University of Reading in
England, Kevin Warwick investigates the use of artificial implants in
the human body, the relationship between man and machine and just wants
to be as one with his computer.

Short-story : MICROSOFT AND THE BAVARIAN ILLUMINATI

A piece of conspiracy fiction by acb about the terrible cosmic secrets
concealed beneath the facade of a successful software company that most
people have heard of these days.
