A new vision: rewiring the brain
Scientists reroute visual inputs to auditory cortex in ferrets
REUTERS
LONDON, April 19 — Scientists say they have rewired the visual input of
young animals to a part of the brain normally used for hearing,
demonstrating a versatility of function that might someday lead to
treatments for brain damage and blindness.
