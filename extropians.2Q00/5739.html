While there have been many designs for 'clean' atomic bombs, they are only clean
in relation to normal bombs, as demonstrated in Project Plowshare, the 'Bombs
for Peace' program, where the AEC tried to develop interest in using nukes for
large scale excavation and mining projects...

Considering the amount of atmospheric testing already done to date, with little
long term ill effects outside of a few small areas, and considering the amount
of mass an Orion can put into orbit (measured in hundreds and thousands of tons,
not in pounds or kg.) I wouldn't mind one or a dozen launches specifically to
bootstrap us into a decent industrial and exploratory capability in space. Heck,
you could launch a full Mars Base mission off of one Orion alone, get
terraforming started early... MARS NEEDS WOMEN!
