Extropians, I have experienced a tremendous epiphany. I changed my
mind about *everything*. Every post I have ever posted, I now believe
the exact opposite of whatever opinion expressed therein, up to and
including this message.

As part of the epiphany, I have also gotten religion, and as a result, I
will now dedicate entire my existence to helping all of you accept the gift
of salvation. Should you reject this salvation of course, my merciful
savior will arrange for your immortal soul to writhe in unspeakable
agony until the heat death of the universe, at which time things will
*really* get bad for you.

And dont forget to set your clocks tonight. spike
