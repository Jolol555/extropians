GPS is not currently usable for navigating supersonic devices very well
at all. It takes a few seconds to lock onto all the necessary satellite
signals (you need more than the standard number because you also need
altitude information), and by the time you have data, you are now
several miles away from where you were when you started, so the locks
you get on each satellite signal are over a large foot print several
miles in diameter. You could actually be anywhere within that foot
print.
