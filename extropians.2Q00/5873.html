Steve van Sickle writes, regarding anti-semitism on Extropians:
> Perhaps it was the thread a few weeks back that included the term ZOG in
> the subject line. I'm not sure if the people using it understood its
> meaning, and there was probably some subject creep.
>
> ZOG stands for "Zionist Occupation Government". It is a term used for the
> U.S. federal government by the sort of degenerate creeps who think the
> "Turner Diaries" is great literature and claim the Holocaust was all a
> jewish hoax. You might as well be waving a swastika.

At the time the owner of the email account which posted that message
explained that he had not sent it, it was sent by a friend who had logged
in under his account. The account owner agreed that the message was
not funny and in poor taste.

Only one person posted that he thought it was funny. Everyone else
either objected or asked what ZOG meant.

This incident can hardly be considered evidence that Extropians are
anti-semitic.

Hal
