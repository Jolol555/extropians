Since someone just pointed out that I didn't tell people *how* to go about
subscribing to the Investing in the Future email list, here's a follow-on:

First, for those of you already getting lots of mail, the investing list is
very low volume. It may be silent for days or weeks, then see a brief-lived
flurry of activity. So don't worry about a flood of mail unless the
subscribership increases greatly.

We have discussed various topics, from mechanical investing/stock screening
to particular companies interesting from a technological point of view,
such as Microvision (MVIS).

To subscribe, you do need to be a member of Extropy Institute. You can join
by going to www.extropy.org/join.htm. Then simply send a request to be
subscribed to <exi-info@extropy.org> (or include the request in the Notes
when you join or renew with ExI).

Onwards to a wealthy future!
