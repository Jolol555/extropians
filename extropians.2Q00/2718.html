Sascha Chislenko upheld transhumanism while he lived.
He would have seen the Singularity.
His death was not necessary.
May his death remind us of what we fight for.
May it be among the last.

Goodbye, Sascha.
I never met you in the flesh, and now I never will.
But the writings that I read, survive you.
They will continue to touch minds, while humanity survives.
You are a part of history now.
I wish that you had been part of the future.
Farewell.
