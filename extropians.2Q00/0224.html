Please contact the following transhuman groups if you are interested in
joining:

1. The Transhumanist Arts transhuman group now has over 500 members and is
located globally on the Internet. The office/studio is in Los Angeles, but
affiliate studios are located internationally. The Web site will be
redesigned by several artists in the next few months.

2. The Florida Transhuman World group has several hundred transhumans and
is located in Miami, Florida and New York and is affiliated presently with
Florida International University. (contact 305-919-5669) or
http://www.transhuman.org/fm2030news.htm. Course title "Liftoff to an
Extraordinary Age. The Next 20 Years."

Natasha
