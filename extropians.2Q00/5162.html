From: Doug Jones <random@qnet.com>, Date: Tue, 13 Jun 2000

>Of course, if you've ever been to a nudist resort, you quickly realize
>that you'd rather *not* see through clothing in most cases...

Why do you say that?

The beautiful thing about what you see at a nudist resort
is the sheer (!) number of different shapes and sizes of people.

I like seeing people completely comfortable inside their own skin,
no matter what their "skin" looks like.

I like the attitude of the folks there: that a positive outlook
and good health is more valuable than their percentage of body fat
(or lack thereof)

And it's a good example for kids too- maybe the next generation
of Amerians won't have such puritan attitudes about sex and nudity
as many people in my generation (something that Americans can learn
from the Europeans, in my opinion)
