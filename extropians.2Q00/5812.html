Another release that Garret Smyth asked me to forward. A complete
provisional programme will be available very soon. In addition to the
intellectual stuff, there'll be many fun things to keep your juices flowing,
including laser-gaming, smart-drugs sampling session, opportunity to explore
the London nightlife, possible excursion to the Alcor facility in Eastbourne
etc. (Registration info at http://www.transhumanism.com/tv2k/)

"Come with me if you want to live!"
A well known phrase from a well known (fictional) cyborg to introduce a well
known *real* cyborg.
Here at TVMM mission control we are pleased to announce that Kevin Warwick,
Professor of cybernetics at Reading University, has agreed to speak at the
conference. His topic title will be "My Life as a Cyborg". You have
doubtless seen Prof. Warwick on TV or in a recent copy of Wired magazine.
Well, now is your chance to meet him in the flesh (and silicon).
