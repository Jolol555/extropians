Although I never met Sash IRL, I encountered his website several years ago
and communicated with him via e-mail, which served as my introduction to
transhumanist ideas.

Even among all the many exceptional people in Extropian and Transhumanist
circles, Sash stood out as among the best of the best.

Let his death remind us all that time is precious and death comes unbidden.
If our ideas are worth anything at all, let's use them to make sure that
Sasha's death is not permanent termination.

We'll meet again, don't know where, don't know when . . . .

Michael LaTorra
