Takes me back to something that happened in the 80's here in Ireland, in a

over "moving statues" and such nonsense. In the village where I lived, we
had a small grotto to the virgin, but no "movement" had been reported. So
of course, a few days later a sign appeared on the grotto gates;

"OUT OF ORDER"
