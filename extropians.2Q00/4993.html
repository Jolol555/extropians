* Michael S. Lorrey <retroman@turbont.net> [000612 17:50]:
> For about 15 minutes, at age 15, I was convinced I was a communist....

I remember when I was, oh, 7 or so, saying something offhand to my
mother along the lines of "from each according to his ability, to
each according to his needs." As I recall I had come up with this
whole theory myself. She said if I wasn't careful people might start
calling me Karl Marx. I had no idea what she was talking about so I
looked it up. After a bit of further reading, that was the end of that!
But it made great sense at the time.
