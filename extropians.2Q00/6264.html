Subject: NW Transhuman/Extropian Mtg Seattle 7/1

Northwest Transhumanists/Extropians will meet at 1:00 PM on Saturday, July 1
in Meeting Room 1, King County Library, 1111 110th Ave. Northeast, Bellevue,
WA. A map of that area can be found at
http://www.kcls.org/brl/bellgen.html#address.

The purpose of this meeting is to get acquainted, exchange ideas on Transhumanism/Extropianism, and explore the possibility of forming a local or regional group or otherwise conducting activities at the local level.

There is an e-mail list for Northwest Transhumanists/Extropians at
http://www.egroups.com/group/NWExtropians. Any interested individuals are
invited to subscribe.

If you are interested but unable to attend the upcoming meeting and would like to be kept posted on future activities, please join the e-mail list or contact me directly.

Regards,
