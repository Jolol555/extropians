Mark Steyn devoted his monthly column in the July 2000 "American Spectator"
magazine to violent crime in Britain, with a particular focus on victims who
were prosecuted for resisting violent attacks. Many Britons are now fearful
of defending themselves, not because they think their assailants will treat
them any worse, but because the police and courts will.
