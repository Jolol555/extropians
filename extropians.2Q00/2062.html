Greetings Everyone!

SETI@Home users and other SETI enthusiasts in the Boston,
Massachusetts area should note a free public lecture/discussion
happening on Sunday, May 7, 2000 at Boston University (see below).

For additional information, see the URLs for The SETI Institute:

http://www.seti.org

and Boston University for directions:

http://www.bu.edu/maps/

If you are taking the MBTA, get off at BU Central on the B train,
also known as the Green Line.

                           May 7, 2000

            PUBLIC SESSION: SETI BEYOND OZMA SYMPOSIUM

Panel discussion and lectures on Project BETA, SETI@Home, Project
Phoenix, and the search for extraterrestrial life and extrasolar
planets.

Speakers and panelists include:

* Frank Drake
* Dan Werthimer
* Kent Cullers
* Paul Horowitz
* David Latham
* Philip Morrison

        ***NOTE: GENERAL PUBLIC WELCOME TO THIS SESSION***

Location, date, and time:

Morse Auditorium
602 Commonwealth Avenue
Boston University

May 7, 2000
2:15 p.m. to 6:00 p.m.
