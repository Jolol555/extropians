The Foresight Institute Europe fields a speaker for TVMM

Phillippe Van Nedevelde, director of Foresight Europe has agreed to speak at
the TVMM conference this July.
Phillippe will be letting us know what the Institute is doing in Europe, and
also he will be giving a run down on UP! which, he says, "is The first
thoroughbred transhumanist entrepreneurial initiative". The (so-called)
internet revolution has made many new millionaires, and quite a few
billionaires, and all it does is shift around bits, imagine what being able
to shift around atoms in the same way will do.
If we don't have at least one neophyte start-up company by the Sunday
evening we should hang our heads in shame.
