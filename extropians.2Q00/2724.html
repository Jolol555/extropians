There are many people who consider themselves transhumanists, but few
who truly live transhuman lives. Sasha was one of them.

What always impressed me with Sasha was the visionary ideas he
constantly came up with, far too many great ideas to test or implement
for anyone, even using all the 24 hours in the day. I hope we can take
up where he left us and implement at least some of his great visions.
