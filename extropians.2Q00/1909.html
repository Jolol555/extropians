Once upon a time, I came across a paper somewhere on the web about
self-reference in intelligent systems. It was headed with a line drawing of a
big clunky, Danger-Will-Robertson-Style, robot. His case was transparent, and
we has standing in front of a mirror looking at his own gears clicking and
churning, while his right hand sketched out his own blueprint on a blackboard.

Anyway - I've haven't been able to find it since. If this rings a bell for
anyone I'd love to get a copy of it or have it printed on a poster or a T-Shirt.

-matt
