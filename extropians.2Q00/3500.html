A few days ago I created a mailing list for Extropians and transhumanists
in the Vancouver\Seattle\Portland area. Topics discussed should be on a
local level...particularly about meetings and events. If you are a
transhumanist resident of the Pacific Northwest join at:

http://www.egroups.com/group/NWExtropians

Alternately, you can join via e-mail by using the following command in the
subject line of your message:

NWExtropians-subscribe@egroups.com
