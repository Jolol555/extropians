For those interested in cryonics, there is a Cryobiology conference being
held at MIT, July 30-Aug 1, 2000. Lots fo talks on increasing the tolerance
of biological systems to freezing etc. 375$ for non-members of the
Society for Cryobiology for the conference.

 If you want to submit an abstract, visit www.healthtech.com/cryoabstract.

If you e-mail Dr. Mehmet Toner, he might be able to provide you with more
details. His address is mtoner@sbi.org.
