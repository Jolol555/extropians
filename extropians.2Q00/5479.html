"Sure, but how much did you pay for the different trucks? Are your

Hmmmm...rough figures....

The first truck cost 40K...insurance was about 250 or so a month...maint was
1k monthly......payments were 1200....a month...

The second truck....less the trade in was 54K....insurance was up to about
350 or so....maint was 1k or so....payments were 1500...

This truck....less trade was about 80K....insurance is 450 or so...maint is

lube)....payments are about 2k..

month....the down time (lost income) while they were being fixed....was
comparable.....zero now (approximately)

gimmicks)....
