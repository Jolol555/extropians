It is quite well accepted in solid-state physics that the removal of the
external clock is necessary for a circuit to change from 'hardwired'
finite-state to 'soft-wired' infinite-state.

What you might argue about is my comparison between the ancient median eye
which seems to function as an 'external clock' for E-2 animals. I think the
case is very strong, Adrian is reluctant to commit.
