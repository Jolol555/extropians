While looking for head-mounted displays I found a page advertising
eyeglass-mounted projectors with photos of their two main products,
one an "integrated" pair of eyeglasses and one a "clip-on" that can
be attached to your own glasses. Below those two photos was a line
of text with a link:

  See "Contacts" page for updated information.

My first thought was "Wow, they're not making them /that/ small
now are they?" Then I realized they just moved their office.
