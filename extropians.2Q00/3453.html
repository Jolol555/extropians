J. Goard <wyattoil@foothill.net> Wrote:

>Your self-awareness must continue into either the guy on the platform who gets
> annihilated, or the guy that comes out the other end, or neither, or both.
> We can rule out both, (even if it could somehow be explained physically)
> since it violates our hypothesis of duplication. If your single
> self-awareness existed in both at the same time, then that wouldn't really
> be duplication, just giving one person two brains and two bodies.

I wrote a wonderful rebuttal to that but I want to keep it and so can't send you a copy.
My E mail message must continue into your computer or stay on my computer
or both. We can rule out both, (even if it could somehow be explained physically)
since it violates our hypothesis of duplication. If my single E mail message
existed in both computers at the same time, then that wouldn't really
be duplication, just putting one message on two computers.
