>we don't have the gadgets to allow people to pay for a download.

You may not have the mechanism for a pay for download scheme, but Fatbrain does. you can sell your book through www.fatbrain.com electronically. There term is e-matter. They of course will take a small cut of the selling price for the service of putting it in a usable format (for Acrobat and E-readers like the rocket book) and hosting it. This scheme would leave you with the task of advertising which I'm sure any number of people would be willing to help.
