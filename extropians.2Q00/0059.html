Wait, Max.. We have stated often enough: to achieve a "green" planet and
ecological balance, we could and should utilize high technology ... this
alone will be the path ... not backing away from it, eyes closed, head
downward...

Electrohippies:
Sure they are deconstructive, that's the nature of protest.
Sure, their cause is not your cause, and perhaps our precious ecosystem will
not be permanently perverted by genetically altering food, on this I'm no
expert, only a curious observer.

But think of it, if we mostly all agree that using technology to fight
pollution and other eco-damage, is this not - in some weird twisted way, an
early example of this?
