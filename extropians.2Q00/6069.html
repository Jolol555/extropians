put you in a good light. Natasha had not participated in any of your
prior discussions, but you for some odd reason felt the need to go off

let the discussion drop at that.

are no longer legal means of settling personal disputes of honor. If you
conduct yourself in your real interpersonal relations as you have here,

idea that you think you can so offhandedly make personal aspersions
about a mans wife and expect no adverse repercussions just boggles the
mind.
