Just a few more minor notes on changes to the Principles:

Since these Principles are on a public forum, there are a few terms which
could potentially confuse or mislead newcomers. The term "neophiles" as
used in Self-Transformation is a bit questionable; "pioneers" might be a
better substitute, due to the importance of a term in this context.
Additionally, something that could be of immense value to the next version
of the Principles is the prescence of links to a glossary. That way, terms
like "neophile" among many others could be used without fear of ambiguity
or misunderstanding. There is a basic glossary in the FAQ, but it would be
a very beneficial project to create one that is very objective and concise.
