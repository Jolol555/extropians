Friends,

Both Nadia and Robert have addressed this situation with grace, and I would
like to ask that we honor Max's request. Sasha was a very close friend of
mine and I am still grieving, as I am sure others are.

In deference to Sasha, please remember that this is not a private email
list and speculations about his death may have some bearing, but his mind
was a brilliant one, full of curiosity and desire to learn and to express
his talents. Sasha handled issues that arose with dignity and integrity.

Please let us think of his integrity and help to give him peace where he
may not have been able to find it himself.

"If everyone were clothed with integrity, if every heart were just, frank,
kindly, the other virtues would be well-nigh useless."

Natasha
