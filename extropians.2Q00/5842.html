To me Divinity is what you make of it. Through religion you can become quite
empowered, as well as disempowered. There are many methods and traditions,
each equally valid. Perhaps you are wondering if I "believe" in an outside
God. God is real because He affects me, indirectly through His worshippers
who "believe" in Him. I personally don't acknowlege any kind of objective
reality, but nor do I reject one. Gods are "real" because they affect me,
just as "capitalism" is real, "homosexuality" is real, "mental illness" is
real, etc.
