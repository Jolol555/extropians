Dear Extropians,

I've been on and off this list for the past 6 years, so perhaps my
mentioning this is unforgivable. However I could no longer resist the
urge to pass it along.

Despite its network marketing overtones, I recently discovered a new
product and service that is worthy in and of itself.

$100/yr gets you:

Unlimited Internet Access.
15MB Web Space.
Web Mail, Calendar & Organizer.
Online Web Educational Tutorial (which for some odd reason is their main
product).

The catch is the internet access requires a task bar with the business
name on it.

Frankly most of us are internet savvy enough that the product may be
redundant. However this product is geared towards enabling the average
"Elmer Fud" to access the web and create their own web site cheaply and
easily. The fact that the internet population is going to explode over
the coming decade further drives this product.

What especially piqued my interest was the fact that you get paid for
referrals. Although you can only sign up two people underneath you,
they too can sign-up two and so on. Like any pyramid scheme, the larger
your "downline" gets the more you keep getting paid. Since they've
eliminating the need for overhead by creating a referral program , is
why I'm assuming its so cheap.

My apologies if this post has offended anyone - I'm only mentioning
this because I know how much as extropians we place our wealth at a
priority. I've already made $135 off this program in two weeks -
thereby paying for my initial $100 investment. The cool thing is you
don't have to quit with your other provider. You can join just to reap
the rewards of the referrals as I'm doing.

You can find out more and join if you like at the somewhat poorly laid
out web site that you automatically get when you sign up.
