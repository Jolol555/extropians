I'm happy to report that a long piece by me about the Singularity (or varieties thereof) as depicted in science fiction has been accepted for a collection of papers in honor of the futurist and scholar I F Clarke, to be called *Histories of the Future*. The collection has been formally accepted for publication by Macmillan in the UK and St Martin's in the USA, and is edited by academics Alan Sandison and Robert Dingley. Should be an intriguing book!
