$ .02 worth

TRANSHUMANISM: A philosophy promoting the idea that the evolutionary course of human beings has not ended, that humans will continue to develop and will eventually use future technologies to transcend biological and psychological limitations, proactively and rationally orchestrating their own evolutionary destinies.
