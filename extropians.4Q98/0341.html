Regarding my tranhumanist story "I": Odyssey's web page is

http://www.jeapes.ndirect.co.uk/odyssey/

which will tell you some about the magazine, and how to order single issues (under "subscribe"). They're pretty expensive.
(Everything in the UK is, despite the fact that nobody there seems to
have much money, a contradiction I would like someday to hear a good explanation of.)

Alternately, if you go to any science fiction conventions in the northeast US, you will probably run into Darryl Schweitzer, who sells Odyssey (among other things). Just look around for a dark-haired, lanky, overcaffeinated fellow in a broad hat and maybe a fringed suede jacket or a Mexican poncho, who will be scanning the room predatorily, looking for people he has not yet sold books to.
