Religious humanism isn't new, it has been around some time now. But in general, it seems that the humanist movement has become rather watered down and divergent. This is actually an area where we can do some good, by showing a new form of humanism, transhumanism, that encompasses the old core humanist values like human value, freedom, rationality and optimism but also has energy, an interest in the future and acceptance of technology.
