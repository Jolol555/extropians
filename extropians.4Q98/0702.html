For some reason, despite my previous wails, Mike keeps misquoting this real-politik observation (which is congenial, I'd have thought, to Libertarians and gun-fanciers). Mousy Dung *actually* said:

`POLITICAL POWER comes from the barrel of a gun'

  • that is, however true, noble and virtuous your political ideas, if you don't have the firepower to back them up you're smoke in the breeze. A moment's checking on the Web would confirm this quote. Cf., e.g.,
