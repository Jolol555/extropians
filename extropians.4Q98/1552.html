Christopher Reeve stars in a remake of 'Rear Window' tonight at 9:00 pm U.S. Eastern Time on ABC . I'm not recommending this for the actual movie, but if you tune it you will get to see Christopher Reeve using the very latest in the types of human interface technology that disabled persons presently have the ability to purchase for home use. Featured are breath-activated and voice activated applications. I hear that they will also show the difficulties in using that technology, such as what happens when a ventilator fails.
