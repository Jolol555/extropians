Last nights program on "48 Hours" covering immortality seemed quite positive, although I only viewed the last 1/2 hour. The importance of cloning organs and brain cells was handled smoothly and openly, suggesting a *need* for cloning organs, especially cloning and replacing our own organs as we are living longer. Cryonics focused on our friends Judy Muhlestein, Mark Muhlestein and two of their sons at their home, along with Linda Chamberlain and Fred Chamberlain at Alcor. I especially appreciated the closing comments which were uplifting, and the young-old women working out in the background.
