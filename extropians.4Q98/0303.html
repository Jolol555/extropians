I am the new chief editor at Journal of Transhumanism (http://www.transhumanist.com/). We have published two articles so far, and hope to gain a reputation as an academic quality journal.

We use peer review to evaluate submissions, and since our subject area is interdisciplinary, we are in special need of qualified "experts" willing to review articles.

If you have some relevant expertize and are willing to review submissions, please send me a paragraph length description of your areas of expertize, and the reasons you consider yourself knowledgeable in those areas (e.g., degrees, publications, projects).

Doing so does not commit you to reviewing anything - you may later decline requests for reasons of a lack of time, conflict of interest, or lack of expertize. But we do seek a rapid written response from those who agree to review - so far our slowest evaluation has taken three weeks.
