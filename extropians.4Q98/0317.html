Robin Hanson wrote:
> If this person thought instead that their decendants in a thousand years
> would be more than human, would we call them a transhumanist?

To call someone a transhumanist, I'd expect them to

  • want to personally exceed "human" limits
  • expect to have a reasonable chance of personally doing so (implies within their lifetime)

I think I'd consider someone who thinks humans might reasonbly exceed human limits only sometime after this person's death a proto-transhumanist or one interested in transhuman topics, but not a transhumanist.
