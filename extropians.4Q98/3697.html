Alexander 'Sasha' Chislenko writes:

> An ID with a picture (of some identifiable body part) and an inscription
> "this person is allowed to buy alcohol" should be sufficient for the cause,
> shouldn't it?

The keyword here is cohesiveness. To print such miniature IDs for all 'legal' citizens would consume time, money and other resources. It's much more simpler for each one to have only a small set of numbers - information printed out on one small card.

One interesting idea might be for the ID itself to have a different color for legal citizens. That way, all you have to do is show the seller the ID color with your big picture printed on it.
