_Ultratech_ is the name given to a line of RPG supplements for Steve Jackson Games GURPS line. The books are for scifi tech; however, they recently did a book called _Biotech_ which is pretty good from a >H POV. They also put out books on various cultures for RPG use which make prretty good history texts.
