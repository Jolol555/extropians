Although this is a complete diversion from my intended line of thought, I'd have to agree with you on this one! Besides, I'm not here to debate RAW's outdated, yet remarkably suggestive prose from 1977. I am however using Tim Leary's 8-circuit model as a launching pad for thinking about what higher intelligence could be. I think now is as good a time as any to take Tim Leary's 8-circuit model and give it a more updated precise scientific re-working. In fact, Tim Leary was quite eager to see such a scientific revision done by those knowledgeable enough to take on the task.

Getting back to my original point, I think that being a so-called slave to an immensely higher intelligence might ironically be more free than being a slave now to our inherited genetic, neurological and sociological constraints. Any thoughts in that regard?
