This one's entitled The Chimeras of Loppet Creek, and it's quite different from The Truth Machine and The First Immortal. Chimeras is an sf adventure yarn with historical information and many anecdotes involving recent advances in cellular biology. At this early stage of the rewriting, both story feedback and scientific fact-checking are desperately required. I'm even willing to pay a couple hundred bucks apiece for the services of a few professionals in biotech or related fields -- in case any are reading this. Other readers would receive a copy of the novel if and when it's published, and, if your feedback is especially useful, a mention in the acknowledgments. The manuscript should be ready to send out next week. My preference is to e-mail it in MS word format, but I could FedX a few hard copies if necessary. If anyone's interested, please e-mail me. I'll respond to all inquiries within a few days.
