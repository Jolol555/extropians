Being overweight can be a health hazard, resulting in days off from work, etc.
With that in mind here is a satiety index for 38 foods from European Journal of Clinical Nutrition 675-690 Vol.49 1995

The higher number the more filling the food. White bread = 100 

FOOD                 SI SCORE
Bakery Products
Croissant            47
Cake                 65
Doughnuts            68 (take a look at Homer Simpson's potbelly)
Cookies             120
Crackers            127

Mars bar             70
