A biiiiig HELLO to you all!!!

We are Jutta and Sabine (mother, 57 years old, and daughter, 28 years old) from Germany. And we are extropians since 1994. We are very happy to belong to the extropian mailing list, for it gives us lots of intellectual "food" - and hope (for a long time we thought we would be alone with our ideas!). About two years ago we found a German pharmazeutical company which is producing "Voltax". It really helps our brains to work better, because it protects the neurons, and increases their stability. This is not a commercial message for this stuff, but if somebody is interested about it, please contact us. We like it, because you don't need to ask a doctor for it, and "Voltax" contains no ingredients which can make you addicted to it. Especially we are interested in mails where extropians give information in how their life attitude effects their social contacts. We, for example, have to be very careful with what we are talking to other people. We found out that many people take science and technology for another kind of ideology, they don't see the proven facts. And they don't understand our interest in being alive forever. They hate to be alive because they haven't got any information about life improvement techniques. So, we are trying to inform as much people as we can, and the extropian mailing list is the best source for this purpose. We wish you a very happy 1999!!!
