Although I have been very displeased with Clinton since 93, especially
his position on civil liberties; what are our alternatives if he is
impeached?

President Gore? (his stance on those issues is worse, he is a
well-known centralist and proponent of a one-world governemtn).

President Colin Powell? (He is also a world federalist, and in favor of
very strong gun-control)

And if the christian right is able to use this Paula Jones thing to its
advantage, we might end up with:

President Gingrich or President Buchannan.

Although I would love to see a strong libertarian candidate make it, I
am not holding my breath.

So where does this leave us? I hope that Clinton is *not* impeached
because the alternative will fare much worse.
