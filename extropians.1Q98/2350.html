"Steve Massey" <zos@hotmail.com> writes:

> Transhuman art - attempts to communicate between
> transhumans at the emotional level - is something else again. I
> can easily picture different groups of transhumans being defined
> by the artistic clade they identify with, and hence the different
> basic view of the world they share.

And of course they will have trouble understanding each other's
art. Egan mentions this problem in _Diaspora_, where a character
needed to load an outlook (i.e a temporary modification of their
personality, cognition and values) to appreciate a certain artwork.
