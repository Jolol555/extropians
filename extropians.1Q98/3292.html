There are lots of other things too.
Mild testosterone boosters (such as Androstene) may help build both muscle
and assertiveness, Ecstasy increases compassion, while its after-effects
dissolve one's respect to artificial psychological and legal boundaries; mixes
of some psychedelics and nootropics (i.e., mescalin and DMAE) enhance
one's perception while preserving cognitive function; GHB fights depression
and improves one's physical state, etc., etc. There are seem to be lots of
drugs that can be safely used for flexing and reconstructing one's personality
architecture. Unfortunately, the majority of both experimenters and
regulators
seem unable to explore the mind-altering substances in an open, meaningful,
and responsible manner, so there is very little data, and grave dangers for
one's well-being from both law-enforcers and law-breakers.
This reminds me of another repressive regime where I had to risk my
health and career to obtain books from black marketeers.
(Talk about indifference of transhumanism to politics...)

Could anybody recommend a serious private discussion groups that deals
with these topics - or be interested in forming one?
