>>>'CULTURAL POLICY IN THE EUROPEAN UNION'
>>>Research Workshop
>>>
>>>City University, Department of Arts Policy & Management
>>>29 April 1998
>>>
>>>The workshop will examine different aspects of cultural policy-making
>>>at national and supra-national levels and their relevance to European
>>>processes cultural, social and political. The framework of approach
>>>is multi-disciplinary. Other objectives include achieving a clearer
>>>delimitation of cultural policy as a field of research in contemporary
>>>European studies, mapping current research in the field and to create
>>>an ongoing forum for discussion and sharing of information.
>>>
