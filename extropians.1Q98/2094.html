I agree SciAm has, for years now, tended to produce less and less
fact and more and more fashionable opinion. I find it now more-
or-less on a par with Discover, while SciAm used to be much better.

check the evidence in each article to see if it justifies the
claims and opinions. The trends are definitely in the wrong direction.
