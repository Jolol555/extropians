Just read a suggestion of implementing secure communications
through authentication, rather than encryption, technology.
The method involves adding "chaff"/noise to the message, with improper
authentication. It can make unencrypted communications as secure as
encrypted. Also, the U.S. gov't doesn't demand registration of
authentication keys.

More info is available at http://theory.lcs.mit.edu/~rivest/chaffing.txt
