I'm not sure who wrote the above comment. And, perhaps this, in the long
run, is beside the point. This issue is sex differences is of relevance to
the postbiological era, as well as the transhuman era that we presently
reside in. This topic and related topics of engineering our bodies and our
minds are *paramount* to our continued evolution. I have tried to
understand what significance negating this topic will bring to further our
understanding and designing of our present and future bodies and our
psychology, but I have not reached a positive result.
