crazy fascist yanks etc.), but from a transhuman point of view it should
be so clear: _both_ systems suck, and pretty bad too. F**k them,
they're so far off the mark that they're not worth defending IMHO. Any
country where you can't do what you like as long as you don't hurt
anyone else is *unreasonably unfree*. By this simple definition we all live in

cases). Peace brothers... ;-)
