Lewis wrote:

"What you fail to see is that BOTH capitalism AND communism, are ways of
FORCING humans to produce and buy goods and services, OR STARVE TO
DEATH."

It's not capitalism that forces people to work or starve to death. It's
*reality* that forces us to work or starve to death. In case you forgot,
your body needs food to live, and food takes work to produce. Capitalism
doesn't force you to buy goods and services; it just provides an
alternative to producing everything yourself.
