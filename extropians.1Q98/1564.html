I'd like to announce the existence of Monadnock Online,
a website devoted to "joy and reason and meaning" (in the
spirit of Monadnock Valley in the Fountainhead). This website
contains:

1. The Monadnock Review, a webzine of art and philosophy

2. The Ism Book, a hypertext dictionary of philosophy

3. "Fan Spaces" devoted to inspiring thinkers and artists

4. My personal website (writings, song lyrics, poetry, etc.)

Now that I have a dedicated domain name, I plan to more
aggressively pursuing content and publicity for this webspace
and to build an online community of like-minded individuals.
If you think you might like to contribute, or if you're just
curious about the site, visit http://www.monadnock.net
for more information.

Thanks!
