Paul Hughes wrote:

> Unless *something* of my self survives the ravages of time, by
> definition and simple logic, *nothing* of my self survives. If nothing
> of my self survives, then *I* am not immortal.

That sounds about right. We and our memory are in a constant flux. THe
further we go back in time, the less we can remember. It will stay this
way until we are redesigned for total recall.

One more reason for Uploading. Instead of dying the million little
deaths of memory loss.
