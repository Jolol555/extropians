"Stephen May" <stevemay@gte.net> writes:

> Is there a good reading list posted on the web of post-human SF works?

There are some transhuman reading lists at
http://www.aleph.se/Trans/Cultural/Art/book_index.html
http://www.seraph.org/Trans/SKMbooks.html
http://www.aleph.se/Trans/Cultural/Art/johnson.091792.txt
but they are not specific *posthuman*. Maybe we should begin one. Here
is my suggestions:

Greg Egan: Permutation City, Diaspora (+ some short stories in Axiomatic)
Vernor Vinge: A Fire Upon the Deep (some posthumans in the background)
Charles Stross: Scratch Monkey
Linda Nagata: The Bohr Maker, Tech Heaven and Deception Well
Bruce Sterling. Schismatrix
David Zindell: Neverness, The Broken God, The Wild, War in Heaven
Greg Bear: Blood Music
Olad Stapledon: Last and First Men, Starmaker
