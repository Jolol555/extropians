On Sun, 01 Mar 1998 Max More <maxmore@primenet.com> Wrote:

>he's looking for good, positive film images of human transformation.
>It's hard to find many non-horror images of humans transforming, so
>I'd welcome any suggestions. The more positive, optimistic, humorous
>images we can put in, the more favorably the transhumanist message
>should be conveyed.

You're right they are rare, there must be more but just off the top of my
head I can only think of two, one of course is 2001, the other is a wonderful
comedy made by Albert Brooks about 5 years ago called "Defending your life".
It's a fantasy comedy not science fiction, but it does have a lot of
Extropian ideas in it and they're very strongly stated, such as " the point
of eternal life is to keep getting smarter", that's not an exact quote but
it's close, it's also a very funny movie. Incidentally Albert Brooks changed
his name, he thought his real name was hard to live up to and not good for a
comedian, his real name is Albert Einstein.
