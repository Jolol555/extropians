INTRODUCTION TO THE DYNAMICS OF COMPLEX SYSTEMS

Where & When:
MIT E51-151 9:00AM-4:30PM January 15 (Thursday)
70 Memorial Drive
http://whereis.mit.edu/bin/map?state=0&pri.x=412&pri.y=145

Cost: Students $70; Academic $220; Corporate $495
(free with MIT ID)

Copies of the textbook: Dynamics of Complex Systems
by Yaneer Bar-Yam will be available at 25% discount.

Subject: concepts, methods and applications to physical,
biological and social complex systems.

Level: introductory/accessible for undergraduates

Outline:

Examples, questions, methods and concepts
Interacting agents and Cellular Automata models
Pattern formation in physical, biological and social systems
Neural networks and pattern recognition
Quantitative complexity and evolutionary change
Human civilization as a complex system
