All this discussion of "Frank" ("Let's kill Frank and
take his stuff", etc.") reminds me of a poem which I
read once, in a Lucius Shepard novel, I believe:

I wish I had an army of a thousand guys like me.
We'd storm the gates of heaven and seize the Trinity.
We'd throw the angels off a cliff to see if they
could fly,
And find God was just an old gray man who can't
remember why.

Irreverant, but funny.

-- Mike Smith
