Eating cannabis can be tricky, though, because it's tougher to control the
dosage this way and you might get a bad trip (or no trip). Vaporizing is a
much quicker and more controllable way to get high (vaporizing tends to
get you high, while smoking has a more "stoned" effect), although it's true
that if you want a truly profound, smooth trip Shiva Bhang balls (small balls
of cannabis mush and some other herbs - an Indian specialty) are probably
better than vaporizing. Another advantage of these balls, that are chewed a
bit and then swallowed, is that they can be used discretely (unlike smoked
pot, that can be detected from a mile away...)
