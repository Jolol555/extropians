the publication "The Logical Verification of the Mystical
Experience" the mathematical proof of holistic identity
and a short outline of Zero Mechanics, which is the meta-
physical system I have developed over the years of which
my holistic identity theory forms the logical basis.

I encourage you to evaluate zero mechanics and offer
any constructive critique that you feel is warranted.
I always present my metaphysical theories in a matter-
of-fact fashion, which does not mean I do not allow
that they may be missing the mark. I believe this
analysis presents the hard mathematical proof of
the theory of holistic identity I have promoted.
