As per previous discourse on hemp, a.k.a. cannabis,
the February 21 issue of New Scientist, the world's
leading weekly science and technology magazine,
highlights the health debate over cannabis:

http://www.newscientist.com/ns/980221/contents.html

With a strong editorial position that states "claims
about the evils of cannabis have been exaggerated,"
and sides favorably with cannabis decriminalization:

http://www.newscientist.com/ns/980221/editorial.html

The main feature cites a World Health Organization
report confirming the relative safety of cannabis:

http://marijuana.newscientist.com

All well worth the read-time and valuable additions
to the overwhelming body of evidence supporting the
relative safety and medical benefits of cannabis.
