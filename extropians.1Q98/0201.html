I just read an interesting article called "Experimental Quantum Teleportation"
in the latest issue of Science Week <http://www.scienceweek.com/sw980102.txt>
in which they describe the quantum teleportation of the exitation state of
rubidium atoms. Since this seems to involve a net energy transfer, it may,
after all, be possible to teleport molecular bonds from one site to
another.
