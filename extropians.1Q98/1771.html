Julian has been one of my recent heros. I remember Jarold K.
O'Neal being a hero back in my high school days. Then one day I found
out he died. It broke my heart. Does anyone know if he was
preserved?

For that matter, is there a list, somewhere of who has been
preserved? I know this is non disclosed information from the cryo
companies, but surely there are many people which have made their
preservation public knowledge. It would be nice to know who is on the
list of publicly know preserved people. It seems like a cryonics FAQ
should contain such information about people who don't mind letting
others know they were preserved (or signed up to be preserved).
