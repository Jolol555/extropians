Yesterday I saw Max & Natasha for the first time on German TV (last part
of "Future Fantastic", BBC documentary), together with Marvin Minsky,
Ralph Merkle, and many others. Although I was a bit disappointed by the
style of presentation (which resembled a mystery series rather than a
serious report), I hope that this reached a broad audience, despite the
broadcasting time 11:15 pm, because it's a nice (and important, in this
country) contribution to the dissemination of certain transhumanist
concepts.
Anyway, I have a copy on videotape and will force everyone within reach to
watch it ;-)
