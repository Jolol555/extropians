The WTA associates list is now up and running. It is intended for
people who want to actively participate in the WTA. Discussions
should be very practical and action-oriented: propose things to be
done; offer your skills and/or resources; take responsibility for
tasks that need doing; evaluate and give feedback on projects that
are pursued.

Those who have already contacted me or Dave have been added to
the list (or will be within 24 hours). Other people who want to
become WTA associates and are willing to make some kind of
contribution can do so by sending a message to
transhumanism@delysid.ontech.net with the Subject set to 'subscribe'.
See you there!
