===============================================================

Charles Krauthammer recommends that scientists who save lives and relieve
human suffering be put to death. After removing his rhetoric I find only one
basic reason to justify this drastic action, brainless clones are icky poo.
Everybody is entitled to their opinion but I couldn't get an idea out of my
mind, perhaps such creatures already exist, perhaps they're employed by major
magazines to write essays.
