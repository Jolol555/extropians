How do these people know who they should invite to discuss their secrets?
If they invite somebody they know they want to talk to, then they know his identity.
If they invite everybody they do not know, they can as well anonymously broadcast
their secrets.

Now, if a person they invite is already known to them, but only under untraceable
pseudonyms, they can invite him, but will never learn his identity.
Except that when enough effort is expended into building the credibility of a
pseudonym, the person's interests may be hurt by actions directed at this
pseudonym - it becomes part of one's identity.
