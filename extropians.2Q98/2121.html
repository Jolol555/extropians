I find myself once more facing the drudgery of a rather visionless IT job,
and, having worked through several months of exceptionally non-Extropian
thinking, think it time to resubscribe to the list. To those of you who
already know me I say, 'Hello again,' and to those that do not I say,
"Hello and www.seraph.org".

The Extropy web site tells me that we are 'Catalysing the Future': I hope
that that is not entirely true. Catalysts remain unchanged at the end of
the process, and I rather hope that I change with time, even if those
changes are designed to prevent other, less desirable ravages of time and
disease.

Anyway, just a short note to say 'Hello,' which I shall close now in the
interests of the lists signal-to-noise ratio.
