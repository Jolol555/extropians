probabilities are that they are written in Aramaic rather than Hebrew.

Scrolls (If not they may be forgeries. There are a lot of them being
produced in the middle east and sold to ignorant tourists.)

been translated by numerous people -- actually mistranslated in many
instances, but that's another story -- of various degrees of
scholarship.
You may wish to check with the Archeology department of any major
University. Most have legitimate experts in this area.

colleges. Mostly, they don't know what they're talking about, but talk
they do, and they never seem to stop providing massive amounts of
