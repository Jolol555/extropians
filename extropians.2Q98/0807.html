Is anyone here familiar with the writing of Julian Huxley? He really
suggest the first transhumanistic philosophy in his various writings,
although he did think of himself more as a humanist that embraced
change. As far as I can tell, he coined the term transhumance when
discussing his own bran of humanism, called EVOLUTIONARY HUMANISM.

Any takers?
