Many Extropians are interested in Objectivist ideas, and many
Objectivists find Extropians interesting, and everyone interested in
either set of ideas seems to end up at the same lectures, along with
some Libertarians. But, and this is where it gets interesting
sometimes--Rand couldn't stand Libertarians! Makes for some noisy
conversations over dinner, sometimes....In the end, I think you have
to examine all sets of ideas and draw the best from each, if only to
keep out of the politics between the groups.
