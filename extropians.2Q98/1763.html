Full Context will be interviewing Douglas J. Den Uyl in a forthcoming
issue. Den Uyl is professor of philosophy at Bellarmine College and author
of _The Virtue of Prudence_ and co-author with Douglas Rasmussen of
_Liberty and Nature: An Aristotelian Defense of Liberal Order_, _The
Philosophic Thought of Ayn Rand_ and _Liberalism Defended_.
