One way that I have found it useful to think about my role as artist
(electronic musician) is that I am entrusted with helping others to find
tools that elevate the human spirit. Artwerks are some of those tools.
Science and scientific discovery are others. Science and Art are both
discliplines which give humanity tools that they can use to elevate
their individual human spirit. They are clearly not the only ones. But
judging by how people prize them, they are important ones.
