The 1998 Tulluride Mountain film festival has as one of its entries a short
documentary entitled 'Grandpa's in the Tuff Shed,' directed by Shelly, Kathy
and Robin Beeck.

Evidently, a resident of Nederland, Colorado, froze his deceased grandfather
inside a wooden storage hut, using tons of dry ice. Trygve Bauge left the
US for Norway a few years ago, but his neighbors still maintain the cryonic
condition of the body in the shed.

Does anyone know if there is a way to obtain a copy of this documentary?
