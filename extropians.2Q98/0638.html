>From some public statements made by atheists around here, I would be
tempted
>to define atheism as a fervent belief in the NON existence of a supreme
being...

A supreme being is theoretically absolutely possible. We are striving to
become supreme beings ourselves, so it is possible that other beings have
already achieved this. The problem is only that so far we do not have any
information about any supreme beings. We can look for such information, but
while we do not have it, supreme beings do not exist for us. Period.
