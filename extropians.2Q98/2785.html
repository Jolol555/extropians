Although I don't agree with all of his conclusions, David Brin makes a pretty
good survey of issues of privacy and tranparency in his book, "The Transparent
Society". Also, the Foresight Institute is carrying on a web-based discussion
of Brin's book, and the general subject of privacy and transparency at:

http://crit.org/http://crit.org/openness

(Note that that IS the right URL -- it looks funny, but you'll see why when
you check the link, if you're not already familiar with Foresight's neat web
commentary tool, the Crit Moderator.)
