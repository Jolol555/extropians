You might want to check out Alexa (alexa.com). It is a service
that you install on your machine to watch where you browse to.
Provides a list of "paths" that other people visiting the
site you're at have taken from there so you can see what
might be related, and it can retrieve old copies of sites
from their big (>12TB) archive if you hit a 404. It is
completely anonymous.
