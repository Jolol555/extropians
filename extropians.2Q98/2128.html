D'oh! I should have recognised his name - quantum `teleportation'. As
another recent *New Scientist* article says:

Transport the piece of incomplete information that
you hold about C down to where B is, and you can construct a
particle that's identical to the original C. This is the trick that
Zeilinger's team pulled off late last year and reported in Nature (vol
390, p 575).
