To whoever wrote the above: Why do you feel the need to "secede"
from the "USG" (so-called)? What *practical* freedoms do you want that you do
not know have? What prevents you from achieving what you want?
In other words, what things can you not now do that you want, or what
things are you now "forced" to do that you don't want? What prevents
you from puttinging in place the practical means to achieve the above?
