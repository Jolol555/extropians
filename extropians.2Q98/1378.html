Subject: New Austrian Journal

The premier issue of the _Quarterly Journal of Austrian
Economics_ (Volume 1, Number 1), the new official journal
of the Ludwig von Mises Institute, published by the
Transaction Periodicals Consortium at Rutgers University,
features:

The Separation of Commercial and Investment Banking:
The Morgans versus the Rockefellers
by Alexander Tabarrok

Against Fiduciary Media
Hans-Hermann Hoppe, with Joerg Guido Huelsmann
and Walter Block

The Intertemporal Adam Smith
by Roger W. Garrison

Murray Rothbard Confronts Adam Smith
by Paul B. Trescott

Murray Rothbard's Adam Smith
by Spencer J. Pack

Book Reviews of
_Socialism and War: Essays, Documents, Reviews:
The Collected Works of F.A. Hayek, Vol. 10_,
edited by Bruce Caldwell
reviewed by Robert Higgs

_Libertarians and Liberalism: Essays in Honour of
Gerard Radnitzky_,
edited by Hardy Bouillon
reviewed by Joerg Guido Huelsmann
