In case anyone is interested in chasing up some of the details from the
comfort and security of their own notepad, here are some urls:

Fiona Steinkamp, at the Koestler Parapsychology unit at Edinburgh
University, has links at

http://www.ed.ac.uk/~ejua35/parapsy.htm

>From there you can try

http://www.ed.ac.uk/~ejua35/for.htm#articles

starting, perhaps, with Bem and Honorton's paper, the discussion following
it, and Jessica Utts' and Ray Hyman's assessments for the US Govt of the
official and long-hidden `Stargate' psi program. And see also Utts' piece
on meta-analysis from *Statistical Science*.

Here's Utts' homepage:

http://www-stat.ucdavis.edu/users/utts/

And despite the occasional squishiness of THE CONSCIOUS UNIVERSE, I still
recommend a quick look (if only in the bookstore) at Dean Radin's summary
charts.
