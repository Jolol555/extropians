> I always thought ethics was about containing

I would put it very differently. Containment is a side-effect of ethics,
not its essential point. Alexander 'Sasha' Chislenko posted a nice
one-line description of ethics earlier in this discussion; the gist was
that ethics is about regulating relations between people and possessions.

That the containment effect you refer to is a side-effect and not the
intended purpose of ethics can also be seen in the fact that often it
is possible to reach a particular end by a variety of means, some ethical,
and some not.
