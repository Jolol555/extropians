The question put forward in the Subject line of this posting is not the
right one to ask. Naturally it is in the beginning of discovering the
philosophies that >H & Extropy covers that ones life will be changed.

After a while when the ideas sink in, it is more like a process of
refinement and personal development.

Those of us who are old sci-fi buffs, and especially those who have
taken part in >H & Extropy for the last ten years will find it rather
difficult to find ANY posts on any lists that will change their lives.

Luckily "rather difficult" is not the same as impossible and i have
found lots of mindcandy on this list and continues to do so. My e-mail
filter removes the most noisy participants on the list. (Imo)
