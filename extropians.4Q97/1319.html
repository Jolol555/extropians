Last week I enjoyed Carla's reading of her new book _Signal To Noise_
(HarperEdge). Carla is not only an automorphing and muting advocate, she is
an influential woman in new media, cofounder of bOING bOING, frequent
contributor to Wired and The Net.

A brief summary from the book cover:

"Carla Sinclair has fused Wired savvy with riot grrl spunk to create one of
the most original voices in new media. Now she turns to fiction with a
smart and sexy thriller that turns the culture o multimedia gulch upside
down ..."
