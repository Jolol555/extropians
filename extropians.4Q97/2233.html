_Gravitation_ (1973), by Misner, Thorne, and Wheeler is probably a bit steep
for most interested parties searching for information on wormholes. If you are
looking for excellent information on wormholes, (Kip) Thorne's 1990ish book
_Black Holes and Time Warps_ is better suited and actually includes more
decisive discussion of wormholes in general.
