After trying to think what parts of the arguments to quote, I gave up
and elided all of them, since they distract from my main question:

What difference does it make to my day-to-day existence whether or not
aliens made crop circles? If they did, should I invest my money
differently? Make a pilgrimage? Vote Republican? If they didn't,
should I fund a lawsuit? Buy real estate? Vote Republican?

People think, claim, prove, denounce, and preach all kinds of things, and
their opposites. I let them, unless it makes a difference in my life.
Conspiracies and coverups exist. So do charlatans and schizotypics. I
don't have time to try to get rid of, denounce, or even complain about,
any or all of those.

What better things could you be doing with *your* time?
