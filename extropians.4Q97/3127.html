1. Eon sends a New Year's wish....

If desire could transform
the oceans between us would wash into these blue screens
and conduct my little lifeboat to you.

Or we would become two tiny pulses of energy
darting through the net
seeking and calling in the millions of bits
dashing across times zones and winding through lighted
roadmaps of all the cities we have not visited.

2. ....and Kathryn, between bites of peanut butter toast, makes the
annual call for resolutions. Any?
