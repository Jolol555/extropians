I am probably not the best person to ask about specific mutual funds right
now. However, I would recommend a "good" balance of Emerging Growth funds,
Global funds, and Tech funds if you want strong growth. I would recommend

severely overfunded). Index funds will provide a decent return over the
long-term but may be lackluster in the short-term.
