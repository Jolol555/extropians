Attention East Coast Cryonicists and Extropians

The first annual Boston CryoFeast is set to take place December 6
, beginning at 5 p.m., hosted by Tony Reno and Roderick A. Carder-Russell.
This is a pot-luck gathering in celebration of life and it's boundless
potential, it is a gathering of cryonicists and life-extension advocates
and anyone is welcome to attend regardless of cryonics organization or
lack thereof.
We are anticipating lots of great food, intriguing conversation,
music, films such as Future Fantastic and Immortality on Ice, perhaps even
a teleconference with the West Coast CryoFeast, hosted by Natasha Vita
More, on a 61 inch screen!
The party will be held in the home of Tony Reno located at 161
Heald St, in Pepperell MA. Directions can be obtained by contacting
Roderick at rodc@shore.net or (603)433-5574. We invite you all to attend!
