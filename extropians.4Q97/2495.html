I would be interested in seeing various Extropian surveys polling the
members of this list. It would be best if this was done via e-mail or
on a web-page so we don't have to read through all the responses.

The latest gun discussion brings up the question of whether Extropians
are more likely to own guns. Extropians are discussing the Wired poll
and trying to decide if we are "connected" enough. People sometimes
wonder what computers Extropians use, how much money we make, what kind
of jobs we have, what services we offer, etc.

Maybe this could be another Extropian service on the ExI page, polls
showing extropian responses by percentage. Maybe only paid members
could actually vote, but anybody could read the results.

Aside, wasn't/isn't there an idea exchange where people voted/invested
e-cash on ideas or predictions? I have lost track of that.
