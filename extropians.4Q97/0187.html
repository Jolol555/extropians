Just to reinforce an idea:

About teleporters, a very economic way to design them is:

1. Scan the structure of the individual or object.
2. Dissociate the body of the object/individual and reutilize its matter for
other application.
3. Transmit just the structure to the new place, and there use new atoms to
reconstruct the individual/object.

It is much more economics than try to send the matter of the entire
individual/object....
