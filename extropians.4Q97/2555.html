I just received a confirmation letter from the European Space Agency (ESA)
Headquarters located in Paris telling me that the Huygens probe is in fact
carrying onboard phrases from the Extropic Art Manifesto!

"We are transhumans. Our art integrates the most eminent progression of
creativity and rationality merged by discovery...."

Out into our solar system the phrase "We are transhumans" is being spread.
It will reach the Saturnian system in 2004.

Imagine, our existence expressed by the word "transhuman" will traveling
beyond the astroid belt!
