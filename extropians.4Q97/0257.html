said "Most people think that they are thinking when they are merely

Let's try for an actual dialog, instead:

Erik, Is it your belief that monopolies are somehow different from The
State? What if they're *the same thing*? What if most monopolies stem from
a systematic violation of the Non-Aggression Principle? That would make
them evil, whether they call themselves the Federation of Slobbovian
States or FriendlyGigantoCorp... Wouldn't it?

The Non-Aggression Principle is concisely formulated as:
"that no one has the right, under any circumstances, to initiate force
against another human being, or to advocate or delegate its initiation."

What say you?
