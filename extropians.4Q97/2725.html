I get quite a few double postings to this list, and I believe that some
of you have your Reply-To: and To: entries set as in the example below.
This creates two messages for me. Please consider finding ways around
this. My email program (Netscape Communicator) has no problem using the
To: field, so how about deleting the Reply-To: entry??

Thank you
